Term Repo Auctions under Liquidity Adjustment Facility
As announced in the revised Liquidity Management Framework on August 22,
2014, Reserve Bank will conduct regular 14 day term repos four times during a
reporting fortnight. Accordingly, following term repo auctions will be conducted during
the reporting fortnight ending on October 17, 2014 (Friday):
Sl.No. Date of Auction Notified Amount Tenor Date of Reversal
(Rs.Crore) (days)
1 October 01, 2014 16,000 16 October 17, 2014
2 October 07, 2014 15,500 14 October 21, 2014
3 October 10, 2014 15,500 12 October 22, 2014
4 October 14, 2014 15,000 14 October 28, 2014
The auctions will be conducted as per the revised guidelines issued on
February 13, 2014.  Successful bidders will get the allotment at their respective bids.
