September 23, 2014
Repurchase of Government Stocks on September 25, 2014
The Government of India had notified the repurchase of its securities through
reverse auction for an aggregate amount of `20,000 crore (face value) on September
12, 2014. The first tranche of reverse auction was conducted on September 16, 2014
wherein an amount of `12,761.416 crore was accepted. It has been decided to
conduct second tranche of reverse auction on September 25, 2014 for an aggregate
amount of `8,000 crore (face value) the security-wise details of which are given as
under:
Sr No Nomenclature Date of Maturity
1
7.32% GS 2014 20-Oct-2014
2
7.56% GS 2014 3-Nov-2014
3
6.49% GS 2015 8-Jun-2015
4
7.17% GS 2015 14-Jun-2015
5
7.38% GS 2015 (conv) 3-Sep-2015
 The repurchase by the Government of India will be undertaken to prematurely
redeem the Government Stocks by utilizing surplus cash balances. The above
repurchase of the Government Stocks is purelyadhoc in nature.
 Auction for securities will be on price based auction format. The auctions will
be conducted using multiple price method. Bids for the auction should be submitted
in electronic format on the Reserve Bank of India Core Banking Solution (E-Kuber)
system on September 25, 2014 (Thursday) between 10.30 a.m. and 12.00 noon. The
result of the auctions will be announced on the same day.
 RBI reserves the right to:
Ø Decide on the quantum of purchase of individual securities.
Ø Accept less than the aggregate amount of `8,000 crore.
Ø Purchase marginally higher than the aggregate amount due to rounding off
effects.
Ø Accept or reject any or all of the offers either wholly or partially without assigning
any reason.
