January 6, 2012 
 
Investment by FIIs under PIS :  M/s. SKS Microfinance Ltd.  
  
 The Reserve Bank of India today notified that M/s. SKS Microfinance Ltd. has 
passed resolutions at the board of directors' level and a special resolution by the 
shareholders agreeing, to enhance the limit for purchase of its equity shares and 
convertible debentures by Foreign Institutional Investors (FIIs) through primary 
market and stock exchanges upto 74 per cent of its paid up capital, under the 
Portfolio Investment Scheme (PIS).
