Draft National Strategy for Financial Education:
Date for receiving Comments extended
The Reserve Bank of India, on July 16, 2012, released on its website the draft
National Strategy for Financial Education for comments and feedback
(http://www.rbi.org.in/scripts/BS_PressReleaseDisplay.aspx?prid=26847).  To enable
a large number of comments and feedback on the paper, the date for receiving
comments has been extended till September 07, 2012.  Comments may be emailed.
