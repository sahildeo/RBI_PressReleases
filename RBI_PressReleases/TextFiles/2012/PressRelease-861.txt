August 10, 2012 
Modified Directions on The Municipal Co-operative Bank Ltd., Ahmedabad 
The period of directions imposed by Reserve Bank of India (RBI) on The 
Municipal Co-operative Bank Ltd., Ahmedabad, vide its directive UBD.CO.BSD II 
No./D-70/12.21.114/2011-12 dated February 01, 2012, expiring on August 14, 2012, 
has since been further extended by six months i.e. up to February 14, 2013, subject 
to review, vide modified directive No. UBD.CO.BSD II/D-02/12.21.114/2012-13 dated 
July 25, 2012, in exercise of the powers vested under sub–section (1) of Section 35 
A of the Banking Regulation Act, 1949 (As Applicable to Co-operative Societies). 
The detailed modified directive is displayed on the bank’s premises for interested 
members of the public to peruse.
