April 23, 2012 
Investment by FIIs/NRIs/PIOs under PIS - Bank of Baroda  
The Reserve Bank of India has today notified that the aggregate share 
holdings in Bank of Baroda by Foreign Institutional Investors (FIIs)/Non Resident 
Indian (NRIs)/Persons of Indian Origins (PIOs) under the Portfolio Investment 
Scheme (PIS) have gone below the prescribed threshold limit stipulated under the 
extant FDI Policy. Hence, the restrictions placed on the purchase of shares of the 
above company are withdrawn with immediate effect.  
It will, therefore, be in order to purchase the shares of above bank in the 
secondary market on behalf of FIIs/NRIs/PIOs clients who have obtained permission 
to invest under the Portfolio Investment Scheme subject to the existing ceilings and 
guidelines in this regard.
