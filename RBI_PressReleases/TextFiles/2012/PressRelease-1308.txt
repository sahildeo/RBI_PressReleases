31 May 2012 
 
RBI places Draft Report of the Working Group to enhance secondary market 
liquidity in G-Sec and Interest Rate Derivatives Markets for Public Comments 
 
 The Reserve Bank of India has, today, placed on its website, the ‘Draft Report of 
the Working Group on Enhancing Liquidity in the Government Securities and Interest 
Rate Derivatives Markets’ for public comments. Comments on the draft report may be 
forwarded, latest by June 22, 2012, to the Chief General Manager, Internal Debt 
rd
Management Department, Reserve Bank of India, Central Office Building, 23 Floor, 
S.B.S. Road, Fort, Mumbai 400001 or emailed. 
 
 
 It may be recalled that the Reserve Bank had announced in the Monetary Policy 
Statement 2012-13 that the Draft Report of the Working Group (Chairman: Shri R 
Gandhi, ED) would be placed on the Reserve Bank’s website by end-May 2012.
