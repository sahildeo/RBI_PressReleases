    December 11, 2012 
Issue of `100 Bank notes within set letter 'L' 
The Reserve Bank of India will shortly issue `100  denomination  Bank notes 
with inset letter 'L', in both the numbering panels, in the  Mahatma Gandhi Series-
2005, bearing the  signature  of Dr. D. Subbarao, Governor , Reserve Bank of India, 
and  the year of printing 2012 printed on the reverse  of the Bank note. 
The design of these notes to be issued now is similar in all respects to the 
`100 Bank notes in Mahatma Gandhi Series- 2005, issued earlier. 
All the Bank notes in the denomination of `100 issued by the Bank in the past 
will continue to be legal tender.
