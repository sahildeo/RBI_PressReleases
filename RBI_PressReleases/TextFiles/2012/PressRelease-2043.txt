January 20, 2012 
 
Investment by FIIs/NRIs/PIOs under PIS :   
M/s. Indiabulls Infrastructure and Power Limited   
 
 The Reserve Bank of India today notified that Foreign Institutional Investors 
(FIIs)/Non Resident Indians (NRIs)/Persons of Indian Origin (PIOs) can now 
purchase under the Portfolio Investment Scheme equity shares and convertible 
debentures of M/s. Indiabulls Infrastructure and Power Limited as under: 
 
• FIIs through primary market and stock exchanges up to 100 per cent of 
the paid up capital of the company. 
  
• NRIs/PIOs through primary market and stock exchanges up to 24 per 
cent of the paid up capital of the company. 
  
M/s. Indiabulls Infrastructure and Power Limited has passed resolutions at the 
board of directors' level and a special resolution by the shareholders, to enhance the 
limit for purchase of its equity shares and convertible debentures by these investors.
