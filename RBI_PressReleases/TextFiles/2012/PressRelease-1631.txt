     April 3, 2012 
 
Revision in the Notified Amount of Government of India Treasury Bills for the 
Auction Scheduled on April 4, 2012 
 
     In view of the improved cash position of Government of India, it has been 
decided, in consultation with Government of India, to reduce the notified amount of 91 
days Treasury Bills from ` 9,000 crore to ` 6,000 crore for the auction scheduled on 
April 4, 2012. The notified amount of 364 days Government of India Treasury Bills, 
however, will remain unchanged at ` 5,000 crore.
