March 15, 2012 
The United Co-operative Bank Ltd., Ahmedabad – Penalised 
The Reserve Bank of India has imposed a monetary penalty of `5.00 lakh 
(Rupees five lakh only) on the The United Co-operative Bank Ltd., Ahmedabad, in 
exercise of powers vested in it under the provisions of Section 47(A)(1)(b) read with 
Section 46(4) of the Banking Regulation Act, 1949 (As applicable to Co-operative 
Societies), for violation of Reserve Bank of India's instructions on Know your 
customer (KYC)  norms, share linking norms, individual exposure ceiling, unsecured 
advances, submission of Cash Transaction Report (CTR) statements and the 
provision of Sections 9, 20 and 46 of the Act observed during the course of bank's 
inspection as on March 31, 2010.  
The Reserve Bank of India had issued a show cause notice to the bank, in 
response to which the bank submitted a written reply. After considering the facts of 
the case and the bank's reply in the matter, the Reserve Bank of India came to the 
conclusion that the violation was substantiated and warranted imposition of the 
penalty.
