December 14, 2012 
Sri Potti Sri Ramulu Nellore District Co-operative Central Bank Ltd., Nellore - 
Penalised 
The Reserve Bank of India has imposed a monetary penalty of `5.00 lakh  
(Rupees Five Lakh only) on Sri Potti Sri Ramulu Nellore District Co-operative Central 
Bank Ltd., Nellore, Andhra Pradesh,  in exercise of the powers vested in it under the 
provisions of Section 47A (1)(b) read with Section 46(4) of the Banking Regulation 
Act, 1949 (As applicable to Co-operative Societies), for non-adherence to Know Your 
Customer (KYC) norms and Anti Money Laundering (AML) guidelines.   
The Reserve Bank of India had issued a show cause notice to the bank in 
response to which the bank submitted a written reply. After considering the facts of 
the case, the bank's reply and personal submissions in the matter, the Reserve Bank 
of India came to the conclusion that the violation was substantiated and warranted 
imposition of the penalty.
