March 28, 2012 
The Harij Nagarik Sahakari Bank Ltd., Harij, Dist. Patan (Gujarat)  – Penalised 
The Reserve Bank of India has imposed a monetary penalty of `1.00 lakh 
(Rupees one lakh only) on The Harij Nagarik Sahakari Bank Ltd., Harij, Dist. Patan 
(Gujarat), in exercise of powers vested in it under the provisions of Section 
47(A)(1)(b) read with Section 46(4) of the Banking Regulation Act, 1949 (As 
applicable to Co-operative Societies), for violation of Reserve Bank of India's 
instructions relating to Section 20A of B.R. Act, 1949 and Anti Money Laundering 
(AML)  guidelines.  
The Reserve Bank of India had issued a show cause notice to the bank, in 
response to which the bank submitted a written reply. After considering the bank's 
reply in the matter and submission made during the personal hearing, the Reserve 
Bank of India came to the conclusion that the violation was substantiated and 
warranted imposition of the penalty.
