September 17, 2012 
RBI Releases Draft Supplementary Guidance on 
Treatment of Illiquid Positions 
The Reserve Bank of India today placed on its website, Draft Supplementary 
Guidance on “Treatment of Illiquid Positions” for comments and feedback. 
Comments/feedback on the draft supplementary guidance may be sent latest 
by October 19, 2012 by mail to the Chief General Manager-in-Charge, Reserve Bank 
of India, Department of Banking Operations and Development, Central Office, 12th 
floor, Central Office Building, Shahid Bhagat Singh Marg, Mumbai-400001 or through 
e-mail.
