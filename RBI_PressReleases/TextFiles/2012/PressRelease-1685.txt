         March 28, 2012 
Buyback of State Development Loans (SDLs) by the Government of Mizoram 
The State Government of Mizoram proposes to buyback SDLs, as listed 
below, for an aggregate amount of  ` 35 crore during the year 2011-12. The 
purchases will be carried out by the Reserve Bank of India, Internal Debt 
Management Department on behalf of the State Government. 
 
Security Maturity Date Amount to be Purchased 
(` Crore) 
9.44% Mizoram SDL 2018 August 27, 2018 35 
Accordingly, the Reserve Bank of India will purchase the SDLs from the 
secondary market in Government securities from March 28, 2012. The deals would 
settle as part of the overall secondary market trades through the Clearing 
Corporation of India Ltd. (CCIL). 
The specific details of the securities and amounts bought back for the year 
2011-12, will be announced to the market, by way of a press release, at the end of 
the operations.
