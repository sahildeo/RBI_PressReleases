Directions under Section 35A of the Banking Regulation Act, 1949 (AACS)
Shri Shivaji Sahakari Bank Ltd., Gadhinglaj, Dist. Kolhapur, Maharashtra
It is hereby notified for the information of the public that validity period of
directions imposed on Shri Shivaji Sahakari Bank Ltd., Gadhinglaj, Dist. Kolhapur
vide our directives UBD.CO.BSD-I.No.D-61/12.22.249/2011-12 dated October 24,
2011 and UBD.CO.BSD-I.No.D-79/12.22.249/2011-12 dated April 13, 2012, has
been extended for a further period of six months from October 25, 2012 to  April 24,

October 16, 2012  subject to review. The other terms and conditions remain
unchanged. A copy of the direction dated October 16, 2012 notifying the above
modification is displayed at the bank’s premises for the perusal of public.
The aforesaid modification by the Reserve Bank of India should not per-se be
construed to imply that Reserve Bank of India is satisfied of substantive
improvement in the financial position of the bank.
