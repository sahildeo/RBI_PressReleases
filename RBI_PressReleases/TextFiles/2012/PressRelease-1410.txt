Monthly Data on India’s International Trade in Services
 for the Month of March 2012
The Reserve Bank releases provisional aggregate monthly data on India's
international trade in services with a lag of 45 days. Accordingly, the data for the
month of February 2012 were released on April 13, 2012.
The exports and imports of services during the month of March 2012 are
given in the following Table.
Table: International Trade in Services
(US$ Million)
Month Receipts (Exports) Payments (Imports)
January 2012 11,027 7,332
February 2012 11,226 6,750
March 2012 12,891 9,080
Note: Data are provisional.
Monthly data on services are provisional and would undergo revision when the
Balance of Payments (BoP) data are released on a quarterly basis. Revised data on
services receipts and payments on quarterly basis (April-June 2011, July-September
2011 and October-December 2011) are available in the press release on
'Developments in India's Balance of Payments during October-December 2011'
placed on RBI website on March 30, 2012.
