RBI places on Its Site Formats for  
Opening Maiden Offices of Foreign Banks 
 
In response to the requests received from foreign banks as also to simplify 
procedures and to make information easily accessible, the Reserve Bank of India 
(RBI) has placed on its website (www.rbi.org.in), formats of applications for 
opening of maiden branch/representative offices by foreign banks. The formats 
are available in ‘Forms’ Section of the site.
