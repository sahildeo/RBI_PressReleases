December 17, 2008 
 
Six State Governments announce Auction of State Development Loans 2018 of 
Rs.4,595.170 crore on December 23, 2008 
 
The following State Governments have announced the sale of their 10-year State 
Development Loans (SDLs) for an aggregate amount of Rs.4,595.170 crore through a yield 
based auction using multiple price auction method.  
 
Sr. No. State Notified Amount 
(Rs. crore) 
1 Bihar 750.000 
2 Jharkhand 195.450 
3 Kerala 800.000 
4 Rajasthan 1,162.000 
5 Tamil Nadu 800.000 
6 West Bengal 887.720 
 Total 4,595.170 
 
Note: Government of Tamil Nadu will have the option to retain additional 
subscription upto a maximum of Rs.200.00 crore over and above their 
notified amount. 
 
The auction will be conducted by the Reserve Bank of India (RBI) at Mumbai on 
December 23, 2008 (Tuesday). Bids in the prescribed form obtainable from the Regional 
Director, Reserve Bank of India, Public Debt Office, Main Building, Fort, Mumbai- 400 001 
should be submitted to that Office on Tuesday, December 23, 2008. The competitive bids by 
the NDS members should be submitted only in electronic format using Primary Market 
Operation (PMO) module of NDS. All bids should be submitted by 12.30 P.M. on December 23, 
2008.  On the basis of bids received, RBI will determine the maximum yield at which offers will 
be accepted. 
 
 The results of the auction will be announced on December 23, 2008 and payment by 
successful bidders will be made during banking hours on December 24, 2008 (Wednesday) at 
Mumbai and at respective Regional Offices of Reserve Bank of India.  
 
The investment in Government Stocks will be reckoned as an eligible investment in 
Government Securities by banks for the purpose of Statutory Liquidity Ratio (SLR) under 
Section 24 of the Banking Regulation Act, 1949. The stocks will qualify for the ready forward 
facility.
