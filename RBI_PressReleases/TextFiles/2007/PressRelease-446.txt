New Coins of Rs.5/- in Ferritic Stainless Steel (FSS) to commemorate 
the occasion of "Golden Jubilee Celebration of Khadi and 
Village Industries Commission" 
 
The Reserve Bank of India will put into circulation New Ferritic Stainless Steel 
(FSS) coins of Rs.5/- issued by the Government of India to commemorate the occasion 
of "Golden Jubilee Celebration of Khadi and Village Industries Commission"

Security Edge : 
 
The edge of the coin shall have security edge on periphery. At the centre of the 
edge there shall be shallow groove with a design inside the two sections separated by 
blank spaces. This design shall consists of chain of beads in relief and each bead being 
followed by one inclined line in relief.  There shall be total 30 lines and 30 beads.   
 
The coin is a legal tender as provided in the Coinage Act, 1906.  The existing 
Five Rupee Coins in circulation shall also continue to be legal tender.
