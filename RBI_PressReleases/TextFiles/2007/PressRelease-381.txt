November 5, 2008 
 
Underwriting auctions for sale of “8.24 per cent Government Stock 2018”  
and sale of ”8.28 per cent Government Stock 2032” 
 
The Government of India have announced the sale (re-issue) of securities 
8.24 per cent Government Stock 2018 for a notified amount of Rs.6,000 crore 
(nominal) and 8.28 per cent Government Stock 2032 for a notified amount of 
Rs.4,000 crore (nominal) through auctions, to be held on Friday, November 7, 2008. 
The underwriting auctions for both the securities will be conducted on Thursday, 
November 6, 2008.  
 
As per the scheme of underwriting dated November 14, 2007, the amounts of 
Minimum Underwriting Commitment (MUC) and the minimum bidding commitment 
under Additional Competitive Underwriting (ACU) for the above auctions are as 
follows: 
 
                                                                                                 (Rupees in crore) 
Nomenclature of the Notified MUC amount Minimum bidding 
Security Amount per PD commitment  per PD under 
ACU auction 
8.24 per cent GS 2018  6000 167 167 
8.28 per cent GS 2032 4000 112 112 
 
The underwriting auctions will be conducted using multiple price based 
auction method. Primary Dealers (PDs) may submit their bids for ACU auctions 
electronically through NDS-PMO module between 10.30 AM to 12.30 PM on 
Thursday, November 6, 2008. 
 
  The underwriting commission will be credited to the current account of the 
respective PDs at the RBI, Fort, Mumbai on the date of issue of security.
