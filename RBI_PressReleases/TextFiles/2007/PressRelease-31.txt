Market Stabilisation Scheme- 
Repurchase of Dated Securities for Rs.10,000 crore on January 1, 2009 
 
Under the Market Stabilisation Scheme (MSS), “5.87 % Government Stock 2010” for 
Rs.5,000  crore (nominal) and “7.55% Government Stock 2010’  for Rs.5,000 crore (nominal)“ 
will be repurchased through price based auction using multiple price method. The auction will 
be conducted by the Reserve Bank of India, Mumbai Office, Fort, Mumbai on January 1, 2009 
(Thursday).          
 
Offers in the prescribed form obtainable from the Regional Director, Reserve Bank of 
India, Mumbai Office (Public Debt Office), Fort, Mumbai-400001 and RBI website 
www.rbi.org.in should be submitted to that Office on January 1, 2009 (Thursday). The NDS 
members should submit the competitive offers in electronic format using Open Market 
Operation (OMO) module of NDS. All offers should be submitted by 12.30 P.M.  
 
The result of the auction will be announced on January 1, 2009 (Thursday) and the 
payment to successful offerers will be made in the forenoon on January 5, 2009 (Monday). 
Successful offerers may note to make available the requisite amount of securities in their 
SGL account in the forenoon on January 5, 2009 (Monday).
