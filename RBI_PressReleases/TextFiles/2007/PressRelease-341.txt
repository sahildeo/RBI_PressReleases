Market Stabilization Scheme - Repurchase of Dated Securities 
 
Under the Market Stabilization Scheme (MSS), “6.65 % Government Stock 2009” for Rs.5,000  crore 
(nominal) and “5.87% GS 2010’  for Rs.5,000  crore (nominal) will be repurchased through price 
based auction using multiple price method. The auction will be conducted by the Reserve Bank of 
India, Mumbai Office, Fort, and Mumbai on November 12, 2008 (Wednesday).          
 
2. Offers in the prescribed form obtainable from the Regional Director, Reserve Bank of India, 
Mumbai Office (Public Debt Office), Fort, Mumbai-400001 and RBI website www.rbi.org.in should be 
submitted to that Office on November 12, 2008. The NDS members should submit the competitive 
offers in electronic format using Open Market Operation (OMO) module of NDS. All offers should be 
submitted by 12.30 P.M . 
 
3. The result of the auction will be announced on November 12, 2008 (Wednesday) and payment to 
successful offerers will be made during banking hours on November 17, 2008 (Monday).
