New Cupro Nickel Coin of Rs. 5 – "Jagath Guru Sree Narayana Gurudev" 
The Reserve Bank of India will shortly put into circulation new Cupro Nickel coins 
of Rs.5 issued by Government of India to commemorate "Jagath Guru Sree Narayana 
Gurudev". The coins shall conform to the following dimensions, designs and 
compositions namely: 
Denomination of Shape, Weight & Outside Metal Composition 
 the Coins diameter 
Five Rupees Circular with 23 mm diameter with Cupro – Nickel Alloy 
Security edges. Weight – 9 gms. Copper – 75% 
Nickel – 25%

Security Edge : 
The edge of the coin shall be security edge and serrated on periphery. At the 
centre of the edge there shall be shallow groove with a design inside the two sections 
separated by blank spaces. This design shall consist of chain of beads in relief and 
each bead being followed by one inclined line in relief. There shall be total 30 lines and 
30 beads. 
The Five Rupee coin is a legal tender as provided in the Coinage Act, 1906. The 
existing Five Rupee Coins in circulation shall also continue to be legal tender.
