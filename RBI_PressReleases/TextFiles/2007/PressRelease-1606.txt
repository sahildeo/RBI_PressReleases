Investment by FIIs/NRIs/PIOs under PIS :  
Canara Bank 
 
The Reserve Bank of India has today notified that Foreign Institutional 
Investors (FIIs)/Non-Resident Indians (NRIs)/Persons of Indian Origin (PIOs) 
can now purchase equity shares of Canara Bank under Portfolio Investment 
Scheme (PIS), through secondary market in India. The Reserve Bank of India 
has stated that Canara Bank has gone below the trigger limit of 20 per cent of 
its paid up capital.
