 December 4, 2008 
 
Eight State Governments announce Auction of State Development  
Loans 2018 of Rs.5,510.190 crore on December 11, 2008 
The following State Governments have announced the sale of their 10-year State 
Development Loans (SDLs) for an aggregate amount of Rs.5,510.190 crore through a yield 
based auction using multiple price auction method.  
Sr. No. State Notified Amount 
(Rs. crore) 
1 Andhra Pradesh 1,500.000 
2 Gujarat 1,000.000 
3 Manipur 200.000 
4 Punjab 702.170 
5 Tamil Nadu 600.000 
6 Sikkim 293.020 
7 Uttar Pradesh 1,000.000 
8 Uttarakhand 215.000 
 Total 5,510.190 
 
Note: Governments of Gujarat and Tamil Nadu will have the option to 
retain additional subscription upto a maximum of Rs.250.00 crore and 
Rs.150.00 crore, respectively, over and above their notified amount. 
 
The auction will be conducted by the Reserve Bank of India (RBI) at Mumbai on 
December 11, 2008 (Thursday). Bids in the prescribed form obtainable from the Regional 
Director, Reserve Bank of India, Public Debt Office, Main Building, Fort, Mumbai- 400 001 
should be submitted to that Office on Thursday, December 11, 2008. The competitive bids 
by the NDS members should be submitted only in electronic format using Primary Market 
Operation (PMO) module of NDS. All bids should be submitted by 12.30 P.M. on December 
11, 2008.  On the basis of bids received, RBI will determine the maximum yield at which 
offers will be accepted. 
 
The results of the auction will be announced on December 11, 2008 and payment by 
successful bidders will be made during banking hours on December 12, 2008 (Friday) at 
Mumbai and at respective Regional Offices of Reserve Bank of India.  
 
The investment in Government Stocks will be reckoned as an eligible investment in 
Government Securities by banks for the purpose of Statutory Liquidity Ratio (SLR) under 
Section 24 of the Banking Regulation Act, 1949. The stocks will qualify for the ready forward 
facility.
