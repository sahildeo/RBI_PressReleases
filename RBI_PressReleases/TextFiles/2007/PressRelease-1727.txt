January 22, 2008 
 
RBI releases 9th Forex Reserves Management Report 
 
The Reserve Bank of India has today placed on its website, the ninth report 
on management of foreign exchange reserves with reference to September 30, 
2007. 
 
It may be recalled that in February 2004, the Reserve Bank had 
commenced a process of compiling half yearly reports and placing them in the 
public domain with a lag of about three months for bringing about more 
transparency and also for enhancing the level of disclosure. The first such report 
with reference to September 30, 2003 was released on February 3, 2004.
