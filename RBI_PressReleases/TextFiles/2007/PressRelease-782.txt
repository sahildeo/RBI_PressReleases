Underwriting auctions for “8.24 per cent Government Stock 2027”  
 
In the underwriting auction for “8.24 per cent Government Stock 2027” 
conducted on August 21, 2008, the Reserve Bank of India has set the cut-off rate 
1.78 paise per Rs. 100 for underwriting commission payable to Primary Dealers. 
 
Auction for the above security will be held on August 22, 2008.
