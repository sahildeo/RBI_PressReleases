Investment by FIIs/NRIs/PIOs under PIS : Vijaya Bank  
 
       The Reserve Bank of India has today notified that under Portfolio 
Investment Scheme (PIS) on behalf of Foreign Institutional Investors 
(FIIs)/Non-Resident Indians (NRIs)/Persons of Indian Origin (PIOs) further 
purchases should be made of equity shares of Vijaya Bank, through 
secondary market in India. The Reserve Bank has stated that Vijaya Bank, 
has gone below the trigger limit of 20% of their paid up capital.
