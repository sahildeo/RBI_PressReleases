July 25, 2008 
Three State Governments announce Auction of  
State Development Loans 2018 on July 31 for Rs.2,100 crore 
 
The following State Governments have announced the sale of 10-year State Development 
Loans for an aggregate amount of Rs. 2,100.000 crore through a yield based auction using multiple 
price auction method.  
 
Sr. No. State Notified Amount 
(Rs. crore) 
1 Andhra Pradesh 1,000.000 
2 Kerala 300.000 
3 West Bengal 800.000 
 Total  2,100.000 
 
The auction will be conducted by the Reserve Bank of India (RBI) at Mumbai on July 31, 2008 
(Thursday). Bids in the prescribed form obtainable from the Regional Director, Reserve Bank of India, 
Public Debt Office, Main Building, Fort, Mumbai- 400 001 should be submitted to that Office on 
Thursday, July 31, 2008. The competitive bids by the NDS members should be submitted only in 
electronic format using Primary Market Operation (PMO) module of NDS. All bids should be submitted 
by 12.30 P.M. on July 31, 2008.  On the basis of bids received, RBI will determine the maximum yield 
at which offers will be accepted. 
 
The results of the auction will be announced on July 31, 2008 and payment by successful 
bidders will be made during banking hours on August 1, 2008 at Mumbai and at respective Regional 
Offices of Reserve Bank of India.  
 
The investment in Government Stocks will be reckoned as an eligible investment in 
Government Securities by banks for the purpose of Statutory Liquidity Ratio (SLR) under Section 24 
of the Banking Regulation Act, 1949. The stocks will qualify for the ready forward facility. 
Bids for underwriting of the West Bengal Government Stock under the auction can be 
submitted by the ‘Primary Dealers’ up to 12.30 P.M. on July 30, 2008 (Wednesday) using PMO 
module of NDS.
