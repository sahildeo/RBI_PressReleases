November 28, 2008 
Financial Markets to function As Usual Today 
Transactions and settlements in Government securities, foreign exchange and 
money markets will take place as usual today. Auctions under the Liquidity Adjustment 
Facility (LAF) will also be conducted as per schedule. Reversals of transactions under the 
LAF auctions conducted on Wednesday and Thursday will take place today. Real Time 
Gross Settlement (RTGS), national Electronic Funds Transfer (NEFT) and clearing houses 
will also function as usual. 
 Alpana Killawala
