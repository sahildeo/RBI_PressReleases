December 22, 2008 
 Statistical Tables Relating  to Banks in India 2007-2008  
The Reserve Bank of India today published “Statistical Tables Relating to Banks in India 
2007-2008”. The publication presents detailed tables on assets and liabilities and earning and 
expenses of Scheduled Commercial Banks for 2007-08. In addition to detailed bank-wise 
tables, the publication provides summary tables giving aggregated data for bank groups as well 
as for all banks. 
The publication also provides data on important banking parameters like maturity and 
ownership pattern of deposits, sector-wise bank credit, investment pattern, state-wise 
distribution of employees, insured deposits, major items of priority sector lending both at state-
wise and bank-wise, monthly data on sector-wise and industry-wise deployment of credit, Non 
Performing Assets (NPAs), bank-wise data on loans subjected to restructuring, corporate debt 
restructured and maturity profile of selected items of the balance sheet at bank group level. In 
addition, the publication also presents the list of amalgamated regional rural banks and bank-
wise list of foreign offices of Indian scheduled commercial banks. 
 The hard copy of the publication is priced at Rs.200 (normal) and Rs.250 (inclusive of 
postage) for inland, US$20 (inclusive of registered airmail) and US$80 (inclusive of courier 
charges) for abroad. The publication is available in CD-ROM format. The CD-ROM, besides 
providing all the tables, graphs and maps given in the publication, provides inter-active data 
querying facility. The CD-ROM is priced at Rs.225 (normal), Rs.250 (inclusive of postage) for 
inland and US$15 (inclusive of registered airmail) and US$45 (inclusive of courier charges) for 
abroad. The publication is also made available on www.rbi.org.in 
 Queries and suggestions on the publication may be sent by email. Copies of the volume
