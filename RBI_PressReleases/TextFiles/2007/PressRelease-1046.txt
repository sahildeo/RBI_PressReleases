June 23, 2008 
 
Three State Governments announce  
Auction of State Development Loans 2018 
 
The following State Governments have announced the sale of 10-year State 
Development Loans for an aggregate amount of Rs.2,300.000 crore through a yield 
based auction using multiple price auction method.  
 
Sr. State Notified Amount 
No. (Rs. crore) 
1 Andhra Pradesh 500.000 
2 Uttar Pradesh  1,000.000 
3 West Bengal 800.000 
 Total 2,300.000 
 
The auction will be conducted by the Reserve Bank of India (RBI) at Mumbai 
on June 27, 2008 (Friday). Bids in the prescribed form obtainable from the Regional 
Director, Reserve Bank of India, Public Debt Office, Main Building, Fort, Mumbai- 400 
001 should be submitted to that Office on Friday, June 27, 2008. The competitive 
bids by the NDS members should be submitted only in electronic format using 
Primary Market Operation (PMO) module of NDS. All bids should be submitted by 
12.30 p.m. on June 27, 2008.  On the basis of bids received, RBI will determine the 
maximum yield at which offers will be accepted. 
 
The results of the auction will be announced on June 27, 2008 and payment 
by successful bidders will be made during banking hours on June 30, 2008 at 
Mumbai and at respective Regional Offices of Reserve Bank of India.  
 
The investment in Government Stocks will be reckoned as an eligible 
investment in Government Securities by banks for the purpose of Statutory Liquidity 
Ratio (SLR) under Section 24 of the Banking Regulation Act, 1949. The stocks will 
qualify for the ready forward facility. 
Bids for underwriting of the West Bengal Government Stock under the auction 
can be submitted by the ‘Primary Dealers’ up to 12.30 P.M. on June 26, 2008 
(Thursday) using PMO module of NDS.
