September 10, 2008 
  
Investment by Foreign Institutional Investors - 
Monitoring of Overall Ceiling : M/s Brandhouse Retails Limited    
 
The Reserve Bank India has notified on September 10, 2008 that no further purchases of 
shares of M/s Brandhouse Retails Limited, should be made through stock exchanges in India on 
behalf of FIIs. The Reserve Bank of India has stated that the aggregate share holding of Foreign 
Institutional Investors (FIIs) under Portfolio Investment Scheme (PIS) in M/s Brandhouse Retails 
Limited, has reached the overall limit of 24 per cent of its paid up equity capital.  Therefore, the 
company has been placed in the ban list for Foreign Institutional Investment under PIS.
