March 19, 2008 
Two More State Governments announce 
Auctions of State Development Loans 2018 for Rs.123.499 crore  
 
The following State Governments have also announced the sale of 10-
year State Development Loans for an aggregate amount of Rs.123.499 crore 
through a yield based auction using multiple price auction method.  
 
Sr. No. States/ Union Territory Notified Amount 
(Rs. crore) 
1 Arunachal Pradesh 8.849
2 Jammu & Kashmir 114.650
 Total 123.499
 
Earlier, on March 18, 2008, Ten State Governments/Union Territories had 
through a Press Release issued by the Reserve Bank of India announced their 
intention to raise an aggregate amount of Rs.3,246.939 crore through sale of 10-
Year State Development Loans. Thus, 12 State Governments/ Union Territory 
propose to raise an aggregate amount of Rs.3,370.438 crore in the auction 
scheduled on March 26, 2008.  All other terms and conditions mentioned in the 
Press Release dated March 18, 2008 remain unchanged.
