November 27, 2008  
No Transactions in G-Secs, Forex and Money Markets Today; 
Today’s LAF Auctions as per Schedule; Settlement Services Available 
There will be no transactions and settlements in Government securities, foreign 
exchange and money markets today. Settlement of all outstanding transactions will 
accordingly get postponed to the next working day, November 28, 2008. Transactions under 
Liquidity Adjustment Facility (LAF) conducted on November 26, 2008 will accordingly be 
reversed on November 28, 2008. 
Notwithstanding this, the Reserve Bank will conduct the LAF auctions today as per schedule. 
RTGS/NEFT are open and are functioning normally and settlement services will be 
available. 
 
 
Alpana Killawala 
Chief General Manager
