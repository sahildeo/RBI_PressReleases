Investment by FIIs and NRIs/PIOs  :  
M/s Networth Stock Broking Limited 
 
 The Reserve Bank of India has notified today that M/s Networth Stock Broking 
Limited has passed resolution at its Board of Directors level and Extra-Ordinary 
General Meeting that equity shares or each series of convertible debentures under 
Portfolio Investment Scheme can be purchased through primary markets or stock 
exchanges in India upto the limits specified against each category as mentioned 
below : 
 
1. Investment by FIIs upto   : 49 per cent 
2. Investment by NRIs/PIOs upto  : 24  per cent
