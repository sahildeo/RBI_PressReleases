February 11, 2008 
 
Thirteen State Governments announce Auctions of State Development  
Loans 2018 for Rs. Rs. 7,778.980 crore on February 15 
 
The following State Governments have announced the sale of 10-year State 
Development Loans for an aggregate amount of Rs. 7,778.980 crore through a yield 
based auction using multiple price auction method.  
 
Sr. No. State Notified Amount (Rs. crore) 
1 Andhra Pradesh 800.000 
2 Arunachal Pradesh 105.843 
3 Himachal Pradesh 274.013 
4 Kerala 637.228 
5 Maharashtra 2,350.000 
6 Manipur 124.375 
7 Meghalaya 50.000 
8 Nagaland 77.093 
9 Punjab 500.000 
10 Rajasthan 422.628 
11 Sikkim 137.800 
12 Tamil Nadu 800.000 
13 Uttar Pradesh 1,500.000 
 Total 7,778.980 
 
Note: Government of Tamil Nadu will have the option to retain additional 
subscription upto a maximum of Rs.200.00 crore over and above the notified 
amount.  
 
The auction will be conducted by the Reserve Bank of India (RBI) at Mumbai 
on February 15, 2008 (Friday). Bids in the prescribed form obtainable from the 
Regional Director, Reserve Bank of India, Mumbai Office (Public Debt Office), Fort, 
Mumbai- 400 001 should be submitted to that Office on Friday, February 15, 2008. 
The competitive bids by the NDS members should be submitted only in electronic 
format using Primary Market Operation (PMO) module of NDS. All bids should be 
submitted by 12.30 P.M. on February 15, 2008.  On the basis of bids received, RBI 
will determine the maximum yield at which offers will be accepted. 
 
The results of the auction will be announced on February 15, 2008 and 
payment by successful bidders will be during banking hours on February 18, 2008 at 
Mumbai and at respective Regional Offices of Reserve Bank of India. The stocks will 
qualify for the ready forward facility.
