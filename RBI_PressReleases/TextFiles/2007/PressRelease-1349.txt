April 16, 2008 
Four State Governments announce  
Auctions of State Development Loans 2018 
The following State Governments have announced the sale of 10-year State Development Loans 
for an aggregate amount of Rs. 2,648.000 crore through a yield based auction using multiple price 
auction method.  
 
Sr. No. State Notified Amount 
(Rs. crore) 
1 Kerala 385.000 
2 Nagaland 160.000 
3 Uttarakhand 250.000 
4 West Bengal 1,853.000 
 Total 2,648.000 
 
The auction will be conducted by the Reserve Bank of India (RBI) at Mumbai on April 22, 2008 
(Tuesday). Bids in the prescribed form obtainable from the Regional Director, Reserve Bank of India, 
Mumbai Office (Public Debt Office), Fort, Mumbai- 400 001 should be submitted to that Office on 
Tuesday, April 22, 2008. The competitive bids by the NDS members should be submitted only in 
electronic format using Primary Market Operation (PMO) module of NDS. All bids should be submitted by 
12.30 P.M. on April 22, 2008.  On the basis of bids received, RBI will determine the maximum yield at 
which offers will be accepted. 
 
  The results of the auction will be announced on April 22, 2008 and payment by successful 
bidders will be made during banking hours on April 23, 2008 at Mumbai and at respective Regional 
Offices of Reserve Bank of India.  
 
The investment in Government Stocks will be reckoned as an eligible investment in Government 
Securities by banks for the purpose of Statutory Liquidity Ratio (SLR) under Section 24 of the Banking 
Regulation Act, 1949. The stocks will qualify for the ready forward facility. 
 
Bids for underwriting of the West Bengal Government Stock under the auction can be submitted 
by the ‘Primary Dealers’ up to 12.30 P.M. on April 21, 2008 (Monday) using PMO module of NDS and 
physical bids to the Regional Director, Reserve Bank of India, Public Debt Office, Fort, Mumbai-400 001.
