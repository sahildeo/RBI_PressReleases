March 3, 2008 
 
Reserve Bank Cancels Certificate of Registration of  
Coastal Construction Leasing Private Limited 
 The Reserve Bank of India has on February 11, 2008 cancelled the certificate of 
registration granted to Coastal Construction Leasing Private Limited having its 
registered office at # 201, Regency House, 680, Somajiguda, Hyderabad – 500082 
(since shifted to 4th Floor, Hotel Green Park, 7-1-26, Greenlands, Begumpet, 
Hyderabad - 500016) as the company has opted to exit from the business of a non-
banking financial institution. Following cancellation of the registration certificate, Coastal 
Construction Leasing Private Limited cannot transact the business of a non-banking 
financial institution.  
 In exercise of the powers conferred under Section 45-IA (6) of the Reserve Bank 
of India Act, 1934, the Reserve Bank can cancel the registration certificate of a non-
banking financial institution. The business of a non-banking financial institution is 
defined in clause (a) of Section 45-I of the Reserve Bank of India Act, 1934.
