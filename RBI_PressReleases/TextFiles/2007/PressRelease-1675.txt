Investment by FIIs under PIS – Karur Vysya Bank Ltd. 
 
       The Reserve Bank of India has today notified that no further purchases of 
equity shares of the Karur Vysya Bank Ltd. should be made on behalf of 
Foreign Institutional Investors (FIIs) under Portfolio Investment Scheme (PIS) 
through primary/secondary markets in India without obtaining the prior 
permission of the Reserve Bank. The Reserve Bank has stated that FII 
investments of this company has reached the 22 per cent of its paid up 
capital.
