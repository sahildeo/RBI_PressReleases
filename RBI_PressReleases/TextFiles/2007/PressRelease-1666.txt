Investment by FIIs under PIS :  
M/s PTC India Limited 
 
 The Reserve Bank of India has today notified that under Portfolio 
Investment Scheme (PIS), Foreign Institutional Investors (FIIs) can now 
purchase equity shares and convertible debentures of M/s PTC India Limited, 
through primary markets and stock exchanges in India. The purchases can be 
made upto 60 per cent of the paid up capital of the company, as it has passed 
the resolutions to this effect at the Board of Directors' level and the Extra-
Ordinary General Body Meeting.
