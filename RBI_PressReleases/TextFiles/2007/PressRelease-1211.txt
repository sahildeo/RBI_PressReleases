May 20, 2008  
 
RBI approves CBoP – HDFC Bank Merger 
 
The Reserve Bank of India has sanctioned the Scheme of Amalgamation of 
Centurion Bank of Punjab Ltd. with HDFC Bank Ltd. The Scheme has been sanctioned 
in exercise of the powers contained in Sub-section (4) of Section 44A of the Banking 
Regulation Act, 1949. The Scheme will come into force with effect from May 23, 2008. 
All the branches of Centurion Bank of Punjab Ltd. will function as branches of HDFC 
Bank Ltd. with effect from May 23, 2008.
