New coins of Rs.5/- in Ferritic stainless steel (FSS) to commemorate the occasion 
of "Lal Bahadur Shastri Birth Centenary, 1904 - 2004" 
 
The Reserve Bank of India will put into circulation New Ferritic Stainless Steel 
(FSS) coins of Rs.5/- issued by the Government of India to commemorate the occasion 
of  "Lal Bahadur Shastri Birth Centenary, 1904 - 2004".

Security edge for Five Rupees :
 
 
The edge of the coin shall be security edged on periphery.  At the centre of the 
edge there shall be groove with a design inside the two section separated by blank 
spaces.  This design shall consist of chain of beads in relief and each bead being 
followed by one inclined line in relief.  There shall be total 30 lines and 30 beads.   
 
The coin is a legal tender as provided in the Coinage Act, 1906.  The existing Five 
Rupee Coins in circulation shall also continue to be legal tender.
