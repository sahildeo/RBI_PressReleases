New Coins of Rs.5/- in Ferritic Stainless Steel (FSS) to commemorate the event 
of  "The First War of Independence" 
 
The Reserve Bank of India will put into circulation New Ferritic Stainless Steel (FSS) 
coins of Rs.5/- issued by the Government of India to commemorate the event of  " The First 
War of Independence "

Security Edge : 
 
The edge of the coin shall have security edge on the periphery.  At the centre of the 
edge there shall be shallow groove with a design inside the two sections separated by blank 
spaces. This design shall consist of chain of beads in relief and each bead being followed by 
one inclined line in relief.  There shall be total 30 lines and 30 beads.   
 
The coin is a legal tender as provided in the Coinage Act, 1906.  The existing Five 
Rupees Coins in circulation shall also continue to be legal tender.
