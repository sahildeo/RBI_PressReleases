March 03, 2008 
 
Four State Governments announce Auctions of  
State Development Loans 2018 for Rs.4,349.175 crore on March 7 
 
The following State Governments have announced the sale of 10-year State 
Development Loans for an aggregate amount of Rs. 4,349.175  crore through a yield 
based auction using multiple price auction method.  
 
Sr. No. State Notified Amount (Rs. crore) 
1 Andhra Pradesh 1,500.000 
2 Gujarat 1,140.000 
3 Punjab 765.175 
4 West Bengal 944.000 
 Total 4,349.175 
 
The auction will be conducted by the Reserve Bank of India (RBI) at Mumbai 
on March 07, 2008 (Friday). Bids in the prescribed form obtainable from the Regional 
Director, Reserve Bank of India, Mumbai Office (Public Debt Office), Fort, Mumbai- 
400 001 should be submitted to that Office on Friday, March 07, 2008. The 
competitive bids by the NDS members should be submitted only in electronic format 
using Primary Market Operation (PMO) module of NDS. All bids should be submitted 
by 12.30 P.M. on March 07, 2008.  On the basis of bids received, RBI will determine 
the maximum yield at which offers will be accepted. 
 
The results of the auction will be announced on March 07, 2008 and payment 
by successful bidders will be made during banking hours on March 10, 2008 at 
Mumbai and at respective Regional Offices of Reserve Bank of India.  
 
The investment in Government Stocks will be reckoned as an eligible 
investment in Government Securities by banks for the purpose of Statutory Liquidity 
Ratio (SLR) under Section 24 of the Banking Regulation Act, 1949. The stocks will 
qualify for the ready forward facility. 
 
Bids for underwriting of the West Bengal Government Stock under the auction 
can be submitted by the ‘Primary Dealers’ up to 12.30 P.M. on March 05, 2008 
(Wednesday) using PMO module of NDS and physical bids to the Regional Director, 
Reserve Bank of India, Public Debt Office, Fort, Mumbai-400 001.
