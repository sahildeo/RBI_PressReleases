October 15, 2008  
 
Special Fixed Rate Repo under LAF  
 
 
The Reserve Bank of India shall conduct a special fixed rate term repo at 9 per cent per 
annum against eligible securities for Rs.16,500 crore today, i.e., October 15, 2008, due 
for reversal on October 29, 2008, with a view to enabling banks to meet the liquidity 
requirements of mutual funds. 
 
It may be recalled that the Reserve Bank had announced and conducted a special fixed 
rate term repo at 9 per cent per annum against eligible securities for a notified amount 
of Rs.20,000 crore on October 14, 2008, with a view to enabling banks to meet the 
liquidity requirements of mutual funds. Banks utilised Rs. 3,500 crore of this facility on 
October 14, 2008. It was further announced earlier today that the Reserve Bank shall 
conduct the special fixed rate term repo under liquidity adjustment facility every day 
until further notice up to a cumulative amount of Rs 20,000 crore for the same purpose.
