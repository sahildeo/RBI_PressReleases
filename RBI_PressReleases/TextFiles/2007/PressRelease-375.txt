November 5, 2008 
 
Eight State Governments announce Auction of  
Loans 2018 for Rs.3,320.000 crore on November 11 
The following State Governments have announced the sale of their 10-year 
State Development Loans (SDLs) for an aggregate amount of Rs.3,320.000 crore 
through a yield based auction using multiple price auction method.  
Sr. State Notified Amount 
No. (Rs. crore) 
1 Andhra Pradesh 1,000.000 
2 Goa 200.000 
3 Himachal Pradesh 200.000 
4 Jammu & Kashmir 200.000 
5 Madhya Pradesh 500.000 
6 Meghalaya 120.000 
7 Rajasthan 500.000 
8 Tamil Nadu 600.000 
 Total 3,320.000 
 
Note: Governments of Madhya Pradesh and Tamil Nadu will have the 
option to retain additional subscription upto a maximum of Rs.125.00 
crore and Rs.150.00 crore, respectively, over and above their respective 
notified amount. 
The auction will be conducted by the Reserve Bank of India (RBI) at Mumbai on 
November 11, 2008 (Tuesday). Bids in the prescribed form obtainable from the 
Regional Director, Reserve Bank of India, Public Debt Office, Main Building, Fort, 
Mumbai- 400 001 should be submitted to that Office on Tuesday, November 11, 
2008. The competitive bids by the NDS members should be submitted only in 
electronic format using Primary Market Operation (PMO) module of NDS. All bids 
should be submitted by 12.30 P.M. on November 11, 2008.  On the basis of bids 
received, RBI will determine the maximum yield at which offers will be accepted. 
The results of the auction will be announced on November 11, 2008 and 
payment by successful bidders will be made during banking hours on November 12, 
2008 (Wednesday) at Mumbai and at respective Regional Offices of Reserve Bank 
of India.  
The investment in Government Stocks will be reckoned as an eligible 
investment in Government Securities by banks for the purpose of Statutory Liquidity 
Ratio (SLR) under Section 24 of the Banking Regulation Act, 1949. The stocks will 
qualify for the ready forward facility.
