Investment by FIIs/NRIs/PIos under PIS : 
Television Eighteen India Ltd.  
 
The Reserve Bank India has notified on February 18, 2008 that no 
further purchases of equity shares of Television Eighteen India Ltd., should be 
made through stock exchanges in India on behalf of Foreign Institutional 
Investors (FIIs)/Non-Resident Indians (NRIs)/Persons of Indian Origin (PIOs) 
under Portfolio Investment Scheme (PIS) without obtaining prior clearance of 
Reserve Bank of India. The Reserve Bank of India has stated that the 
aggregate share holding of Foreign Institutional Investors (FIIs) under 
Portfolio Investment Scheme (PIS) in Television Eighteen India Ltd., has 
reached the stipulated limit of 26 per cent of its paid up capital.
