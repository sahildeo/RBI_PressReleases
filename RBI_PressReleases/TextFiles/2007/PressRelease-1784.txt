January 9, 2008 
 
Underwriting auctions for “7.99 per cent Government Stock 2017” 
and “8.33 per cent Government Stock 2036” 
 
The Government of India have announced the sale (re-issue) of “7.99 per cent 
Government Stock 2017” for a notified amount of Rs. 6,000 crore (nominal) and 
“8.33 per cent Government Stock 2036” for a notified amount of Rs. 4,000 crore 
(nominal) through auctions, to be held on January 11, 2008. The underwriting 
auctions for both the securities will be conducted on January 10, 2008. 
 
As per the ‘Revised Scheme of Underwriting Commitment and Liquidity 
Support’ dated November 14, 2007 the amounts of Minimum Underwriting 
Commitment (MUC) and the minimum bidding commitment under Additional 
Competitive Underwriting (ACU) for the above auctions are as follows: 
(in Rs. crore) 
Nomenclature of the Notified MUC amount Minimum bidding 
Security Amount per PD commitment under 
ACU amount per PD 
7.99 per cent GS 2017 6000 158 158 
8.33 per cent GS 2036 4000 106 106 
 
The underwriting auctions will be conducted using multiple price based 
auction method. Primary Dealers (PDs) may submit their bids for ACU auctions 
electronically through NDS-PMO module between 10.30 AM and 12.30 PM on 
January 10, 2008. 
 
The underwriting commission will be credited to the current account of the 
respective PDs at the RBI, Fort, Mumbai on the date of issue of security.
