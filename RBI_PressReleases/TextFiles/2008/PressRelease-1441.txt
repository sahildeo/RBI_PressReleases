March 27, 2008 
 
RBI Offices to Function As Usual on April 1, 2008 
  
 All the offices of the Reserve Bank of India, including its Mumbai and Navi 
Mumbai offices, will function as usual on Tuesday, April 1, 2008. Commercial 
banks will remain closed for public transactions on April 1, 2008 for their yearly 
closing of accounts as it is declared a holiday under the Negotiable Instruments 
Act 1881.
