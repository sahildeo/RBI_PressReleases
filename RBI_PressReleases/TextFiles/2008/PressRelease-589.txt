September 30, 2008 
Three State Governments announce Auction of State Development 
 Loans 2018 on October 7 for Rs.2,011.800 crore 
The following State Governments have announced the sale of 10-year State 
Development Loans for an aggregate amount of Rs. 2,011.800 crore through a yield based 
auction using multiple price auction method.  
 
Sr. No. State Notified Amount 
(Rs. crore) 
1 Jammu & Kashmir  211.800 
2 Kerala 800.000 
3 Uttar Pradesh 1,000.000 
 Total 2,011.800 
 
The auction will be conducted by the Reserve Bank of India (RBI) at Mumbai on 
October 7, 2008 (Tuesday). Bids in the prescribed form obtainable from the Regional 
Director, Reserve Bank of India, Public Debt Office, Main Building, Fort, Mumbai- 400 001 
should be submitted to that Office on Tuesday, October 7, 2008. The competitive bids by the 
NDS members should be submitted only in electronic format using Primary Market Operation 
(PMO) module of NDS. All bids should be submitted by 12.30 P.M. on October 7, 2008.  On 
the basis of bids received, RBI will determine the maximum yield at which offers will be 
accepted. 
The results of the auction will be announced on October 7, 2008 and payment by 
successful bidders will be made during banking hours on October 8, 2008 (Wednesday) at 
Mumbai and at respective Regional Offices of Reserve Bank of India.  
The investment in Government Stocks will be reckoned as an eligible investment in 
Government Securities by banks for the purpose of Statutory Liquidity Ratio (SLR) under 
Section 24 of the Banking Regulation Act, 1949. The stocks will qualify for the ready forward 
facility.
