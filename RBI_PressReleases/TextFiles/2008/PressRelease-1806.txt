January 3, 2008 Six State Governments announce Auctions of State Development Loans 2018 for Rs.5,833.300 crore on January 7 The following State Governments have announced the sale of 10-year State Development Loans for an aggregate amount of Rs. 5,833.300 crore through a yield based auction using multiple price auction method. Sr. No. 1 2 3 4 5 6 State Gujarat Himachal Pradesh Kerala Maharashtra Rajasthan Uttar Pradesh Total Notified Amount (Rs. crore) 1,000.000 300.000 833.300 2,000.000 600.000 1,100.000 5,833.300

The auction will be conducted by the Reserve Bank of India (RBI) at Mumbai on January 7, 2008 (Monday). Bids in the prescribed form obtainable from the Regional Director, Reserve Bank of India, Mumbai Office (Public Debt Office), Fort, Mumbai- 400 001 should be submitted to that Office on Monday, January 7, 2008. The competitive bids by the NDS members should be submitted only in electronic format using Primary Market Operation (PMO) module of NDS. All bids should be submitted by 12.30 P. M. on January 7, 2008. On the basis of bids received, RBI will determine the maximum yield at which offers will be accepted. The results of the auction will be announced on January 7, 2008 and payment by successful bidders will be during banking hours on January 8, 2008 at Mumbai and at respective Regional Offices of Reserve Bank of India. The stocks will qualify for the ready forward facility. Ajit Prasad Manager Press Release : 2007-2008/884
