June 12, 2008 
Supreme Court order on SIFCL  
 The Hon'ble Supreme Court has, by its order dated June 9, 2008, in Civil 
Appeal No.4193 of 2008 has directed, inter alia, that Sahara India Financial 
Corporation Ltd. (SIFCL) shall appear before the designated authority of the Reserve 
Bank of India on June 12, 2008 when the matter shall be heard. The hearing will be 
held in the Reserve Bank of India at 3.00 p.m. in Mumbai. A copy of the order dated 
June 9, 2008 of the Hon'ble Supreme Court is placed below.
