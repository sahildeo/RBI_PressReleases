February 15, 2008 Regulatory Framework for Mortgage Guarantee Company The Reserve Bank of India today posted on its website final Guidelines on Registration and Operations of Mortgage Guarantee Company finalised after consultation with stakeholders. The guidelines will be applicable with immediate effect. It may be recalled that the Reserve Bank first issued draft guidelines on Registration and Operations of Mortgage Guarantee Company on April 2, 2007. The draft guidelines were modified in the wake of public comments received and were placed once again on the Bank's website on January 23, 2008 for comments by a wider audience. The guidelines have been finalised on the basis of comments received. The Reserve Bank has earlier clarified that a Mortgage Guarantee Company will be treated as Non-Banking Financial Company. The Reserve Bank has today placed the prudential norms and investment norms applicable to Mortgage Guarantee Companies on the Reserve Bank's website www.rbi.org.in G.
