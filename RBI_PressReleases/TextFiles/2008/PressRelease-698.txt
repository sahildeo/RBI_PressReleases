September 10, 2008 
 
Underwriting auctions for sale (re-issue) of “8.24 per cent Government Stock 2018” and sale 
 (re-issue) of” 7.95 per cent Government Stock 2032” 
 
The Government of India have announced the sale (re-issue) of 8.24 per cent Government Stock 
2018 for a notified amount of Rs. 5,000 crore (nominal) and sale (re-issue) of 7.95 per cent Government 
Stock 2032 for a notified amount of Rs. 3,000 crore (nominal) through auctions, to be held on September 
12, 2008. The underwriting auctions for both the securities will be conducted on September 11, 2008.  
 
As per the scheme of underwriting dated November 14, 2007, the amounts of Minimum 
Underwriting Commitment (MUC) and the minimum bidding commitment under Additional Competitive 
Underwriting (ACU) for the above auctions are as follows: 
 
                                                                                                             Rs. crore 
Nomenclature of the Notified MUC amount Minimum bidding commitment  per 
Security Amount per PD PD under ACU auction 
8.24 per cent GS 2018  5000 132 132 
7.95 per cent GS 2032 3000 79 79 
 
The underwriting auctions will be conducted using multiple price based auction method. Primary 
Dealers (PDs) may submit their bids for ACU auctions electronically through NDS-PMO module between 
10.30 AM to 12.30 PM on September 11, 2008. 
 
  The underwriting commission will be credited to the current account of the respective PDs at the 
RBI, Fort, Mumbai on the date of issue of security.
