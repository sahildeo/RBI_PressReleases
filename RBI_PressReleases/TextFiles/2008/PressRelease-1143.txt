June 4, 2008 
 
Underwriting auctions for sale(re-issue) of “8.24 percent Government Stock 2018” and sale (re-sue) 
of” 7.95 percent Government Stock 2032” 
 
The Government of India have announced the sale (re-issue) of  8.24 percent Government Stock 
2018 for a notified amount of Rs. 6,000 crore (nominal) and sale (re-issue) of 7.95 per cent Government 
Stock 2032 for a notified amount of Rs. 4,000 crore (nominal) through auctions, to be held on June 6, 
2008. The underwriting auctions for both the securities will be conducted on June 5, 2008.  
 
As per the scheme of underwriting dated November 14, 2007, the amounts of Minimum 
Underwriting Commitment (MUC) and the minimum bidding commitment under Additional Competitive 
Underwriting (ACU) for the above auctions are as follows: 
Rs. crore 
Nomenclature  Notified MUC amount  Minimum bidding commitment  
of the Security Amount per PD per PD under ACU auction 
8.24 percent GS 2018  6000 158 158 
7.95 percent GS 2032 4000 106 106 
 
The underwriting auctions will be conducted using multiple price based auction method. Primary 
Dealers (PDs) may submit their bids for ACU auctions electronically through NDS-PMO module between 
10.30 AM to 12.30 PM on June 5, 2008. 
 
The underwriting commission will be credited to the current account of the respective PDs at the 
RBI, Fort, Mumbai on the date of issue of security.
