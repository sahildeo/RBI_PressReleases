March 12, 2008 
 
Reserve Bank Cancels Certificate of Registration of  
M/s Srishti Fincap Private Limited, New Delhi 
 The Reserve Bank of India has on February 14, 2008 cancelled the certificate of 
registration granted to M/s Srishti Fincap Private Limited having its registered office at 
C-359, Sector 1, Avantika, Rohini, New Delhi-110085 (new address 54, B Block, 
Mahatta Towers, Community Centre, Janakpuri, New Delhi-110058 as per internet site 
of the Ministry of Corporate Affairs, Government of India, New Delhi) for carrying on the 
business of a non-banking financial institution. Following cancellation of the registration 
certificate, M/s Srishti Fincap Private Limited cannot transact the business of a non-
banking financial institution.  
 In exercise of the powers conferred under Section 45-IA (6) of the Reserve Bank 
of India Act, 1934, the Reserve Bank can cancel the registration certificate of a non-
banking financial institution. The business of a non-banking financial institution is 
defined in clause (a) of Section 45-I of the Reserve Bank of India Act, 1934.
