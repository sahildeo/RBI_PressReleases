RESERVE BANK OF INDIA
 
April  17, 2008 
 
Investment by FIIs : M/s. Prajay Engineers Syndicate Limited   
 
 The Reserve Bank of India has today notified that that Foreign Institutional Investors (FIIs) can 
purchase equity shares and convertible debentures of M/s Prajay Engineers Syndicate Limited  
through primary market or stock exchanges in India upto 100 per cent of its paid up capital.  
 
M/s Prajay Engineers Syndicate Limited has passed resolutions at its Board of Directors  and 
Extra-Ordinary General Meeting to this effect.
