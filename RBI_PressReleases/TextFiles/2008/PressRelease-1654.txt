February 6, 2008 Underwriting auctions for "8.20 per cent Government Stock 2022" and "8.33 percent Government Stock 2036" The Government of India have announced the sale (re-issue) of "8.20 per cent Government Stock 2022" for a notified amount of Rs. 5,000 crore (nominal) and "8.33 per cent Government Stock 2036" for a notified amount of Rs. 4,000 crore (nominal) through auctions, to be held on February 8, 2008. The underwriting auctions for both the securities will be conducted on February 7, 2008. As per the scheme of underwriting dated November 14, 2007, the amounts of Minimum Underwriting Commitment (MUC) and the minimum bidding commitment under Additional Competitive Underwriting (ACU) for the above auctions are as follows: Rs. crore Nomenclature of the Security 8.20 per cent GS 2022 8.33 percent GS 2036 Notified Amount 5000 4000 MUC amount per PD 132 106 Minimum bidding commitment per PD under ACU auction 132 106

The underwriting auctions will be conducted using multiple price based auction method. Primary Dealers (PDs) may submit their bids for ACU auctions electronically through NDS-PMO module between 10.30 AM to 12.30 PM on February 7, 2008. The underwriting commission will be credited to the current account of the respective PDs at the RBI, Fort, Mumbai on the date of issue of security.
