Investment by FIIs under PIS :  
M/s. Arshiya International Limited   
 
 The Reserve Bank of India has today notified that under Portfolio 
Investment Scheme (PIS), Foreign Institutional Investors (FIIs) can now 
purchase equity shares and convertible debentures of M/s. Arshiya 
International Limited, through primary markets and stock exchanges in India. 
The purchases can be made upto 49 per cent of the paid up capital of the 
company, as it has passed resolutions to this effect at the Board of Directors' 
and the Extra-Ordinary General Meetings.
