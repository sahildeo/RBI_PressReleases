November 11, 2008 
 
Underwriting auctions for sale of “7.56 per cent Government Stock 2014”  
and ”7.95 per cent Government Stock 2032” 
 
The Government of India have announced the sale (re-issue) of securities 7.56 per cent 
Government Stock 2014 for a notified amount of Rs.6,000 crore (nominal) and 7.95 per cent 
Government Stock 2032 for a notified amount of Rs.4,000 crore (nominal) through auctions, to be 
held on Friday, November 14, 2008. The underwriting auctions for both the securities will be 
conducted on  November 12, 2008 ( November 13, 2008 being a holiday).  
 
As per the scheme of underwriting dated November 14, 2007, the amounts of Minimum 
Underwriting Commitment (MUC) and the minimum bidding commitment under Additional Competitive 
Underwriting (ACU) for the above auctions are as follows: 
 
                                                                                                 (Rupees in crore) 
Nomenclature of the Notified MUC amount Minimum bidding commitment  
Security Amount per PD per PD under ACU auction 
7.56 per cent GS 2014  6000 167 167 
7.95 per cent GS 2032 4000 112 112 
 
The underwriting auctions will be conducted using multiple price based auction method. 
Primary Dealers (PDs) may submit their bids for ACU auctions electronically through NDS-PMO 
module between 10.30 AM to 12.30 PM on Wednesday, November 12, 2008. 
 
  The underwriting commission will be credited to the current account of the respective PDs at 
the RBI, Fort, Mumbai on the date of issue of security.
