Underwriting auction for “8.24 percent Government Stock 2018” 
 
In the underwriting auction for “8.24 percent Government Stock 2018” 
conducted on July 23, 2008, the Reserve Bank of India has set the cut-off rate of 
0.82 paise per Rs.100 for underwriting commission payable to Primary Dealers.  
 
Auction for the security will be held on July 24, 2008.
