Underwriting auction for sale (re-issue) of “8.24 percent Government Stock 2018”  
 
The Government of India has announced the sale (re-issue) of 8.24 percent Government Stock 
2018 for a notified amount of Rs. 6,000 crore (nominal) through auction, to be held on July 24, 2008. The 
underwriting auction for the security will be conducted on June 23, 2008.  
 
As per the scheme of underwriting dated November 14, 2007, the amount of Minimum 
Underwriting Commitment (MUC) and the minimum bidding commitment under Additional Competitive 
Underwriting (ACU) for the above auction are as follows: 
Rs. crore 
Nomenclature of the Notified MUC amount per Minimum bidding commitment per 
Security Amount PD PD under ACU auction 
8.24 percent GS 2018  6000 158 158 
 
The underwriting auction will be conducted using multiple price based auction method. Primary 
Dealers (PDs) may submit their bids for ACU auctions electronically through NDS-PMO module between 
10.30 AM to 12.30 PM on July 23, 2008. 
 
The underwriting commission will be credited to the current account of the respective PDs at the 
RBI, Fort, Mumbai on the date of issue of security.
