December 5, 2008 
Issuance Calendar for Marketable Dated Securities 
In view of the additional expenditure following the approval of the first Supplementary 
Demand for Grants by the Parliament, it has been considered necessary by the Government of 
India to raise additional resources. Therefore, in continuation of the RBI Press Release 2008-
2009/400 dated September 26, 2008, the Government of India, in consultation with the Reserve 
Bank of India, has decided to issue marketable dated securities for an amount of Rs.45,000 
crore during the remaining period of the current fiscal year. Accordingly, an indicative calendar 
for issue of dated securities covering the period from December 1, 2008 to March 31, 2009 (as 
given below) is being issued in consultation with the Government of India.

All the auctions covered by the calendar will have the facility of non-competitive bidding 
scheme under which 5 per cent of the notified amount will be reserved for specified retail 
investors. 
Variable rate bonds may be issued depending upon market conditions. 
 As in the past, the Central Government/Reserve Bank will continue to have the flexibility 
to bring about modifications in the above calendar in terms of notified amount, issuance period, 
maturity, etc., keeping in view the emerging requirements of the Government, market conditions 
and other relevant factors after giving due notice.
