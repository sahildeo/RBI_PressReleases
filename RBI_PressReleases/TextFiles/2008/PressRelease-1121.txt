 June 9, 2008 
 
Reserve Bank Rejects Application for Certificate of Registration of  
Al-Fazal Mutual Benefit Ltd. 
 
 The Reserve Bank of India has on May 20, 2008 rejected the application for 
certificate of registration of Al-Fazal Mutual Benefit Ltd., the Ganj Plaza, 2nd Floor, 42, 
Hazarat Ganj, Lucknow-226001 for carrying on the business of a non-banking financial 
institution. Following rejection of the application for certificate of registration Al-Fazal 
Mutual Benefit Ltd., cannot transact the business of a non-banking financial institution.  
 
 The Reserve Bank can reject the application for Certificate of Registration of a 
non-banking financial company under powers conferred by Section 45-IA (6) of the 
Reserve Bank of India Act, 1934. The business of a non-banking financial institution is 
defined in clause (a) of Section 45-I of the Reserve Bank of India Act, 1934.
