October 15, 2008 
 
RBI extends Special Fixed Rate 14 Day Repo Under LAF 
  
The Reserve Bank of India announced and conducted a special fixed rate 14 day 
repo at 9 per cent per annum against eligible securities for a notified amount of Rs. 20,000 
crore yesterday, i.e., October 14, 2008 with a view to enabling banks to meet the liquidity 
requirements of mutual funds. Rs. 3,500 crore of this facility were utilised by the banks. 
 
The 14 day repo facility will now be conducted everyday until further notice up to a 
cumulative amount of Rs. 20,000 crore for the same purpose.
