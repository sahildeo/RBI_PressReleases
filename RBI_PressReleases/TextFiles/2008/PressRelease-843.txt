August 6, 2008 
Directions on Trading of Currency Futures  
in Recognised Exchanges 
The Reserve Bank of India today placed on its website the directions covering 
the framework for the trading of Currency Futures in recognised exchanges, which 
have been finalised in consultation with Securities and Exchange Board of India 
(SEBI), and representatives of various market participants.  These directions also lay 
down the prudential parameters for the participation of banks in the currency futures 
market. The directions will come into effect immediately. 
It may be recalled that as announced in the Annual Policy Statement for the 
Year 2008-09 (para 130), an RBI-SEBI Standing Technical Committee on Exchange 
Traded Currency Futures was constituted to advise on operational aspects in regard 
to trading of currency futures on recognised exchanges. The Committee submitted 
its Report on May 29, 2008, which is available on the websites of Reserve Bank and 
the SEBI.
