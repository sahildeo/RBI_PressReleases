Issue of Rs. 100 Denomination Banknotes with Inset Letter 'A' 
 
The Reserve Bank of India will shortly issue Rs. 100 denomination banknotes 
with inset letter 'A' in both numbering panels in Mahatma Gandhi Series - 2005 bearing 
the signature of Dr. Y.V. Reddy, Governor. Except for the change in the inset letter, the 
design of these notes to be issued now is similar in all respects to the banknotes in 
Mahatma Gandhi Series - 2005, with additional / new security features issued on 
August 24, 2005. All banknotes in the denomination of Rs. 100 issued by the Reserve 
Bank in the past will continue to be legal tender.
