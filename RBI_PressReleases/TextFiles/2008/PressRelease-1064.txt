Cut-off rate for underwriting for “8.24 percent Government Stock 2027” 
 
In the underwriting auction conducted on June 19, 2008 for the “8.24 
percent Government Stock 2027”, the Reserve Bank of India has set the cut-off 
rate of 16 paise per Rs. 100 for underwriting commission payable to Primary 
Dealers.  
 
Auction for the security will be held on June 20, 2008.
