December 31, 2008 
 
Underwriting auctions of “7.46 per cent Government Stock 2017” 
and “7.40 percent Government Stock 2035” 
 
The Government of India have announced the sale (reissue) of securities “7.46 per cent 
” 
Government Stock 2017 for a notified amount of Rs. 6,000 crore (nominal) and “7.40 per cent 
Government Stock 2035” for a notified amount of Rs. 4,000 crore (nominal) through auctions, 
to be held on January 2, 2009 (Friday). The underwriting auctions for both the securities will be 
conducted on January 1, 2009. 
 
 As per the scheme of underwriting dated November 14, 2007, the amounts of Minimum 
Underwriting Commitment (MUC) and the minimum bidding commitment under Additional 
Competitive Underwriting (ACU) for the above auctions are as follows:

 The underwriting auctions will be conducted using multiple price based auction method. 
Primary Dealers (PDs) may submit their bids for ACU auctions electronically through NDS-PDO 
module between 10.30 AM and 12.30 PM on January 1, 2009 (Thursday). 
 
  The underwriting commission will be credited to the current account of the respective 
PDs at the RBI, Fort, Mumbai on the date of issue of securities.
