Reserve Bank Rejects Application for Certificate of Registration of  
Samrat Mutual Benefit Ltd. 
 
 The Reserve Bank of India has on March 4, 2008 rejected the application for 
certificate of registration of Samrat Mutual Benefit Ltd., Tikonia Road, Near Roadways, 
Haldwani, Nainital for carrying on the business of a non-banking financial institution. 
Following rejection of the application for certificate of registration Samrat Mutual Benefit 
Ltd., cannot transact the business of a non-banking financial institution.  
 
 The Reserve Bank can reject the application for Certificate of Registration of a 
non-banking financial company under powers conferred by Section 45-IA (6) of the 
Reserve Bank of India Act, 1934. The business of a non-banking financial institution is 
defined in clause (a) of Section 45-I of the Reserve Bank of India Act, 1934.
