September 19, 2008 
 
Four State Governments/Union Territory announce Auction of State Development 
Loans 2018 on September 25 for Rs.1,712 crore 
 
The following State Governments/ Union Territory have announced the sale of 10-
year State Development Loans for an aggregate amount of Rs. 1,712.000 crore through a 
yield based auction using multiple price auction method. 
 
Sr. No. State Notified Amount 
(Rs. crore) 
1 Himachal Pradesh 112.000 
2 Puducherry 100.000 
3 Punjab 500.000 
4 Rajasthan 1,000.000 
 Total  1,712.000 
 
The auction will be conducted by the Reserve Bank of India (RBI) at Mumbai on 
September 25, 2008 (Thursday). Bids in the prescribed form obtainable from the Regional 
Director, Reserve Bank of India, Public Debt Office, Main Building, Fort, Mumbai- 400 001 
should be submitted to that Office on Thursday, September 25, 2008. The competitive bids 
by the NDS members should be submitted only in electronic format using Primary Market 
Operation (PMO) module of NDS. All bids should be submitted by 12.30 P.M. on September 
25, 2008.  On the basis of bids received, RBI will determine the maximum yield at which 
offers will be accepted. 
 
The results of the auction will be announced on September 25, 2008 and payment by 
successful bidders will be made during banking hours on September 26, 2008 (Friday) at 
Mumbai and at respective Regional Offices of Reserve Bank of India.  
 
The investment in Government Stocks will be reckoned as an eligible investment in 
Government Securities by banks for the purpose of Statutory Liquidity Ratio (SLR) under 
Section 24 of the Banking Regulation Act, 1949. The stocks will qualify for the ready forward 
facility.
