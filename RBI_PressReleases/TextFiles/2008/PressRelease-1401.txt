Buyback of State Development Loans (SDLs) 
by the Government of Orissa 
 
The Reserve Bank of India made secondary market purchases of State 
Development Loans (SDLs) as part of the buyback operations by the State 
Government of Orissa. As notified by the Government of Orissa on February 22, 
2008, the securities stand prematurely redeemed to the extent of the amounts 
bought back. The details of security wise amounts bought back and the current 
outstanding stock (after premature redemption of stock bought back) in these 
securities is given below.
