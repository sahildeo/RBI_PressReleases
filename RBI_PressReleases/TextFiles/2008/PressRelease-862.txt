Basic Statistical Returns of Scheduled Commercial Banks in India 
Volume-36, March 2007 
The Basic Statistical Returns of Scheduled Commercial banks in India – Volume-36, March 2007 is 
available for sale. The Department of Statistics and Information Management of Reserve Bank of India 
brings out the publication “Basic Statistical Returns of Scheduled Commercial Banks in India” annually. The 
Volume, thirty-sixth in the series, presents comprehensive data on Number of Offices, Employees, Deposits 
and Credit based on Basic Statistical Returns in respect of the offices of the scheduled commercial banks in 
India as at end-March 2007. The Volume is divided into five different Sections providing credit and deposit 
data of scheduled commercial banks with reference to various classificatory characteristics. Section 1 
present's general information on commercial banking as also the summary data on deposits and credit at all-
India and State level. Section 2 gives State/District-wise distribution of deposits and outstanding credit 
classified according to population groups and bank groups. Data on deposits by type of deposits are 
presented in Section 3. Classification of outstanding credit according to different categories such as size of 
credit limit, rate of interest, type of organisation, type of account, bank group, State and population group, 
etc. are given in Section 4. In Section 5, these are further classified according to occupation of borrower. 
District and occupation-wise distribution of outstanding credit is also given in the final section.  
The publication is also available separately on CD-ROM. Index/search allows easy navigation. The 
tables are in MS Excel formats, which are easy to download/copy.
