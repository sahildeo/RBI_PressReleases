Underwriting auctions for “7.38 per cent Government Stock 2015”  
and  "7.95 percent Government Stock 2032"

"7.95 percent Government Stock 2032" conducted on April 10, 2008, the Reserve Bank 
of India has set the cut-off rate for underwriting commission payable to Primary Dealers 
as given below.

(Rs. crore) Rs.100)
