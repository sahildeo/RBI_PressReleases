Investment by FIIs : M/s. Parekh Aluminex Limited   
 
 The Reserve Bank of India has notified today that M/s Parekh Aluminex 
Limited has passed resolutions at its Board of Directors level and Extra-Ordinary 
General Meeting that Foreign Institutional Investors (FIIs) can purchase equity 
shares and convertible debentures through primary market or stock exchanges in 
India upto 74 per cent of its paid up capital.
