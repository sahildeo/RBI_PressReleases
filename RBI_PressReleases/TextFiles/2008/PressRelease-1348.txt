Underwriting auctions for “sale of Government of India Stock of 10 years” 
and sale (reissue) of” 8.33 percent Government Stock 2036” 
 
The Government of India have announced the sale of “Government of India Stock of 10 years” for 
a notified amount of Rs. 6,000 crore (nominal) and sale (reissue) of “8.33 per cent Government Stock 
2036” for a notified amount of Rs. 4,000 crore (nominal) through auctions, to be held on April 21, 2008. 
The underwriting auctions for both the securities will be conducted on April 17, 2008.  
 
As per the scheme of underwriting dated November 14, 2007, the amounts of Minimum 
Underwriting Commitment (MUC) and the minimum bidding commitment under Additional Competitive 
Underwriting (ACU) for the above auctions are as follows:

The underwriting auctions will be conducted using multiple price based auction method. Primary 
Dealers (PDs) may submit their bids for ACU auctions electronically through NDS-PMO module between 
10.30 AM to 12.30 PM on April 17, 2008. 
 
 The underwriting commission will be credited to the current account of the respective PDs at the 
RBI, Fort, Mumbai on the date of issue of security.
