July 2, 2008 
 
Underwriting auctions for “8.24 per cent Government Stock 2018” 
and “8.28 percent Government Stock 2032” 
The Government of India have announced the sale (re-issue) of  “8.24 per cent 
Government Stock 2018” for a notified amount of Rs. 6,000 crore (nominal) and “8.28 
per cent Government Stock 2032” for a notified amount of Rs. 4,000 crore (nominal) 
through auctions, to be held on July 4, 2008. The underwriting auctions for both the 
securities will be conducted on July 3, 2008.  
As per the scheme of underwriting dated November 14, 2007, the amounts of 
Minimum Underwriting Commitment (MUC) and the minimum bidding commitment 
under Additional Competitive Underwriting (ACU) for the above auctions are as 
follows:

The underwriting auctions will be conducted using multiple price based auction 
method. Primary Dealers (PDs) may submit their bids for ACU auctions electronically 
through NDS-PMO module between 10.30 AM to 12.30 PM on July 3, 2008. 
 The underwriting commission will be credited to the current account of the 
respective PDs at the RBI, Fort, Mumbai on the date of issue of security.
