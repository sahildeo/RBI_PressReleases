September 26, 2008 
 
Half-yearly closing of banks’ accounts – September 30, 2008 
 
 The Government of Maharashtra has declared Tuesday, September 30, 
2008 as public holiday under Negotiable Instruments Act, 1881, on account of half-
yearly closing of banks’ accounts. The above holiday will not be applicable for the 
Reserve Bank of India and its offices in Mumbai and Navi Mumbai will therefore 
function normally on September 30, 2008.
