August 20, 2008 
 
Three State Governments announce Auction of State  
Development Loans 2018 of Rs.2,059.600 crore on August 26 
 
The following State Governments have announced the sale of 10-year State Development 
Loans for an aggregate amount of Rs.2,059.600 crore through a yield based auction using multiple 
price auction method.  
 
Sr. No. State Notified Amount 
(Rs. crore) 
1 Mizoram 59.600 
2 Punjab 1,000.000 
3 Uttar Pradesh 1,000.000 
 Total 2,059.600 
 
The auction will be conducted by the Reserve Bank of India (RBI) at Mumbai on August 
26, 2008 (Tuesday).  Bids in the prescribed form obtainable from the Regional Director, Reserve 
Bank of India, Public Debt Office, Main Building, Fort, Mumbai- 400 001 should be submitted to 
that Office on Tuesday, August 26, 2008. The competitive bids by the NDS members should be 
submitted only in electronic format using Primary Market Operation (PMO) module of NDS. All bids 
should be submitted by 12.30 P.M. on August 26, 2008.  On the basis of bids received, RBI will 
determine the maximum yield at which offers will be accepted. 
 
The results of the auction will be announced on August 26, 2008 and payment by 
successful bidders will be made during banking hours on August 27, 2008 at Mumbai and at 
respective Regional Offices of Reserve Bank of India. 
 
The investment in Government Stocks will be reckoned as an eligible investment in 
Government Securities by banks for the purpose of Statutory Liquidity Ratio (SLR) under Section 
24 of the Banking Regulation Act, 1949. The stocks will qualify for the ready forward facility.
