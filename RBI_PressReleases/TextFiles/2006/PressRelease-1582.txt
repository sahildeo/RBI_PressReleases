Reserve Bank Cancels Certificate of Registration of
Jeba Finance Limited, Kanyakumari
The Reserve Bank of India, has on January 31, 2006 cancelled the certificate of
registration granted to Jeba Finance Limited, having its registered office at 7/60 C, Main
Road, Anjugramam, Kanyakumari-629401 for carrying on the business of a non-banking
financial institution. Following cancellation of registration certificate, Jeba Finance
Limited, cannot transact the business of a non-banking financial institution.
Under powers conferred by Section 45-IA (6) of the Reserve Bank of India Act,
1934, the Reserve Bank can cancel the registration certificate of non-banking financial
company. The business of a non-banking financial institution is defined in clause (a) of
Section 45-I of the Reserve Bank of India Act, 1934.
