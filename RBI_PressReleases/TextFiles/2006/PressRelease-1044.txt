               June 1, 2006 
 
Government of India announce the sale of two dated securities on June 1, 2006 
 
The Government of India have announced the sale (re-issue) of "9.39 percent 
Government Stock 2011" for a notified amount of Rs 6,000 crore (nominal) through a 
price based auction using multiple price method. The Government of India have also 
announced the sale of a new 30-year Government Stock for a notified amount of 
Rs.4,000 crore through a yield based auction using uniform price method.  The 
auctions will be conducted by the Reserve Bank of India, Mumbai Office on Tuesday, 
June 6, 2006. 
 
Up to 5% of the notified amount of the sale of the stocks will be allotted to 
eligible individuals and institutions as per the Scheme for Non-Competitive Bidding 
Facility in the Auction of Government Securities for both the auctions. 
 
Bids in the prescribed form obtainable from the Regional Director, Reserve 
Bank of India, Mumbai Office, Public Debt Office, Fort, Mumbai-400 001 and RBI 
website www.rbi.org.in should be submitted to that Office on Tuesday, June 6, 2006. 
The NDS members should submit competitive as well as non-competitive bids in 
electronic format using Primary Market Operation (PMO) module of NDS. All bids 
should be submitted by 12.30 P.M.  
 
The result of the auctions will be announced on June 6, 2006 (Tuesday) and 
payment by successful bidders will be during banking hours on June 7, 2006 
(Wednesday).  
 
The Stocks will qualify for the ready forward facility. 
 
The underwriting of the Government Securities under auctions by the ‘Primary 
Dealers’ will be as per the “Revised Scheme of Underwriting and Liquidity Support” 
announced by the Reserve Bank vide circular RBI/2005-06/347 dated    April 4, 
2006. Bids for underwriting of the Additional Competitive Underwriting (ACU) portion 
can be submitted by ‘Primary Dealers’ up to 12.30 P.M. on June 5, 2006 (Monday) 
using PMO module of NDS.
