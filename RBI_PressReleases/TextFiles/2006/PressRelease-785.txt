                                                                                               July 21, 2006
Government of India announce the sale of a
dated security on July 27, 2006
The Government of India have announced the sale (re-issue) of “7.55 per cent
Government Stock 2010” for a notified amount of Rs. 4,000 crore (nominal) through
price based auction using multiple price auction method. The auction will be
conducted by the Reserve Bank of India, Mumbai Office, Fort, Mumbai on July 27,
2006 (Thursday).
Up to 5% of the notified amount of the sale of the stock will be allotted to
eligible individuals and Institutions as per the Scheme for Non-Competitive Bidding
Facility in the Auction of Government Securities.
Bids in the prescribed form obtainable from the Regional Director, Reserve
Bank of India, Mumbai Office (Public Debt Office), Fort, Mumbai-400 001 and RBI
website www.rbi.org.in should be submitted to that Office on July 27, 2006. The NDS
members should submit competitive as well as non-competitive bids in electronic
format using Primary Market Operation (PMO) module of NDS. All bids should be
submitted by 12.30 p.m.
The result of the auction will be announced on July 27, 2006 and payment by
successful bidders will be during banking hours on July 28, 2006 (Friday).
The underwriting of the Government Security under auction by the ‘Primary
Dealers’ will be as per the “Revised Scheme of Underwriting and Liquidity
Support” announced by the Reserve Bank vide circular RBI/2005-06/347 dated April
4, 2006. Bids for underwriting of the Additional Competitive Underwriting (ACU)
portion can be submitted by ‘Primary Dealers’ up to 12.30 P.M. on July 26, 2006
(Wednesday) using PMO module of NDS.
It may be recalled that as per the indicative calendar for issuance of
marketable dated securities, the Government of India was to auction a 15-19 year
security for a notified amount of Rs. 5,000 crore during the period July 17-25, 2006.
Taking into account all relevant factors, Government of India, in consultation with the
Reserve Bank of India, have decided to auction a security of 4 years tenure for
Rs. 4,000 crore on July 27, 2006. With this reduction in notified amount, the additional
amount of Rs. 4,000 crore raised in the auction held on June 22, 2006 stands fully
adjusted.
