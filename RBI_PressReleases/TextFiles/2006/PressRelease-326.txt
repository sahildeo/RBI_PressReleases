Use/Accept 25 Paise Coins for all transactions : RBI
The Reserve Bank of India continues to receive complaints that traders,
shopkeepers and the general public in certain parts of the country are reluctant to accept
25 paise coins. It is clarified and reiterated that 25 paise coins have not been withdrawn
from circulation, are legal tender and may be validly used/accepted for all transactions.
