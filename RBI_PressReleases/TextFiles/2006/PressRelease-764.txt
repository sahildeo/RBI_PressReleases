July 26, 2006
Investment by FIIs/NRIs/PIOs under PIS : ICICI Bank
The Reserve Bank of India on July 26, 2006 has notified that further
purchases of equity shares of ICICI Bank, on behalf of Foreign Institutional
Investors (FIIs)/Non-Resident Indians (NRIs) and Persons of Indian Origin
(PIOs) through stock exchanges in India under Portfolio Investment Scheme
(PIS) would be allowed with prior clearance of the Reserve Bank. The
Reserve Bank has stated that foreign share holdings in ICICI Bank have gone
down below the limit of 74 per cent of its paid up capital.
