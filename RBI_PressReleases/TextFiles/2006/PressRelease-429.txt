Mid-term Review of the Annual Policy Statement  
for the year 2006-07 on October 31, 2006     
 
 As announced in the First Quarter Review of Annual Statement on 
Monetary Policy for the year 2006-07, Dr.Y.V. Reddy, Governor, Reserve 
Bank of India will present the Mid-term Review of Annual Policy Statement 
for 2006-07 on Tuesday, October 31, 2006.  The Governor will meet the 
chief executives of major scheduled commercial banks at 11.00 a.m. on 
that day in the Reserve Bank of India, Central Office, Mumbai.
