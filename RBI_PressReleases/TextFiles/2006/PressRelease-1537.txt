February 22, 2006
Monitoring of Foreign Investment under PIS
The Reserve Bank of India on February 22, 2006 has notified that no
further purchases of equity shares of Jagran Prakashan Limited, under
Portfolio Investment Scheme (PIS) should be made on behalf of Foreign
Institutional Investors (FIIs)/Non-Resident Indians (NRIs) and Persons of
Indian Origin (PIOs) through stock exchanges in India without obtaining its
prior approval. The Reserve Bank has stated that Jagran Prakashan Limited
has reached the caution limit of 24 per cent of its paid up capital.
