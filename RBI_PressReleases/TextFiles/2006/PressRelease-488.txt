Monitoring of investment limits of FIIs/NRIs/PIOs under PIS :
Andhra Bank
       The Reserve Bank of India has notified on September 19, 2006 that the
aggregate foreign share holdings by Foreign Institutional Investors (FIIs)/
Non-Resident Indians (NRIs)/ Persons of Indian Origin (PIOs) in Andhra
Bank has reached the limit of 18 per cent of its paid up capital. The Reserve
Bank of India has stated that further purchases of equity shares on behalf of
FIIs/NRIs/PIOs will be allowed only on obtaining its prior approval.
