Investment by FIIs/NRIs/PIOs under PIS : Television Eighteen Ltd.
The Reserve Bank of India on August 2, 2006 has notified that further
purchases of equity shares of Television Eighteen Ltd., on behalf of Foreign
Institutional Investors (FIIs)/Non-Resident Indians (NRIs) and Persons of
Indian Origin (PIOs) through stock exchanges in India under Portfolio
Investment Scheme (PIS) would be allowed only with prior clearance of the
Reserve Bank. The Reserve Bank has stated that foreign share holdings in
Television Eighteen Ltd., have reached the caution the limit of 24 per cent of
its paid up capital.
