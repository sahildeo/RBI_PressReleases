Centurion Bank of Punjab Ltd: Investment by FIIs under PIS
       The Reserve Bank of India on June 8, 2006 has notified that no further
purchases of equity shares of Centurion Bank of Punjab Ltd., should be made
on behalf of Foreign Institutional Investors (FIIs)/Non-Resident Indians (NRIs)
and Persons of Indian Origin (PIOs) through stock exchanges in India. The
Reserve Bank has stated that Centurion Bank of Punjab Ltd., has reached the
trigger limit of 72 per cent of its paid up capital.
