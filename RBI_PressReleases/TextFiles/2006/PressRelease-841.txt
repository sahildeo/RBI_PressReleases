July 12, 2006
RBI confirms Normal Functioning
In response to queries from a section of the press in the light of
yesterday's developments in Mumbai, the Reserve Bank of India stated that it
had made all arrangements yesterday to ensure uninterrupted continuation of
normal services rendered by it. The Reserve Bank added that its critical payment
systems such as, Real Time Gross Settlement (RTGS) System and Negotiated
Dealing System (NDS) would function normally today and all auctions, including
Liquidity Adjustment Facility and treasury bills, would be conducted as
scheduled. The Reserve Bank would as usual also continue to closely monitor
the financial markets, it assured.
