RBI to issue Rs.50 denomination banknotes with inset letter `E'
The Reserve Bank of India will shortly issue Rs.50 denomination
banknotes with inset letter `E' in both numbering panels in Mahatma Gandhi
Series – 2005 bearing the signature of Dr. Y.V.Reddy, Governor.  Except for
the change in inset letter, the design of these notes to be issued now is similar
in all respects to the banknotes in Mahatma Gandhi Series with
additional/new security features issued on August 24, 2005. All banknotes in
the denomination of Rs.50 issued by the Bank in the past will continue to be
legal tender.
