January 13, 2006
Two State Government announce the auction of
State Developments Loans, 2016
The following State Governments have announced the sale of 10-year State
Development Loans for an aggregate amount of Rs.482.458 crore through a yield
based auction using multiple price auction method.
Sr.No. State Notified Amount
(Rs.in crore)
1 Andhra Pradesh 316.525
2 Haryana 165.933
Total 482.458
The auction will be conducted by the Reserve Bank of India (RBI) at Mumbai on
January 19, 2006 (Thursday). Bids in the prescribed form obtainable from the Regional
Director, Reserve Bank of India, Mumbai Office (Public Debt Office). Fort, Mumbai-
400001 should be submitted to that Office on Thursday, January 19, 2006. The
competitive bids by the NDS members should be submitted only in electronic format
using NDS. All bids should be submitted by 12.30 p.m. On the basis of bids received,
RBI will determine the maximum yield at which offers will be accepted.
The results of the auction will be announced on January 19, 2006 and payment
by successful bidders will be during banking hours on January 20, 2006 (Friday) at
Mumbai and at respective Regional Offices of the Reserve Bank of India. The stocks
will qualify for ready forward facility.
