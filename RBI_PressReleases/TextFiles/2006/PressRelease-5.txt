Investment by FIIS under PIS :
Development Credit Bank Ltd.
The Reserve Bank of India has notified on December 29, 2006 that
Foreign Institutional Investors (FIIs) can now purchase under Portfolio
Investment Scheme (PIS) equity shares of Development Credit Bank Ltd.,
through primary markets and stock exchanges in India upto 49 per cent of its
paid up capital, as the bank has passed a resolution to this effect at the Board
of Directors' and a special resolution in its Extra Ordinary General Body
Meetings.
