                                                                                                       November 17, 2006
Government of India to sell dated securities
for Rs.5,000 crore on November 24
Government of India have announced the sale (re-issue) of “8.07 per cent
Government Stock 2017” for a notified amount of Rs. 5,000 crore (nominal).The
Government Stock will be sold through price based auction using multiple price
method.  The auction will be conducted by the Reserve Bank of India, Mumbai Office,
Fort, Mumbai on November 24, 2006 (Friday).
Up to 5% of the notified amount of the sale of the stock will be allotted to
eligible individuals and Institutions as per the Scheme for Non-Competitive Bidding
Facility in the Auction of Government Securities.
Bids in the prescribed form obtainable from the Regional Director, Reserve
Bank of India, Mumbai Office (Public Debt Office), Fort, Mumbai-400 001 and RBI
website www.rbi.org.in should be submitted to that Office on November 24, 2006. The
NDS members should submit competitive as well as non-competitive bids in electronic
format using Primary Market Operation (PMO) module of NDS. All bids should be
submitted by 12.30 P.M.
The result of the auction will be announced on November 24, 2006 and
payment by successful bidders will be during banking hours on November 27, 2006
(Monday).
The Stock will be eligible for “When Issued” trading for a period commencing
from November 20, 2006 to November 24, 2006 in accordance with the guidelines on
‘When Issued’ transactions in Central Government Securities’ issued by the
Reserve Bank of India vide circular No. RBI /2006-07/178 dated November 16, 2006.
The stock will qualify for the ready forward facility.
The underwriting of the Government Security under auction by the ‘Primary
Dealers’ will be as per the “Revised Scheme of Underwriting and Liquidity
Support” announced by the Reserve Bank vide circular RBI/2005-06/347 dated April
4, 2006. Bids for underwriting of the Additional Competitive Underwriting (ACU)
portion can be submitted by ‘Primary Dealers’ up to 12.30 P.M. on November 23,
2006 (Thursday) using PMO module of NDS.
