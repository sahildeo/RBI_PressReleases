Money Market Operations :
Overnight Rates and CBLO/Market Repo Volumes
The Reserve Bank of India will, with effect from tomorrow release information
on its website on overnight rates and volume for Collateralised  Borrowings and
Lending Obligations (CBLO) and Market Repo. The information will be provided as
part of the daily press release on Money Market Operations.
It may be recalled that the Annual Policy Statement for 2006-07 released on
April 18, 2006 announced in paragraph No.118 that the information on overnight
rates and volume for this information would be in addition to the call/notice money
market provided on the website on a daily basis.
