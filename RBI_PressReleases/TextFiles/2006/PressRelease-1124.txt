May 17, 2006
Investment by FIIs under PIS : The Ashok Leyland Ltd.
       The Reserve Bank of India on May 17, 2006 has notified that no further
purchases of equity shares of The Ashok Layland Ltd., should be made on
behalf of Foreign Institutional Investors (FIIs) through primary and secondary
markets in India without obtaining the prior permission of the Reserve Bank.
The Reserve Bank has stated that The Ashok Layland Ltd., has reached the
limit of 22 per cent of its paid up capital.
