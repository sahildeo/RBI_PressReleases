Monitoring of Overall Ceiling - Investment by FIIs
       The Reserve Bank of India has notified on March 28, 2006 that the
holdings of Foreign Institutional Investors (FIIs) under the portfolio investment
scheme (PIS) in the paid up capital of undernoted companies have gone
below the caution limit. It will, therefore, be in order for FIIs to purchase the
shares of these companies in the secondary market under the portfolio
investment scheme. The investments will be subject to the existing ceilings
and guidelines in this regard.
1. Reliance Capital Venture Ltd.
2. Reliance Communication Venture Ltd.
3. Reliance Energy Venture Ltd.
4. Reliance Natural Resources Ltd.
