Monitoring FIIs investment in Indian Companies : Tata Steel Ltd.
       The Reserve Bank of India has notified on October 30, 2006 that Foreign
Institutional Investors (FIIs) can now purchase under Portfolio Investment
Scheme (PIS) equity shares of Tata Steel Ltd.,  through the secondary market
in  India.  The Reserve Bank of India has stated that the holdings of FIIs in the
company have gone down below 22 per cent limit of its paid up capital.
