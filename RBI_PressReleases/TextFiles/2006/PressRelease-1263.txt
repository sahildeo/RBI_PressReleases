April 19, 2006
Monitoring of investment limits of FIIs under PIS
       The Reserve Bank of India has notified on April 19, 2006 that the
aggregate foreign share holdings by Foreign Institutional Investors (FIIs) in
the following companies have gone below the limit of 22 per cent of its paid up
capital :
1. Jagran Prakashan Pvt. Ltd.
2. PTC India Ltd.
The Reserve Bank has stated that it will be in order for FIIs to purchase
the shares of the company in the secondary markets under the portfolio
investment scheme subject to the existing ceilings and guidelines.
