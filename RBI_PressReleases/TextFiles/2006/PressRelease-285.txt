Underwriting by Primary Dealers
The Reserve Bank of India has set the cut-off rate at 0.87 paise per
Rs.100 in the underwriting auction conducted for the auction of “7.40%
Government Stock 2012” and at 1.30 paise per Rs. 100 in the underwriting
auction conducted for the auction of “7.50% Government Stock 2034” to be
held on November 3, 2006.
