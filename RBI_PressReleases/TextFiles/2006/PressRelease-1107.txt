Investment by FIIs under PIS : Prajay Engineering Work Ltd.
       The Reserve Bank of India on May 19, 2006 has notified that no further
purchases of equity shares of Prajay Engineering Work Ltd., should be made
on behalf of Foreign Institutional Investors (FIIs) through primary and
secondary markets in India without obtaining the prior permission of the
Reserve Bank. The Reserve Bank has stated that Prajay Engineering Work
Ltd., has reached the limit of 22 per cent of its paid up capital.
