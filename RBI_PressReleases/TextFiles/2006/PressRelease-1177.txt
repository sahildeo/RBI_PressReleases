Investment by FIIs/NRIs/PIOs under PIS -
Monitoring of Overall Ceiling
The Reserve Bank of India on May 5, 2006 has notified that the
aggregate share holding of Foreign Institutional Investors (FIIs)/Non-Resident
Indians (NRIs) and Persons of Indian Origin (PIOs) with Jagran Prakashan
Ltd., has reached the limit of 24 per cent of its paid up capital. The Reserve
Bank has stated that no further purchases of shares of Jagran Prakashan
Ltd., should, therefore, be made through stock exchanges in India on behalf of
FIIs/NRIs and PIOs without its prior approval.
