Investment by FIIs under PIS : Lloyd Electric & Engineering Ltd.
       The Reserve Bank of India on December 22, 2006 has notified that no
further purchases of equity shares of Lloyd Electric & Engineering Ltd., should
be made on behalf of Foreign Institutional Investors (FIIs) through primary and
secondary markets in India without obtaining the prior permission of the
Reserve Bank. The Reserve Bank has stated that Lloyd Electric &
Engineering Ltd., has reached the limit of 22 per cent of its paid up capital.
