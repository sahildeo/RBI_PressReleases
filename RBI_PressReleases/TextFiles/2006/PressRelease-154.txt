November 29, 2006
Investment by FIIs under PIS : K. Sera Sera Productions Ltd.
       The Reserve Bank of India on November 29, 2006 has notified that no
further purchases of equity shares of K. Sera Sera Productions Ltd., should
be made on behalf of Foreign Institutional Investors (FIIs) through primary and
secondary markets in India without obtaining the prior permission of the
Reserve Bank. The Reserve Bank has stated that K. Sera Sera Productions
Ltd., has reached the limit of 22 per cent of its paid up capital.
