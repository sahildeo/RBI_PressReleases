Reserve Bank of India to accept tax payments
beyond banking hours on March 31
For the convenience of taxpayers, the Reserve Bank of India has made special
arrangements.  At its Mumbai and Navi Mumbai offices, the Reserve Bank will receive
cash deposits representing taxes at the counters upto  4.00 p.m. on March 31, 2006.
Cheques/other instruments representing payment of taxes to the Government would be
received upto 6.00 p.m. on March 31, 2006. Other offices of the Reserve Bank of India
which accept government taxes wil also accept tax payments beyond banking hours on
March 31, 2006.
Instructions have also been issued to agency banks, viz., State Bank of India and
its associates, public sector banks and designated private sector banks to receive taxes
beyond normal banking hours on March 31, 2006.  Over 890 branches of commercial
banks in Mumbai are authorised to receive tax payments.
The Reserve Bank has requested taxpayers to take advantage of these facilities
provided for the financial year ending March 31, 2006.
