March 22, 2006
Nine State Governments announce the auction of
State Development Loans 2016 on March 27, 2006
The following State Governments have announced the sale of 10-year State
Development Loans for an aggregate amount of Rs.1851.262 crore through a yield
based auction using multiple price auction method.
Sr. State Notified Amount
No. (Rs. in Crore)
1 Assam 100
2 Jammu & Kashmir 75
3 Manipur 85
4 Meghalaya 50
5 Mizoram 50
6 Nagaland 45
7 Punjab 580
8 Tamilnadu 640.013
9 Uttaranchal 226.249
Total 1851.262
The auction will be conducted by the Reserve Bank of India (RBI) at Mumbai on
March 27, 2006 (Monday). Bids in the prescribed form obtainable from the Regional
Director, Reserve Bank of India, Mumbai Office (Public Debt Office), Fort, Mumbai-400
001 should be submitted to that Office on Monday, March 27, 2006. The competitive bids
by the NDS members should be submitted only in electronic format using NDS. All bids
should be submitted by 12.30 P.M. On the basis of bids received, RBI will determine the
maximum yield at which offers will be accepted.
 The results of the auction will be announced on March 27, 2006 and payment by
successful bidders will be during banking hours on March 28, 2006 (Tuesday) at Mumbai
and at respective Regional Offices of Reserve Bank of India. The stocks will qualify for
the ready forward facility.
Bids for underwriting of the Punjab and Uttaranchal Government Securities under
auction can be submitted by the ‘Primary Dealers’ up to 12.30 P.M. on March 24, 2006
(Friday) using PMO module of NDS and physical bids to the Regional Director, Reserve
Bank of India, Public Debt Office, Fort, Mumbai-400 001.
