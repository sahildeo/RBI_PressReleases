Monitoring FIIs investment in Private Sector Banks
The Reserve Bank of India has notified on March 22, 2006 that Foreign
Institutional Investors (FIIs)/Non-Resident Indians (NRI)/Persons of Indian
Origins (PIOs) can now purchase under Portfolio Investment Scheme (PIS)
equity shares and convertible debentures of The Federal Bank Ltd.,  through
primary markets and stock exchanges in India upto 49 per cent limits of its
paid up capital, as the company has passed resolutions to this effect at its
Board of Directors' and General Body Meetings.
