September 27, 2006
Investment by FIIs under PIS : Anant Raj Industries Ltd.
       The Reserve Bank of India on September 27, 2006 has notified that no
further purchases of equity shares of Anant Raj Industries Ltd., should be
made on behalf of Foreign Institutional Investors (FIIs) through primary and
secondary markets in India without obtaining the prior permission of the
Reserve Bank. The Reserve Bank has stated that Anant Raj Industries Ltd.,
has reached the limit of 22 per cent of its paid up capital.
