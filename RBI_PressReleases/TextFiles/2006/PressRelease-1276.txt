April 17, 2006
Investment by FIIs/NRIs/PIOs under PIS
       The Reserve Bank of India on April 17, 2006, has notified that no further
purchases of equity shares of Union Bank of India  should be made on behalf
of Foreign Institutional Investors (FIIs)/Non-Resident Indians (NRIs)/Persons
of Indian Origin (PIOs) through stock exchanges in India without the prior
permission of the Reserve Bank. The Reserve Bank has stated that Union
Bank of India has reached the limit of 20 per cent of its paid up capital.
