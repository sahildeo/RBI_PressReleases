January 16, 2006
Tap sale of 7.61 per cent SDL 2016 closed
The 'on-tap' sale of 7.61 per cent ten-year State Development Loan, 2016 has
been closed in respect of all the eight states, viz, Arunachal Pradesh, Gujarat, Madhya
Pradesh, Manipur, Mizoram, Nagaland, Sikkim and Tripura on receipt of the respective
target amounts.  Amounts of allotment in respect of these states are being advised to
the investors concerned.
It may be recalled that the sale opened on January 16, 2006 on behalf of eight
states for an aggregate amount of around Rs. 700 crore. Against this, applications for
an aggregate amount of about Rs. 3750 crore were received of which, subscriptions for
an aggregate amount of Rs. 700 crore are being retained.
As notified earlier, payment will be required to be made by the allottees on
January 18, 2006, in respect of subscriptions received on January 16, 2006.
