Investment by FIIs under PIS : Grasim Industries Ltd.
       The Reserve Bank of India on November 8, 2006 has notified that no
further purchases of equity shares of Grasim Industries Ltd., should be made
on behalf of Foreign Institutional Investors (FIIs) through primary and
secondary markets in India without obtaining the prior permission of the
Reserve Bank. The Reserve Bank has stated that Grasim Industries Ltd., has
reached the limit of 22 per cent of its paid up capital.
