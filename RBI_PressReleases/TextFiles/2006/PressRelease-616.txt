Monitoring FIIs investment in Indian Companies :
UTI Bank Ltd.
The Reserve Bank of India has notified on August 24, 2006 that Foreign
Institutional Investors (FIIs) can now purchase under Portfolio Investment
Scheme (PIS) equity shares of UTI Bank Ltd., through primary markets and
stock exchanges in India upto 49 per cent of its paid up capital, as the bank has
passed a resolution at its Board of Directors' and a special resolution in its
General Body Meetings to this effect.
