Monitoring FIIs investment in Indian Companies :  
Sujana Metal Products Ltd. 
 
 The Reserve Bank of India has notified on November 10, 2006 that 
Foreign Institutional Investors (FIIs) can now purchase under Portfolio 
Investment Scheme (PIS) equity shares and convertible debentures of Sujana 
Metal Products Ltd., through primary markets and stock exchanges in India 
upto 100 per cent of its paid up capital, as the company has passed resolutions 
at its Board of Directors' and General Body Meetings to this effect.
