Investment by FIIs/NRIs/PIOs under PIS :
Television Eighteen India Ltd.
The Reserve Bank of India on August 1, 2006 has notified that further
purchases of shares of Television Eighteen India Ltd., on behalf of Foreign
Institutional Investors (FIIs)/Non-Resident Indians (NRIs) and Persons of
Indian Origin (PIOs) in secondary market in India under Portfolio Investment
Scheme (PIS) would be allowed. The Reserve Bank has stated that foreign
share holdings in Television Eighteen India Ltd. have gone down below the
limit of 24 per cent of its paid up capital.
