Underwriting by Primary Dealers 
 
The Reserve Bank of India has set the cut-off rate at 0.63 paise per   
Rs.100 in the underwriting auction conducted for the auction of “7.37% 
Government Stock 2014” and at 1.50 paise per Rs. 100 in the underwriting 
auction conducted for the auction of “8.33% Government Stock 2036” to be 
held on December 8, 2006.
