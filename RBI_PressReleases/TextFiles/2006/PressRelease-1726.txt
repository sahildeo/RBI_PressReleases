Tap Sale of State Government Loans
Eight State Governments, namely, Arunachal Pradesh, Gujarat, Madhya
Pradesh, Manipur, Mizoram, Nagaland, Sikkim and Tripura have offered to sell 7.61
per cent State Development Loan, 2016 having maturity of ten years aggregating
around Rs.700 crore on January 16, 2006 by way of tap sale under the approved
market borrowing programme. The sale will be held at Mumbai for all the eight states
as also at the respective Regional Offices of the Reserve Bank of India.
The Reserve Bank of India reserves the right to close the sale during or after
close of banking hours on or after January 16, 2006 having regard to the subscription
received. The payment by the successful applicants will be made on January 18,
2006
Applications in the prescribed form obtainable from the Public Debt Offices of
the Reserve Bank of India will be received at the aforesaid office during banking
hours while the sale is open.
