External Commercial Borrowings (ECB) Policy: Clarification
The Reserve Bank of India had announced the comprehensive ECB
guidelines vide A.P. (DIR Series) Circular No. 5 dated August 1, 2005. Enquiries
have been received on whether airports and companies engaged in telecom
equipment manufacturing are eligible to raise ECB.
It is clarified that the companies in airport sector as well as those engaged in
telecom equipment manufacturing activity are eligible to raise ECB in accordance
with the existing ECB guidelines.
