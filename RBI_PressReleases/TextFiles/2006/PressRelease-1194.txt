Underwriting by Primary Dealers
The Reserve Bank of India has set the cut-off rate at Re. 0.035 (three point
five paise) per Rupees 100 and Re 0.16 (sixteen paise) per Rupees 100 in the
underwriting for the auction of “7.59 Government Stock 2016’’ and “7.50%
Government Stock 2034” respectively, to be held on May 04, 2006.
