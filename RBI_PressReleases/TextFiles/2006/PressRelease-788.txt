Monitoring FIIs Investment  : Karnataka Bank Ltd.
The Reserve Bank of India has notified on July 21, 2006 that Foreign
Institutional Investors (FIIs) can now purchase under Portfolio Investment
Scheme (PIS) equity shares and convertible debentures of Karnataka Bank
Ltd., through primary markets and stock exchanges in India upto 49 per cent
limits of its paid up capital, as the bank has passed resolutions to this effect at
its Board of Directors' and  General Body Meetings.
