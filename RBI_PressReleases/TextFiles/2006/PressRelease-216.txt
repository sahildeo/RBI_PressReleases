Monitoring FII Investments in Indian Companies :
Gateway Distriparks td.
The Reserve Bank of India has notified on November 16, 2006 that
Foreign Institutional Investors (FIIs) can now purchase under Portfolio
Investment Scheme (PIS) equity shares and convertible debentures of
Gateway Distriparks Ltd., through stock exchanges in India upto 100 per cent
of its paid up capital, as the company has passed resolutions to this effect at
its Board of Directors' and General Body Meetings.
