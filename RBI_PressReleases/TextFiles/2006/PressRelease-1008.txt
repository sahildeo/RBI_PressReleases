RBI  increases Repo/Reverse Repo Rates
On a review of current macroeconomic and overall monetary conditions,
the Reserve Bank of India has decided to increase the reverse repo rate under
the Liquidity Adjustment Facility (LAF) by 25 basis points to 5.75 per cent from
5.50 per cent with immediate effect.
Simultaneously, the repo rate under the LAF, which continues to be linked
to the reverse repo rate with a spread of 100 basis points, is also being increased
by 25 basis points to 6.75 per cent from 6.50 per cent with immediate effect.
