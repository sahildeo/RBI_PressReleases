 February 22, 2006
Twelve State Governments announce the auction of
State Development Loans 2016 on February 27, 2006
The following State Governments have announced the sale of 10-year State
Development Loans 2016 for an aggregate amount of Rs.3724.207 crore through a
yield based auction using multiple price auction method.
Sr. No. State Notified Amount
(Rs. in Crore)
1 Assam 200.000
2 Goa 50.000
3 Kerala 328.000
4 Madhya Pradesh 200.000
5 Maharashtra 286.893
6 Punjab 619.300
7 Rajasthan 500.000
8 Sikkim 65.650
9 Tamilnadu 600.000
10 Tripura 25.030
11 Uttaranchal 250.000
12 Uttar Pradesh 599.334
Total 3724.207
The auction will be conducted by the Reserve Bank of India (RBI) at Mumbai on
February 27, 2006 (Monday). Bids in the prescribed form obtainable from the Regional
Director, Reserve Bank of India, Mumbai Office (Public Debt Office), Fort, Mumbai-
400001 should be submitted to that Office on Monday, February 27, 2006. The
competitive bids by the NDS members should be submitted only in electronic format
using NDS. All bids should be submitted by 12.30 p.m. On the basis of bids received,
Reserve Bank will determine the maximum yield at which offers will be accepted.
 The results of the auction will be announced on February 27, 2006 and payment
by successful bidders will be during banking hours on February 28, 2006 (Tuesday) at
Mumbai and at respective Regional Offices of the Reserve Bank of India. The stocks
will qualify for the ready forward facility.
Bids for underwriting of the Goa, Punjab, Uttaranchal and Uttar Pradesh
Government Securities under auction may be submitted by the Primary Dealers' up to
12.30 p.m. on February 24, 2006 (Friday) to the Chief General Manager, Internal Debt
Management Department, Reserve Bank of India, Central Office, Fort, Mumbai-400001.
