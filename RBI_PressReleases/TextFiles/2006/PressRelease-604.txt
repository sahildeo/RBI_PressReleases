August 25, 2006
Three more Banks authorised to undertake PD business
Three more banks, namely Hongkong and Shanghai Banking Corporation
Limited, Bank of America, N.A. and J.P.Morgan Chase Bank, N.A., have been
authorised to undertake Primary Dealer business with effect from Monday, August 28,
2006. Concomitantly, the authorisation given to HSBC Primary Dealership (India)
Private Limited, Bank of America Securities (India) Private Limited and J.P.Morgan
Securities India Private Limited, as Primary Dealer, stands withdrawn.
The Reserve Bank of India had announced in its Annual Policy Statement for the
year 2005-06 that the permitted structure of Primary Dealership business would be
expanded to include banks, which fulfill certain minimum eligibility criteria. Accordingly,
the Reserve Bank of India had issued guidelines on February 27, 2006 on eligibility
conditions for banks to undertake Primary Dealership business departmentally.
