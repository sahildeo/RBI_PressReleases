A Profile of Banks : 2005-06
The Reserve Bank of India today released "A Profile of Banks : 2005-2006.
The publication is an attempt to provide quick and handy access to data on
scheduled commercial banks, excluding regional rural banks. The publication
covers 18 important indicators, such as, business per employee, profit per
employee, return assets, Capital to Risk Weighted Assets Ratio (CRAR) and net
Non-Performing Assets (NPA) ratio. Data are presented in a bank-centric way with
user-friendliness as the main objective. The publication contains bank-wise data for
the years 2001-02 to 2005-06. For quick comparison with the peer group, the
publication also provides averages at bank group level for 2005-06 and also at all
banks' level for 2005-06.
This publication is the second volume in the series, the first volume being
"A Profile of Banks 2004-05".
 The publication is priced at Rs.90/- (normal) and Rs.120/- (inclusive of
postage) for inland and US$55 (inclusive of courier charges) and US$20 (inclusive
of registered airmail) for abroad.
