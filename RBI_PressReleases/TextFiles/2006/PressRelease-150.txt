Investment by FIIS/NRIs/PIOs under PIS
The Reserve Bank of India has notified on November 29, 2006 that
Foreign Institutional Investors (FIIs)/Non-Resident Indians (NRIs) and
Persons of Indian Origin (PIOs) can now purchase under Portfolio Investment
Scheme (PIS) equity shares and convertible debentures of the following
compaies, through primary markets and stock exchanges in India upto
100 per cent of its paid up capital, as these companies have passed
resolutions to this effect at their  Board of Directors' and General Body
Meetings.
1. AZTEC Software and Technology Services Ltd.
2. Sujana Universal Industries Ltd.
