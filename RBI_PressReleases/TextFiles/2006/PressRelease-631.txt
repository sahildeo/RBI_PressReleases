August 21, 2006
4 State Governments announce the auction of
State Development Loans 2016
The following State Governments have announced the sale of 10-year
State Development Loans for an aggregate amount of Rs.1,050.825 crore through
a yield based auction using multiple price auction method.
Sr. State Notified Amount
No. (Rs. in Crore)
1 Assam 215.825
2 Kerala 300.00
3 Rajasthan 500.00
4 Tripura 35.00
Total 1,050.825
The auction will be conducted by the Reserve Bank of India (RBI) at
Mumbai on August 25, 2006 (Friday). Bids in the prescribed form obtainable from
the Regional Director, Reserve Bank of India, Mumbai Office (Public Debt Office),
Fort, Mumbai-400 001 should be submitted to that Office on Friday, August 25,
2006. The competitive bids by the NDS members should be submitted only in
electronic format using Primary Market Operation (PMO) module of NDS. All bids
should be submitted by 12.30 P.M. On the basis of bids received, RBI will
determine the maximum yield at which offers will be accepted.
 The results of the auction will be announced on August 25, 2006 and
payment by successful bidders will be during banking hours on August 28, 2006
(Monday) at Mumbai and at respective Regional Offices of Reserve Bank of India.
The stocks will qualify for the ready forward facility.
