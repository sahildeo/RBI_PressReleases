Monitoring of Investments by FIIs under PIS
The Reserve Bank of India on May 30, 2006 has notified that  further
purchases of equity shares of the following companies on behalf of Foreign
Institutional Investors (FIIs) through stock exchanges in India under Portfolio
Investment Scheme (PIS) would be allowed without prior clearance of the
Reserve Bank. The Reserve Bank has stated that foreign share holdings in these
companies have gone down below the limit of 22 per cent of their paid up capital.
1. The Associated Cement Companies Ltd.
2. Ashok Leyland Ltd.
3. Bharat Heavy Electricals Ltd.
