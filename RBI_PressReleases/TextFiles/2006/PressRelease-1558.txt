Monitoring FII Investments under PIS
The Reserve Bank of India has on February 17, 2006 notified that the
Foreign Institutional Investors (FIIs) holdings in the undernoted companies
have reached the limit of 22 per cent of their paid up capital. The Reserve
Bank has further notified that further purchases of equity shares of these
companies through stock exchanges in India on behalf of Foreign Institutional
Investors (FIIs) under Portfolio Investment Scheme (PIS) would be allowed
only after obtaining prior clearance of the Reserve Bank till 24 per cent limit is
reached.
1. Power Trading Corporation of India
2. Rico Auto Industries Ltd.
