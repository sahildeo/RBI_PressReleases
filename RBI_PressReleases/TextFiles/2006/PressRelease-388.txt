Underwriting auctions for “7.59 per cent Government Stock, 2016”
 and “8.33 per cent Government Stock, 2036”
The Government of India has notified sale (re-issue) of “7.59 per cent
Government Stock 2016” for an amount of Rs.6000 crore and “8.33 per cent
Government Stock 2036” for an amount of Rs. 3000 crore on October 13, 2006. The
underwriting auctions for both the stocks will be conducted on October 12, 2006.  As
per the revised scheme of Underwriting Commitment dated April 4, 2006, the
amounts of Minimum Underwriting Commitment (MUC) and Additional Competitive
Underwriting (ACU) for the above auctions are as follows:
                                                                                                       ( Rs.Crore)
Nomenclature of Notified MUC for each ACU for which

7.59% GS 2016 6000 177 2991
8.33% GS 2036 3000 89 1487
The underwriting auctions will be conducted by using multiple price based
auction method. Primary Dealers (PDs) may submit their bids for ACU auctions
electronically through NDS-PMO module between 10.30 AM to 12.30 PM on October
12, 2006.
The underwriting commission will be credited to the current account of the
respective PDs at the RBI, Fort, Mumbai on the date of issue of security.
