Underwriting auction for “8.07 per cent Government Stock, 2017” 
The Government of India has notified sale (re-issue) of “8.07 per cent 
Government Stock 2017” for an amount of Rs. 5000 crore on November 24, 2006. 
The underwriting auction for the above stock will be conducted on November 23, 
2006.  As per the revised scheme of Underwriting Commitment dated April 4, 2006, 
the amounts of Minimum Underwriting Commitment (MUC) and Additional 
Competitive Underwriting (ACU) for the above auction are as follows: 
    (Rs. Crore) 
Nomenclature of Notified MUC for ACU for which 
the Security Amount each PD bidding has to be 
 done by PDs 
collectively 
8.07% GS 2017 5000 148 2484 
The underwriting auction will be conducted by using multiple price based 
auction method. Primary Dealers (PDs) may submit their bids for ACU auction 
electronically through NDS-PMO module between 10.30 AM to 12.30 PM on 
November 23, 2006.  
The underwriting commission will be credited to the current account of the 
respective PDs at the RBI, Fort, Mumbai on the date of issue of security.
