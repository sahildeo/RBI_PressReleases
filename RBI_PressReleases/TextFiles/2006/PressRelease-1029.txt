Underwriting by Primary Dealers
The Reserve Bank of India has set the cut-off rate at Re. 0.08 (eight paise)
and Re 0.0238 (two point three eight paise) per Rupees 100 in the underwriting for
the auctions of “Government Stock of 30 years’’ and “9.39% Government Stock
2011” respectively, to be held on June 6, 2006.
