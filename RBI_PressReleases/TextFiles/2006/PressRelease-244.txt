November 10, 2006
11 State Governments announce the auction of
State Development Loans 2016
The following State Governments have announced the sale of 10-year State
Development Loans for an aggregate amount of Rs.2,431.225 crore through a yield
based auction using multiple price auction method.
Sr. State Notified Amount
No. (Rs. in Crore)

7 Nagaland 43.190
8 Punjab 243.360
9 Rajasthan 274.010
10 Sikkim 50.260
11 West Bengal 466.665
Total 2431.225
The auction will be conducted by the Reserve Bank of India (RBI) at Mumbai
on November 16, 2006 (Thursday). Bids in the prescribed form obtainable from the
Regional Director, Reserve Bank of India, Mumbai Office (Public Debt Office), Fort,
Mumbai-400 001 should be submitted to that Office on Thursday, November 16,
2006. The competitive bids by the NDS members should be submitted only in
electronic format using Primary Market Operation (PMO) module of NDS. All bids
should be submitted by 12.30 P.M. On the basis of bids received, RBI will determine
the maximum yield at which offers will be accepted.
 The results of the auction will be announced on November 16, 2006 and
payment by successful bidders will be during banking hours on November 17, 2006
(Friday) at Mumbai and at respective Regional Offices of Reserve Bank of India. The
stocks will qualify for the ready forward facility.
