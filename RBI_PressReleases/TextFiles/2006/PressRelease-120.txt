Underwriting auctions for “7.37 per cent Government Stock, 2014” 
 and “8.33 per cent Government Stock, 2036” 
 
The Government of India has notified sale (re-issue) of “7.37 per cent 
Government Stock 2014” for an amount of Rs. 5000 crore and “8.33 per cent 
Government Stock 2036” for an amount of Rs. 4000 crore on December 8, 2006. The 
underwriting auctions for both the stocks will be conducted on December 7, 2006.  
As per the revised scheme of Underwriting Commitment dated April 4, 2006, the 
amounts of Minimum Underwriting Commitment (MUC) and Additional Competitive 
Underwriting (ACU) for the above auctions are as follows: 
                   (Rs. Crore)

auction method. Primary Dealers (PDs) may submit their bids for ACU auctions 
electronically through NDS-PMO module between 10.30 AM to 12.30 PM on 
December 7, 2006.  
 
3.  The underwriting commission will be credited to the current account of the 
respective PDs at the RBI, Fort, Mumbai on the date of issue of security.
