Monitoring FIIs investment in Indian Companies :
Anant Raj Industries Ltd.
       The Reserve Bank of India has notified on December 22, 2006 that
Foreign Institutional Investors (FIIs) can now purchase under Portfolio
Investment Scheme (PIS) equity shares of Anant Raj Industries Ltd., through
the secondary market in India.  The Reserve Bank of India has stated that the
holdings of FIIs in the company have gone down below 24 per cent limit of its
paid up capital.
