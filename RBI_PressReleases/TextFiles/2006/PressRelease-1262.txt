Ways and Means Advances to the Government of India
for the financial year 2006-2007
It has been decided in consultation with the Government of India that the following
arrangements will be in force for the fiscal year 2006-07.
The WMA limits for the fiscal 2006-07 would be fixed on a quarterly basis as under:
First Quarter: April – June Rs. 20,000 crore
Second Quarter: July-September Rs.10,000 crore
Third Quarter: October- December Rs.6,000 crore
Fourth Quarter: January - March Rs.6,000 crore
The Reserve Bank would retain the flexibility to revise the limits any time in
consultation with the Government of India taking into consideration the transitional
issues and prevailing circumstances.
The interest rate on WMA will be at Repo Rate and that on Overdraft will be at
Repo  Rate plus two per cent.
