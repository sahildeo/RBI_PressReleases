February 20, 2006
GOI cancels the auction of dated security
Scheduled during February 14-22, 2006
It may be recalled that as per the calendar for issuance of Government of
India dated securities, an auction of dated security with 10 years to 14 years
tenure is scheduled during February 14-22, 2006 for Rs.5,000 crore.
On a review of the current status of Central Government finances, the
Government of India in consultation with the Reserve Bank of India have decided
to cancel the scheduled auction.
