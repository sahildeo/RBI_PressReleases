April 25, 2006
Investment by FIIs/NRIs/PIOs under PIS
       The Reserve Bank of India on April 25, 2006 has notified that no further
purchases of equity shares of Andhra Bank, should be made on behalf of
Foreign Institutional Investors (FIIs)/Non-Resident Indians (NRIs) and
Persons of Indian Origin (PIOs) under Portfolio Investment Scheme (PIS)
through stock exchanges in India without the prior permission of the Reserve
Bank. The Reserve Bank has stated that Andhra Bank has reached the limit
of 18 per cent of its paid up capital.
