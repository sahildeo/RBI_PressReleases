Underwriting by Primary Dealers
The Reserve Bank of India has set the cut-off rate at 1.39 paise per
Rs.100 in the underwriting auction conducted for the auction of “7.59%
Government Stock 2016” and at 2.70 paise per Rs. 100 in the underwriting
auction conducted for the auction of “7.50% Government Stock 2034” to be
held on September 8, 2006.
