Tata Steel Ltd.: Investment by FIIs under PIS
       The Reserve Bank of India on February 3, 2006 has notified that no
further purchases of equity shares of Tata Steel Ltd., should be made on
behalf of Foreign Institutional Investors (FIIs) through primary/secondary
markets in India without the prior permission of the Reserve Bank. The
Reserve Bank has stated that Tata Steel Ltd., has reached the limit of 22 per
cent of its paid up capital.
