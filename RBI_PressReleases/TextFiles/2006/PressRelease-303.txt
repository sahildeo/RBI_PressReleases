Investment by Foreign Institutional Investors -
Monitoring of Overall Ceiling : Sujana Metal Products Ltd.
The Reserve Bank of India on October 30, 2006 has notified that the
aggregate share holding of Foreign Institutional Investors (FIIs) in Sujana
Metal Products Ltd., has reached the overall limit of 24 per cent of its paid up
capital. The Reserve Bank has stated that no further purchases of shares of
Sujana Metal Products Ltd., should, therefore, be made through stock
exchanges in India on behalf of FIIs.
