July 25, 2006
NDS-OM ready for  ‘When Issued’ Transactions in G-Sec Market
The Reserve Bank of India has today announced the readiness of NDS-OM
platform for enabling 'When Issued' transactions in the Government securities market.
It may be recalled that the Reserve Bank had issued detailed guidelines on
'When Issued' market on May 3, 2006. It was mentioned in the guidelines that the
operations will commence after the required system is developed on NDS-OM.
'When Issued' transactions can be undertaken in Central Government securities
that are being reissued and on NDS-OM platform. 'When Issued' trading commences on
the date of announcement of an auction and ceases on the working day immediately
preceding the date of issue of securities.
Eligibility of a Government security to trade on 'When Issued' basis will be
specified in the auction notification.
