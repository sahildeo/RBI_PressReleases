May 30, 2006
Investment by FIIs/NRIs/PIOs under PIS
The Reserve Bank of India on May 30, 2006 has notified that further
purchases of equity shares of Andhra Bank, on behalf of Foreign Institutional
Investors (FIIs)/Non-Resident Indians (NRIs) and Persons of Indian Origin
(PIOs) through stock exchanges in India under Portfolio Investment Scheme
(PIS) would be allowed without prior clearance of the Reserve Bank. The
Reserve Bank has stated that foreign share holdings in Andhra Bank have
gone down below the limit of 18 per cent of its paid up capital.
