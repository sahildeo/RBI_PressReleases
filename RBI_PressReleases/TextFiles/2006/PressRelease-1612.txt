February 6, 2006
Underwriting by Primary Dealers
The Reserve Bank of India has set the cut-off rate for underwriting bids by
Primary Dealers for the auctions of "7.46 per cent Government Stock, 2017" and
"7.40 per cent Government Stock, 2035" to be held on February 7, 2006 at Re.0.03
(three paise) and Re.0.0694 (six point nine four paise) per Rupees 100
respectively.
