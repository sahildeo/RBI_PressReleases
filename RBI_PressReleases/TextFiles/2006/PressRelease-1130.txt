May 16, 2006
RBI announces rate of interest on Floating Rate Bonds, 2006
The rate of interest on the Floating Rate Bonds, 2006 (FRB, 2006) applicable for
the half-year May 22, 2006 to November 21, 2006 shall be 6.30 percent per annum.
It may be recalled that the rate of interest on the FRB, 2006 was set at a mark-up
(as decided in the auction held on November 21, 2001) over and above the variable
base rate. The variable base rate for payment of interest shall be the average rate
(rounded off up to two decimal places) of the implicit yields at cut-off prices of the last
six auctions of Government of India 364-day Treasury Bills held up to the
commencement of the respective half yearly coupon period. The variable base rate
based on the average rate of the implicit yields at cut-off prices of the said last six
auctions of Government of India 364-day Treasury Bills worked out to be 6.35 per cent.
The mark-up decided in the auction held on November 21, 2001 was (-) 0.05 per cent
(minus 0.05 per cent). The coupon rate has been fixed accordingly.
