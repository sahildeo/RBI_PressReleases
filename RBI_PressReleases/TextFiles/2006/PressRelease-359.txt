Monitoring FIIs Investments : Unichem Laboratories Ltd.   
 
 
 The Reserve Bank of India has notified on October 16, 2006 that 
Foreign Institutional Investors (FIIs) can now purchase under Portfolio 
Investment Scheme (PIS) equity shares and convertible debentures of 
Unichem Laboratories Ltd., through primary markets and stock exchanges in 
India upto 39 per cent of its paid up capital, as the company has passed 
resolutions to this effect at its Board of Directors' and General Body Meetings.
