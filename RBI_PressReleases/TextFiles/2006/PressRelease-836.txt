Monitoring FIIs investment in Indian Companies
The Reserve Bank of India has notified on July 12, 2006 that Foreign
Institutional Investors (FIIs) can now purchase under Portfolio Investment
Scheme (PIS) equity shares and convertible debentures of the Aptech Ltd.,
through primary markets and stock exchanges in India upto 74 per cent of
their paid up capital, as the company has passed resolution to this effect at its
Board of Directors' and special resolution in its General Body Meetings.
