Underwriting by Primary Dealers
The Reserve Bank of India has set the cut-off rate at 1.47 paise per Rs. 100
in the underwriting auction conducted for the auction of “8.07% Government Stock
2017”and at 3.35 paise per Rs. 100 in the underwriting auction conducted for the
auction of “8.33% Government Stock 2036” to be held on August 18, 2006.
