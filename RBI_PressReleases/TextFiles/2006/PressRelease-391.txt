Monitoring FIIs investment in Indian Companies : PTC India Ltd.  
 
 The Reserve Bank of India has notified on October 10, 2006 that Foreign 
Institutional Investors (FIIs) can purchase under Portfolio Investment Scheme (PIS) 
equity shares and convertible debentures of PTC India Ltd., through primary markets 
and stock exchanges in India upto only 40 per cent of its paid up capital as the 
information furnished by the company on September 18, 2006 was treated as null 
and void by the Bank.
