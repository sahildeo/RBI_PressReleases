Investment by FIIs under PIS
       The Reserve Bank of India on June 21, 2006 has notified that no further
purchases of equity shares of the following companies, should be made on
behalf of Foreign Institutional Investors (FIIs) under the Portfolio Investment
Scheme (PIS) through primary and secondary markets in India without
obtaining the prior permission of the Reserve Bank. The Reserve Bank has
stated that these companies have reached the limit of 22 per cent of its paid
up capital.
1. Cranes Software International Ltd.
2. Power Trading Corporation of India
