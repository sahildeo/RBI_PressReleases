Monitoring of Investments by FIIs under PIS :
Hindustan Petroleum Corporation Ltd. (HPCL)
The Reserve Bank of India on September 21, 2006 has notified that
further purchases of equity shares of Hindustan Petroleum Corporation Ltd.
(HPCL) on behalf of Foreign Institutional Investors (FIIs) through secondary
market in India under Portfolio Investment Scheme (PIS) would be allowed
without prior clearance of the Reserve Bank. The Reserve Bank has stated that
foreign share holdings in this company has gone down below the caution limit of
its paid up capital.
