Moratorium on Ganesh Bank of Kurundwad Ltd.
The Central Government on an application made by Reserve Bank of India
has issued an order of moratorium on the Ganesh Bank of Kurundwad Ltd. for the
period from the close of business on July 7, 2006 up to and inclusive of October 6,
2006 under the provisions of sub-Section (2) of Section 45 of the Banking Regulation
Act, 1949.
The moratorium has been declared in public interest, in the interest of the
depositors and the banking system.
