New RBI sub-office at Dehradun
The Reserve Bank of India has opened a sub-office at Dehradun for
the State of Uttaranchal. The sub-office was inaugurated by the Hon'ble
Chief Minister, Shri N.D. Tiwari on June 30, 2006. The sub-office will focus
particularly on issues relating to rural credit and cooperative banks in the
State of Uttaranchal.
It may be recalled that Dr. Y.V. Reddy, Governor, Reserve Bank of
India had, on May 5, 2006, announced the setting up of a sub-office in
Dehradun.  The sub-office will initially have two departments, namely,
Rural Planning and Credit Department (RPCD) and Urban Banks
Department (UBD). The sub-office is located at 97, Rajpur Road,
Dehradun 248 001 and is headed by Shri Manoj Sharma, General
Manager. The telephone numbers of the sub-office are.
