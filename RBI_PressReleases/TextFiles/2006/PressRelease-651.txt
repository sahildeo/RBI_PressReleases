August 17, 2006
Monitoring of investment by FIIs under PIS -
Logix Microsystems Ltd.
The Reserve Bank of India on August 17, 2006 has notified that the
aggregate share holding of Foreign Institutional Investors (FIIs) with Logix
Microsystems Ltd. has reached the overall limit of 24 per cent of its paid up
capital. The Reserve Bank has stated that no further purchases of shares of
Logix Microsystems Ltd., on behalf of FIIs would be allowed through stock
exchanges in India.
