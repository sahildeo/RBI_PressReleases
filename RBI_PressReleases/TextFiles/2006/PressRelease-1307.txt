Underwriting by Primary Dealers
The Reserve Bank of India has set the cut-off rate at Re. 0.04 (four paise) per
Rs. 100 and Re 0.09 (nine paise) per Rs. 100 in the underwriting for the auction of
“Government Stock of 10 years’’ and “7.50% GS 2034” respectively, to be held on April
10, 2006.
