Underwriting by Primary Dealers
The Reserve Bank of India has set the cut-off rate for underwriting bids by
Primary Dealers for the auctions of "9.39" per cent Government Stock, 2011" and
"7.40 per cent Government Stock, 2035" to be held on January 9, 2006 at
Re.0.0250 (two point five zero paise) and Re.0.0489 (four point eight nine paise)
per Rupees 100 respective.
