Monitoring FIIs Investment in Indian Companies  –
Deccan Chronicle Holdings Ltd.
The Reserve Bank of India has notified on September 7, 2006 that Deccan
Chronicle Holdings Ltd., has obtained the approval of Foreign Investment Promotion
Board (FIPB) and decided to restrict the overall foreign shareholdings to 24 per cent,
Foreign Direct Investment (FDI) and Foreign Institutional Investors (FIIs) taken
together, with a ceiling of 14 per cent for FIIs. The Reserve Bank has further notified
that its monitoring limit for investments in this company would be 12 per cent for FIIs
and 22 per cent for overall foreign investments.
