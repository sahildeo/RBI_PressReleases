February 14, 2006
Investment by FIIs/NRIs under PIS
The Reserve Bank of India on February 14, 2006 has notified that no
further purchases of equity shares of Canara Bank should be made on behalf
of Foreign Institutional Investors (FIIs) and Non-Resident Indians (NRIs)
through stock exchanges in India under Portfolio Investment Scheme (PIS)
without the prior permission of the Reserve Bank. The Reserve Bank has
stated that Canara Bank has reached the limit of 18 per cent of its paid up
capital.
