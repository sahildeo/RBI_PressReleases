April 5, 2006
 ICICI Bank Ltd.
Investment by FIIs/NRIs/PIOs under PIS
       The Reserve Bank of India on April 5, 2006 has notified that no further
purchases of equity shares of ICICI Bank Ltd., should be made on behalf of
Foreign Institutional Investors (FIIs)/Non-Resident Indians (NRIs) and Persons
of Indian Origin (PIOs) under Porfolio Investment Scheme (PIS) through stock
exchanges in India. The Reserve Bank has stated that ICICI Bank Ltd., has
reached the limit of 74 per cent of its paid up capital.
