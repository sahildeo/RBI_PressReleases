January 10, 2006
RBI releases six-monthly Report on Forex Reserves
The Reserve Bank of India has today placed on its website, the Fifth report on
management of foreign exchange reserves with reference to September 30, 2005.
It may be recalled that in February 2004, the Reserve Bank had commenced a
process of compiling half-yearly reports and placing them in the public domain with a lag
of about three months for bringing about more transparency and also for enhancing the
level of disclosure. The first such report with reference to September 30, 2003 was
released on February 3, 2004, the second report with reference to March 31, 2004 was
released on July 5, 2004, the third report with reference to September 30, 2004 was
released on January 7, 2005 and the fourth report with reference to March 31, 2005 was
released on July 6, 2005.
