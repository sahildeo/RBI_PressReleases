July 7, 2006 
 
Seven State Governments announce the auction of  
State Development Loans 2016 on July 13, 2006 
 
The following State Governments have announced the sale of 10-year State 
Development Loans for an aggregate amount of Rs.1683.324 crore through a yield 
based auction using multiple price auction method.  
 
Sr. State Notified Amount 
No. (Rs. in Crore) 
1 Andhra Pradesh 742.155 
2 Jharkhand 78.374 
3 Madhya Pradesh 300.00 
4 Meghalaya 29.425 
5 Mizoram 19.02 
6 Rajasthan 450.00 
7 Sikkim 64.35 
 Total 1683.324 
 
The auction will be conducted by the Reserve Bank of India (RBI) at Mumbai 
on July 13, 2006 (Thursday). Bids in the prescribed form obtainable from the 
Regional Director, Reserve Bank of India, Mumbai Office (Public Debt Office), Fort, 
Mumbai-400 001 should be submitted to that Office on Thursday, July 13, 2006. The 
competitive bids by the NDS members should be submitted only in electronic format 
using NDS. All bids should be submitted by 12.30 P.M. On the basis of bids 
received, RBI will determine the maximum yield at which offers will be accepted. 
 
  The results of the auction will be announced on July 13, 2006 and payment by 
successful bidders will be during banking hours on July 14, 2006 (Friday) at Mumbai 
and at respective Regional Offices of Reserve Bank of India. The stocks will qualify 
for the ready forward facility. 
 
Bids for underwriting of the Andhra Pradesh Government Stock under auction 
can be submitted by the ‘Primary Dealers’ up to 12.30 P.M. on July 12, 2006  
(Wednesday) using PMO module of NDS and physical bids to the Regional Director, 
Reserve Bank of India, Public Debt Office, Fort, Mumbai-400 001.
