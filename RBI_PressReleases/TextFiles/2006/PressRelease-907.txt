June 28, 2006
RBI Central Board Reconstituted
The Government of India has reconstituted the Central Board of Directors
of the Reserve Bank of India. The following distinguished personalities have
been nominated to be the Members of the Central Board of Directors of the
Reserve Bank of India, for a period of four years with effect from June 27, 2006:
1. Shri Suresh Tendulkar, Economist and Member, Prime Minister's
Economic Advisory Council (to represent Eastern Area Local Board).
2. Prof. U. R. Rao, former Chairman ISRO and Chairman Research Council,
Physical Research Laboratory, Department of Space (to represent
Northern Area Local Board).
3. Shri Lakshmi Chand, IAS (Retd.) (to represent Southern Area Local
Board).
4. Shri Azim Premji, Chairman, WIPRO Ltd.
5. Shri Kumar Mangalam Birla, Chairman Aditya Birla Group of Companies.
6. Smt. Shashi Rekha Rajagopalan, Consultant, Co-operatives.
7. Shri Suresh Kumar Neotia, Chairman, Ambuja Cement.
8. Dr. A. Vaidyanathan, Madras Institute of Development Studies.
9. Dr. Man Mohan Sharma, FRS, Former Director, Mumbai University
Institute of Chemical Technology.
10. Dr. D. Jayavarthanavelu, Chairman and Managing Director, Laxmi
Machine Works Ltd.
The following were re-nominated to the Board:
1. Shri Y.H. Malegam, Chartered Accountant, (to represent Western
Area Local Board).
2. Shri H. P. Ranina, Supreme Court Advocate.
3. Dr. Ashok S. Ganguly, Member, Investment Commission and
Knowledge Commission and Chairman, ICICI OneSource Ltd.Shri Ratan Tata, Shri N.R. Narayana Murthy, Prof. Mihir Rakshit, Shri K.
Madhava Rao, Prof. V. S. Vyas, Shri K.P. Singh, Dr. Amrita Patel, Shri Suresh
Krishna retired as Directors of the Central Board of the Bank.
