Investment by FIIs/NRIs/PIOs under PIS : Allahabad Bank
       The Reserve Bank of India on December 13, 2006 has notified that no
further purchases of equity shares of Allahabad Bank should be made on
behalf of Foreign Institutional Investors (FIIs)/Non-Resident Indians (NRIs)
and Persons of Indian Origin (PIOs) under Portfolio Investment Scheme (PIS)
through stock exchanges in India without the prior permission of the Reserve
Bank. The Reserve Bank has stated that Allahabad Bank has reached the
limit of 18 per cent of its paid up capital.
