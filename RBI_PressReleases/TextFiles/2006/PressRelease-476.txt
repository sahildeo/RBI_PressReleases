September 21, 2006
Investment by FIIs/NRIs/PIOs under PIS :
Centurion Bank of Punjab Ltd.
       The Reserve Bank of India on September 21, 2006 has notified that no
further purchases of equity shares of Centurion Bank of Punjab Ltd., should
be made on behalf of Foreign Institutional Investors (FIIs)/Non-Resident
Indians (NRIs) and Persons of Indian Origin (PIOs) under Porfolio Investment
Scheme (PIS) through stock exchange/s in India. The Reserve Bank has
stated that Centurion Bank of Punjab Ltd., has reached the limit of 74 per cent
of its paid up capital.
