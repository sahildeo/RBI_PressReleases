December 8, 2006
Eight State Governments announce the auction of
State Development Loans 2016
The following State Governments have announced the sale of 10-year State
Development Loans for an aggregate amount of Rs.1,963.236 crore through a yield
based auction using multiple price auction method.
Sr. State Notified Amount
No. (Rs. crore)
1 Andhra Pradesh 408.780
2 Assam 166.455
3 Jharkhand 192.564
4 Kerala 400.000
5 Meghalaya 55.437
6 Nagaland 40.000
7 Rajasthan 300.000
8 Tamil Nadu 400.000
Total 1963.236
Note: Government of Tamil Nadu will have the option to retain
additional subscription upto a maximum of Rs.100.00 crore (Nominal)
over and above the notified amount of Rs.400.00 crore.
The auction will be conducted by the Reserve Bank of India (RBI) at Mumbai on
December 14, 2006 (Thursday). Bids in the prescribed form obtainable from the Regional
Director, Reserve Bank of India, Mumbai Office (Public Debt Office), Fort, Mumbai-400
001 should be submitted to that Office on Thursday, December 14, 2006. The
competitive bids by the NDS members should be submitted only in electronic format
using Primary Market Operation (PMO) module of NDS. All bids should be submitted by
12.30 P.M. On the basis of bids received, RBI will determine the maximum yield at which
offers will be accepted.
 The results of the auction will be announced on December 14, 2006 and payment
by successful bidders will be during banking hours on December 15, 2006 (Friday) at
Mumbai and at respective Regional Offices of Reserve Bank of India. The stocks will
qualify for the ready forward facility.
