Investment by Foreign Institutional Investors -
Monitoring of Overall Ceiling : Development Credit Bank Ltd.
The Reserve Bank of India on November 29, 2006 has notified that the
aggregate share holding of Foreign Institutional Investors (FIIs) in
Development Credit Bank Ltd.,  has reached the overall limit of 24 per cent of
its paid up capital. The Reserve Bank has stated that no further purchases of
shares of Development Credit Bank Ltd., should, therefore, be made through
stock exchanges in India on behalf of FIIs.
