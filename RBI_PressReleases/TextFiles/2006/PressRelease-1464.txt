March 9, 2006
Monitoring of Foreign Investment under PIS
The Reserve Bank of India on March 9, 2006 has notified that no
further purchases of equity shares of D.S.Kulkarni Dev Ltd., should be made
on behalf of Foreign Institutional Investors (FIIs) under Portfolio Investment
Scheme (PIS) through stock exchanges in India. The Reserve Bank has
stated that D.S.Kulkarni Dev Ltd., has reached the overall limit of 24 per cent
of its paid up capital.
