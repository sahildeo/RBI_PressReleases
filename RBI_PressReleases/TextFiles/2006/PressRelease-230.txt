Statistical tables relating to banks in India 2005-2006
The Reserve Bank of India has published Statistical Tables Relating to
Banks in India 2005-2006.
This publication has a long history. After bringing out the publication for
twenty-five years till 1938, the Department of Commercial Intelligence & Statistics
handed over the responsibility to the Reserve Bank of India. With a view to give this
historical perspective, the cover page of the publication in 1938 and the first page of
the publication for the years 1939 & 1940, are included in the current edition of the
publication. Including the volumes brought out by Department of Commercial
Intelligence & Statistics, this is the 86th edition of this publication.
The publication presents detailed tables on assets and liabilities and earning
and expenses of SCBs for the year 2005-06. In addition to detailed bank-wise tables,
the publication provides summary tables giving aggregated data for bank groups as
well as for all banks.
The publication also gives data on important parameters like maturity and
ownership pattern of deposits, sector-wise bank credit, investment pattern, state-
wise distribution of employees, insured deposits, major items of priority sector
lending both at state-wise and bank-wise, monthly data on sector-wise and industry-
wise deployment of credit, NPAs, bank-wise data on loans subjected to restructuring,
corporate debt restructured and maturity profile of selected items of the balance
sheet at bank group level. In addition, the publication also presents the list of
amalgamated regional rural banks and bank-wise list of foreign offices of Indian
scheduled commercial banks.
The publication is also being brought out in the CD-ROM format. The CD-
ROM, besides providing all the tables, graphs and maps given in the publication,
gives inter-active data querying facility.
The data published in this volume are also made available through Internet at
http://www.rbi.org.in
