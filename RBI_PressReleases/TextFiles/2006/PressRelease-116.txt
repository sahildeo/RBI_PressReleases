Investment by FIIs under PIS – EMCO Ltd.
The Reserve Bank of India has notified on December 6, 2006 that Foreign
Institutional Investers (FIIs) can now purchase under Portfolio Investment Scheme
(PIS) equity shares and convertible debentures of EMCO Ltd., through primary
markets and stock exchange in India upto the limits of 49 per cent of its paid up
capital. The Reserve Bank has further stated that the FII ceiling of 40 per cent as
stated in respect of EMCO Ltd. on September 18, 2006 has been amended.
