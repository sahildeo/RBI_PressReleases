RBI to issue Rs.20 denomination banknotes with inset letter `E'
The Reserve Bank of India will shortly issue Rs.20 denomination
banknotes with inset letter `E' in both numbering panels in Mahatma Gandhi
Series bearing the signature of Dr. Y.V. Reddy, Governor. Except for the
change in the inset letter, the design of these notes to be issued now is similar
in all respects to the banknotes issued earlier in Mahatma Gandhi Series. All
banknotes in the denomination of Rs.20 issued by the Bank in the past will
continue to be legal tender.
