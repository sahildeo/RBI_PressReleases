Underwriting auctions for “9.39 per cent Government Stock, 2011” and
“7.59 per cent Government Stock, 2016”
The Government of India has notified sale (re-issue) of “9.39 per cent
Government Stock, 2011” for an amount of Rs. 6000 crore and “ 7.59 per cent
Government Stock, 2016” for an amount of Rs. 3000 crore on August 8, 2006. The
underwriting auctions for the stocks will be conducted on August 7, 2006.  As per the
revised scheme of Underwriting Commitment dated April 4, 2006, the amounts of
Minimum Underwriting Commitment (MUC) and Additional Competitive Underwriting
(ACU) for the above auctions are as follows:
Nomenclature of the Notified MUC for ACU for which bidding
Security Amount each PD has to be done by PDs
collectively
9.39% GS 2011 6000 177 2991
7.59% GS 2016 3000 89 1487
The underwriting auctions will be conducted by using multiple price based
auction method. Primary Dealers (PDs) may submit their bids for ACU auctions
electronically through NDS-PMO module between 10.30 AM to 12.30 PM on August
7, 2006.
The underwriting commission will be credited to the current account of the
respective PDs at the RBI, Fort, Mumbai on the date of issue of security.
