November 12, 2013 
G-Sec Auction Preponed to November 14, 2013  
The scheduled auction of four Government of India dated securities for        
` 15,000 crore has been preponed to November 14, 2013 from November 15, 
2013. Accordingly, the "When Issued" trading in these securities will be allowed for 
the period November 12, 2013 - November 14, 2013. Other terms and conditions 
of the auction remain the same. 
The change has been effected in view of the Government of Maharashtra 
cancelling the earlier declared holiday of November 14, 2013 to November 15, 
2013 (Friday) as holiday on account of Moharrum. The holiday has been declared 
under the Negotiable Instrument Act, 1881.
