Monthly Data on India’s International Trade in Services 
 for the Month of September 2013 
The Reserve Bank releases provisional aggregate monthly data on India’s 
international trade in services with a lag of around 45 days. Accordingly, the data for 
the month of August 2013 were released on October 15, 2013. 
The value of exports and imports of services during the month of September 
2013 are given in the following Table.   
Table: International Trade in Services 
(US$ Million)
Month Receipts (Exports) Payments (Imports) 
July 2013 12,941 6,619 
August 2013 12,310 6,295 
September 2013 12,290 6,792 
Note: Data are provisional. 
 Monthly data on services are provisional and would undergo revision when the 
Balance of Payments (BoP) data are released on a quarterly basis.
