May 13, 2013 
RBI releases its monthly Bulletin for May 2013 
The Reserve Bank today released the May 2013 issue of its monthly Bulletin. 
The Bulletin contains the Governor’s Statement on Monetary Policy 2013-14, 
Macroeconomic and Monetary Developments in 2012-13, the first Prof. Tendulkar 
Memorial Oration by Professor Abhijit Banerjee and regular features, viz., speeches 
of the top management and current statistics.
