September 26, 2013 
Investment by FIIs under PIS - Incorporation in Caution List :  
M/s Multi Commodity Exchange of India Limited  
The Reserve Bank of India has today notified that the aggregate net purchases 
of equity shares in M/s Multi Commodity Exchange of India Limited by Foreign 
Institutional Investors (FIIs) in the primary/secondary markets under Portfolio 
Investment Scheme (PIS) have reached the trigger limit. Hence, further purchases of 
equity shares of this company would be allowed only after obtaining prior approval of 
the Reserve Bank of India.
