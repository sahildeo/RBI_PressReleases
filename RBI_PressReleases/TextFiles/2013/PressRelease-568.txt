October 09, 2013 
 
RBI appoints Technical Committee on Mobile Banking 
 
The Reserve Bank of India has announced the constitution of a Technical Committee 
on Mobile Banking, to examine the options/alternatives including the feasibility of using 
encrypted SMS based funds transfer using an application that can run on any type of 
handset for expansion of mobile banking in the country. 
 
Terms of Reference of the Committee are: 
 
a. to conduct an in-depth study of the challenges faced by banks in taking mobile 
banking forward to the desired level;  
b. to study the challenges faced by banks in introducing the Unstructured 
Supplementary Service Data (USSD) channel and suggest solutions, if any, to take 
this forward;  
c. to consider the advantages/challenges of having a single application across all 
handsets in an SMS encrypted environment; 
d. to examine any other optimum solution that would take mobile banking to every 
nook and corner of the country; and accordingly, 
e. to draw up a road map for implementation of the solutions/options recommended 
The Committee will comprise: 
1. Shri B. Sambamurthy, Director, Institute for Development & Research in Banking 
Technology (Chairman) 
2. Shri Pulak Kumar Sinha, General Manager (Payment Systems)- State Bank of India 
3. Shri Rahul Joshi , Joint General Manager (Mobile Banking & Payments)- ICICI Bank  
4. Shri Anand Rao, Vice President (Alternate Delivery Channels) Axis Bank 
5. Shri Dilip Asbe, Chief Operating Officer, National Payments Corporation of India  
6. Shri Anthony Thomas, Chief Information Officer, Vodafone India Ltd 
7. Shri C. N. Ram, Ex-Chief Information Officer, HDFC Bank and  
8. Shri Pravir Vohra, Ex-President & Group -Chief Technical Officer, ICICI Bank 
9. Shri Vijay Chugh, Chief General Manager, Reserve Bank of India- Member Secretary 
The Technical Committee may include specialists in the field of mobile payments as 
permanent invitees. 
  
The Committee will submit its report by end of December 2013. 
 
The Reserve Bank of India, Department of Payment and Settlement Systems, 
Central Office, Mumbai has invited suggestions from the public based on the terms of 
reference of the Technical Committee for consideration of the Committee. The comments 
may be mailed latest by October 31, 2013.
