Monthly Data on India’s International Trade in Services 
 for the Month of April 2013 
 
The Reserve Bank releases provisional aggregate monthly data on India’s 
international trade in services with a lag of 45 days. Accordingly, the data for the 
month of March  2013 were released on May  15, 2013. 
 
The exports and imports of services during the month of April 2013 are given 
in the following Table.   
 
Table: International Trade in Services 
(US$ Million)
Month Receipts (Exports) Payments (Imports) 
January 2013 
13,899 7,522 
February 2013 
12,287 6,349 
March 2013 
12,253 6,805 
April 2013 
12,842 7,378 
Note: Data are provisional. 
 
 Monthly data on services are provisional and would undergo revision when the 
Balance of Payments (BoP) data are released on a quarterly basis. Revised data on 
services receipts and payments on quarterly basis (October-December 2012 and 
Partially Revised data for the First Quarter (April-June 2012) and Second Quarter 
(July-September 2012) are available in the press release on 'Developments in India’s 
Balance of Payments during the Third Quarter (October-December 2012)' placed on 
RBI website on March 28, 2013.
