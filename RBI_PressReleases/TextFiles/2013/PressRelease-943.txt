August 22, 2013 
RBI releases its Annual Report for 2012-13 
The Reserve Bank of India today released its Annual Report for 2012-13, a 
statutory Report of its Central Board of Directors. It covers (i) the assessment of the 
macroeconomic performance during 2012-13 and the prospects for 2013-14, and (ii) 
the working and operations of the Reserve Bank and its financial accounts for the 
year 2012-13.
