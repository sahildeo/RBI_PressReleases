July 2, 2013 
Investment by FIIs under PIS - Removal from the Ban List : 
M/s Yes Bank Limited 
The Reserve Bank of India has today notified that the aggregate share 
holdings in M/s Yes Bank Limited by Foreign Institutional Investors (FIIs) under the 
Portfolio Investment Scheme (PIS) have gone below the prescribed threshold limit 
stipulated under the extant FDI Policy. Hence, the restrictions placed on the purchase 
of shares of the above company is withdrawn with immediate effect.
