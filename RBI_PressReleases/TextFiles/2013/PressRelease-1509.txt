June 3, 2013 
 
M/s Base Financials Limited, New Delhi 
Cancellation of  Certificate of Registration   
 
 
It is hereby notified for the information of the public that the Reserve Bank of 
India in exercise of powers conferred on it under Section 45-IA(6) of the Reserve 
Bank of India Act, 1934, has cancelled on May 6, 2013, the Certificate of 
Registration bearing No.14.00679 dated April 24, 1998 issued to M/s Base 
Financials Limited, New Delhi (having its Registered Office at 904-A, Chiranjiv 
Tower, 43, Nehru Place, New Delhi - 110019), to carry on the business of a Non-
Banking Financial Institutions.  
 
As such, the above company cannot transact the business of a Non-Banking 
Financial Institution, as defined in clause (a) of Section 45-I of the Reserve Bank of 
India Act, 1934.
