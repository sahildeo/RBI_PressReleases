 November 8, 2013 
Revision of Ways and Means Advances to State Governments 
The aggregate Normal Ways and Means Advances (WMA) limit for the State 
Governments inclusive of Union Territory of Puducherry is currently placed at 
`10,240 crore for the year 2013-14. On a review, it has been decided to increase the 
WMA limit for the State Governments by 50 per cent to ` 15,360 crore with effect 
from November 11, 2013.  Other terms and conditions of the WMA scheme would 
remain unchanged.
