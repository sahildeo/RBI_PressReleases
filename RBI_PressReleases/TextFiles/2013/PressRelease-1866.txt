April 4, 2013 
Directions under Section 35A of the Banking Regulation Act, 1949 (AACS) 
Shri Siddhivinayak Nagari Sahakari Bank Ltd., Rasayani, Dist. Raigad, 
Maharashtra 
It is hereby notified for the information of the public that validity period of 
directions imposed on Shri Siddhivinayak Nagari Sahakari Bank Ltd., Rasayani, Dist. 
Raigad vide RBI directive UBD.CO.BSD-I.No.D-56/12.22.332/2011-12 dated 
September 29, 2011 has been extended further vide modified directive 
UBD.CO.BSD-I.No.D-36/12.22.332/2012-13 dated March 20, 2013 for a period of six 
months i.e. from March 29, 2013 to September 28, 2013. The other terms and 
conditions remain unchanged. A copy of the direction dated March 20, 2013 notifying 
the above modification is displayed at the bank’s premises for the perusal of public. 
The aforesaid modification by the Reserve Bank of India should not per-se be 
construed to imply that Reserve Bank of India is satisfied of substantive improvement 
in the financial position of the bank
