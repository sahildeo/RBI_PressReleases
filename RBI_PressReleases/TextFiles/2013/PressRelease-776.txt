September 11, 2013 
Investment by FIIs/NRIs/PIOs/FDI/ADRs/GDRs under PIS -   
Removal from Ban List: M/s IDFC Ltd.  
The Reserve Bank of India has today notified that foreign share holdings in 
M/s IDFC Ltd. by Foreign Institutional Investors (FIIs)/Non-Resident Indians (NRIs)/ 
Persons of Indian Origin (PIOs)/Foreign Direct Investment (FDI)/American Depository 
Receipts (ADRs)/Global Depository Receipts (GDRs) under the Portfolio Investment 
Scheme (PIS) have gone below the prescribed threshold limit stipulated under the 
extant FDI Policy. Hence, the restrictions placed on the purchase of shares of M/s 
IDFC Ltd. are withdrawn with immediate effect.
