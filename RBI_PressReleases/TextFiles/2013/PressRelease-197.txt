December 3, 2013 
RBI launches Consumer Confidence Survey (CCS) – December 2013 
The Reserve Bank of India has been conducting the Consumer Confidence 
Survey of Households (CCS) on a quarterly basis since June 2010. The survey aims 
at capturing subjective assessments of around 5,400 respondents across 6 
metropolitan cities viz., Bangalore, Chennai, Hyderabad, Kolkata, Mumbai and New 
Delhi. The results of this Survey are being used by Reserve Bank as one of the 
important inputs for monetary policy formulation. The latest round of the Survey with 
December 2013 as the reference period has been launched today. The survey seeks 
responses on questions pertaining to economic conditions, household circumstances, 
income & spending, perceptions on prices, employment prospects, outlays for major 
purchases such as motor vehicle, house, durable goods etc. 
The agency M/s Sigma Research & Consulting Pvt. Ltd. has been engaged to 
conduct the field work for this round of the Survey on behalf of the Reserve Bank of 
India. For this purpose, the respondents will be approached by the agency and the 
selected respondents are requested to provide the required information. Other 
individuals who are not approached by the agency can also participate in this survey 
round by downloading the survey schedule from the link. The filled in survey 
schedule may be e-mailed to us as per contact details given below. In case of any 
query/clarification, kindly contact at the following address:
