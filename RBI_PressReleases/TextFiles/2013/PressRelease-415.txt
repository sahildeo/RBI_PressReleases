 November 1, 2013 
The Udhna Citizen  Co-operative Bank Ltd., Surat, (Gujarat) now under  
RBI Directions till May 1, 2014 
 
The Reserve Bank of India has extended the period of directions imposed on 
The Udhna Citizen Co-operative Bank Ltd., Surat for further six months. The Bank 
will thus continue to be under directions from November 2, 2013 to May 1, 2014. The 
modified directive has been displayed on the bank premises for interested members 
of public to peruse. 
 
The Reserve Bank has initially imposed directions on The Udhna Citizen Co-
operative Bank Ltd., Surat from the close of business on November 2, 2010. The 
directions were last modified in April 2013. 
 
  The Reserve Bank of India issued directions on co-operative banks in exercise 
of the powers vested in it under Sub-section (1) of Section 35A of Banking Regulation 
Act, 1949 (As Applicable to Co-operative Societies) and in public interest. The 
directions are subject to review.
