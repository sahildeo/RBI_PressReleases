May 14, 2013 
 
 
RBI Releases Disaggregated Data on India’s Invisibles 
for Third Quarter (October-December) of 2012-13 
 
The Reserve Bank today released data on India’s invisibles as per the IMF’s 
Balance of Payments and International Investment Position Manual (BPM6)  format for 
October-December 2012 and revised data for April-June 2012 and July-September 
2012 along with corresponding data for previous year. 
 
In order to enhance data dissemination, the disaggregated data on India’s 
Invisibles would be released on a quarterly basis on the RBI website with about one 
month’s lag from the date of press release of BoP. These data would also be available 
on RBI’s DBIE database. (http://dbie.rbi.org.in/DBIE/dbie.rbi?site=home)
