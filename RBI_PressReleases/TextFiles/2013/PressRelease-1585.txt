May 22, 2013 
 
Government of India nominates  
Dr. Nachiket M. Mor on RBI Central Board 
 
 
The Central Government has nominated Dr. Nachiket Madhusudan Mor as a 
Member of the Local Board, Eastern Area of Reserve Bank of India, for a period of 
th
four years with effect from 16 May 2013. 
 
Further, the Central Government has also nominated Dr. Nachiket Mor to be a 
Director of the Central Board of Reserve Bank of India.   
 
Dr. Nachiket Mor holds a Post-Graduate Diploma in Finance Management 
from the Indian Institute of Management, Ahmedabad and a Doctorate in Finance 
and Econometrics from the Wharton School, University of Pennsylvania, 
Philadelphia, USA. He is a Yale World Fellow (2004).
