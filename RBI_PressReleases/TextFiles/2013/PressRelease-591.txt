October 7, 2013 
Adjustment in Bank Rate 
As part of the measures announced today by the Reserve Bank to improve 
liquidity conditions, the marginal standing facility (MSF) rate has been reduced by 50 
basis points from 9.5 per cent to 9.0 per cent with immediate effect. 
Accordingly, the Bank Rate also stands adjusted to 9.0 per cent with 
immediate effect.
