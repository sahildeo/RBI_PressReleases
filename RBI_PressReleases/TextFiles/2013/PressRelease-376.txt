November 7, 2013  
RBI to release MMO Data around 10.30 a.m. from Tomorrow 
The Reserve Bank of India will, from November 8, 2013, make available the 
money market data (Money Market Operations or MMO) on RBI website around 
10.30 a.m. in view of the change in the timings of the Marginal Standing Facility 
(MSF) operations. The MSF operations are being conducted between 7.00 p.m. and 
7.30 p.m. from November 5, 2013 as announced in the Second Quarter Review of 
Monetary Policy 2013-2014.  
Earlier, the MSF operations were conducted between 4.45 p.m. and 5.15 p.m. 
and the Reserve Bank was making available the MMO data on RBI webiste on the 
next working day by 9.00 a.m.
