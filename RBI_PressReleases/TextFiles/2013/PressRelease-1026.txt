     August 8, 2013 
RBI announces 35 days & 34 days Cash Management Bills  
The Reserve Bank of India announced the auction of the 35 days & 34 days 
Government of India Cash Management Bills as per the following details: 
 Auction Date Settlement/Issue  Date of Maturity Notified 
Date Amount 
1 August 12, 2013 August 13, 2013 September 17, 2013 ` 11,000 crore 
2 August 13, 2013 August 14, 2013 September 17, 2013 ` 11,000 crore 
   Total ` 22,000 crore
The auctions will be conducted using "Multiple Price Auction" method. The 
competitive bids for the auction should be submitted in electronic format on the 
Reserve Bank of India Core Banking Solution (E-Kuber) between 10.30 a.m. and 
12.00 noon. Result of the auctions will be announced on the same day.  
The Cash Management Bills will have the generic character of Treasury Bills 
and their sale will be subject to the terms and conditions specified in the General 
Notification No. F.2 (12)-W&M/97 dated March 31, 1998 issued by Government of 
India and as amended from time to time.
