September 6, 2013  
Names of Applicants for New Bank Licences in the Private Sector 
The Reserve Bank of India has today communicated two changes in the 
names of applicants for new bank licences in the private sector. One applicant, viz., 
Value Industries Limited, Aurangabad has withdrawn its application and another 
applicant, viz., K. C. Land & Finance Ltd., Chandigarh whose application was 
received at the Reserve Bank, Central Office, Mumbai on July 1, 2013 but was 
inadvertently not included in the list of applicants for new bank licences in the private 
sector, is now being included in the list. The Reserve Bank of India had, on July 1, 
2013 placed on its website a list of 26 applicants for new bank licences in the private 
sector through a Press Release.
