December 9, 2013 
Directions under Section 35A of the Banking Regulation Act, 1949 (AACS) 
The Merchants' Co-operative Bank Ltd, Dhule, Maharastra 
 
It is hereby notified for the information of the public that validity period of 
directions imposed on The Merchants’ Co-operative Bank Ltd., Dhule, vide our 
directives UBD.CO.BSD-I.No.D-23/12.22.147/2012-13 dated December 12, 2012 has 
been extended for a further period of six months from December 13, 2013 to June 
12, 2014 vide our modified directive UBD.CO.BSD-I.No.D-19/12.22.147/2013-14 
dated December 3, 2013 subject to review. The other terms and conditions remain 
unchanged. A copy of the direction dated December 3, 2013 notifying the above 
modification is displayed at the bank’s premises for the perusal of public. 
 
The aforesaid modification by the Reserve Bank of India should not per-se be 
construed to imply that Reserve Bank of India is satisfied of substantive improvement 
in the financial position of the bank.
