August 07, 2013 
RBI imposes penalty on State Bank of India for  
violation of the terms of agreement with RBI 
 The Reserve Bank of India has imposed a penalty of ` 5,62,555/-  (Rupees 
Five Lakhs Sixty Two Thousand Five Hundred Fifty Five only) on July 12, 2013 on 
State Bank of India for violation of the terms of agreement with RBI (for opening and 
maintaining currency chests) under para 3 (v) of circular DCM (CC) No.G-
3/03.39.01/2012-13 dated July 02, 2012 and para 1 (e) of DCM (CC) No.G-
2/03.35.01/2012-13 dated July 02, 2012.  The above penalty has been levied in 
connection with the deficiencies/lapses observed in the operation and maintenance 
of the Currency Chest at SBI, Secunderabad branch.
