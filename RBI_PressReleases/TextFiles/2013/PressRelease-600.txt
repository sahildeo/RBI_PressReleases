October 4, 2013 
 
RBI Committee on Comprehensive Financial Services for Small 
 Businesses and Low-Income Households invites 
 suggestions from stakeholders 
 
The RBI Committee on Comprehensive Financial Services for Small Businesses and 
Low-Income Households invites suggestions from stakeholders in its first meeting held on 
September 30, 2013 decided to invite suggestions from various stakeholders with a view to 
enabling them to share their thoughts on the subject. Interested stakeholders who wish to 
make a formal submission to the Committee should email it by October 31, 2013. 
 
It may be recalled that the Reserve Bank of India constituted a Committee on 
Comprehensive Financial Services for Small Businesses and Low-Income Households 
under the Chairmanship of Dr. Nachiket Mor, Member of the Central Board of Directors, 
Reserve Bank of India. The Committee has members drawn from the formal financial sector; 
from organisations that are engaged in developing new models of financial services delivery, 
in rural as well as urban contexts and have subject matter expertise. Shri A. Udgata, 
Principal Chief General Manager, Rural Planning & Credit Department, Reserve Bank of 
India is the Member Secretary.
