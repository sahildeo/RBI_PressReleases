 March 20, 2013 
 
Investment by NRIs/PIOs  under PIS :   
M/s Shilchar Technologies Limited  
  
 The Reserve Bank of India today notified that M/s Shilchar Technologies 
Limited has passed resolutions at the board of directors' level and a special 
resolution by the shareholders, to enhance the limit for purchase of its equity shares 
and convertible debentures by NRIs/PIOs through primary market and stock 
exchanges upto 24 per cent of its paid up capital, under the Portfolio Investment 
Scheme (PIS).
