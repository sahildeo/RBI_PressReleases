   Sources of Variation in Foreign Exchange Reserves in India during 2012-13 
 
Earlier today, the Reserve Bank of India released the Balance of Payments (BoP) data 
for 2012-13 on its website (www.rbi.org.in). On the basis of these data, the sources of variation 
in foreign exchange reserves during 2012-13 have been compiled. 
 
Sources of Variation in Foreign Exchange Reserves: 2012-13 
During 2012-13, there was a decline in the foreign exchange reserves. The sources of 
variation in the foreign exchange reserves are set out in Table 1.

On a balance of payments basis (i.e., excluding valuation effects), the foreign exchange 
reserves increased by US$ 3.8 billion during 2012-13 as against a decline of US$ 12.8 billion 
during 2011-12. The foreign exchange reserves in nominal terms (including the valuation 
effects) declined by US$ 2.4 billion during 2012-13 as compared to a  decline of US$ 10.4 
billion during the previous year (Table 2).
