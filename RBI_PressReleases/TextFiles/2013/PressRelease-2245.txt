            January 31, 2013 
RBI Releases Review of Prudential Guidelines on Restructuring of  
Advances by Banks and Financial Institutions – Draft Guidelines 
The Reserve Bank of India today released on its website, ‘Review of Prudential 
Guidelines on Restructuring of Advances by Banks and Financial Institutions’ – Draft 
Guidelines for comments and feedback. 
Comments / feedback on the draft guidelines may be sent by February 28, 
2013 to the Chief General Manager-in-Charge, Department of Banking Operations 
and Development, Reserve Bank of India, Central Office Building, 12th Floor, S.B. 
Singh Marg, Mumbai –400001 by email. 
Background 
The Monetary Policy Statement of April 2012 had indicated review of the 
existing prudential guidelines on restructuring of advances by banks/financial 
institutions by a Working Group (Chairman: Shri B. Mahapatra). The report of the 
Working Group was submitted in July 2012 and was placed on the website of the 
Reserve Bank inviting comments from the stakeholders. Accordingly, it was 
announced in the second quarter review of the monetary policy for 2012-13 that the 
recommendations of the Working Group as also the comments / suggestions received 
in this regard were under examination and draft guidelines would be issued by end-
January 2013.
