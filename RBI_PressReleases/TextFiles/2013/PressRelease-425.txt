RBI’s Special Repo Window for Liquidity Requirements of Mutual Funds 
On July 17, 2013, Reserve Bank had opened a special 3-day repo window to 
meet the liquidity requirements of mutual funds. 
As announced in the Second Quarter Review of Monetary Policy 2013-14 on 
October 29, 2013, Reserve Bank has decided to close the window with immediate 
effect.
