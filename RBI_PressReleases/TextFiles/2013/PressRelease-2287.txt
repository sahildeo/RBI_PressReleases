Investment by FIIs under PIS : M/s Pantaloon Retail (India) Limited     
  The Reserve Bank of India today notified that M/s Pantaloon Retail (India) 
Limited has crossed the overall limit of 24 per cent of its paid-up capital. Therefore, no 
further purchases of share of this company would be allowed through stock 
exchanges in India on behalf of Foreign Institutional Investors.
