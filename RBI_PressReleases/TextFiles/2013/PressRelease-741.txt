  September 17, 2013 
RBI penalises Karjan Nagrik Sahakari  Bank Ltd., Karjan  
Dist.Vadodara (Gujarat) 
The Reserve Bank of India has imposed a monetary penalty of `1.00 lakh  
(Rupees One Lakh only) on the Karjan Nagrik Sahakari Bank Ltd., Karjan Dist. 
Vadodara (Gujarat)  in exercise of the powers vested in it under the provisions of 
Section 47A (1)(b) read with Section 46(4) of the Banking Regulation Act, 1949 (As 
applicable to Co-operative Societies), for violation of RBI instructions relating to KYC 
norms.  
The Reserve Bank of India had issued a Show Cause Notice to the bank 
based on the finding of inspection for the financial position as on 31.03.2012, in 
response to which the bank submitted a written reply. After considering the facts of 
the case, the bank's reply in the matter, RBI came to the conclusion that the violation 
was substantiated and warranted imposition of penalty.
