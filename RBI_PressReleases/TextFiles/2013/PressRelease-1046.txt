Investment by FIIs under PIS - Removal from the Ban List : 
 M/s Maruti Suzuki India Ltd.  
The Reserve Bank of India has today notified that the foreign share holdings in 
M/s Maruti Suzuki India Ltd. by Foreign Institutional Investors (FIIs) under the 
Portfolio Investment Scheme (PIS) have gone below the prescribed threshold limit 
stipulated under the extant FDI Policy. Hence, the restrictions placed on the purchase 
of shares of M/s Maruti Suzuki India Ltd. are withdrawn with immediate effect.
