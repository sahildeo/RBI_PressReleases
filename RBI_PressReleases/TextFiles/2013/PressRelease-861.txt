August 31, 2013 
 No Proposal to convert Idle Gold into Bullion: RBI clarifies 
There have been some news stories in the media in the last couple of days 
about the Reserve Bank of India discussing/considering various options of converting 
idle gold, including that available with temple trusts, into bullion. 
The Reserve Bank clarifies that no such proposal is under its consideration at 
this juncture.
