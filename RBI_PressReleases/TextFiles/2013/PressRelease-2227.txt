Shri Rajiv Takru nominated on RBI Central Board
The Central Government has nominated Shri Rajiv Takru, Secretary,
Department of Financial Services, Ministry of Finance, New Delhi as a director on the
Central Board of Directors of the Reserve Bank of India vice Shri D. K. Mittal.
Shri Takru's nomination is with effect from February 4, 2013 and until further orders.
