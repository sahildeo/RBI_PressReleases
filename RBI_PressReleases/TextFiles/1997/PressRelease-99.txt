Satellite Dealers in the Government Securities Market 
The Reserve Bank of India granted approval to three more entities for registration as Satellite Dealers on April 7. These are:  
1. DSP Merrill Lynch Ltd.  
2. Kotak Mahindra Capital Company (Unlimited)  
3. Escorts Securities Ltd.
