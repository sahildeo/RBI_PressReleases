Chancellor Housing Development Finance & Investment (I) Ltd. prohibited from accepting 
deposits 
The Reserve Bank of India, in exercise of the powers vested in it under sub-section (4) of Section 45K 
of the Reserve Bank of India Act, 1934, has prohibited the Chancellor Housing Development 
Finance & Investment (I) Ltd., Registered Office at Nutan Bazar, P.O. Dhubulia, Dist. Nadia, West 
Bengal, from accepting deposits from any person including the existing depositors/certificate holders, 
in any form, whether by way of subscription/instalment to any scheme conducted by the company or by 
way of a renewal under any such schemes/deposits or otherwise, with immediate effect.  
The company has also been directed not to dispose of any assets, including withdrawal of bank 
deposits/securities, etc., lodged with banks, except for the purpose of payment of the dues to its 
depositors/certificate holders.  
Alpana Killawala
