Report on Trend and Progress of Banking in India 1996-97 (July-June) 
The Report on Trend and Progress of Banking in India, 1996-97 (July-June) presents a 
comprehensive review of the various issues and developments relating to the banking 
system in India. The Report also covers some significant developments in the banking 
arena in the first half of 1997-98. For the first time, bank-wise data on a number of 
variables such as non-performing assets, capital adequacy ratio and operating profits in 
relation to working funds have been provided in the Report.  
There are, in all four chapters in the Report. Chapter I presents a broad overview of the 
developments in the banking sector in India, with reference to banking sector reforms, 
such as, its performance, soundness, capital adequacy, non-performing assets, 
evaluation of their operations, etc., as also the vital policy measures undertaken and 
relevant perspectives. In Chapter II, a comprehensive coverage of various issues and 
operational aspects relating to the trends in provided. It also presents an analysis of 
bank group-wise working results of all commercial banking and major developments in 
banking reforms is provided. It also presents an analysis of bank group-wise working 
results of all commercial banks, their organisational matters, etc. This Chapter also 
presents, for the first time, bank-wise major financial parameters relating to their 
performance and soundness since the beginning of the latest phase of financial sector 
reforms, viz., 1991-92 to 1996-97. Besides, the performance of Regional Rural Banks 
(RRBs) has been analysed. Chapter III covers developments in co-operative banking, 
wherein the progress of primary (urban) co-operative banks and co-operative credit 
movement, as also NABARD's functional relationship with the co-operative institutions 
are discussed. Chapter IV briefly discusses the developments in the non-bank segment 
of the financial sector, viz., term lending institutions, investment institutions, mutual funds 
and non-banking financial companies. Apart from the above, the Report contains a 
detailed Annexure on `Progress of Financial Sector Reforms during 1992-93 to 1996-97'.
