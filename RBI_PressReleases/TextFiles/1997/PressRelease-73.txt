Helios Finance &amp; Investment Ltd. and Helios Corporation Ltd. prohibited from 
accepting deposits 
In exercise of the powers vested in it under sub-section (4) of Section 45K of the Reserve Bank of 
India Act, 1934, the Reserve Bank of India has prohibited Helios Finance & Investment Ltd. 
and Helios Corporation Ltd., Registered Office (of both companies) at: Helios Bhawan, 
Station Road, Patna 800001, from accepting deposits from any person including the existing 
depositors/certificate holders, in any form, whether by way of subscription/instalment to any 
scheme conducted by the companies or by way of renewal under any such schemes/deposits or 
otherwise, with immediate effect.  
The companies have also been directed not to dispose of any assets, including withdrawal of 
bank deposits/securities, etc., lodged with banks/kept with themselves, even for the purpose of 
payment of the dues to their depositors/certificate holders.  
Alpana Killawala
