28 Oct, 1997 
 
RBI statement on market developments 
 
Today the global developments have had some impact on transactions on the National Stock Exchange. The 
nature of transaction and participants indicate that, both domestic and foreign institutional investors have been 
active on either side of the market. The impact of this on foreign exchange market is not likely to b significant. 
The rupee should, therefore, remain stable and the RBI will ensure orderly condition in the foreign exchange 
market.  
(Alpana Killawala) 
General Manager
