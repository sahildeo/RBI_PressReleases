Foreign  currency  loans RBI to give approvals under USD  3  mio. Scheme 
Corporates and institutions which propose to raise foreign currency loans under the Foreign Currency - 
USD three million Scheme will now have to submit their applications for approval to the Reserve Bank 
of India. The applications will have to be made in Form ECB6, in duplicate, direct to the Chief General 
Manager, Exchange Control Department, IMD-II, Reserve Bank of India, Central Office, Mumbai 
400001. Application forms (ECB6) are available with the regional offices of the Reserve Bank of India. 
Details of drawal, utilisation and repayments, will have to be reported by the borrowers to the 
concerned regional offices of the Reserve Bank as at present.  
So far Indian entities desirous of raising foreign cur rency loans up to a maximum amount of USD three 
million or its equivalent were required to obtain the approvals from the Government of India, Ministry of 
Finance. The approvals were subject, inter alia, to the conditions that the loan should be for a minimum 
period of three years and no other loans under the scheme should be outstanding.
