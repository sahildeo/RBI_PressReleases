Issuance Calendar for Marketable Dated Securities 
It has been decided to continue with the practice of releasing indicative 
calendar for issuance of Government of India dated securities to enable institutional 
and retail investors to plan their investment efficiently and, at the same time, provide 
transparency and stability to the Government securities market. Accordingly, an 
indicative calendar for issuance of Government dated securities for the second half 
of the fiscal year 2010-11 (October 1, 2010 to March 31, 2011) as given below,  is 
being issued in consultation with the Government of India.

As hitherto, all the auctions covered by the calendar will have the facility of 
non-competitive bidding scheme under which five per cent of the notified amount will 
be reserved for the specified retail investors.  
Variable rate securities may be issued depending upon the market conditions. 
As in the past, the  Reserve Bank of India / Government of India will continue 
to have the flexibility to bring about modifications in the above calendar in terms of 
notified amount, issuance period, maturities, etc. depending upon the requirement of 
the Government of India, evolving market conditions  and other relevant factors after 
giving due notice.
