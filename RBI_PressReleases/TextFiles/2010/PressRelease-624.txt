August 30, 2010 
Investment by FIIs/NRIs/PIOs under PIS : ING Vysya Bank Ltd.   
The Reserve Bank of India has today notified that the aggregate net 
purchases of equity share holdings in ING Vysya Bank Ltd. Foreign Institutional 
Investors (FIIs)/Non-Resident Indians (NRIs)/Persons of Indian Origin (PIOs) under 
Portfolio Investment Scheme (PIS) have reached the trigger limit. Therefore, further 
purchases of equity shares in the primary/secondary market of this company would 
be allowed only after obtaining prior approval of the Reserve Bank of India.
