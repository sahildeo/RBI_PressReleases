April 5, 2010 
RBI further expands coverage of its ìDatabase on Indian Economyî 
The Reserve Bank of India has further expanded the coverage of its 
"Database on Indian Economy", an on-line database built on the data warehousing 
technology with enhanced functionality, to include static reports on Urban 
Cooperative Banks and Rural Cooperative Banks. With the release of these data 
series, the Database on Indian Economy would now have 451 static reports (subject 
and frequency wise), 74 subject wise and 80 frequency wise data query templates 
covering various sectors of the Indian economy.  
 The Reserve Bank of India's Database on Indian Economy is available on its 
website http://www.rbi.org.in under the Database Section. This can also be directly 
accessed through URL http://dbie.rbi.org.in. The coverage of the Database is 
continuously being expanded on the basis of the feedback received from the users of 
the database.
