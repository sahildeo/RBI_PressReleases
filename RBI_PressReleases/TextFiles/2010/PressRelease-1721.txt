January 21, 2010 
Investment by FIIs/NRIs/PIOs under PIS :   
M/s. Interworld Digital Limited (formerly Interworld Fashion Limited) 
 The Reserve Bank of India today notified that M/s. Interworld Digital Limited 
(formerly Interworld Fashion Limited) has agreed to enhance the limit for purchase of 
its equity shares by Foreign Institutional Investors (FIIs)/Non Resident Indians 
(NRIs)/Persons of Indian Origin (PIOs) under the Portfolio Investment Scheme up to 
100 per cent of its total paid up capital. The company has passed a resolution at the 
board of directors' level and extra-ordinary general meeting by its shareholders to do 
so. 
 Foreign Institutional Investors can now purchase under the Portfolio 
Investment Scheme through primary market and stock exchanges, equity shares and 
convertible debentures of M/s. Interworld Digital Limited provided :  
(i) The total purchases by FIIs does  not exceed the applicable overall ceiling limits 
of 100 per cent of total paid-up capital of the company. 
(ii) The purchases of equity shares by a single FII/SEBI approved sub-account of a 
registered FII in the company does not exceed 10% (Ten per cent) of the paid-
up equity capital of the company.  
(iii) The revised FII limit of 100% is within the overall 100% FDI limit applicable to 
the company.
