ICICI Bank Ltd. ñ Penalised 
 
The Reserve Bank of India has imposed a penalty of Rs.5.00 lakh (Rupees 
Five Lakh only) on ICICI Bank Ltd., in exercise of powers vested in it under the 
provisions of Section 47(A)(1)(b) read with Section 46(4) of the Banking Regulation 
Act, 1949  for violation of the guidelines on Know Your Customer (KYC)/Anti Money 
Laundering (AML) standards issued by Reserve Bank of India November 24, 2004.  
 
The Reserve Bank of India had issued a show cause notice dated April 9, 
2009 to the bank  in response to which the bank had submitted written replies. On a 
careful examination of the bank's replies and the oral submission made during the 
personal hearing held on August 3, 2009, the Reserve Bank of India found that the 
violation was conclusively established and the penalty was accordingly, imposed.
