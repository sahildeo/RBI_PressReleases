RBI offices to function as usual on April 01, 2010 
  
 All the offices of the Reserve Bank of India, including its Mumbai and        
Navi Mumbai offices, will function as usual on Thursday, April 01, 2010. Commercial 
banks will remain closed for public transactions on April 01, 2010 for their yearly 
closing of accounts as it is declared a public holiday under the Negotiable 
Instruments Act 1881 by the Government of Maharashtra.
