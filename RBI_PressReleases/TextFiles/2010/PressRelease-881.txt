Investment by FIIs under PIS :  
M/s. Panyam Cements & Minerals Industries Ltd.  
The Reserve Bank of India has today notified that the aggregate net 
purchases of equity share holdings in M/s. Panyam Cements & Minerals Industries 
Ltd.  by Foreign Institutional Investors (FIIs) under Portfolio Investment Scheme 
(PIS) have reached the trigger limit. Therefore, the company has been included in 
the ban list of Reserve Bank of India (for purchase of equity shares by FIIs). 
Accordingly, no further purchases of shares of this company would be allowed 
through stock exchanges in India on behalf of FIIs.
