Underwriting Auctions for Government Securities  
The Government of India have announced the sale (reissue)of ì7.02 per cent 
Government Stock 2016 î  for a notified amount of Rs. 5,000 crore (nominal) ì8.20 
per cent Government Stock 2022î for a notified amount of Rs.5,000 crore (nominal)
