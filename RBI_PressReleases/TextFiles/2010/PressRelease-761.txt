RBI revises Portfolio Allocation of Deputy Governors 
 
The Reserve Bank of India has today revised the portfolios of its Deputy 
Governors. The revised portfolios are: 
 
Smt. Shyamala Gopinath 
1. Department of External Investments and Operations 
2. Department of Government and Bank Accounts 
3. Department of Payments and Settlement Systems 
4. Department of Non banking Supervision 
5. Financial Markets Department 
6. Financial Stability Unit 
7. Foreign Exchange Department 
8. Internal Debt Management Department 
9. Secretary's Department 
10. Co-ordination 
 
Smt. Usha Thorat 
1. Central Security Cell 
2. Department of Banking Operations and Development 
3. Department of Banking Supervision 
4. Department of Currency Management 
5. Department of Expenditure and Budgetary Control 
6. Legal Department 
7. Premises Department 
8. Rural Planning and Credit Department 
9. Urban Banks Department 
  
Dr. K. C . Chakrabarty 
1. Customer Services Department 
2. Department of Information Technology 
3. Inspection Department 
4. Rajbhasha Department 
  
Dr. Subir Gokarn 
1. Department of Administration and Personnel Management (including Right to 
Information Act) 
2. Department of Communication 
3. Department of Economic Analysis and Policy 
4. Department of Statistics and Information Management 
5. Human Resources Development Department 
6. Monetary Policy Department 
7. Deposit Insurance and Credit Guarantee Corporation 
 
The revision will take effect from August 4, 2010.
