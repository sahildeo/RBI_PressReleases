Underwriting Auctions for Government securities  
The Government of India have announced the sale (reissue) of ì7.02 per cent 
Government Stock 2016 ì for a notified amount of Rs. 6,000 crore (nominal) ìGOI 
Floating Rate Bonds 2020î for a notified amount of Rs.3,000 crore (nominal)  and 
"8.26 per cent Government Stock 2027"  for a notified amount of Rs.3,000 crore 
(nominal) respectively, through auctions to be held on  April 23, 2010. The 
underwriting auctions for the captioned securities will be conducted on April 22, 2010 
(Thursday).
