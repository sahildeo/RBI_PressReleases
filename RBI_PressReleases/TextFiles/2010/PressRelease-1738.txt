Issue of new Commemorative Circulation Coin of Rs.5/- denomination     
(Nickel Brass) on the occasion of the Birth Centenary of "Perarignar Anna"

commemorative circulation coins of Rs.5/- denomination (Nickel Brass) issued by 
Government of India on the occasion of the Birth Centenary of "Perarignar Anna".
