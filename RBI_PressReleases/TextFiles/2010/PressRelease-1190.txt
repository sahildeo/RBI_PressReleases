May 17, 2010 
 RBI announces auction of 28-day Cash Management Bills  
The Reserve Bank of India has today announced the auction of 28-day 
Government of India Cash Management Bills for notified amount of Rs. 6,000 crore. 
The auction will be conducted on May 18, 2010 using "Multiple Price Auction" 
method. The Cash Management Bills will be repaid at par on June 16, 2010. 
It may be recalled that the Government of India, in consultation with the 
Reserve Bank of India, has decided to issue 28-day Cash Management Bills to meet 
the temporary cash flow mismatches of the Government.  
The sale will be subject to the terms and conditions specified in the General 
st 
Notification No. F.2 (12)-W & M/97 dated 31 March, 1998 issued by Government of 
India and as amended from time to time.  
The competitive bids for the auction should be submitted in electronic format 
on the Negotiated Dealing System (NDS) between 10.30 a.m. and 12.30 p.m on 
Tuesday, May 18, 2010. Results will be announced on the same day. Payments by 
successful bidders will be on Wednesday May 19, 2010. 
Press Release No. 2009-2010/227 issued by the Reserve Bank of India on 
August 10, 2009 and the Government of India Press Communique dated August 10, 
2009 contain details of the features of the Cash Management Bills.
