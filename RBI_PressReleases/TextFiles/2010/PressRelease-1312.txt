April 21, 2010 
Investment by FIIs/NRI/PIOs under PIS : Union Bank of India 
The Reserve Bank of India has today notified that the aggregate share 
holdings in Union Bank of India by Foreign Institutional Investors (FIIs)/Non-Resident 
Indians (NRIs)/Persons of Indian Origin (PIOs) under Portfolio Investment Scheme 
(PIS) have reached the trigger limit of their paid up capital. Therefore, further 
purchases of equity shares in the primary/secondary market of this company would 
be allowed only after obtaining prior approval of the Reserve Bank of India.
