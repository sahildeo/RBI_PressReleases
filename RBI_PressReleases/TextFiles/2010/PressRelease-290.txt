November 9, 2010 
 
Smt. Usha Thorat lays down office as Deputy Governor 
Smt. Shyamala Gopinath to hold temporary charge of her portfolios 
 
Smt. Usha Thorat, Deputy Governor relinquished charge of her office at the 
close of business on November 09, 2010 after completing her 5 year term. The 
portfolios handled by her will temporarily be looked after by Deputy Governor,      
Smt. Shyamala Gopinath until further orders.
