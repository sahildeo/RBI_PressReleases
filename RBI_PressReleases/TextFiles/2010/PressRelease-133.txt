December 9, 2010 
HLCC on Financial Markets meets 
A meeting of the High Level Coordination Committee on Financial Markets 
(HLCCFM) was held today at Kolkata. Dr. D. Subbarao, Governor, Reserve Bank of 
India chaired the meeting. Shri Ashok Chawla, Finance Secretary;  Dr. Kaushik 
Basu, Chief Economic Advisor, Ministry of Finance, Government of India; Shri C.B. 
Bhave, Chairman, Securities and Exchange Board of India (SEBI); Shri J. Hari 
Narayan, Chairman, Insurance Regulatory and Development Authority (IRDA); and 
senior officials of the Government of India, RBI, SEBI and PFRDA attended the 
meeting. 
The HLCCFM is a high level forum for interface among the financial sector 
regulators. 
The HLCCFM deliberated on the issues, inter alia, pertaining to the 
development of the corporate bond market including private placement of bonds and 
better understanding of regulatory gaps in the non-banking financial sector activities.
