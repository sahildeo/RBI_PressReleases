Market Stabilisation Scheme: Ceiling For Fiscal Year 2010-11 
In accordance with the provisions of the Memorandum of Understanding 
(MoU) on the Market Stabilisation Scheme (MSS), the ceiling for the outstanding 
under the MSS for the fiscal year 2010-11 has been fixed at Rs.50,000 crore. This 
ceiling will be reviewed when the outstandings reach the threshold limit of Rs.35,000 
crore.  
The current MSS outstanding balance (face value) is Rs.2,737 crore which is 
due for redemption during the fiscal year 2010-11.
