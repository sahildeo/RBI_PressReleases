January 25, 2010 
Investment by FIIs/NRIs/PIOs under PIS : M/s. Uttam Galva Steels Ltd. 
The Reserve Bank of India has today notified that the aggregate share 
holdings in M/s. Uttam Galva Steels Ltd. by Foreign Institutional Investors (FIIs)/Non 
resident Indians (NRIs)/Persons of Indian Origins (PIOs) under Portfolio Investment 
Scheme (PIS) have gone below the prescribed threshold limit stipulated under the 
extant FDI policy. Therefore, the restrictions placed on the purchase of shares of 
M/s. Uttam Galva Steels Ltd. have been withdrawn with immediate effect.
