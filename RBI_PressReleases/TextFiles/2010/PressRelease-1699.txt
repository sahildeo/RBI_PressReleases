RBI's Public Debt Office Shifted 
 
The Public Debt Office (PDO) of the Reserve Bank of India, Byculla will be 
functioning from Reserve Bank of India, Main Building, Fort, Mumbai - 400 001 with 
effect from January 28, 2010. All correspondence relating to Relief/Savings Bond 
and other schemes may henceforth be addressed to the Regional Director, Reserve 
Bank of India, Public Debt Office, Mumbai Office, Main Building, Shahid Bhagat 
Singh Road, Fort, Mumbai - 400 001. Phone No. 22701299, Fax No. 22695895 or
