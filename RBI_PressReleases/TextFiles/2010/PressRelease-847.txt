Reserve Bank extends  
Second LAF (SLAF) on a Daily Basis 
  
On an assessment of current overall liquidity conditions and with a view to 
providing flexibility to scheduled commercial banks and primary dealers in their 
liquidity management, it has been decided to extend the second liquidity adjustment 
facility (SLAF) on a daily basis till July 30, 2010.
