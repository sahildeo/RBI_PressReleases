August 30, 2010 
Investment by FII under PIS : Television Eighteen India Limited 
The Reserve Bank of India has today notified that the aggregate share 
holdings by Foreign Institutional Investors (FIIs) under Portfolio Investment Scheme 
(PIS) in Television Eighteen India Limited have gone below the prescribed threshold 
limit stipulated under the FDI Policy. Therefore, the restrictions placed on the 
purchase of shares of the above company are withdrawn with immediate effect.
