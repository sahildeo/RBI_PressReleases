March 29, 2010 
Investment by FIIs/NRIs/PIOs under PIS : M/s. Pantaloon Retails (India) Ltd. 
The Reserve Bank of India has today notified that the foreign shareholding in 
M/s. Pantaloon Retail (India) Ltd. by Foreign Institutional Investors (FIIs)/Non-
Resident Indians (NRIs)/Persons of Indian Origin (PIOs) under Portfolio Investment 
Scheme (PIS) has reached the overall limit of 25.77 percent of its paid up capital. 
Therefore, no further purchases of shares of the above company would be allowed in 
the stock exchanges in India on behalf of FIIs/NRIs/PIOs.
