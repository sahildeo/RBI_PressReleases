January 5, 2010 
Investment by FIIs under PIS : Maruti Suzuki  
The Reserve Bank of India has today notified that the aggregate net 
purchases of equity shares in Maruti Suzuki by Foreign Institutional Investors (FIIs) 
under Portfolio Investment Scheme (PIS) have reached the trigger limit of their paid 
up capital. Therefore, further purchases of equity shares in the primary/secondary 
markets of this company would be allowed only after obtaining prior approval of the 
Reserve Bank of India.
