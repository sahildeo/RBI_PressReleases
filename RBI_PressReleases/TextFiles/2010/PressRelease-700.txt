RBI releases Final Guidelines on  
Regulatory Framework for Core Investment Companies (CICs) 
 
The Reserve Bank of India (RBI) has issued the final guidelines on 
'Regulatory Framework for Core Investment Companies (CICs). The Reserve Bank 
had, on April 21, 2010, issued draft guidelines on regulatory framework for core 
investment companies for feedback from market participants. The guidelines have 
been finalised after considering the feedback received.
