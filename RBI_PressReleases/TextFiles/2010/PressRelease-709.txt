Branches of Bank of Rajasthan Ltd. to be  
Branches of ICICI Bank Ltd. from August 13 
All branches of Bank of Rajasthan Ltd. will function as branches of ICICI Bank 
Ltd. with effect from August 13, 2010. This is consequent upon the Reserve Bank of 
India sanctioning the Scheme of Amalgamation of Bank of Rajasthan Ltd. with ICICI 
Bank Ltd. The Scheme has been sanctioned in exercise of the powers contained in 
Sub-section (4) of Section 44A of the Banking Regulation Act, 1949. The Scheme 
will come into force with effect from close of business on August 12, 2010.
