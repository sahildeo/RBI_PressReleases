July 6, 2010 
RBI releases Handbook of Statistics on State Government Finances 
The Reserve Bank of India today released the second volume of its statistical 
publication Handbook of Statistics on State Government Finances. The first volume 
of the Handbook was published in June 2004.  
The publication is a major initiative of the Reserve Bank aimed at improving 
data dissemination on finances of State Governments. Through this publication, the 
Reserve Bank has been providing time series consolidated as well as State-wise 
detailed data on the transactions in the revenue and capital accounts of 28 State 
Governments and two Union Territories (New Delhi and Puducherry).  
The first part of current volume contains 28 tables covering consolidated data 
on some major fiscal indicators from 1990-91 to 2009-10 (BE).  The second part 
contains four appendices (I to IV) which cover State-wise detailed data from 2002-03 
to 2009-10 (BE) on the transactions in the revenue and capital accounts.  
The Handbook is brought out in three versions ñ print, CD and web. CD 
version incorporates intelligent search features and also covers data from 1980-81 
onwards in the first part and State-wise detailed data from 1990-91 onwards in the 
second part. 
This is an outcome of efforts of Division of State and Local Finances in 
Department of Economic Analysis and Policy. Feedback/comments on the
