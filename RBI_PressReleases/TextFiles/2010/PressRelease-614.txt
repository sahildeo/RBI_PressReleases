RBI releases Branch Banking Statistics-Volume 4: March 2009 
The Reserve Bank of India (Department of Statistics and Information 
Management) today published its "Branch Banking Statistics-Volume 4: March 
2009". The publication is based on the latest available information on bank branches. 
The volume contains 25 tables providing the number of branches/offices of all 
commercial banks in India, at different levels of aggregation, namely, region, 
state/union territory, district, population group, authorised dealer category, bank 
group and bank, as on March 31, 2009. The publication also provides information on 
number of branches/offices of commercial banks opened and closed during the last 
three years, state/district wise list of lead banks and bank and country-wise number 
of branches of Indian Banks functioning at overseas centres.  
The publication is available on RBI Website (http://www.rbi.org.in). Copies of 
the publication are also available in hard copy and CD format from Sales Section, 
Division of Reports, Reviews and Publications, Department of Economic Analysis 
and Policy, Reserve bank of India, Amar Building, P.M. Road, Mumbai-400 001.
