Investment by FIIs/NRIs/PIOs under PIS :  
M/s. Entertainment Network India Ltd. and  
M/s. Sterling Holiday Resorts (India) Limited 
 
The Reserve Bank of India has today notified that the aggregate share 
holdings in M/s. Entertainment Network India Ltd. and M/s. Sterling Holiday Resorts 
(India) Limited by Foreign Institutional Investors (FIIs)/ Non Resident Indians 
(NRIs)/Persons of Indian Origin (PIOs) under Portfolio Investment Scheme (PIS) 
have gone below the prescribed threshold limit stipulated under the extant FDI 
Policy. Therefore, the restrictions placed on the purchase of shares of the above 
company may be treated as withdrawn with immediate effect.
