June 14, 2010 
Investment by FIIs/NRI/PIOs under PIS : Dena Bank  
The Reserve Bank of India has today notified that the aggregate net 
purchases of equity share holdings in Dena Bank by Foreign Institutional Investors 
(FIIs)/Non-Resident Indians (NRIs)/Persons of Indian Origin (PIOs) under Portfolio 
Investment Scheme (PIS) have reached the trigger limit of their paid up capital. 
Therefore, further purchases of equity shares in the primary/secondary market of this 
company would be allowed only after obtaining prior approval of the Reserve Bank of 
India.
