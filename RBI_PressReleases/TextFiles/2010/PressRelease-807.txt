Investment by FIIs/NRIs/PIOs under PIS : Mid Day Multimedia Limited 
The Reserve Bank of India has today notified that the restrictions placed on 
the purchase of shares of Mid Day Multimedia Limited by Foreign Institutional 
Investors (FIIs)/Non-Resident Indians (NRIs)/Persons of Indian Origin (PIOs) under 
Portfolio Investment Scheme (PIS) are withdrawn with immediate effect. Accordingly, 
the shares of the company can be purchased on the secondary market on behalf of 
those FIIs/NRIs/PIOs who have obtained permission to invest under the Portfolio 
Investment Scheme subject to the existing ceilings and guidelines in this regard.
