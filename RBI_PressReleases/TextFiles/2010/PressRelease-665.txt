NSDC exempt from RBI Regulations 
 The Reserve Bank of India has communicated that the provisions of Sections 
45IA, 45IB and 45IC of the Reserve Bank of India Act, 1934 (2 of 1934) will not apply 
to National Skill Development Corporation (NSDC). The Reserve Bank has stated that 
NSDC is a company registered as non-profit company under Section 25 of the 
Companies Act, 1956 and regulatory oversight to NSDC is provided through the board 
of trustees of  National Skill Development Fund (NSDF). 
 The exemption is subject to the conditions that NSDC will : 
(i) confine its activities to the specific objectives for which it has been set up 
and not issue loans and advances except for the specific purpose; 
(ii) not accept or  hold public deposits; and 
(iii) not carry on any other financial activity except as (i) above.
