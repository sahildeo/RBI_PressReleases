November 26, 2010 
Certificate of Registration of  
M/s. Rakuja Investments Private Limited - Cancelled 
The Reserve Bank of India has on October 7, 2010 cancelled the certificate of 
registration granted on October 11, 2002 to M/s. Rakuja Investments Private Limited 
rd
having its registered office at U-203, 3 Floor, Shakarpur, Delhi-110092 for carrying 
on the business of a non-banking financial institution. Following cancellation of the 
registration certificate the company cannot transact the business of a non-banking 
financial institution.  
By the powers conferred under Section 45-IA (6) of the Reserve Bank of India 
Act, 1934, the Reserve Bank can cancel the registration certificate of a non-banking 
financial company. The business of a non-banking financial institution is defined in 
clause (a) of Section 45-I of the Reserve Bank of India Act, 1934.
