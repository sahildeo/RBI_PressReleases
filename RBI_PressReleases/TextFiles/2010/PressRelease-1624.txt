Issue of new Commemorative Circulation Coin of Rs.10/- denomination  
(bi-metallic) on the occasion of "HOMI BHABHA BIRTH CENTENARY YEAR" 
 
The Reserve Bank of India will shortly put into circulation new commemorative 
circulation coins of Rs.10/- denomination (bi-metallic) issued by Government of India 
on the occasion of " HOMI BHABHA BIRTH CENTENARY YEAR".
