January 22, 2010 
RBI announces rate of interest on FRB 2035 
The rate of interest on the Floating Rate Bonds, 2035 (FRB 2035) applicable 
for the period January 25, 2010 to January 24, 2015 shall be 7.17 per cent per 
annum. 
  It may be recalled that the FRB 2035 were issued on January 25, 2005 by the 
Government of India to the Reserve Bank of India on private placement basis 
against the transfer of subordinated debt of IDFC. The rate of interest of the bonds 
shall be reset by the Bank every five years at the prevailing 5 year yield on 
Government of India securities as on the last working day prior to commencement of 
each period of five years. Accordingly, the coupon of the FRB 2035 has been fixed 
on the basis of secondary market transactions in Government of India securities as 
on January 22, 2010.
