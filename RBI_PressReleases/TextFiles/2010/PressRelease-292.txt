November 9, 2010 
 
Issue of ` 5/- denomination banknotes without inset letter in the numbering 
panels, in Mahatma Gandhi Series with electro type water mark in numerals, 
windowed security thread, Year of Printing and floral see through design  
bearing the signature of Dr. D. Subbarao, Governor 
 
The Reserve Bank of India will shortly issue ` 5/- denomination banknotes 
without inset letter in the numbering panels in Mahatma Gandhi Series with new/ 
improved security features bearing the signature of Dr. D. Subbarao, Governor, 
Reserve Bank of India.  Except for the signature of Dr. D. Subbarao, Governor, the 
design of these notes to be issued now is similar in all respects to the banknotes in 
Mahatma Gandhi Series with additional / new security features issued earlier.  All 
banknotes in the denomination of ` 5/- issued by the Bank in the past will continue to 
be legal tender.
