Investments by FIIs/NRIs/PIOs under PIS : Indusind Bank Limited 
The Reserve Bank of India has today notified that the aggregate net 
purchases of equity shares in Indusind Bank Limited by Foreign Institutional 
Investors (FIIs)/Non-Resident Indians (NRIs)/Persons of Indian Origin (PIOs) under 
Portfolio Investment Scheme (PIS) in the primary/secondary markets have reached 
the trigger limit. Hence, the company has been included in the ban list of Reserve 
Bank of India (for purchase of equity shares by FIIs/NRIs/PIOs). Accordingly, no 
further purchases of shares of this company would be allowed through stock 
exchanges in India on behalf of FIIs/NRIs/PIOs.
