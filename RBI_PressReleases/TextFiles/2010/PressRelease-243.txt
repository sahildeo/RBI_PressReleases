Repurchase of Government of India Dated Securities 
 
As notified by the Government of India vide its Notification 
F.No.4(3)/W&M/2010 dated October 21, 2010, in addition to the repurchase auctions 
held on October 25, 2010,  the Reserve Bank of India has also made secondary 
market purchases of the Government of India securities through NDS-OM as part of 
the repurchase operations by the Government of India. The securities stand 
prematurely redeemed to the extent the amounts repurchased. The details of 
repurchase through NDS-OM and the current outstanding stock (after premature
