The Reserve Bank of India will shortly issue Rs.10/- denomination banknotes 
with 'M' inset letter in both numbering panels in Mahatma Gandhi Series ñ 2005 
bearing the signature of Dr. D. Subbarao, Governor.  Except for the change in the 
inset letter, the design of these notes to be issued now is similar in all respects to the 
banknotes in Mahatma Gandhi Series ñ 2005, with additional / new security features 
issued on April 27, 2006.  All banknotes in the denomination of Rs.10/- issued by the 
Bank in the past will continue to be legal tender.
