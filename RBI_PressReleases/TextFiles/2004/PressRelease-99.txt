 December 8, 2004
Tap sale of 7.32 per cent State Development Loan, 2014
closed for nineteen States
The 'on-tap' sale of 7.32 per cent ten-year State Development Loan, 2014 has closed
in respect of nineteen States, viz, Andhra Pradesh, Arunachal Pradesh, Assam,
Chhattisgarh, Goa, Gujarat, Haryana, Himachal Pradesh, Jharkhand, Karnataka, Madhya
Pradesh, Maharashtra, Mizoram, Nagaland, Rajasthan, Sikkim, Tripura, Uttaranchal and
West Bengal on receipt of the respective target amounts.  Amounts of allotment in respect of
these states are being advised to the investors concerned.
The on-tap sale in respect of loans for remaining four States, viz., Bihar, Jammu &
Kashmir, Orissa and Uttar Pradesh will be kept open on December 9, 2004 to collect the
balance target amount.  Applications in the prescribed form can be obtained from the Public
Debt Office of the respective offices of the Reserve Bank of India and Mumbai Office.
Applications will be received at these offices during banking hours while the tap sale
is open.  The Reserve Bank of India reserves the right to close the tap sale during the
banking hours on December 9, 2004 having regard to the borrowing requirements of the
State Governments and the subscriptions received.
As notified earlier, payment will be required to be made by the investors on
December 10, 2004 in respect of subscriptions received / allotted both on December 8 and
December 9, 2004.
