November 2, 2004
Tap sale of 7.36 per cent state Development Loans 2014
closed for seven states
The sale 'on tap' of 7.36 per cent ten years State Development Loans,
2014 has closed in respect of seven States, i.e. Goa, Gujarat, Himachal
Pradesh, Karnataka, Madhya Pradesh, Tamil Nadu and West Bengal on
receipt of the respective target amounts.  Amounts of allotment in respect of
these states are being advised to the investors concerned.
The sale on tap in respect of loans for remaining eighteen States, viz.,
Andra Pradesh, Arunachal Pradesh,  Assam, Bihar, Haryana, Jammu &
Kashmir, Jharkhand, Maharashtra, Manipur, Meghalaya, Mizoram,
Nagalanad, Orissa, Rajasthan, Sikkim, Tripura, Uttar Pradesh and Uttaranchal
will be kept open on November 3, 2004 to collect the balance target amount.
Applications in the prescribed form can be obtained from the Public Debt
Office of the respective offices of the Reserve Bank of India and Mumbai
Office.
Applications will be received at these offices during banking hours
while the tap sale is open.  The Reserve Bank of India reserves the right to
close the tap sale during the banking hours on November 3, 2004 having
regard to the borrowing requirements of the State Governments and the
subscriptions received.
As notified earlier, payment will be required to be made by the
investors on November 4, 2004 in respect of subscriptions received / allotted
both on November 2 and November 3, 2004.
