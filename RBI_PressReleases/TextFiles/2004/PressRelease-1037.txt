April 13, 2004 Tap Sale of State Government Loans All the 28 State Governments have offered to sell 5.60 per cent State Development Loan, 2014 having maturity of ten years aggregating about Rs.6500 crore on April 21, 2004 by way of tap sale under the normal market borrowing programme. The sale will be held at Mumbai for all the 28 States as also at the respective Regional Offices of the Reserve Bank of India. In case of over subscription, the States have the option to retain excess subscription to the extent of 20% of their respective target amounts. The Reserve Bank of India reserves the right to close the sale during or after close of banking hours on or after April 21, 2004 having regard to the subscriptions received. The payment by the successful applicants will be made on April 23, 2004. Applications in the prescribed form obtainable from the Public Debt Offices at the Reserve Bank of India will be received at the aforesaid Offices during banking hours while the sale is open.
