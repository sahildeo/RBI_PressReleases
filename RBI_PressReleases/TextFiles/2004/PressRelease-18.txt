December 29, 2004
Monitoring FIIs investment in Indian Companies
The Reserve Bank of India has notified on December 29, 2004 that
Foreign Institutional Investors (FIIs) can now purchase under Portfolio
Investment Scheme (PIS) equity shares and convertible debentures of Amtek
India Ltd.  through primary markets and stock exchanges in India upto 74 per
cent limits of its paid up capital, as the company has passed resolutions to
this effect at its Board of Directors' and  General Body Meetings.
