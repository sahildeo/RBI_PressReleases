February 17, 2004
Investment by FIls/ NRIs/PIOs -
Monitoring of Overall Ceiling
The Reserve Bank of India on February 17, 2004 has notified that no further purchases of
shares of ING Vyasya Bank Ltd., should be made on behalf of Foreign Institutional Investors
(FIIs) without the prior approval of the Reserve Bank of India. The Reserve Bank has stated that
ING Vyasya Bank Ltd., has reached the overall limit of 74 per cent of its paid up capital.
