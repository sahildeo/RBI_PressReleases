July 27, 2004
 Bank of Baroda
Investment by FIIs/NRIs/PIOs under PIS
       The Reserve Bank of India on July 27, 2004 has notified that no further
purchases of equity shares of Bank of Baroda should be made on behalf of
Foreign Institutional Investors (FIIs)/Non-Resident Indians (NRIs)/Persons of
Indian Origin (PIOs) through Stock Exchange/s in India without the prior
permission of the Reserve Bank. The Reserve Bank has stated that Bank of
Baroda  has reached the trigger limit of 18 per cent of its paid up capital.
