March 27, 2004 RBI to conduct Reverse Repo Auction on March 29 and 31 at Fixed Rate of 6% p.a. - LAF Auction Calendar The Reserve Bank of India has clarified that the reverse repo auctions to be conducted on March 29, 2004 and March 31, 2004 will be at fixed rate of 6% p.a. As such, there will be no variable reverse repo auction on March 29 and 31, 2004.
