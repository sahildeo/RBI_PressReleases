September 15, 2004
Monitoring FIIs investment in Indian Companies
The Reserve Bank of India has notified on September 15, 2004 that
Foreign Institutional Investors (FIIs) can now purchase under Portfolio
Investment Scheme (PIS) equity shares and convertible debentures of The
Jammu & Kashmir Bank Ltd. through primary markets and stock exchanges in
India upto 33 per cent limits of its paid up capital, as the company has passed
resolution to this effect at its Board of Directors' meeting and a special
resolution in its General Body Meeting.
