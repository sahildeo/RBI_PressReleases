May 27, 2004
Monitoring of Foreign Investment Limits in
Public and Private Sector Bank under PIS
The Reserve Bank of India has notified on May 27, 2004 that the percentage
of foreign holdings under the portfolio investment scheme (PIS) in the following
banks have gone below 18 percent and 72 per cent, respectively of their paid up
capital.
1. Oriental Bank of Commerce
2. Centurion Bank
The Reserve Bank has further notified that it will be in order for Foreign
Institutional Investors (FIIs)/Non-Resident Indians (NRIs) to purchase the shares of
the banks in the secondary market under the PIS subject to the existing ceilings and
guidelines in this regard.
