February 10, 2004  
 
 
Investment by FIIs  
 
       The Reserve Bank of India on February 10, 2004 has notified that no further 
purchases of equity shares of The Associated Cement Company Ltd., (ACC Ltd.) should 
be made on behalf of Foreign Institutional Investors (FIIs) through stock exchanges in 
India without the prior permission of the Reserve Bank.  The Reserve Bank has stated 
that The Associated Cement Company Ltd., has reached the overall ceiling of 24 per 
cent of its paid up equity capital to be held by all registered FIIs.
