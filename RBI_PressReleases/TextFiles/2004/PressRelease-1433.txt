January 7, 2004 RBI appoints Banking Ombudsman at Jaipur The Reserve Bank of India has appointed Shri Rajendra Singh as the Banking Ombudsman for the state of Rajasthan with effect from January 5, 2004. As Banking Ombudsman, Shri Singh will have his office at Reserve Bank of India Building, Rambagh Circle, Tonk Road, Jaipur-302 004. The Banking Ombudsman Scheme was introduced in June 1995 with a view to providing to the bank customers quick and low-cost redressal of grievances relating to banking services. Its scope of authority and functions include redressal of grievances against deficiency in banking services, concerning loans and advances and other specified matters and arbitration for specified disputes. All commercial banks, regional rural banks and scheduled primary co-operative banks are covered under the Banking Ombudsman Scheme. Ajith Prasad Manager Press Release : 2003-2004/833
