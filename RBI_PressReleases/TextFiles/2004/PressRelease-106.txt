Monitoring of investment limits of FIIs/NRIs/PIOs under PIS
       The Reserve Bank of India has notified on December 07, 2004 that
Foreign Institutional Investors (FIIs/Non-Resident Indians (NRIs)/Persons of
Indian Origin (PIOs) can now purchase under Portfolio Investment Scheme
(PIS) equity shares of Bharti Tele-Ventures Ltd., through the secondary
markets in  India.  The Reserve Bank of India has stated that the holdings of
FIIs/NRIs/PIOs in the company have gone down below 48 per cent limit of its
paid up capital.
