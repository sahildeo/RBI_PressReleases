May 26, 2004
Tap sale of State Development Loans for eleven States closed
The sale on tap of 5.70 per cent ten year State Development Loans,
2014 in respect of eleven State Governments has been closed.
Eleven states received subscriptions in excess of their target amounts
and excess subscriptions upto 20 per cent of the target amounts have been
retained by these states.
The sale on tap in respect of loans of remaining eight states, viz.,
Assam, Bihar, Jammu & Kashmir, Jharkhand, Maharashtra, Orissa, Uttar
Pradesh and West Bengal will be kept open on May 27, 2004 to collect the
balance target amount. Applications in the prescribed form can be obtained
from the Public Debt Office of the respective offices of the Reserve Bank of
India and Mumbai Office.
As notified earlier, payments will be required to be made by the
investors on May 28, 2004 in respect of subscriptions received/allotted.
