January 20, 2004 
RBI Cancels license of The Mahalaxmi Cooperative Urban 
Bank Ltd., Hyderabad, Andhra Pradesh 
 
 The Reserve Bank of India has cancelled the license of The Mahalaxmi 
Cooperative Urban Bank Ltd., Hyderabad, Andhra Pradesh to carry on 
banking business. The bank is precluded from transacting the business 
of ‘banking’ as defined in the Banking Regulation Act, 1949 (As 
applicable to Co-operative Societies) and it will not be in order for it, 
among other things, to accept deposits from public and allow 
withdrawals by cheque, draft, order or otherwise. 
  
 The Reserve Bank has cancelled the license under Section 22 of the 
Banking regulation Act, 1949  (as applicable to Co-operative Societies).
