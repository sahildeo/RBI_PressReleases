July 3, 2004
Investment by Foreign Institutional Investors -
Monitoring of Overall Ceiling
The Reserve Bank of India on July 3, 2004 has notified that the
aggregate share holding of Foreign Institutional Investors (FIIs) in Arvind Mills
Ltd., has reached the overall limit of 24 per cent of its paid up capital. The
Reserve Bank has stated that no further purchases of shares of Arvind Mills
Ltd., should, therefore, be made through stock exchanges in India on behalf of
FIIs.
