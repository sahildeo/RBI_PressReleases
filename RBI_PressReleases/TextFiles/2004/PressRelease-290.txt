October 18, 2004
GoI cancels the auction of dated security
scheduled during October 18-25, 2004
Taking into account all relevant factors, it has been decided to cancel the
auction of dated security to be held during October 18-25, 2004. As per the indicative
calendar for issuance of Government securities issued on September 20, 2004 the
Government of India had scheduled one auction of dated security for Rs. 5,000 crore
during the period October 18-25, 2004.
