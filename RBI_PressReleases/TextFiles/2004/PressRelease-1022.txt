Investment by FIIs under PIS
The Reserve Bank of India on April 17, 2004 has notified that no further purchases of equity shares of M/s. Mphasis BFL Software Ltd., should be made on behalf of Foreign Institutional Investors (FIIs) through primary/secondary markets in India without the prior permission of the Reserve Bank. The Reserve Bank has stated that M/s. Mphasis BFL Software Ltd., has reached the trigger limit of 22 per cent of its paid up capital. P. V. Sadanandan Manager
