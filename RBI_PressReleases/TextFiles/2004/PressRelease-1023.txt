The Reserve Bank of India on April 17, 2004 has notified that no further purchases of equity shares of Oriental Bank of Commerce, should be made on behalf of Foreign Institutional Investors (FIIs)/Non-Resident Indians (NRIs) and Persons of Indian Origin (PIOs) through primary/secondary markets in India without the prior permission of the Reserve Bank. The Reserve Bank has stated that Oriental Bank of Commerce has reached the trigger limit of 18 per cent of its paid up capital. P. V. Sadanandan Manager
