December 07, 2004
Monitoring of investment limits of FIIs/NRIs/PIOs under PIS
       The Reserve Bank of India has notified on December 07, 2004 that the
aggregate foreign share holdings by Foreign Institutional Investors ( FIIs)/
Non-Resident Indians (NRIs)/ Persons of Indian Origin (PIOs) in the State
Bank of India have gone below the limit of 20 per cent of its paid up capital.
The Reserve Bank of India has stated that further purchases of equity shares
on behalf of FIIs/NRIs/PIOs will be allowed only on obtaining its prior
clearance.
