May 3, 2004
Investment by Foreign Institutional Investors -
Monitoring of Overall Ceiling
The Reserve Bank of India on May 3, 2004 has notified that no further
purchases of shares of ICICI Bank Ltd., should be made on behalf of Foreign
Institutional Investors (FIIs) without the prior approval of the Reserve Bank of
India. The Reserve Bank has stated that ICICI Bank Ltd., has reached the
trigger limit of 47 per cent of its paid up capital.
