June 1, 2004
ECBs Worth USD 1756 mio. approved in April
The Reserve Bank of India today released, on its website, the data on
External Commercial Borrowings (ECBs) (both under the Automatic Route
and the Approval Route) for April  2004. According to the data, a total of 73
proposals for ECBs went through the automatic route. The amount raised
through these proposals totalled USD 1356 million. Under the approval route,
two proposals, viz., Exim Bank and Power Finance Corporation were
approved for a maturity period of five years each. The total amount under the
approval route was USD 400 million. The total amount of External Commercial
borrowings for the month of April 2004 was USD 1756 million.
