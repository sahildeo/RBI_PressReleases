Investment by FIIs/NRIs/PIOs under PIS
The Reserve Bank of India has notified on April 13, 2004 that Foreign Institutional Investors (FIIs) can now purchase equity shares and convertible debentures of the following companies through primary markets and stock exchanges upto 49 per cent of their paid up capital, as these companies have passed resolutions to this effect at their Board of Directors' and General Body Meetings. 1. Alok Industries Ltd. 2. Amtek Auto Ltd. The Reserve Bank has also notified that Alok Industries Ltd., has also passed a resolution at their General Body Meeting agreeing for the purchase of its equity shares and convertible debentures by Non-Resident Indians (NRIs) and Persons of Indian Origin (PIOs) upto 24 per cent of its paid up capital through stock exchanges under Portfolio Investment Schemes (PIS).
