November 4, 2004
RBI announces rate of interest on FRBs 2012
The rate of interest on the Floating Rate Bonds, 2012 (FRB, 2012)
applicable for the year (November 10, 2004 to November 9, 2005) shall be
5.60 percent per annum.
It may be recalled that the rate of interest on the FRB, 2012 was set at
a mark-up (as decided in the auction held on November 7, 2003) over and
above the variable base rate to be announced annually. The variable base
rate for payment of interest shall be the average rate (rounded off up to two
decimal places) of the implicit yields at cut-off prices of the last three auctions
of Government of India 364 day Treasury Bills held up to November 10. The
variable base rate based on the average rate of the implicit yields at cut-off
prices of the said last three auctions of Government of India 364 day Treasury
Bills worked  out to be 5.51 per cent. The mark-up decided in the auction held
on November 7, 2003 was (+) 0.09 (plus 0.09). The coupon rate has been
fixed accordingly.
