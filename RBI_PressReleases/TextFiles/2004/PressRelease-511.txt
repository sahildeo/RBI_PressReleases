August 19, 2004
Three State Governments to raise funds from market on August 24, 2004
Three state Governments have announced the sale of 10-year stock to the
public by auction for an aggregate amount of about Rs.1186 crore as detailed below:
State                       Amount (Rs.crore)
Kerala 355.66
Tamilnadu 450.00
West Bengal            380.00
                              Total   1185.66
The auction will be yield based under multiple yield format and will be
conducted by Mumbai (Fort) office of the Reserve Bank of India on Tuesday,  August
24, 2004. Tenders should be submitted in the prescribed form on Tuesday, August
24, 2004 before 12.30 p.m. NDS members shall submit their applications through
NDS only. Results will be announced the same evening. Payment by successful
bidders will be on Wednesday, August 25, 2004.
