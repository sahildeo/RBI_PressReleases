January 20, 2004 
Monitoring FIIs investment in Indian Companies 
 
 The Reserve Bank of India has notified on January 20, 2004 that Foreign 
Institutional Investors (FIIs) can now purchase equity shares and 
convertible debentures of the following companies, through primary 
markets and stock exchanges in India up to the limits indicated against 
them, as these companies have passed resolutions to this effect at their 
Board of Director’s and General Body Meetings. 
  
1. Alok Industries Ltd., - 30 per cent. 
2. Welspun Gujarat Stahal Rohren Ltd., - 40 per cent.
