May 27, 2004
Tap sale of 5.70 per cent State Development Loans, 2014 closed
The sale ‘on tap’ of 5.70 per cent State Development Loan, 2014 in respect of
the remaining eight State Governments viz., Assam, Bihar, Jammu & Kashmir,
Jharkhand, Maharashtra, Orissa, Uttar Pradesh and West Bengal has been closed
today. It may be recalled that the sale opened on May 26, 2004 for 19 State
Governments for a target amount of Rs. 8000 crore on account of Debt Swap
Scheme with an option to retain additional subscription up to 20% and Rs.380 crore
on account of normal market borrowings. Total subscription received on both the
days aggregated Rs. 9350 crore, of which Rs.8471 crore have been retained.
As notified earlier, payment will be required to be made by the investors on
May 28, 2004 in respect of subscriptions received both on May 26 and May 27, 2004.
