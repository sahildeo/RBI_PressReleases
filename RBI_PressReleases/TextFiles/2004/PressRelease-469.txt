September 1, 2004
 Reserve Bank rejects application for certificate of
registration of Madras Credit and Investments Limited
The Reserve Bank of India, has on August 23, 2004 cancelled the certificate
of registration issued to Madras Credit and Investments Limited having its office at
Flat No.115, Block-A, First Floor, Shivalaya Building, No.196, Ethiraj Salai, Old
No.16 (26), Commander in Chief Road, Egmore, Chennai-600 105.
Following cancellation of certificate of registration, the Madras Credit and
Investments Limited can not transact the business of a non-banking financial
institution.
The Reserve Bank of India has taken this action under powers conferred on
it by Section 45IA of the Reserve Bank of India Act, 1934.
