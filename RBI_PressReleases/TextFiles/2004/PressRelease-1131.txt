March 25, 2004 Investment by Foreign Institutional Investors Monitoring of Overall Ceiling The Reserve Bank of India on March 25, 2004 has notified that no further purchases of shares of M/s. Mahindra & Mahindra Ltd., should be made on behalf of Foreign Institutional Investors (FIIs) without the prior approval of the Reserve Bank of India. The Reserve Bank has stated that M/s. Mahindra & Mahindra Ltd., has reached the trigger limit of 22 per cent of its paid up capital. P. V. Sadanandan Manager
