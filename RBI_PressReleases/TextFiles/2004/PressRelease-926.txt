May 13, 2004
GoI issues Moratorium on
South Gujarat Local Area Bank Ltd., Navsari, Gujarat
On the recommendations of the Reserve Bank of India, the
Government of India has issued an Order of Moratorium in respect of the
South Gujarat Local Area Bank Ltd., Navsari, Gujarat. The moratorium will
remain in force from the close of business on May 13, 2004 up to and
inclusive of August 12, 2004. During the period of moratorium, the bank is
permitted to make only payments as have been specified in this regard in the
Order of Moratorium.  Issued under Section 45(2) of the Banking Regulation
Act, 1949, the Order of Moratorium is in the interest of the depositors and the
banking system.
