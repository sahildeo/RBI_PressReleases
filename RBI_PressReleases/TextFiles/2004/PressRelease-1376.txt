January 20, 2004 
 
 
Tap sale of 5.90 per cent SDL 2017 closed 
 
 The sale `on tap' of 5.90 per cent State Development Loan, 2017 in 
respect of the remaining 14 State Governments has been closed today. It may 
be recalled that the sale opened on January 19, 2004 for 27 State 
Governments for a target amount of Rs.8050 crore and the sale was closed 
for 13 States on January 19, 2004.
