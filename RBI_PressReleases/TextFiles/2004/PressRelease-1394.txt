January 15, 2004 
Reserve Bank rejects application for Certificate of Registration of 
 M/s. Lloyds Finance Limited 
 
 The Reserve Bank of India, has on January 13, 2004 rejected the 
application for certificate of registration submitted by M/s. Lloyds Finance Limited 
having its office at Unit No.3, Ground floor, Raheja Centre, Nariman Point,  
Mumbai 400 021 for carrying on the business of a non-banking financial 
institution. 
 Following rejection of application for certificate of registration, M/s. Lloyds 
Finance Limited cannot transact the business of a non-banking financial 
institution. The company has also been prohibited from acceptance of deposit 
and alienation of assets.   
 The Reserve Bank of India has taken this action under powers conferred 
on it by Section 45 of the Reserve Bank of India Act, 1934.
