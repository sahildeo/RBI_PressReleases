November 4, 2004
Monitoring FIIs investment in Indian Companies
The Reserve Bank of India has notified on November 4, 2004 that
Foreign Institutional Investors (FIIs) can now purchase equity shares and
convertible debentures of Pritish Nandy Communications Ltd. through primary
markets and stock exchanges in India up to 60 per cent of its paid up capital,
as the company has passed resolutions to this effect at their Board of
Directors' and  General Body Meetings.
