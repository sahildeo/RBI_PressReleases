May 19, 2004
Investment by NRIs/PIOs-
Monitoring of Overall Ceiling
The Reserve Bank of India on May 19, 2004 has notified that no further
purchases of shares of the following companies, should be made through stock
exchanges in India on behalf of NRIs/PIOs under PIS Scheme. The Reserve Bank
has stated that the NRIs/PIOs holdings of the following companies have reached the
overall ceiling of 10 per cent of its paid up capital.
i) Chandraprabhu Housing Ltd.
ii) Coxswain Technology Ltd. (Kaveri Biotech Ltd.)
iii) Dev Sugars Ltd.
iv) Dharendra Industries LTD.
v) Fintech Communications
vi) Rama Phosphates Ltd.
vii) SquareD Biotech Ltd.
