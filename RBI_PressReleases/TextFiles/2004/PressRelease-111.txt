844 Bank Branches to accept IT
The Reserve Bank of India has advised income-tax assessees in the
cities of Mumbai and Navi Mumbai to take advantage of paying their income
tax dues at any of the designated bank branches. The income-tax department
has authorised 865 computerised branches of public sector banks and private
sector banks in Mumbai and Navi Mumbai to accept payment of income-tax
dues. Of these, 808 are public sector bank branches, 23 branches of HDFC
Bank, 9 branches of ICICI Bank, 16 Branches of UTI Bank and 9 branches of
IDBI Bank.
The Reserve Bank has also advised income-tax assessees to avoid
last minute rush at the Reserve Bank's/designated banks' counters and to
deposit the income-tax dues sufficiently in advance.
