March 1, 2004 RBI Reconstitutes Technical Advisory Committee on Money, Foreign Exchange and Government Securities Markets The Reserve Bank of India has decided to reconstitute the Technical Advisory Committee on Money and Government Securities Markets for a further term of two years from the day of its first meeting. The scope of the Committee has been widened to include foreign exchange market keeping in view of its interlinkages with money and government securities markets and its implications for monetary policy implications. The Committee would advise the Reserve Bank, on an ongoing basis, on the development of healthy and vibrant money, foreign exchange and government securities markets.
