RBI to issue Rs.50 denomination bank notes with inset letter `E' in
numbering panel in Mahatma Gandhi series bearing the signature of
Dr.Y.V.Reddy, Governor
The Reserve Bank of India will shortly be issuing Rs.50 denomination
bank notes with inset letter `E'  in numbering panel in Mahatma Gandhi Series
bearing the  signature of Dr.Y.V. Reddy, Governor.  Except for the change in
inset letter, the design of these notes to be issued now is similar in all respects to
those issued earlier in Mahatma Gandhi Series. All notes in the denomination of
Rs.50 issued by the Reserve Bank in the past will continue to be legal tender.
