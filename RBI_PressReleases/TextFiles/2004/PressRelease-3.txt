RBI permits trading in Power Bonds
The Reserve Bank of India (RBI) has permitted transfer of/trading in the
Power Bonds maturing on October 1, 2008 and April 1, 2009, issued by various
states to Central Public Sector Undertakings (CPSUs). These Bonds were issued in
terms of a Tripartite Agreement among the 27 State Governments, Ministry of
Power, Government of India and the Reserve Bank, under one time settlement of
dues of State Electricity Boards. It may be recalled that as provided in the Tripartite
Agreement, these bonds were to be released for trading in a phased manner.
