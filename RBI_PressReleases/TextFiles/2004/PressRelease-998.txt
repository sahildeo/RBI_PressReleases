April 22, 2004
Investment by FIIs/NRIs/PIOs under PIS
The Reserve Bank of India has notified on April 22, 2004 that the holdings
of foreign institutional investors (FIIs)/non-resident Indians (NRIs) and persons of
Indian origin (PIOs) under the portfolio investment scheme (PIS) in  the paid up
capital of ICICI Bank Ltd., have gone below 72 per cent. The Reserve Bank has
further notified that it will be in order for FIIs/NRIs and PIOs to purchase the
shares of ICICI Bank Ltd. in the secondary market under the portfolio investment
scheme subject to the existing ceilings and guidelines in this regard.
