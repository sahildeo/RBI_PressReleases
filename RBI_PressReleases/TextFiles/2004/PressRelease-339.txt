Statistical Tables Relating to Banks in India, 2003-2004
The Reserve Bank of India has published “Statistical Tables Relating to
th
Banks in India 2003-2004”. This is 59 edition of the volume.
The publication contains detailed tables relating to assets and liabilities
and earnings and expenses of Scheduled Commercial Banks (excluding RRBs)
in India for the year 2003-2004.  In addition to detailed bank-wise tables, the
publication provides summary tables giving aggregated data for bank groups as
well as for all banks.
The publication also gives data on important parameters like maturity and
ownership pattern of deposits, sector-wise bank credit, investment pattern, state-
wise distribution of employees, insured deposits, risk-weighted assets and NPAs.
Tables giving important ratios like return on assets, cost of funds, capital
adequacy ratio and business per employee have also been included. The tables
have been supplemented with graphs and maps.
The CD-ROM, besides providing all the tables, graphs and maps given in
the publication, gives inter-active data querying facility. It also provides facility to
calculate growth rates and ratios.
The data published in this volume are also made available through Internet
at http://www.rbi.org.in
