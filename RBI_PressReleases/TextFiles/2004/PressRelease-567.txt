Underwriting by Primary Dealers
The Reserve Bank of India has set the cut-off rate for underwriting bids by Primary
Dealers for the auctions of "Government of India FRB, 2015 (II)" and "30 Year Government
Stock" to be held on August 9, 2004 at Re.0.04 (four paise) and Re.0.10 (ten paise) per
Rs.100 respectively.
