November 5, 2004
The Grasim Industires Ltd. - Investment by FIIs under PIS
       The Reserve Bank of India on November 5, 2004 has notified that no
further purchases of equity shares of The Grasim Industires Ltd., should be
made on behalf of Foreign Institutional Investors (FIIs) through
primary/secondary markets in India without the prior permission of the
Reserve Bank. The Reserve Bank has stated that The Grasim Industires Ltd.,
has reached the trigger limit of 22 per cent of its paid up capital.
