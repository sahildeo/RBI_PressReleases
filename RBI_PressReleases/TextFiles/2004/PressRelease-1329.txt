Foreign Exchange Reserves 
 
It is proposed to compile half yearly reports on management of foreign exchange 
reserves for bringing about more transparency and also for enhancing the level of disclosure in 
st
this regard. These reports will be prepared with reference to positions as on 31 March and 30th 
September each year, with a time lag of about 3 months and put in the public domain by the RBI. 
The first such report with reference to September 30, 2003 is now being placed by RBI on its   
website.
