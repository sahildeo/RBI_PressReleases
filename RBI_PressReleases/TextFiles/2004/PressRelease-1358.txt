Monitoring FIIs investment in Indian Companies 
 
 The Reserve Bank of India has notified on January 24, 2004 that Foreign Institutional 
Investors (FIIs) can now purchase equity shares and convertible debentures of Tata Motors Ltd., 
through primary and secondary markets in India up to 35 per cent of its paid up capital,  as the 
company has passed a resolution to this effect at its Board of Directors' and  General Body 
Meetings.
