July 26, 2004
On Tap Sale of State Government Loans
for Rs.8600 crore, on July 28, 2004
Chhattisgarh will not be participating in the tap sale for the State
Development Loans to be floated on July 28, 2004. Thus, the target amount
will be reduced to about Rs.8500 crore. Other terms and conditions of the tap
sale will remain same.
It was announced earlier that 20 State Governments will be raising
market loans for about Rs.8600 crore with coupon rate of 6.35 per cent per
annum for a tenor of nine years on July 28, 2004 with payment being made on
July 30, 2004.
