December 9, 2004
Bank of Baroda :
Investment by FIIs/NRIs/PIOs under PIS
       The Reserve Bank of India on December 9, 2004 has notified that no
further purchases of equity shares of Bank of Baroda, should be made on
behalf of Foreign Institutional Investors (FIIs)/Non-Resident Indians
(NRIs)/Persons of Indian Origin (PIOs) through stock exchanges in India. The
Reserve Bank has stated that Bank of Baroda has reached the overall limit of
20 per cent of its paid up capital.
