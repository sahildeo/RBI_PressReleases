June 30, 2004
Investment by Foreign Institutional Investors -
Monitoring of Overall Ceiling
The Reserve Bank of India on June 30, 2004 has notified that no
further purchases of shares of The Arvind Mills Ltd., should be made on
behalf of Foreign Institutional Investors (FIIs) without the prior approval of the
Reserve Bank of India. The Reserve Bank has stated that The Arvind Mills
Ltd., has reached the trigger limit of 22 per cent of its paid up capital.
