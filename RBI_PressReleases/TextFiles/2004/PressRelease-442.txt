September 8, 2004
Underwriting by Primary Dealers
The Reserve Bank of India has set the cut-off rate for underwriting bids
by Primary Dealers for the auctions of "Government of India Floating Rate
Bonds, 2013", "6.65% Government Stock, 2009 at Re.0.04 (four paise) and
Re.0.02 (two paise) per Rs.100 respectively and rejected all the underwriting
bids for "8.35% Government Stock, 2022".
