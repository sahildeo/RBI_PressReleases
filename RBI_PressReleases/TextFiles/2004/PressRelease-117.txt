December 4, 2004
Bharti Tele-Ventures Ltd. :
Investment by FIIs/NRIs/PIOs under PIS
       The Reserve Bank of India on December 4, 2004 has notified that no
further purchases of equity shares of Bharti Tele-Ventures Ltd., should be
made on behalf of Foreign Institutional Investors (FIIs)/Non-Resident Indians
(NRIs)/Persons of Indian Origins (PIOs) through Stock Exchange/s in India
without the prior permission of the Reserve Bank. The Reserve Bank has
stated that Bharti Tele-Ventures Ltd., has reached the trigger limit of 48 per
cent of its paid up capital.
