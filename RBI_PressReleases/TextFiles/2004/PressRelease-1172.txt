RBI releases ECB Data for February 2004

The Reserve Bank of India today released the information relating to External Commercial Borrowings (ECB) under both - automatic and approval routes for the month of February 2004. It may be recalled that in the revised ECB Guidelines (A.P. (DIR Series) Circular No.60 dated January 31, 2004) it was indicated that with a view to facilitating greater transparency, information relating to relating to External Commercial Borrowings (ECB) under both - automatic and approval routes will be disseminated through the RBI website. The information will be disseminated on a monthly basis. Accordingly, the data on ECB under both - automatic and approval routes - during February 2004 have been placed on the Reserve Bank website.
