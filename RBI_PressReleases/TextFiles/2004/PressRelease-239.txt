November 1, 2004
Aftek Infosys Ltd. - Investment by FIIs under PIS
       The Reserve Bank of India on November 1, 2004 has notified that no
further purchases of equity shares of Aftek Infosys Ltd., should be made on
behalf of Foreign Institutional Investors (FIIs) through primary/secondary
markets in India without the prior permission of the Reserve Bank. The
Reserve Bank has stated that Aftek Infosys Ltd., has reached the trigger limit
of 22 per cent of its paid up capital.
