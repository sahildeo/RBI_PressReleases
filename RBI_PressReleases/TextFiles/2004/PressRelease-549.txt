RBI to respond to Developments in Measured Manner:
RBI Governor reassures
In response to press queries, Dr. Y V Reddy, Governor, Reserve Bank
of India reiterated, "The Reserve Bank is monitoring the developments
carefully. In particular, the Reserve Bank is analysing how the imported oil
shock is turning out to be globally and how is it absorbed domestically. The
Governor reassured that the Reserve Bank will respond to the developments
in a measured manner on an ongoing basis with a view to maintaining price
stability over the year."
Alpana Killawala
Chief General Manager
