December 1, 2004
GoI cancels the auction of dated security
scheduled during December 1-8, 2004
Taking into account all relevant factors, the Government of India, in
consultation with the Reserve Bank of India, has decided to cancel the auction of
dated security for Rs. 8,000 crore to be held during December 1-8, 2004. It may
be recalled that as per the indicative calendar for issuance of Government
securities issued on September 20, 2004, the Government of India had
scheduled auctions of (a) 10-14 year security for Rs. 6,000 crore and (b) 20-year
and above security for Rs.2,000 crore, during the period December 1-8, 2004.
