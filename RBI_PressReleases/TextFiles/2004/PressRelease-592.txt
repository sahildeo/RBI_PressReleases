July 31, 2004
Divis Laboratory Ltd. : Investment by FIIs under PIS
       The Reserve Bank of India on July 31, 2004 has notified that no further purchases of equity shares of Divis
Laboratory Ltd., should be made on behalf of Foreign Institutional Investors (FIIs) through Stock Exchange/s in
India without the prior permission of the Reserve Bank. The Reserve Bank has stated that Divis Laboratory Ltd.,
has reached the trigger limit of 22 per cent of its paid up capital.
