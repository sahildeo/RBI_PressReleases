July 23, 2004
Reserve Bank Cancels Certificate of Registration of
Mont Blanc Financial Services Limited, Chennai
The Reserve Bank of India, has on July 14, 2004 cancelled the certificate of
registration issued to Mont Blanc Financial Services Limited, having its registered office at
1st Floor, Abhinav Centre, No.4, Co-operative Colony, Off Chamiers Road, Chennai-600
018 for carrying on the business of a non-banking financial institution.
Under powers conferred by Section 45IA of the Reserve Bank of India Act, 1934, the
Reserve Bank can cancel the registration certificate of non-banking financial company. The
business of a non-banking financial institutions is defined in clause (a) of Section 45IA of
the Reserve Bank of India Act, 1934.
