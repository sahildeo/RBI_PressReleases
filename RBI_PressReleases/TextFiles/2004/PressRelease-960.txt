May 5, 2004
Monitoring of Overall Ceiling - Investment by FIIs
       The Reserve Bank of India has notified on May 5, 2004 that the holding of Foreign
Institutional Investors (FIIs) under the portfolio investment scheme (PIS) in the paid up capital
of Bank of Baroda have gone below 18 per cent. It will, therefore, be in order for FIIs to
purchase the shares of Bank of Baroda in the secondary market under the portfolio
investment scheme. The investments will be subject to the existing ceilings and guidelines in
this regard.
