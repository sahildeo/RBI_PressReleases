Availability of Liquidity for Settlement Banks

touch with major settlement banks and both the major stock exchanges to ensure
that the payment obligations on the exchanges are met smoothly. Banks can get in
touch with the Reserve Bank for any liquidity assistance at Tel.No.2261-4228 (Smt.
