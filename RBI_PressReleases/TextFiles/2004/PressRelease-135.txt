November 30, 2004
Investment by FIls -
Monitoring of Overall Ceiling
The Reserve Bank of India on November 30, 2004 has notified that no
further purchases of shares of The Associated Cement Companies Ltd.,
should be made on behalf of Foreign Institutional Investors (FIIs). The Reserve
Bank has stated that The Associated Cement Companies Ltd., has reached
the overall limit of 24 per cent of its paid up equity capital.
