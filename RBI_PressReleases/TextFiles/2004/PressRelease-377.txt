September 23, 2004
New Executive Director appointed in RBI
Shri V.K. Sharma has today taken over as Executive Director of the
Reserve Bank of India. He will look after the Internal Debt Management
Department and Premises Department. Prior to this, Shri Sharma was
Regional Director for West Bengal, Sikkim, Andaman & Nicobar Islands.
Alpana Killawala
