December 15, 2004
Investment by FIls/NRIs/PIOs -
Monitoring of Overall Ceiling
The Reserve Bank of India on December 15, 2004 has notified that no
further purchases of shares of the State Bank of India should be made on behalf
of Foreign Institutional Investors (FIIs)/Non-Resident Indians (NRIs)/Persons of
Indian Origin (PIOs) through stock exchanges in India. The Reserve Bank has
stated that State Bank of India has reached the overall limit of 20 per cent of its
paid up equity capital.
