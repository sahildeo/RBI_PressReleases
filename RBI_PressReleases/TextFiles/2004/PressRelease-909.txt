May 17, 2004
RBI announces rate of interest on FRBs 2014
The rate of interest on the Floating Rate Bonds, 2014 (FRB, 2014) applicable
for the half-year (May 20, 2004 to May 19, 2005) shall be 4.59 per cent per annum. It
may be recalled that the rate of interest on the FRB, 2014 was set at a mark-up (as
decided in the auction held on May 19, 2003) over and above the variable base rate.
The variable base rate for payment of interest shall be average rate (rounded off up
to two decimal places) of the implicit yields at cut-off prices of the last three auctions
of Government of India 364-day Treasury Bills held up May 19. The variable base
rate based on the average rate of the implicit yields at cut-off prices of the said last
three auctions of Government of India 364-day Treasury Bills worked out to be 4.45
per cent. The mark-up decided in the auction held on May 19, 2003 was (+) 0.14
(Plus 0.14). The coupon rate has been fixed accordingly.
