Banking Statistics : Basic Statistical Returns 1 & 2
Volume 1 to 31, 1972 to 2002
The ‘Banking Statistics : Basic Statistical Returns 1 & 2 Volume 1 to 31, 1972 to 2002’
is available for sale. The Department of Statistical Analysis and Computer Services of the
Reserve Bank of India  has brought out this special publication in CD-ROM. The Reserve
Bank has been collecting detailed information on deposits and credit of scheduled commercial
banks through Basic Statistical Returns 1 & 2 and publishing in the form of BSR Volumes
since 1972. In order to make available three decades data at one place, this CD-ROM has
been brought out, which is based on the BSR Volumes and presents the data in PDF format.
These data are tabulated according to population group, bank group, state and district. The
data on credit as per sanction and utilisation are classified according to occupation, size of
credit limit, interest rate range, type of account and organisation. The data on deposits are
classified according to type of deposits, broad ownership category, maturity pattern and
interest rate range. The CD-ROM also gives category-wise information on employees of
scheduled commercial banks.
The publication is covered in 2 CDs (1 set). The first CD covers the Volumes 1 to 15
(1972 to 1986) and the second from 16 to 31 (1987 to 2002). The  publication (1 set) is
available at inland price of  Rs. 420/-  (normal) and  Rs 475/- (inclusive of postage) and for
overseas buyers at US $ 59 (inclusive of courier charges) and US $ 27 (inclusive of registered
airmail charges).
Copies of the CD are available in the Sales Section (DRRP), Amar Building (Ground
