September 4, 2004
RBI relaxes loan impairment norm for UCBs
The Reserve Bank of India had advised Urban Co-operative Banks to apply 90
day impairment norm to gold loans and small loans upto Rs.One lakh with effect from
financial year ending March 31, 2005 for the purpose of assets classification, income
recognition and provisioning.
Taking into consideration representations received from Urban Cooperative
Banks (UCBs), it has been decided that these loans will be governed by the 90 day
impairment norm from the financial year ending March 31, 2007. Till then, these loans
will be governed by 180 day norm as hitherto.
