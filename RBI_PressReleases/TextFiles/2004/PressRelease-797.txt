Investment by FIIs/NRIs/PIOs under PIS
       The Reserve Bank of India has notified on June 11, 2004 that the holding of
Foreign Institutional Investors (FIIs)/Non-Resident Indians (NRIs)/Persons of
Indian Origin (PIOs) under the portfolio investment scheme (PIS) in the paid up
capital of the following companies have gone below as shown against them. It
will, therefore, be in order for FIIs/NRIs/PIOs to purchase the shares of these
companies in the secondary market under the portfolio investment scheme. The
investments will be subject to the existing ceilings and guidelines in this regard.
1. Associated Cement Corporation Ltd. (22 % for FIIs)
2. Kopran Ltd. (22 % for FIIs)
3. Television Eighteen India Ltd. (24 % for FIIs/NRIs/PIOs)
