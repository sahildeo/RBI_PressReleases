June 28, 2004
Investment by Foreign Institutional Investors -
Monitoring of Overall Ceiling
The Reserve Bank of India on June 28, 2004 has notified that no
further purchases of shares of The Associated Cement Companies Ltd., (ACC
Ltd.) should be made on behalf of Foreign Institutional Investors (FIIs) without
the prior approval of the Reserve Bank of India. The Reserve Bank has stated
that The Associated Cement Companies Ltd., has reached the trigger limit of
22 per cent of its paid up capital.
