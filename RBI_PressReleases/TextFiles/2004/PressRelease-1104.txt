March 29, 2004 Calendar for Regular Auction of Government of India Treasury Bills The Reserve Bank of India has announced the calendar of Treasury Bills issuance for the period April 1, 2004 to March 31, 2005. Type of of T-Bills 91-Day 364-Day Periodicity Notified Amount (Rs. Crore) 500 1000 Day of Auction Every Wednesday Wednesday Preceding the Reporting Friday Day of Payment Following Friday Reporting Friday

Weekly Fortnightly

The issuance of Treasury Bills covered by this Calendar will be in addition to the issuances of Treasury Bills as given in the indicative Schedule for issuance of Treasury Bills/Bonds under Market Stabilisation Scheme (MSS) released on March 25, 2004 by the Reserve Bank. The Reserve Bank of India retains the right to vary the amount and timing, if emerging circumstances warrant, without assigning any reason. The calendar is thus subject to change, if circumstances so warrant, including for reasons such as intervening holidays. Such changes, if any, will be communicated through regular press releases. The auction of treasury bills will be subject to the terms and conditions specified in the General Notification No. F 2(12)- W&M/97 dated March 31, 1998 issued by the Government of India, amended from time to time.
