RBI releases Payment Vision Document for Public Comment
The Reserve Bank of India today released a vision document for Payment
Systems in India on its website. The Reserve Bank has invited public comment
on the document. Feedback/comments may be mailed to the Reserve Bank at
psvision@rbi.org.in or faxed to the Chief general manager-in-charge, Department
of Information Technology, Reserve Bank of India at 022 2269 1557.
