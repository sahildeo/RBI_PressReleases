January 10, 2004 Investment by Foreign Institutional Investors Monitoring of Overall Ceiling The Reserve Bank of India on January 10, 2004 has notified that no further purchases of shares of M/s. Alok Industries Ltd., should be made on behalf of Foreign Institutional Investors (FIIs) without the prior approval of the Reserve Bank of India. The Reserve Bank has stated that M/s. Alok Industries Ltd., has reached the trigger limit of 22 per cent of its paid up capital. Ajit Prasad Manager Press Release : 2003-2004/850
