May 8, 2004
RBI Cancels registration certificate of Vulcan Leasing and
Investments Limited, Bangalore as `surrendered'
The Reserve Bank of India has cancelled as surrendered on April 20,
2004 the registration certificate of Vulcan Leasing and Investments Limited,
No.29/30, II floor, SNS Plaza, 41, Kumarakrupa Road, Bangalore-660 001 to
carry on the business of a non-banking financial institution as the company
has opted to exit from the business of non-banking financial institution. The
company has also been prohibited from acceptance of deposit from public
under Section 45 MB (1).
The Reserve Bank has cancelled the registration certificate of the
above company under Section 45-1A (6) of the Reserve Bank of India Act,
1934
