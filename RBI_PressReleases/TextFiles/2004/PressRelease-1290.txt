Reserve Bank Rejects application for certificate of registration of Sun Aero Limited, New
Delhi
The Reserve Bank of India, in exercise of the powers conferred on it under  Section 45 IA
of the Reserve Bank of India Act, 1934, has rejected on February 5, 2004 the application for
certificate of registration submitted by Sun Aero Limited, A-7, First Floor, Geetanjali Enclave, New
Delhi-110 017 for carrying on the business of a Non-Banking Financial Institution.
As such, the above company cannot transact the business of a non-banking financial
institution as defined in Clause (a) of Section 45 IA of the Reserve Bank of  India Act, 1934.
