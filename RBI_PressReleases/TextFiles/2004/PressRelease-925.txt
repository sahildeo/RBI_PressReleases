May 13, 2004
Monitoring of investments by FIIs/NRIs/POIs under PIS
The Reserve Bank of India has notified on May 13, 2004 that Foreign
Institutional Investors (FIIs) can now purchase equity shares and convertible
debentures of Crest Communications Ltd. upto 50 per cent and Non-Resident
Indians (NRIs)/ Persons of Indian Origin (POIs) upto 24 per cent of its paid up
capital through stock exchanges in India, as the company has passed resolutions
to this effect at its Board of Directors’ and General Body Meetings.
