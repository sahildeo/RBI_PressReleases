February 5, 2004 
 
RBI cancels  licence of  The Vijaya Cooperative Urban 
Bank Ltd., Hyderabad, Andhra Pradesh 
 
 The Reserve Bank of India has cancelled the licence of The Vijaya Cooperative Urban 
Bank Ltd., (For Women), Hyderabad, Andhra Pradesh to carry on banking business. The bank is 
precluded from transacting the business of `banking' as defined in the Banking Regulation Act, 
1949 (As applicable to Co-operative Societies) and it will not be in order for it, among other 
things, to accept deposits from public and allow withdrawals by cheque, draft, order or otherwise. 
 
 The Reserve Bank has cancelled the licence under Section 22 of the Banking regulation 
Act, 1949 (as applicable to Co-operative Societies).
