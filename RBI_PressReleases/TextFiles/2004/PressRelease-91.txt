December 9, 2004
Tap sale of 7.32 per cent State Development Loan, 2014 closed
The 'on-tap' sale of 7.32 per cent State Development Loan, 2014 in
respect of the remaining four State Governments viz., Bihar, Jammu & Kashmir,
Orissa and Uttar Pradesh has been closed today.
It may be recalled that the sale opened on December 8, 2004 on behalf of
23 States for a target amount of about Rs. 4,000 crore. Against this, applications
for an aggregate amount of Rs. 5,056 crore were received of which, subscription
for an aggregate amount of Rs. 4,261 crore have been retained.
As notified earlier, payment will be required to be made by the allottees on
December 10, 2004, in respect of subscriptions received both on December 8
and December 9, 2004.
