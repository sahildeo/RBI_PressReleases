March 3, 2004 Television Eighteen India Ltd. : Investment by FIIs/NRIs/PIOs under PIS The Reserve Bank of India on March 3, 2004 has notified that no further purchases of equity shares of Television Eighteen India Ltd., should be made on behalf of Foreign Institutional Investors (FIIs)/Non-Resident Indians (NRIs)/Person Indian Origins (PIOs) through Stock Exchange/s in India without the prior permission of the Reserve Bank. The Reserve Bank has stated that Television Eighteen-India Ltd. has reached the trigger limit of 24 per cent of its paid up capital. Ajit Prasad Manager
