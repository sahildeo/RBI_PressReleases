February 19, 2004
Tap sale of 5.85 per cent State Development Loans 2015
 (Second Series) closed for eight states
The sale `on tap' of 5.85 per cent eleven years State Development Loans, 2015 (Second
Series) has closed in respect of eihght States, i.e., Bihar, Karnataka, Kerala, Maharashtra,
Meghalaya, Punjab, Rajasthan and Tamil Nadu on receipt of the respective target amounts.
Amounts of allotment in respect of these states are being advised to the investors concerned.
The sale on tap in respect of loans of remaining nine States, viz., Arunachal Pradesh,
Madhya Pradesh, Manipur, Mizoram, Nagaland, Orissa, Tripura, Uttar Pradesh and West Bengal
will be kept open on February 20, 2004 to collect the balance target amount. Applications in the
prescribed form can be obtained from the Public Debt Office of the respective offices of the
Reserve Bank of India and Mumbai Office.
Applications will be received at these offices during banking hours while the tap sale is
open. The Reserve Bank of India reserves the right to close the tap sale during the banking hours
on February 20, 2004  having regard to the borrowing requirements of the State Governments
and the subscriptions received.
As notified earlier, payment will be required to be made by the investors on February 21,
2004 in respect of subscriptions received/allotted both on February 19 and February 20, 2004.
