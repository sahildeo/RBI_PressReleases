October 27, 2004
On Tap Sale of State Development Loans
for Rs. 6200 crore, on November 2, 2004
The Governments of 25 States have offered to sell 7.36% State
Development Loan, 2014 having maturity of ten years, aggregating about
Rs. 6200 crore, on November 2, 2004 by way of tap sale under the normal
market borrowing programme. The States which are not participating in the
tap sale are Chhattisgarh, Kerala and Punjab.
The sale will be held at the respective regional offices of the Reserve
Bank of India and at Mumbai (for all the 25 States).  The Reserve Bank of
India reserves the right to close the sale during the banking hours on or after
November 2, 2004 having regard to the requirements of the State
Governments and the subscriptions received. The date of payment will be
November 4, 2004.
Applications in the prescribed form obtainable from the Public Debt
Offices at the Reserve Bank of India will be received at the Reserve Bank
offices during banking hours while the sale is open.
