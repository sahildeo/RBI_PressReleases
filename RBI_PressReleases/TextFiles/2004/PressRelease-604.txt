July 28, 2004
Tap sale of 6.35 per cent state Development Loans 2013
(Second Series) closed for six states
The sale 'on tap' of 6.35 per cent nine years State Development Loans,
2013(Second Series) has closed in respect of six States, i.e. Andhra Pradesh,
Haryana, Himachal Pradesh, Karnataka, Kerala, and Tamil Nadu on receipt of
the respective target amounts.  Amounts of allotment in respect of these
states are being advised to the investors concerned.
The sale on tap in respect of loans for remaining thirteen States, viz.,
Assam, Bihar, Gujarat, Jammu & Kashmir, Jharkhand, Madhya Pradesh,
Maharashtra, Nagalanad, Orissa, Punjab, Rajasthan, Uttar Pradesh and West
Bengal will be kept open on July 29, 2004 to collect the balance target
amount.  Applications in the prescribed form can be obtained from the Public
Debt Office of the respective offices of the Reserve Bank of India and Mumbai
Office.
Applications will be received at these offices during banking hours
while the tap sale is open.  The Reserve Bank of India reserves the right to
close the tap sale during the banking hours on July 29, 2004 having regard to
the borrowing requirements of the State Governments and the subscriptions
received.
As notified earlier, payment will be required to be made by the
investors on July 30, 2004 in respect of subscriptions received / allotted both
on July 28 and July 29, 2004.
