Tap sale of State Development Loans for all States Closed
The sale on tap of 5.60 per cent 10 year State Development Loans, 2014 in respect of 28 State
Governments has been closed.
Twenty states received subscriptions in excess of their target amounts and excess subscriptions upto 20
percent of the target amounts has been retained by these states. Remaining eight states also received their
target amounts.
As notified earlier, payments will be required to be made by the investors on April 23, 2004 in respect of
subscriptions received/ allotted.
It may be recalled that on April 13, 2004 it was notified that 28 State Governments will float 5.60 per cent
10 year State Development Loan, 2014 on April 21, 2004 for Rs. 6500 crore with an option to retain
additional amount upto 20 per cent of their target amounts.
