June 2, 2004
RBI announces rate of interest on FRBs 2009
The rate of interest on the Floating Rate Bonds, 2009 (FRB, 2009)
applicable for the half-year June 6, 2004 to December 5, 2004 shall be 4.41
per cent per annum. It may be recalled that the rate of interest on the FRB,
2009 was set at a mark-up (as decided in the auction held on December 5,
2001) over and above the variable base rate. The variable base rate for
payment of interest shall be average rate (rounded off up to two decimal
places) of the implicit yields at cut-off prices of the last six auctions of
Government of India 364-day Treasury Bills held up to the commencement of
the respective half-yearly coupon period. The variable base rate based on the
average rate of the implicit yields at cut-off prices of the last six auctions of
Government of India 364-day Treasury Bills worked out to be 4.42 per cent.
The mark-up decided in the auction held on December 5, 2001 was (-) 0.01
(minus 0.01). The coupon rate has been fixed accordingly.
