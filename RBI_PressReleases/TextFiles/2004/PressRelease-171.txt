November 19,  2004
Monitoring FIIs investment in Indian Companies
       The Reserve Bank of India has notified on November 19, 2004 that
Foreign Institutional Investors (FIIs) can now purchase under Portfolio
Investment Scheme (PIS) equity shares of Divis Laboratory Ltd.  through the
secondary market in  India.  The Reserve Bank of India has stated that the
holdings of FIIs in the company have gone down below 22 per cent limit of its
paid up capital.
