Monitoring FIIs investment in Indian Companies
The Reserve Bank of India has notified on June 23, 2004 that Foreign
Institutional Investors (FIIs) can now purchase under Portfolio Investment
Scheme (PIS) equity shares and convertible debentures of SSI Ltd. through
primary markets and stock exchanges in India upto 74 per cent limits of its
paid up capital, as the company has passed resolutions to this effect at its
Board of Directors' and General Body Meetings.
