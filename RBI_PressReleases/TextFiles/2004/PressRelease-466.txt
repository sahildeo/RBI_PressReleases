Pritish Nandi Communications Ltd.:
Investment by FIIs under PIS
       The Reserve Bank of India on September 2, 2004 has notified that no
further purchases of equity shares of Pritish Nandi Communications Ltd.
should be made on behalf of Foreign Institutional Investors (FIIs) through
primary and secondary markets in India without prior permission of the
Reserve Bank. The Reserve Bank has stated that Pritish Nandi
Communications Ltd. has reached the trigger limit of 22 per cent of its paid up
capital.
