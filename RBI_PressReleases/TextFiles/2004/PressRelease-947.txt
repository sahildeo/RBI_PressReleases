Monitoring of overall ceiling - Investment by FIIs
The Reserve Bank of India has notified on May 7, 2004 that Foreign
Institutional Investors (FIIs) can now purchase under Portfolio Investment
Scheme (PIS) equity shares and convertible debentures of Mahindra &
Mahindra Ltd. through primary markets and stock exchanges in India upto 35
per cent of its paid up capital, as Mahindra & Mahindra have passed
resolutions to this effect at its Board of Directors' and  General Body
Meetings.
