August 6, 2004
Monitoring FIIs investment in Indian Companies
The Reserve Bank of India has notified on August 6, 2004 that Foreign
Institutional Investors (FIIs) can now purchase under Portfolio Investment
Scheme (PIS) equity shares and convertible debentures of Godrej Consumer
Products Ltd. through primary markets and stock exchanges in India upto 35
per cent limits of its paid up capital, as the company has passed resolutions to
this effect at its Board of Directors' and General Body Meetings.
