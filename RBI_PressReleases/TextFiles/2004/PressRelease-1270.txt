February 17, 2004
Investment by FIIs/ NRIs/PIOs
       The Reserve Bank of India has notified on February 17, 2004 that Foreign Institutional
Investors (FIIs)/Non-Resident Indians (NRIs)/Persons of Indian Origins (PIOs) can now purchase
equity shares of Television Eighteen India Ltd., through stock exchanges in India as the
aggregate net purchase of equity shares of Television Eighteen Indian Ltd. has gone down below
24 per cent of its paid up equity capital.
