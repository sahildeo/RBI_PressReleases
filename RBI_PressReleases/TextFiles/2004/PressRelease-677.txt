July 10, 2004
Television Eighteen India Ltd. :
Investment by FIIs/NRIs/PIOs under PIS
       The Reserve Bank of India on July 10, 2004 has notified that no further
purchases of equity shares of Television Eighteen India Ltd., should be
made on behalf of Foreign Institutional Investors (FIIs)/Non-Resident Indians
(NRIs)/Persons of Indian Origin (PIOs) through Stock Exchange/s in India
without the prior permission of the Reserve Bank. The Reserve Bank has
stated that Television Eighteen India Ltd., has reached the trigger limit of 24
per cent of its paid up capital.
