April 1, 2004 Investment by FIIs/NRIs/PIOs under PIS The Reserve Bank of India on April 1, 2004 has notified that no further purchases of equity shares of Bank of Baroda, should be made on behalf of Foreign Institutional Investors (FIIs)/Non-Resident Indians (NRIs)/Persons of Indian Origin (PIOs) through stock exchange/s in India without the prior permission of the Reserve Bank. The Reserve Bank has stated that Bank of Baroda has reached the trigger limit of 18 per cent of its paid up capital.
