The Reserve Bank of India on April 10, 2004 has notified that no further purchases of shares of M/s. Amtek Auto Ltd., should be made on behalf of Foreign Institutional Investors (FIIs) without the prior approval of the Reserve Bank of India. The Reserve Bank has stated that M/s. Amtek Auto Ltd., has reached the trigger limit of 22 per cent of its paid up capital. P. V. Sadanandan Manager
