Monitoring FIIs investment in Indian Companies
The Reserve Bank of India has notified on February 17, 2004 that Foreign Institutional
Investors (FIIs) can now purchase equity shares and convertible debentures of Welspun India
Ltd. through primary markets and stock exchanges in India up to 40 per cent of its paid up capital,
as the company has passed resolutions to this effect at their Board of Directors' and  General
Body Meetings.
