November 2, 2004
Shasun Chemical & Drugs Ltd. - Investment by FIIs under PIS
       The Reserve Bank of India on November 2, 2004 has notified that no
further purchases of equity shares of Shasun Chemical & Drugs Ltd., should
be made on behalf of Foreign Institutional Investors (FIIs) through
primary/secondary markets in India without the prior permission of the
Reserve Bank. The Reserve Bank has stated that Shasun Chemical & Drugs
Ltd., has reached the trigger limit of 22 per cent of its paid up capital.
