                                                                                                            July 29, 2004
Tap sale of 6.35 per cent State Development Loans 2013
(Second Series) closed
The sale  'on tap' of 6.35 per cent nine years State Development Loans
2013 ( II series) in respect of remaining thirteen ,viz., Assam, Bihar, Gujarat,
Jammu & Kashmir, Jharkhand, Madhya Pradesh, Maharashtra, Nagaland,
Orissa, Punjab, Rajasthan, Uttar Pradesh and West Bengal has been closed
today. As notified earlier, payment will be required to be made by the investors
on July 30, 2004 in respect of subscriptions received both on July 28 and July 29,
2004.It may be recalled that the tap sale opened on July 28, 2004, for 19 State
Governments for a target amount of about Rs.8500 crore and the sale was
closed for six States on July 28, 2004.
Of the total subscriptions of Rs.6663 crore received, Rs.6288 crore has
been retained.
