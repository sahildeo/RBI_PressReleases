April 17, 2004 Change in LAF Auction Schedule The Reserve Bank of India today announced the following changes in the repo auction calendar : 1. Calendar for 7 day Repo Auctions Date of Auction April 19, 2004 Date of Reversal (Notified) April 26, 2004 Revised Date of Repo Period (days) Reversal April 23, 2004 4

2. Calendar for Overnight Reverse Repo Auctions Date of Auction April 23, 2004 Date of Reversal (Notified) April 26, 2004 Revised Date of Repo Period (days) Reversal April 27, 2004 4

3. No auction will be conducted under the Liquidity Adjustment Facility on April 26, 2004. The changes have been announced in view of the Government of Maharashtra declaring April 26, 2004 as a holiday under the Negotiable Instruments Act, 1881 due to elections. The Reserve Bank had, on March 25, 2004, issued a press release (2003-2004/1129) announcing the Calendar for auctions to be conducted under the Liquidity Adjustment Facility for the quarter April-June 2004.
