Tap Sale of 5.90 per cent State Development Loans 2017 Closed for 13 states The sale `on tap' of 5.90 per cent State Loans, 2017 was closed in respect of 13 States, i.e., Andhra Pradesh, Chhattisgarh, Goa, Haryana, Himachal Pradesh, Jammu and Kashmir, Jharkhand, Karnataka, Kerala, Madhya Pradesh, Punjab, Rajasthan and Tamilnadu on receipt of the respective target amounts. Amounts of allotment received in respect of these states are being advised to the investors concerned. The sale on tap in respect of remaining 14 States, viz., Arunachal Pradesh, Assam, Bihar, Gujarat, Maharashtra, Manipur, Meghalaya, Mizoram, Nagaland, Orissa, Sikkim, Tripura, Uttar Pradesh and West Bengal will be open on January 20, 2004 to collect the balance target amount. Applications in the prescribed form can be obtained from the Public Debt Office of the respective offices of the Reserve Bank of India and Mumbai Office. Applications will be received at these offices during banking hours while the tap sale is open. The Reserve Bank of India reserves the right to close the tap sale during the banking hours on January 20, 2004 having regard to the borrowing requirements of the State Governments and the subscriptions received. As notified earlier, payment will be required to be made by the investors on January 21, 2004 in respect of subscriptions received both on January 19 and January 20, 2004.
