June 16, 2004
Underwriting by Primary Dealers
The Reserve Bank of India has set the cut-off rate for underwriting bids
by Primary Dealers for the auction of 7.38 per cent Government Stock, 2015
to be held on June 17, 2004 at Re.0.03 (three paise) per Rs.100 and has
rejected all underwriting bids for the auction of 6.13 per cent Government
Stock, 2028.
