February 20, 2004
Tap sale of 5.85 per cent State Development Loans 2015  (Second Series) closed
The sale `on tap' of 5.85 per cent State Development Loan 2015 (Second Series) in
respect of the remaining nine State Governments viz. Arunachal Pradesh, Madhya Pradesh,
Manipur, Mizoram, Nagaland, Orissa, Tripura, Uttar Pradesh and West Bengal has been closed
today. It may be recalled that the sale opened on  February 19, 2004 for 17 State Governments
for a target amount of Rs.4220 crore and  the  sale  was  closed  for eight States on February 19,
2004. Of the total subscriptions of Rs.4982 crore received on both the days, Rs.4036 crore has
been retained.
As notified earlier, payment will be required to be made by the investors on February 21,
2004 in respect of subscriptions received both on February 19 and February 20, 2004.
