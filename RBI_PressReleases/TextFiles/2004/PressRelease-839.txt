June 1, 2004
Monitoring of Foreign Investment Limits under PIS
The Reserve Bank of India has notified on June 1, 2004 that the
percentage of foreign holdings under the portfolio investment scheme (PIS) in
the ICICI bank Ltd. has gone below 47 percent of its paid up capital.
The Reserve Bank has further notified that it will be in order for Foreign
Institutional Investors (FIIs) to purchase the shares of ICICI Bank Ltd. in the
secondary market under the PIS subject to the existing ceilings and guidelines
in this regard.
