Investment by FIIs/NRIs/PIOs under PIS
The Reserve Bank of India on April 10, 2004 has notified that no further purchases of equity shares of Centurion Bank Ltd., should be made on behalf of Foreign Institutional Investors (FIIs)/Non-Resident Indians (NRIs) and Persons of Indian Origin (PIOs) through stock exchange/s in India without the prior permission of the Reserve Bank. The Reserve Bank has stated that Centurion Bank Ltd., has reached the trigger limit of 72 per cent of its paid up capital. P. V. Sadanandan Manager
