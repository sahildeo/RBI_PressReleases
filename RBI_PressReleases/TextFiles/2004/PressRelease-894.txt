May 19, 2004
Investment by NRIs/PIOs in
shares of Indian companies
The Reserve Bank of India on May 19, 2004 has notified that no further purchases
equity shares of the following companies should be made through stock exchanges on behalf
of non-resident Indians (NRIs) and persons of Indian origin (PIOs) without obtaining prior
clearance of the Reserve Bank.  The Reserve Bank has stated that the net purchases of
equity shares of the companies have reached the limit of 8 per cent of their paid up capital.
i) DCM Ltd.
ii) DCM Shriram Consolidated Ltd.
