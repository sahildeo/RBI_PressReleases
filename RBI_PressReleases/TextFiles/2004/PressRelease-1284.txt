February 13, 2004
Tap Sale of State Government Loans
Chhattisgarh will not be participating in the tap sale for the State Development Loans to
be floated on February 19, 2004.  Thus, the target amount will be reduced to about Rs.4200
crore. The other terms and conditions will remain same.
It was announced earlier that 18 State Governments will be raising market loans for
about Rs.4300 crore with coupon rate of 5.85 per cent annum for a tenor of 11 years on February
19, 2004 with payment being made on February 21, 2004.
