February 6, 2004 
Borrowings by the Government of India 
and State Governments 
 
 The Government of India has decided to postpone the auction scheduled to be held 
during the period February 2-9, 2004.  The auction is now  proposed to be held between February 
23-27, 2004. It may be recalled that in the Issuance Calendar for Marketable Dated Securities 
released on September 29, 2003, it was indicated that the calendar is subject to variations 
depending on market conditions and other relevant factors.    
Eighteen States will also raise around Rs. 4,300 crore in the third week of February 2004 
by way of tap sale towards the additional market borrowing as also for pre-payment of high cost 
debt to the Government of India under the Debt Swap Scheme.  
The exact dates of auction/tap sale and other details will be notified later.
