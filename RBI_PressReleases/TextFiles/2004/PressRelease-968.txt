April 30, 2004
Investment by FIIs under PIS
       The Reserve Bank of India on April 30, 2004 has notified that no further purchases of equity
shares of M/s. Kopran Ltd., should be made on behalf of Foreign Institutional Investors ( FIIs)
through primary/secondary markets in India without the prior permission of the Reserve Bank.
The Reserve Bank has stated that M/s. Kopran Ltd. has reached the trigger limit of 22 per cent of
its paid up capital.
