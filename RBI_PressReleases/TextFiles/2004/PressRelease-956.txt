May 5, 2004
Underwriting by Primary Dealers
The Reserve Bank of India has set the cut-off rate for underwriting bids by
Primary Dealers for the auctions of 6.01 per cent Government Stock, 2028 and
Government of India FRB, 2016 to be held on May 6, 2004 at Re.01 (one paisa) and
Re.0.0075 (0.75 paisa) per Rs.100 respectively.
