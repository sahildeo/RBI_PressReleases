November 29, 2004
Issue of Rs.500 denomination bank notes with
inset letter `C' in both numbering panels in Mahatma Gandhi Series
bearing the signature of Dr. Y.V.Reddy, Governor
The Reserve Bank of India will shortly issue Rs.500 denomination
banknotes with inset letter `C' in both numbering panels in Mahatma Gandhi
Series bearing the signature of Dr. Y.V.Reddy, Governor. Except for the change
in the inset letter, the design of these notes to be issued now is similar in all
respect to those issued earlier in the Mahatma Gandhi Series. All notes in the
denomination of Rs.500 issued by the Bank since September 1987 will continue
to be legal tender.
