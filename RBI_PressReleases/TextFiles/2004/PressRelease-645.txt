Simplification of RBI Note Refund Rules
The Reserve Bank of India (Note Refund) Rules, 1975, as amended
from time to time, dealing with the adjudication of mutilated notes have been
posted on our website www.rbi.org.in. With a view to simplify the same, the
members of public are requested to make suggestions. The suggestions may
be sent either to helpdcm@rbi.org.in or may be sent by post to the Chief
General Manager, Department of Currency Management, Reserve Bank of
