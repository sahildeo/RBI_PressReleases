January 14, 2004 
  
Investment by Foreign Institutional Investors - 
Monitoring of Overall Ceiling 
 
The Reserve Bank of India on January 14, 2004 has notified that no 
further purchases of equity shares of M/s. Welspun Gujrat Sthal Rohern Ltd., 
should be made on behalf of Foreign Institutional Investors (FIIs) without the prior 
approval of the Reserve Bank of India. The Reserve Bank has stated that M/s. 
Welspun Gujrat Shal Rohern Ltd., has reached the trigger limit of 22 per cent of 
its paid up capital.
