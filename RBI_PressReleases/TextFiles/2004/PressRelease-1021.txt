Investment by FIIs/NRIs/PIOs ­ Monitoring of Overall ceiling under PIS

The Reserve Bank of India has advised that whenever the holdings of shares of M/s. Bharati Televentures Ltd., by Foreign Institutional Investors (FIIs)/Non-Resident Indians (NRIs) and Persons of Indian Origin (PIOs) reach 48 per cent of its paid up capital, the name of the company would be placed under the caution list under Portfolio Investment Scheme (PIS). The Reserve Bank further advised that it would ban the purchase of shares of the above company by FIIs/NRIs/PIOs under PIS, whenever such holdings reach 48.4 per cent of its paid up capital. This has been done to facilitate the monitoring of the aggregate foreign shareholding permitted by the Government in view of the recent Foreign Currency Convertible Bonds (FCCB) issue of M/s. Bharati Televentures Ltd. P. V. Sadanandan Manager
