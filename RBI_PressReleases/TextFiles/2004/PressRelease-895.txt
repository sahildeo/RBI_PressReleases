May 19, 2004
Investment by NRIs/PIOs under PIS
The Reserve of India has notified on May 19, 2004 that the holdings of Non-Resident
Indians (NRIs) and Persons of India Origin (PIOs) under the portfolio investment scheme
(PIS) in the paid up capital of the following companies, have gone below 8 per cent. The
Reserve Bank has further notified that it will be in order for NRIs and PIOs to purchase the
shares of the following companies in the secondary market under the portfolio investment
scheme subject to the existing ceilings and guidelines in this regard.
i) Astra IDL Ltd.
ii) IDL Industries Ltd.
iii) Seirra Optima Ltd.
iv) The Baroda Rayon Corporation
