May 23, 2000
The Reserve Bank of India on May 23, 2000 has notified that no further purchases
without the prior approval of the Reserve Bank should be made of equity shares of
Padmini Polymers Ltd. And Nalco Chemicals India Ltd. on behalf of non-resident
Indians (NRIs)/overseas corporate bodies (OCBs)/persons of Indian origin (PIOs). The
Reserve Bank has stated that the aggregate net purchases of equity shares of these
companies by NRIs/OCBs/PIOs in the secondary market has reached the caution limit of
