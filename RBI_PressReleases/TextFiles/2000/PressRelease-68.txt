91 Day Treasury Bills - Auction Announced
The Reserve Bank of India has announced the auction of 91 day Government of India treasury
bills for a notified amount of Rs.100 crore.  The auction will be conducted using "Uniform
Price Auction" method .  The allocation to non-competitive bidders will be outside the notified
amount, at the discretion of the Bank. The sale will be subject to terms and conditions specified
in the General Notification No.F.2(12)-W&M/97 dated 31 March 1998 issued by Government of
Tenders should be submitted in the prescribed form on Friday, December 22, 2000  before 1430
hours.  The results will be announced the same evening.  Payment by successful bidders will be
on Tuesday, December 26, 2000.
