RBI refuses licence to The 
Sahakari Bank Ltd.
The Reserve Bank of India has rejected the application submitted by The 
Kamgar Nagari Sahakari Bank Ltd., Ichalkaranji, Maharashtra for grant of licence to
carry on banking business. Thus, the bank is precluded from transacting the business of
banking as defined in the Banking Regulation Act, 1949 (as applicable to Co-operative
Societies). Thus, it will not be in order for it among other things to accept deposits from
