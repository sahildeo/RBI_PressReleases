Investment by Foreign Institutional Investors
The Reserve Bank of India has today notified that Foreign Institutional Investors (FIIs) can
now purchase equity shares and convertible debentures of M/s. Satyam Computer Services Ltd., up
to 40 per cent of its paid up capital through primary and secondary markets in India. The company
has passed a resolution at its Board of Directors' and General Body Meeting for purchase of its
equity shares and convertible debentures by FIIs upto 40% of its paid-up capital through
