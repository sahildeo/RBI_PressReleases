RBI cancels licence granted to The 
Co-operative Bank Ltd.
The Reserve Bank of India has cancelled the licence granted to The Latur Peoples Co-operative
Bank Ltd., Latur (Maharashtra) to carry on banking business in India.  The bank is precluded from
transacting the business of banking as defined in the Banking Regulation Act, 1949 (As applicable
to Co-operative Societies) and it will not be in order for it, among other things, to accept deposits
cheque, draft, order or otherwise.
