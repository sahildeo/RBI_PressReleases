close of banking hours at the offices of the Reserve Bank of India at 
Bangalore, Bhubaneswar, Calcutta, Chennai, Guwahati, Hyderabad, Jaipur, 
Mumbai(Fort), Nagpur, New Delhi, Patna and Thiruvananthapuram. The subscriptions for

State Bank of India / Associate Banks at the specified district headquarters. In the event of
September 26, 2000 being declared holiday by any State Government under the Negotiable
Instruments Act ,1881, the subscriptions will be received by the receiving offices concerned
