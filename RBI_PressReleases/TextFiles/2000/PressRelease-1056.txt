Repo Auction under LAF
July 8, 2000
The Reserve Bank of India under the Scheme of Liquidity Adjustment Facility, has
announced a one day repo auction and reverse repo auction in Government of India dated
securities and treasury bills of all  except 14-day treasury bills on July 10, 2000
(Monday) for scheduled commercial banks and Primary Dealers holding SGL Account and
The eligible banks/institutions may submit separate applications before 11.00 a.m. on July
10, 2000 indicating the amount in face value and the repo rate in per cent rounded off to the
nearest 5 basis points for repo auction and reverse repo auction in the prescribed form
obtainable at Internal Debt Management Cell, Reserve Bank of India ,  Central Office
Building, 16th Floor, Mumbai. ( www.rbi.org.in .)
The applications should be addressed to the Chief General Manager, Internal Debt
Management Cell, Reserve Bank of India, Central Office, Mumbai and the tenders should be
Payment by the successful bidders in the repo auction and release of funds to successful
bidders in the reverse repo auction will be on July 10, 2000 up to 2.30 p.m. and the
