August 28, 2000
The Reserve Bank of India has announced the auction of 14 day Government of India treasury
bills for a notified amount of Rs.100 crore. The allocation to non-competitive bidders will be
outside the notified amount, at the discretion of the Bank. The sale will be subject to terms and
conditions specified in the General Notification No.F.2(12)-W&M/97 dated 31 March 1998
Tenders should be submitted in the prescribed form on Thursday, August 31, 2000 before 1430
hours. The results will be announced the same evening. Payment by successful bidders will be on
Monday, September 4, 2000.
