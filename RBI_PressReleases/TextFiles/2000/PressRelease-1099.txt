July 3, 2000
The Reserve Bank of  India has been issuing circulars and directives from time to time.
While issuing such circulars/directives, every effort is made to ensure clarity of the
instructions with rationale and logic.  There have been occasions when banks, members
of public, etc., sought clarifications or interpretation of the instructions conveyed.  It was,
therefore, felt that if a booklet was compiled containing answers to off repeated
Accordingly, a bilingual booklet titled `Replies to Questions, frequently asked on
Banking Matters' has been prepared by the Department of Banking Operations and
Development (DBOD) of the Reserve Bank of India.  The booklet contains answers to
frequently asked questions on deposits, advances, hiring of premises by banks, etc.
The booklet has been priced at Rs.12 and is available at the Sale Section of the
Department of Economic Analysis and Policy, Reserve Bank of India at Amar Building,
Fort, Mumbai 400 001.  The booklet is also available on the RBI website under a special
