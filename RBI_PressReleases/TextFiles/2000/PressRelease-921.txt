July 29, 2000
The Reserve Bank of India has advanced the timing of announcement of the results of Liquidity
Adjustment Facility (LAF) by half-an-hour.  From Monday, July 31, 2000, the LAF results will be
announced at 12.30 p.m. instead of 1.00 p.m.  All other conditions for LAF remain unchanged.  LAF
