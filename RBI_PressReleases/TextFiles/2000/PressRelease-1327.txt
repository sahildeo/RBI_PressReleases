May 23, 2000
The sale of 10.79 per cent Government Stock, 2015 which opened for subscription on
May 19, 2000 on tap at the Reserve Bank of India, Mumbai was closed at the close of
banking hours today.  In all, 55 applications for Rs.2 ,683.45 crore (nominal), were
received. All the applications were accepted.
