July 28, 2000
Two securities, viz., 11.00% 2006 and 11.30% 2010 which were placed with Reserve
Bank of India for Rs.3000 crore each on private placement cum tap sale basis were put on
the sale list today.  The stock 11.00% 2006 was oversubscribed.  Applicants upto Rs.25
crore were given full allotment and others were given proportional allotment to the extent
of 74 per cent.  Applicants for 11.30% 2010 received full allotment.  This security
