RBI Reference Rate for US $
April 19, 2000
The Reserve Bank of India’s Reference Rate for the US dollar is Rs.43.65 on 19.04.2000
as against Rs.43.64 on 18.04.2000. The reference rate is based on 12 noon rates of a few select
banks in Mumbai. The SDR-rupee rate will be based on this rate.
