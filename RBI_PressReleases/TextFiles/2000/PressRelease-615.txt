The Reserve Bank of India, in exercise of the powers conferred on it under Section 45 IA
of the Reserve Bank of India Act, 1934, has rejected on September 18, 2000 the
application for certificate of registration submitted by M/s. Jai  Investments &
Leasing Finance Ltd.,  ,  , Basavana 
Road, Bijapur-586 104.
As such, the above company cannot transact the business of Non-Banking Financial
Institution as defined in Clause (a) of Section 45 I of the Reserve Bank of  India  Act,
1934.
