91 Day Treasury Bills - Auction Announced
June 5, 2000
The Reserve Bank of India has announced the auction of 91 day Government of India
treasury bills for a notified amount of Rs.100 crore.  The auction will be conducted
using "Uniform Price Auction" method .  The allocation to non-competitive bidders will
Tenders should be submitted in the prescribed form on Friday, June 9, 2000 before 1430
hours.  The results will be announced the same evening.  Payment by successful bidders
will be on Monday, June 12, 2000.
