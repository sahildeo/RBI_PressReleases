14 Day Treasury Bills - Auction Announced
November 13, 2000
The Reserve Bank of India has announced the auction of 14 day Government of India
Treasury Bills for a notified amount of Rs.100 crore. The allocation to non-competitive
bidders will be outside the notified amount, at the discretion of the Bank. The sale will be
subject to terms and conditions specified in the General Notification No.F.2(12)-
Tenders should be submitted in the prescribed form on Friday, November 17, 2000
before 1430 hours.  The results will be announced the same evening.  Payment by
successful bidders will be on Monday, November 20, 2000.
