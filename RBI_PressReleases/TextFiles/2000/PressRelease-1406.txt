May 9, 2000
The Reserve Bank of India has today notified that Foreign Institutional Investors (FIIs) can now
purchase equity shares and convertible debentures of M/s. Global Tele Systems Ltd., up to 40 per
cent of its paid up capital through primary and secondary markets in India. The company has
passed a resolution at its Board of Directors' and General Body Meetings for purchase of its
equity shares and convertible debentures by FIIs upto 40% of its paid-up capital through
