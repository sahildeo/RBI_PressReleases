Commercial Bank Maryadit, Bilaspur, Madhya   to carry on  banking business in
India.  The bank is precluded from transacting the business of ‘banking’ as defined in the
Banking Regulation Act, 1949 (as applicable to Co-operative Societies). Thus, it will not
be in order for it, among other things, to accept deposits from the public and allow
