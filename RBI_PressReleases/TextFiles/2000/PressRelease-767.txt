The Reserve Bank of India has cancelled the licence of Shree Veraval Vibhagiya Nagrik 
Bank Ltd., Veraval, Dist. Junagadh ( Gujarat) to carry on  banking busines in India.  The bank is
precluded from transacting the business of ‘banking’ as defined in the Banking Regulation Act,
1949 (as applicable to Co-operative Societies). Thus, it will not be in order for it, among other
things, to accept deposits from the public and allow withdrawals by cheque, draft, order or
