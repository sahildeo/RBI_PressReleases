The Reserve Bank of India on August 11, 2000 has notified that non-resident Indians ( )

covertible debentures of M/s Burr Brown (India) Ltd  upto 40 per
cent of its paid up capital through primary / secondary markets in India, under portfolio
investment scheme. The Reserve Bank has stated that the Company has passed a
board/general body resolution to this effect.
