India  5-year Stock Auction announced
July 7, 2000
Government of India have announced an auction of five year Stock for Rs 3,000
The auction will be yield based and will be conducted by the Reserve Bank of
India (RBI) at Mumbai on Tuesday, July 11, 2000. Bids in the prescribed form obtainable
from the Regional Director, Reserve Bank of India, Public Debt Office, Fort, Mumbai
should be submitted on Tuesday , July 11,  2000 during banking hours. On the basis of
bids received, Reserve Bank of India will determine the maximum rate of yield at which
offers will be accepted. The results of the auction will be announced on July 11, 2000 and
payment by successful bidders will be during banking hours on July 12, 2000. The new
Bids for underwriting of the Government Security under auction can be submitted
by the ‘Primary Dealers in the Government Securities Market’ up to 2.30 p.m. on July 10,
