Reserve Bank of India Act, 1934, has rejected on May 31, 2000 the application for certificate of
registration submitted by Royal India House  Ltd., Royal India House, Jai  ,
 843 302.
As such, the above company cannot transact the business of a non-banking financial institution as
of  India Act, 1934.
