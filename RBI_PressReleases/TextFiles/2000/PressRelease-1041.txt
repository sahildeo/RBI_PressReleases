Handout : March 2000
July 11, 2000
The “Banking Statistics: Quarterly Handout – March 2000” providing data on aggregate deposits
and gross bank credit of scheduled commercial banks as on March 31, 2000, with various
classifications, has been published by Reserve Bank of India. The primary data have been
collected from the Scheduled Commercial Banks through the BSR-7 return. Data have been
presented according to top hundred centres, population groups, districts, states and bank groups.
The publication is available on on the RBI website ( ) under Quarterly

total deposits. Similarly, the top hundred  according to size of bank credit
constituted 74.7 per cent of total bank credit.
