Investments by NRIs/ OCBs
in the shares of Indian Companies
June 16, 2000
The Reserve Bank of India on June 16, 2000 has notified that non-resident Indians ( NRIs), overseas
corporate bodies ( OCBs) can now purchase equity shares and convertible debentures of M/s. 
Phosphates Ltd.  up to 24 per cent of its paid up capital through stock exchanges under the portfolio
investment scheme (PIS), as the company has passed a resolution at its General Body Meeting to
this effect.
