For the convenience of income tax (IT) assessees, arrangements have been made for
receipt of advance income tax at 415 specified bank branches, the Reserve Bank of India
has stated in a press release. Seventy-six branches of Bank of Baroda, 72 branches of
Bank of India, 23 branches of Bank of Maharashtra, 71 branches of Central Bank of
India, 21 branches of Dena Bank, 37 branches of Indian Overseas Bank, 70 branches of
State Bank of India, 17 branches of United Bank of India and 28 branches of UCO Bank
The Reserve Bank has advised assessees to take advantage of these standing
in Mumbai and New Mumbai that in order to avoid inconvenience and to avoid last
minute rush at the Reserve Bank's counters, they may deposit their income tax dues
