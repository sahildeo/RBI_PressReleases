14 Day Treasury Bills - Auction Announced
May 15, 2000
The Reserve Bank of India has announced the auction of 14 day Government of India
Treasury Bills for a notified amount of Rs.100 crore. The allocation to non-competitive
Tenders should be submitted in the prescribed form on Friday, May 19, 2000 before 1430
hours.  The results will be announced the same evening.  Payment by successful bidders
will be on Monday, May 22, 2000.
