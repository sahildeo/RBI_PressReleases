Investment by OCBs/
November 29, 2000
The Reserve Bank of India has on November 29, 2000, notified that foreign investments
in ICICI Ltd. under the portfolio investment scheme has gone below 49 per cent. Foreign
Institutional Investors ( FIIs)/Non-resident Indians ( NRIs)/overseas corporate bodies
( PIOs) can, therefore, after obtaining prior clearance
of the Reserve Bank of India purchase equity shares of ICICI Ltd. through stock
exchanges under the portfolio investment scheme (PIS).
