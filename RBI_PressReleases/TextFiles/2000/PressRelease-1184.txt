RBI cancels licence of the Sahakari Bank Ltd.
June 16, 2000
The Reserve Bank of India has cancelled the licence of the Indira Sahakari Bank Ltd., 
Maharashtra to carry on  banking busines in India.  The bank is precluded from transacting the
business of ‘banking’ as defined in the Banking Regulation Act, 1949 (as applicable to Co-operative
Societies). Thus, it will not be in order for it, among other things, to accept deposits from the public
