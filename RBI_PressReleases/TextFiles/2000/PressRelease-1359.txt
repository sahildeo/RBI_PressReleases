May 17, 2000
The Reserve Bank of India, in exercise of the powers conferred on it under Section 45 IA of the
Reserve Bank of India Act, 1934, has rejected on May 17, 2000 the application for certificate of
registration submitted by M/s. Magnum Trust Ltd., 1st Floor,  Market, Near Clock
Tower, , Punjab.
As such, the above company cannot transact the business of a non-banking financial institution
as defined in Clause (a) of Section 45 IA of the Reserve Bank of India Act, 1934.
