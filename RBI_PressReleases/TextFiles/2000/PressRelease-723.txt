Issue of Rs.10 
August 31, 2000
The Reserve Bank of India will shortly be issuing Rs.10 notes in Mahatma Gandhi Series
bearing the signature of Dr. Bimal , Governor with capital letter "S" inset in both
numbering panels.  Except for this change, the design of the Rs.10 notes to be issued now
is similar to those issued earlier in the 'Mahatma Gandhi' Series.  All Rs.10 denomination
