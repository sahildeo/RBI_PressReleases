May 31, 2000
The Reserve Bank of India, in exercise of the powers conferred on it under Section 45 IA
of the Reserve Bank of India Act, 1934, has rejected on May 30, 2000 the application for
certificate of registration submitted by M/s. Steel City Finance Limited,  
, , Jamshedpur-831 006.
As such, the above company cannot transact the business of a non-banking financial
institution as defined in Clause (a) of Section 45 IA of the Reserve Bank of  India  Act,
1934.
