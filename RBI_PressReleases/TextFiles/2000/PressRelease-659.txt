The Reserve Bank of India, in exercise of the powers conferred on it under Section 45 IA of the
Reserve Bank of India Act, 1934, has rejected on September 11, 2000 the application for certificate
of registration submitted by M/s. Festoon Finance and Investment (India) Ltd. having its Registered
Office at  Thakurbari Road, Hojal, District Nagaon, Assam for carrying on the business of a Non-
Banking Financial Institution.  However, the company is required to repay to the existing depositors
As such, the above company cannot transact the business of a non-banking financial institution as
of  India Act, 1934.
