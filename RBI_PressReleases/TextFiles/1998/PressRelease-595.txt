April 03, 1998 
 
Public contact departments of RBI, Mumbai Offices to function full day on 
April 11 and 12, 1998 
Members of the public are hereby informed that the Reserve Bank of India has decided 
that the following departments of its Mumbai (Fort) Office and Byculla (Mumbai Central) 
Office will remain open from 10.30 a.m. to 2.30 p.m. for public transactions on Saturday, 
April 11, 1998: Public Debt Office, Deposit Accounts Department, Public Accounts 
Department, Clearing House, cash and exchange counters of the Issue Department and 
the public contact areas in the Exchange Control Department. These departments will 
also remain open for public transactions between 10.30 a.m. and 2.30 p.m. on Sunday, 
April 12, 1998.  
Cheque clearing operations will be carried out on these days by the Brihanmumbai 
Bankers' Clearing House.  
(Alpana Killawala)
