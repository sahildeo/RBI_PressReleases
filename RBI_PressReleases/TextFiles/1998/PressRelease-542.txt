July 17, 1998 
Reserve Bank notifies GIVO Limited for further FII purchase 
The Reserve Bank of India has notified that equity shares and convertible debentures of 
GIVO Limited (formerly KB & T Limited) can now be purchased through primary market 
and stock exchanges by foreign institutional investors. The Reserve Bank of India has 
advised that GIVO Limited has passed a resolution at its board and general body 
meetings agreeing for purchase of its equity shares and convertible debentures by 
foreign institutional investors up to 30 per cent of their issued and paid up capital through 
primary and stock exchanges.  
(Alpana Killawala) 
General Manager
