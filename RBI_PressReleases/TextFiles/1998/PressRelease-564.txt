29 May,1998 
 
Statement issued by RBI on 29-5-1998 
 
Reacting to newspaper reports regarding unofficial kerb deals last evening after close of markets, the 
Reserve Bank of India has issued the following statement at 9.00 a.m. "Yesterday's developments have no 
financial implications for us. Financial markets are expected to continue to function normally. RBI will closely 
monitor the market developments including forex markets and take such action as may be necessary to 
ensure continued orderly conditions. For any further clarification, Authorised Dealers are requested to 
approach RBI, DEIO Dealing Room (Phone No. 2664454).  
(Alpana Killawala) 
General Manager
