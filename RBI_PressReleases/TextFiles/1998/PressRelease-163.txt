December 1, 1998
The Reserve Bank of India has advised income tax assessees to take advantage of paying
advance income tax at branches of designated banks. For the convenience of income tax
assessees, arrangements have been  made for receipt of advance income tax at specified branches
of Bank of Baroda, Bank of India, Bank of Maharashtra, Central Bank of India, Dena Bank,
Indian Overseas Bank, State Bank of India and United Bank of India. The designated branches of
these banks have been authorised by the Income Tax Department to accept payment of income
tax dues.
The Reserve Bank of India has also requested income tax assessees in Mumbai city to arrange to
deposit their income tax dues sufficiently in advance of the last date to avoid the inconvenience
of waiting in long queues as also, to avoid last minute rush at the Reserve Bank's counters.
