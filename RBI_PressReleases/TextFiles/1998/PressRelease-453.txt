October 7, 1998
RBI Decaution on Rajasthan Spinning
The Reserve Bank of India has notified that the Non-Resident Indians (NRIs)/Overseas
Corporate Bodies (OCBs)/Persons of Indian Origins (PIOs) holdings in M/s. Rajasthan
Spinning and Weaving Mills Ltd. have gone below 8 per cent of its paid up capital.
The restriction placed on the purchase of shares of this company on behalf of
NRIs/OCBs/PIOs therefore, has been withdrawn.
