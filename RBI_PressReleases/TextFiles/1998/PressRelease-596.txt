April 02, 1998 
 
RBI reduces Bank Rate to 10% 
The Reserve Bank of India has reduced the Bank Rate by one half of one percentage 
point, i.e. from `10.5 per cent per annum' to `10.0 per cent per annum' with effect from 
the close of business on April 2, 1998.
