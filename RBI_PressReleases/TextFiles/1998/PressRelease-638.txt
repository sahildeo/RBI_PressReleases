Jan 06, 1998 
Interbank Dealings - Position 
The Reserve Bank of India today communicated to authorised dealers that till further 
notice, they should maintain a square or near square position as at the end of each day. 
It further clarified that near square position is intended only to cater to pipeline 
transactions, vostro funding transactions after banking hours in India as also amounts 
that are not marketable lots.
