October 13, 1998
12.50 per cent State Development
Loans, 2008 Oversubscribed
The 12.50 per cent ten-year State Development Loans in respect of twenty-five State
Governments opened for subscription for an aggregate notified amount of Rs.3,100.28 crore
on October 12, 1998. The subscriptions received aggregated Rs. 4,462.40 crore. Some
subscriptions received at designated branches of State Bank of India/Associate Banks at
district headquarters of the States are still being reported. The excess subscriptions received
by some State Governments will be retained.
Alpana Killawala
General Manager
