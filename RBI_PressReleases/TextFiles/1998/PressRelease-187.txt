The sale of 12.60 per cent Government Stock, 2018 which opened for subscription on
November 23, 1998 on tap at Reserve Bank of India, Mumbai was closed at the close of banking
hours today. In all, 13 applications for Rs.1,131.88 crore (nominal), were received. All the
applications were accepted.
