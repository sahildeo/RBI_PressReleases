April 03, 1998 
 
Bank branches and RBI will  work on Saturday and Sunday , April 11 and 
12, 1998 
The Reserve Bank of India has advised all banks including commercial banks, regional 
rural banks and primary co-operative banks to keep their branches open for the full day 
on Sunday, April 12, 1998. This will be in addition to the advice of the Reserve Bank 
asking banks to keep their branches open for full day on Saturday, April 11, 1998.  
The Reserve Bank will also keep open all its department having public contact on both. 
The departments having public contact in the Reserve Bank are public Accounts 
Department, Deposit Accounts Department, Public Debt Office, Clearing House, cash 
and exchange counters of the Issue Department and the public contact areas in the 
Exchange Control Department. The above instructions have been issued by the Reserve 
Bank in public interest to obviate the inconveniance likely to be caused to bank 
customers on account of the consecutive bank holiday's from April 8 to 10, 1998 in many 
States and April 11 and 12, 1998 being a Saturday and Sunday, respectively.  
These instructions will not apply to administrative offices of banks.
