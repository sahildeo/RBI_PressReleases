91 Day Treasury Bills - Auction Announced
October 5, 1998
The Reserve Bank of India has announced the auction of 91 day Government of India
treasury bills for a notified amount of Rs.500 crore. The allocation to non-competitive
bidders will be outside the notified amount, at the discretion of the Bank.
Tenders should be submitted in the prescribed form on Friday, October 9, 1998 before
1430 hours. The results will be announced the same evening. Payment by successful
bidders will be on Saturday, October 10, 1998.
