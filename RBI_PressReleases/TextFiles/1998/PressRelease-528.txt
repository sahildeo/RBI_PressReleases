September 01, 1998 
No Proposal to grant Moratorium on FI Loans 
This refers to the news item published in a section of the Press today stating that the 
Reserve Bank of India, in consultation with the Ministry of Finance is considering grant of 
moratorium on loan repayments to financial institutions by corporates for three years on 
a case-to-case basis; and that the moratorium will be a part of a five point package being 
finalised by the Reserve Bank of India for the revival of the economy.  
The Reserve Bank of India would like to clarify that there is no such proposal under 
examination of the Reserve Bank of India for granting moratorium on repayment of loans 
or interest to financial institutions of Banks by the corporates. The Reserve Bank of India 
is also not considering any changes in the definition of non-performing assets of a 
general reduction in debt-equity ratio.
