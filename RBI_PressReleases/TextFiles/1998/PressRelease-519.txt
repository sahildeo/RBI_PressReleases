Rejection of application for certificate of registration
The Reserve Bank of India, in exercise of the powers conferred on it under Section 45 IA of the
Reserve Bank of India Act, 1934, has rejected the application for certificate of registration on
September 17, 1998 submitted by M/s. Keerthi Hire Purchase & Credits Ltd., having its
Registered Office at Excel Building, Sastri Road, Kottayam 686001.
 As such, M/s. Keerthi Hire Purchase & Credits Ltd., is precluded from carrying on the business
of a non-banking financial institution as defined in Clause (a) of Section 45 I of the Reserve
Bank of India Act, 1934.
 
(Alpana Killawala)
General Manager
