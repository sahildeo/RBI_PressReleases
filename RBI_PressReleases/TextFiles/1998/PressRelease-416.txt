October 13, 1998
14 Day Treasury Bills - Auction Announced
The Reserve Bank of India has announced  the auction of 14 day Government of India
treasury bills for a notified amount of Rs.500 crore. The allocation to non-competitive
bidders will be outside the notified amount at the discretion of the Bank.
Tenders should be submitted in the prescribed form on Friday, October 16, 1998
before 14.30 hours. The results will be announced the same evening. Payment by successful
bidders will be on Saturday, October 17, 1998.
