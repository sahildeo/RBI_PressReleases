November 2, 1998
Investment by NRIs/OCBs/PIO -
 Monitoring of overall ceiling under PIS
The Reserve Bank of India has advised that  no further purchases of shares of M/s. Bharti Telecom Ltd. and
Madras Aluminium Co. Ltd. should be made on behalf of non-resident Indians/overseas corporate
bodies/Persons of Indian Origin.  The overall ceiling of 10 per cent of paid up equity capital permitted to be held
by NRIs/OCBs/PIO under the Portfolio Investment Scheme has already been reached in the case of these
companies.
Alpana Killawala
General Manager
