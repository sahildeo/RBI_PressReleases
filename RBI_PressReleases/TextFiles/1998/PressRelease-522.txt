RBI files criminal complaint against C.U.Marketing
The Reserve Bank of India filed a criminal complaint before the Additional Chief Metropolitan
Magistrate, against C.U.Marketing, Mumbai for luring the public to deposit monies in the guise
of 'Advance for goods' in violation of the provisions of Section 45S of the Reserve Bank of India
Act, 1934. The learned Magistrate passed an order on September 17, 1998 referring the
complaint to police authorities for investigation and to submit the report to the court before
October 5, 1998.
