Investment by FIIs - Purchase of equity shares up to 30% of paid up capital 
Equity shares and convertible debentures of Zee Telefilms Ltd. can now be purchased up to 30 per cent of its total paid 
up equity capital and each series of convertible debentures through primary market and stock exchanges subject to 
certain conditions. The company has passed a resolution at its board and general body meetings agreeing for the 
purchase of its equity shares and convertible debentures by foreign institutional investors up to 30 per cent of its issued 
and paid up capital through primary and stock exchanges. It may be mentioned that in terms of the Government of 
India's press release dated April 4, 1997 and June 22, 1998 the aggregating ceiling of FIIs' investment in Indian 
companies had been raised from 24 per cent to 30 per cent of their issued and paid up capital.  
Alpana Killawala 
General Manager
