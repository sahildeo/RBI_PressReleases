Rejection of application for certificate of registration
The Reserve Bank of India, in exercise of the powers conferred on it under Section 45 IA of the
Reserve Bank of India Act, 1934, has rejected the application for certificate of registration on
September 17, 1998 submitted by M/s. K.M. Capital Ltd., 303, Vardhman Plaza, D-14, Central
Market, Prashant Vihar, New Delhi-110 085.
As such, M/s. K.M. Capital Ltd. cannot transact the business of a non-banking financial
institution as defined in Clause (a) of Section 45 I of the Reserve Bank of India Act, 1934.
(Alpana Killawala)
General Manager
