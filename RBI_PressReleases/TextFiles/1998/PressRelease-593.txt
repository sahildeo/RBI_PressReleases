April 06, 1998 
 
Reserve Bank announces modifications regarding full day working of bank 
branches on April 12, 1998 
The Reserve Bank of India has advised all commercial banks, including regional rural 
banks and cooperative banks that -  
i. in States where there are no three consecutive bank holidays from April 8 to 10, 
1998, bank branches need not function on April 12, 1998 (Sunday);  
ii. in other States where there are three consecutive banks holidays from April 8 to 
April 10, 1998, and bank branches are to function for the full day on April 12, 
1998, the employees observing Easter Sunday will be exempted from attending 
office on April 12, 1998. It will be a normal weekly holiday for them;  
iii. there is no change in the decision to have Saturday, April 11, 1998 as a full 
working day in all States announced earlier.  
iv. the same instructions will also apply to the offices of the Reserve Bank of India.  
These modifications have been announced by the Reserve bank following 
representations.
