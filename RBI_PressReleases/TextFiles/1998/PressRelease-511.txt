 
 
21 Sep 1998 
 
364 Day Treasury Bills - Auction Announced 
The Reserve Bank of India has announced the auction of 364 day Government of India treasury 
bills for a notified amount of Rs.200 crore.  
Tenders should be submitted in the prescribed form on Wednesday, September 23, 1998 before 
1430 hours. Results will be announced the same evening. Payment by successful bidders will be 
on Friday, September 25, 1998.
