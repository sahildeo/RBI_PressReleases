Investment by FIIs/NRIs/OCRs under portfolio investment scheme 
Infosys Technologies Ltd. 
The Reserve Bank of India has communicated that the holdings of foreign institutional 
investors (FIIs)/non-resident Indians (NRIs)/overseas corporate bodies (OCBs) in 
Infosys Technologies Ltd. has come down below 26 per cent. As such it would be in 
order for FIIs/NRIs/OCBs to make further purchases of the shares of Infosys 
Technologies Ltd. with prior approval of the Reserve Bank of India.  
Punjab Tractors Ltd. 
Similarly, the holdings of FIIs/NRIs/OCBs in Punjab Tractors Ltd. has gone down below 
20 per cent. It will also be in order to purchases shares of Punjab Tractors Ltd. from the 
secondary market on behalf of FIIs, NRIs and OCBs who have obtained the Reserve 
Bank's permission to invest under the portfolio investment scheme subject to the existing 
ceilings and guidelines. Foreign holding in Infosys Technologies Ltd. is permitted up to 
30 per cent and in Punjab Tractors Ltd. up to 24 per cent. (Alpana Killawala) 
General Manager
