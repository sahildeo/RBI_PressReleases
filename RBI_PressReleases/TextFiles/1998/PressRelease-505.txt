91 Day Treasury Bills - Auction Announced
The Reserve Bank of India has announced the auction of 91 day Government of India treasury
bills for a notified amount of Rs.200 crore. The allocation to non-competitive bidders will be
outside the notified amount, at the discretion of the Bank.
Tenders should be submitted in the prescribed form on Friday, September 25, 1998 before 1430
hours. The results will be announced the same evening. Payment by successful bidders will be on
Satuday, September 26, 1998.
