March 3, 2005
Union Bank of India : Investment by FIIs/NRIs/PIOs under PIS
       The Reserve Bank of India on March 3, 2005 has notified that no further
purchases of equity shares of Union Bank of India should be made on behalf
of Foreign Institutional Investors (FIIs)/non-resident Indians(NRIs)/Persons of
Indian Origin (PIOs) through stock exchanges in India without the prior
permission of the Reserve Bank. The Reserve Bank has stated that Union
Bank of India has reached the trigger limit of 18 per cent of its paid up capital.
