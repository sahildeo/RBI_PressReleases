Underwriting by Primary Dealers
The Reserve Bank of India has set  the cut-off rate for underwriting bids
by Primary Dealers for the auctions of the "7.49 per cent Government Stock,
2017" and "7.40 per cent Government Stock, 2035" to be held on November
8, 2005 at Re.0.07 (seven paise) and Re.0.07 (seven paise) per Rupees 100
respectively.
