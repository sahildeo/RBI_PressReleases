Holidays declared on July 27 and 28, 2005
under Negotiable Instruments Act, 1881 by
the Government of Maharashtra
The Government of Maharashtra has declared public holidays on July
27 and 28, 2005, under the Negotiable Instruments Act, 1881 in the districts of
Mumbai, Thane and Raigad, due to heavy rains and consequent dislocation of
transport services. Consequently, Reserve Bank offices in Mumbai and
Belapur will also remain closed.
