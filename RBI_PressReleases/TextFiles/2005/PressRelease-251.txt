November 9, 2005
IQMS Software Ltd.: Investment by NRIs/ PIOs under PIS
       The Reserve Bank of India on November 9, 2005 has notified that no
further purchases of equity shares of IQMS Software Ltd., should be made on
behalf of Non-Resident Indians (NRIs) and Persons of Indian Origin (PIOs) in
the secondary markets in India. The Reserve Bank has stated that IQMS
Software Ltd., has reached the overall limit of 10 per cent of its paid up
capital.
