Monitoring of Investments by FIIs/NRIs/PIOs
The Reserve Bank of India on September 28, 2005 has notified that
no further purchases of equity shares of undernoted company/bank should be
made under portfolio investment scheme (PIS) on behalf of Foreign
Institutional Investors(FIIs)/Non-Resident Indians (NRIs)/Persons of Indian
Origin (PIOs) through stock exchanges in India. The Reserve Bank has stated
that  these companies have reached the overall limit of their paid up equity
capital.
1. Bharti Tele-Ventures Ltd.
2. ICICI Bank Ltd.
