Reliance Capital Ltd. :
Investment by FIIs under PIS
       The Reserve Bank of India has notified on July 18, 2005 that the holding of
Foreign Institutional Investors (FIIs) under the portfolio investment scheme
(PIS) in the paid up capital of the Reliance Capital Ltd., has gone below the limit
of 22 per cent of its paid up capital. The Reserve Bank has stated that purchase
of equity shares of Reliance Capital Ltd., through secondary markets in India on
behalf of such FIIs, who have obtained its permission to invest under PIS is in
order.
