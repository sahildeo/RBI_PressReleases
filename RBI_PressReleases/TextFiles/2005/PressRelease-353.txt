October 13, 2005
GoI cancels the auction of dated security
scheduled during October 18-25, 2005
On a review of the current borrowing requirements of the Government of India
(GoI), it has been decided, in consultation with GoI, to cancel the auction of dated
security scheduled for October 18-25, 2005 for an amount of Rs.4,000 crore .
It may be recalled that vide our Press Release 2005-2006/355 of September 19,
2005 an indicative calendar of issuance of dated securities for the second half of the
year 2005-2006 was released.
