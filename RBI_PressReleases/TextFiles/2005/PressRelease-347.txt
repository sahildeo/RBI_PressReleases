Statistical Tables Relating To Banks In India 2004-05
The Reserve Bank of India has published Statistical Tables Relating to Banks in
India 2004-05. This is the 60th edition of the volume.
The publication presents detailed tables on assets and liabilities and earning and
expenses of Scheduled Commercial Banks (SCBs) for the year 2004-05. In addition to
detailed bank-wise tables, the publication provides summary tables giving aggregated
data for bank groups as well as for all banks. Although bank-wise tables do not cover
Regional Rural Banks (RRBs), Tables 2.1 and 2.2 include data on RRBs and other
Scheduled Banks at group level.
The publication also gives data on important parameters like maturity and
ownership pattern of deposits, sector-wise bank credit, investment pattern, state-wise
distribution of employees, insured deposits, major items of priority sector lending both at
state-wise and bank-wise, monthly data on sector-wise and industry-wise deployment of
credit, NPAs, etc. In addition, a few new tables, like bank-wise data on loans subjected
to restructuring, Corporate Debt Restructured (CDR) and maturity profile of selected
items of the balance sheet at bank group level, are introduced in the current edition.
The publication is also being brought out in the CD-ROM format. The CD-ROM,
besides providing all the tables, graphs and maps given in the publication, gives inter-
active data querying facility.
The data published in this volume are also made available through Internet at
http://www.rbi.org.in
