Underwriting by Primary Dealers
The Reserve Bank of India has set the cut-off rate for underwriting bids by
Primary Dealers for the auction to be held on November 24, 2005 of “8.35%
Government Stock 2022” at Re.0.03 (three paise) per Rs.100.
