Srei Infrastructure Finance Ltd.,
Monitoring FIIs investment
The Reserve Bank of India has notified on November 23, 2005 that
Foreign Institutional Investors (FIIs) can now purchase under Portfolio
Investment Scheme (PIS) equity shares and convertible debentures of the
Srei Infrastructure Finance Ltd., through primary markets and stock
exchanges in India upto 64 per cent of its paid up capital, as the company
has passed resolutions to this effect at its Board of Directors' and  General
Body Meetings.
