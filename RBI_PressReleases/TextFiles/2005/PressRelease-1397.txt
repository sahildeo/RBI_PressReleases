Underwriting by Primary Dealers
The Reserve Bank of India has set the cut-off rate for underwriting bids
by Primary Dealers for the auction of the "8.35 per cent Government Stock,
2022" to be held on February 7, 2005 at Re.0.25 (twenty five paise) per
Rupees 100.
