August 10, 2005
 Reserve Bank rejects application for certificate of
registration of M/s. Usha Martin Telematics Limited, Kolkata
The Reserve Bank of India, has on July 19, 2005 rejected the application for
certificate of registration submitted by M/s. Usha Martin Telematics Limited having
its office at 3A, Landmark, 3rd Floor, 228-a AJC Bose Road, Kolkata-700 020.
Following rejection of application for certificate of registration, M/s. Usha
Martin Telematics Limited can not transact the business of a non-banking financial
institution.
The Reserve Bank of India has taken this action under powers conferred on
it by Clause (a) of Section 45-I of the Reserve Bank of India Act, 1934.
