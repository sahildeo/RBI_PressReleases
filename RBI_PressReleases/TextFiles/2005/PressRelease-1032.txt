May 10, 2005
On Tap Sale of State Development Loans
Aggregating around Rs. 7300 crore, on May 17, 2005
All the State Governments excluding Chhatisgarh and Punjab have offered
to sell 7.77 per cent State Development Loan, 2015 having maturity of ten years
aggregating about Rs. 7300 crore on May 17, 2005 by way of tap sale under the
normal market borrowing programme.
The sale will be held at Mumbai for all the 26 States as also at the
respective Regional Offices of the Reserve Bank of India. In case of over
subscription, the States have the option to retain excess subscription to the
extent of 10.0 per cent of their respective target amounts. The Reserve Bank of
India reserves the right to close the sale during or after close of banking hours on
or after May 17, 2005 having regard to the subscriptions received. The payment
by the successful applicants will be made on May 19, 2005.
Applications in the prescribed form obtainable from the Public Debt Offices
at the Reserve Bank of India will be received at the aforesaid offices during
banking hours while the sale is open.
