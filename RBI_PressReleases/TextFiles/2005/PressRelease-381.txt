October 5, 2005
Underwriting by Primary Dealers
The Reserve Bank of India has set the cut-off rate for underwriting bids
by Primary Dealers for the auctions of the "11.83 per cent Government Stock,
2014" and "7.40 per cent Government Stock, 2035" to be held on October 6,
2005 at Re.0.11 (eleven paise) and Re.0.14 (fourteen paise) per Rupees 100
respectively.
