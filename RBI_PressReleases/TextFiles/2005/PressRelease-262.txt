RBI releases Annual Accounts of Scheduled Commercial Banks
(including RRBs) : 1979 to 2004 in CD-ROM
The Reserve Bank of India has released it publication entitled Annual Accounts of
Scheduled Commercial Banks (including RRBs) : 1979 to 2004 in CD-ROM. The CD-
ROM provides data on balance sheet and profit and loss accounts of all scheduled
commercial banks (including Regional Rural Banks) for the 25 year period from 1979 to
2004. Data are presented in two Sections : (i) Balance Sheet and (ii) Profit & Loss
Accounts. The data for the years 1979 to 1987 relate to the calendar year (i.e., January-
December), while those for the year 1988-89, pertain to the 15 month period from
January 1, 1988 to March 31, 1989 and for the years from 1989 onwards, the data relate
to the financial year period (i.e. April-March).
The CD-ROM is priced at Rs.85 (Rs.135 by post) and US$ 25 (by courier) and
US$ 15 (by registered airmail). The publication can be obtained from the Director,
Division of Reports, Reviews and Publications (Sales section), Department of Economic
Analysis and Policy, Reserve Bank of India, Amar Building (Ground Floor), P. B.No. 1036,
Mumbai 400 001.
Cheques/drafts should be drawn in favour of the "Reserve Bank of India", payable
at Mumbai.
