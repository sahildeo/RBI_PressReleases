ICICI Bank Ltd.: Investment by FIIs/NRIs/PIOs
The Reserve Bank of India has, on March 31, 2005 advised that due to
the recent sponsored American Depositary Shares (ADS) issue, the Foreign
Institutional Investors (FII) shareholding in ICICI Bank Ltd. has gone below the
limit of 47 per cent whereas the aggregate foreign shareholding has reached
72 per cent of its paid up capital.
The Reserve Bank has stated that further purchase of equity shares of
ICICI Bank Ltd. on behalf of Foreign Institutional Investors (FIIs)/Non-Resident
Indians (NRIs)/Persons of Indian Origin (PIOs) through stock exchanges in
India would be made only with its prior approval.
