May 18, 2005
Tap sale of 7.77 per cent State Development Loan, 2015 closed
The 'on-tap' sale of 7.77 per cent State Development Loan, 2015 in respect of
Uttar Pradesh has been closed today.
It may be recalled that the sale opened on May 17, 2005 on behalf of 26 States
for an aggregate amount of around Rs.7,300 crore with a green shoe option of 10% of
the target amount. Against this, applications for an aggregate amount of Rs.23,051
crore were received of which, subscriptions for an aggregate amount of Rs.7,554 crore
are being retained.
As notified earlier, payment will be required to be made by the allottees on May
19, 2005, in respect of subscriptions received both on May 17 and May 18, 2005.
