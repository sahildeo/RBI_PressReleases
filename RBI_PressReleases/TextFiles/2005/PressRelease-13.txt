December 29, 2005
Federal Bank Limited: Investment by FIIs
The Reserve Bank of India on December 29, 2005 has notified that no
further purchases of equity shares of Federal Bank Limited, under Portfolio
Investment Scheme (PIS) should be made on behalf of Foreign Institutional
Investors (FIIs) through stock exchanges in India. The Reserve Bank has
stated that Federal Bank Limited has reached the overall limit of 24 per cent
of its paid up capital.
