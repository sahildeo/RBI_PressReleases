Hindustan Petroleum Corporation Ltd.: Investment by FIIs under PIS
       The Reserve Bank of India on July 9, 2005 has notified that no further
purchases of equity shares of Hindustan Petroleum Corporation Ltd., should
be made on behalf of Foreign Institutional Investors (FIIs) through
primary/secondary markets in India without the prior permission of the
Reserve Bank. The Reserve Bank has stated that Hindustan Petroleum
Corporation Ltd., has reached the limit of 22 per cent of its paid up capital.
