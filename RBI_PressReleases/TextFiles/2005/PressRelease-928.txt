June 04, 2005
Underwriting by Primary Dealers
The Reserve Bank of India has set the cut-off rate for underwriting bids by
Primary Dealers for the auction to be held on June 6, 2005 of “7.37%
Government Stock 2014” and 10.25% Government Stock 2021” at Re.0.05 (five
paise) and Re.0.19 (nineteen paise) per Rs.100, respectively.
