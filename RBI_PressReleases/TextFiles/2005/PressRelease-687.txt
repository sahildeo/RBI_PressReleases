July 27, 2005
Cancellation of Liquidity Adjustment Facility (LAF) and Secondary
Markets transactions in Government Securities
The Government of Maharashtra has declared public holidays on July
27 and 28, 2005, under the Negotiable Instruments Act, 1881.
Consequently, there will no transactions and settlements in
Government Securities and Money Markets on these two days. Settlement of
all outstanding transactions will accordingly get postponed to the next working
day i.e. July 29, 2005.
Transactions under Liquidity Adjustment Facility (LAF) conducted on
July 26, 2005 will accordingly be reversed on July 29, 2005.
This press release is being issued further to RBI's press releases
No.122 and 124 issued on July 27, 2005 in these regards.
