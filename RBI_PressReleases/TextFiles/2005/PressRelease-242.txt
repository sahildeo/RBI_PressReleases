November 10, 2005
Government of Andhra Pradesh announces the auction of Andhra Pradesh State
Development Loan 2015
The State Government of Andhra Pradesh have announced the sale of a 10 year
Andhra Pradesh State Development Loan for a notified amount of Rs.375 crore through
a yield based auction using multiple price auction method. The auction will be conducted
by the Reserve Bank of India (RBI) at Mumbai on November 17, 2005 (Thursday).
Bids in the prescribed form obtainable from the Regional Director, Reserve Bank
of India, Mumbai Office (Public Debt Office), Fort, Mumbai-400 001 should be submitted
to that Office on Thursday, November 17, 2005. The competitive bids by the NDS
members should be submitted only in electronic format using NDS. All bids should be
submitted by 12.30 P.M. On the basis of bids received, RBI will determine the maximum
yield at which offers will be accepted.
 The results of the auction will be announced on November 17, 2005 and payment
by successful bidders will be accepted during banking hours on November 18, 2005
(Friday) at Reserve Bank of India, Mumbai and Hyderabad. The stock will qualify for the
ready forward facility.
