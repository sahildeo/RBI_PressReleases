March 14, 2005
Tap sale of 7.20 per cent State Development Loan, 2017 closed
The 'on-tap' sale of 7.20 per cent State Development Loan, 2017 in
respect of both the states, viz., Maharashtra and Mizoram has been closed
today.
It may be recalled that the sale opened today, i.e; March 14, 2005 on
behalf of these two states for an aggregate amount of around Rs. 400 crore.
Against this, applications for an aggregate amount of Rs. 433.196 crore were
received and the full amount is being retained.
As notified earlier, payment will be required to be made by the allottees on
March 16, 2005, in respect of subscriptions received today, i.e; March 14, 2005.
