Reserve Bank Cancels Certificate of Registration of
Dheva Investments & Finance (India) Pvt. Limited, Chennai
The Reserve Bank of India, has on September 8, 2005 cancelled the certificate of
registration granted to Dheva Investments & Finance (India) Pvt. Limited, having its
registered office at 5C, Mookambika Complex, No.4, Lady Deslkachary Road, Mylapore,
Chennai for carrying on the business of a non-banking financial institution.
Following cancellation of registration certificate, Dheva Investments & Finance
(India) Pvt. Limited cannot transact the business of a non-banking financial institution..
Under powers conferred by Section 45-IA (6) of the Reserve Bank of India Act, 1934,
the Reserve Bank can cancel the registration certificate of non-banking financial company.
The business of a non-banking financial institutions is defined in clause (a) of Section 45-I
of the Reserve Bank of India Act, 1934.
