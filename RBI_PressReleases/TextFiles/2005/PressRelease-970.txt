May 25, 2005
Sivagangai District Central Co-operative Bank Ltd.
There have been enquiries regarding the present status of the
Sivagangai District Central Co-operative Bank Ltd., which has been placed
under directions.
The National Bank for Agriculture and Rural Development (NABARD)
has since completed the inspection of the Sivagangai District Central Co-
operative Bank Ltd., and submitted its report to the Reserve Bank of India. A
copy of the report has also been forwarded to the Sivagangai District Central
Co-operative Bank Ltd. Based on the report, suggestions for remedying
critical deficiencies in its working and its financial position have been made to
the Sivagangai District Central Co-operative Bank Ltd.
Relaxations in the directions issued to the Sivagangai District Central
Co-operative Bank Ltd., will be considered based on its response.
The Reserve Bank of India has also kept the Government of Tamil
Nadu informed.
Alpana Killawala
Chief General Manger
