July 1, 2005
RBI opens Centralised Pension Cell
The Reserve Bank of India has centralised the Pension Payments of its
staff who retired from Mumbai by setting up a Centralised Pension Cell. The
cell was inauguarated today by Deputy Governor, Smt. Shyamala Gopinath.
Speaking at the function, she mentioned that while decentralisation  is
beneficial for better customer service, in the era of computerisation
centralisation of processing is most ideal from the point of view of cost and
convenience. The Cell will be headed by Mr. Philip Sequeira, Deputy General
Manager and he can be contacted at his Bandra Office on Tel.No.2657 1549
for any of the matters relating to pension of RBites upto CGM level who retired
from Mumbai. The pension work of retired Executives will continue to be
handled by DEBC, Central Office.
