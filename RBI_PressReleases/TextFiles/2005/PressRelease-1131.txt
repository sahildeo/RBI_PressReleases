April 15, 2005
Government of Kerala announces the auction of
Kerala State Government Loan 2015
The State Government of Kerala have announced the sale of a 10 year Kerala
State Development Loan for a notified amount of Rs.300 crore through a yield based
auction using multiple price auction method. The auction will be conducted by the
Reserve Bank of India (RBI) at Mumbai on April 20, 2005 (Wednesday).
Bids in the prescribed form obtainable from the Regional Director, Reserve
Bank of India, Mumbai Office (Public Debt Office), Fort, Mumbai-400 001 should be
submitted to that Office on Wednesday, April 20, 2005. The competitive bids by the
NDS members should be submitted only in electronic format using NDS. All bids
should be submitted by 12.30 P.M. On the basis of bids received, RBI will determine
the maximum yield at which offers will be accepted.
 The results of the auction will be announced on April 20, 2005 and payment
by successful bidders will be during banking hours on April 21, 2005 (Thursday) at
Mumbai and Thiruvananthapuram. The stock will qualify for the ready forward facility.
Bids for underwriting of the Kerala Government Securities under auction may
be submitted by the ‘Primary Dealers’ up to 12.30 p.m. on April 19, 2005 (Tuesday)
to the Chief General Manager, Internal Debt Management Department, Reserve
Bank of India, Central Office, Fort, Mumbai 400 001.
