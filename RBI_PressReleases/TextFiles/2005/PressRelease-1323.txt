February 23, 2005
Tap sale of 7.17 per cent State Development Loan, 2017 closed
The 'on-tap' sale of 7.17 per cent State Development Loan, 2017 in
respect of the remaining ten State Governments viz., Assam, Bihar,
Maharashtra, Manipur, Meghalaya, Nagaland, Sikkim, Tripura, Uttar Pradesh
and West Bengal has been closed today.
It may be recalled that the sale opened on February 22, 2005 on behalf
of 16 States for an aggregate amount of around Rs. 6,300 crore. Against this,
applications for an aggregate amount of Rs. 3,760 crore were received of
which, subscriptions for an aggregate amount of Rs. 3,031 crore are being
retained.
As notified earlier, payment will be required to be made by the allottees
on February 24, 2005, in respect of subscriptions received both on February
22 and February 23, 2005.
