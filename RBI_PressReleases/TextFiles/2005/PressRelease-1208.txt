RBI to announce Annual Policy for 2005-06
on April 28, 2005
Dr. Y.V. Reddy, Governor, Reserve Bank of India will announce the
annual policy Statement for the year 2005-06 on Thursday, April 28, 2005. He
will announce the Policy in a meeting of chief executives of major scheduled
commercial banks on that date at 11.00 a.m. in the Reserve Bank of India,
Central Office, Mumbai.
Alpana Killawala
Chief General Manger
