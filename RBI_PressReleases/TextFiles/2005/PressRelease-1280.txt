March 7, 2005
On Tap Sale of State Development Loans
Aggregating around Rs. 400 crore, on March 14, 2005
The Governments of Maharashtra and Mizoram have offered to sell
7.20 per cent State Development Loan, 2017 having a maturity of twelve years,
aggregating around Rs. 400 crore, on March 14, 2005 by way of tap sale for
repayment of Rural Infrastructure Development Fund (RIDF) loans to the
National Bank for Agriculture and Rural Development (NABARD).
The sale will be held at Reserve Bank of India, Guwahati and at Mumbai
(for both the States). The Reserve Bank of India reserves the right to close the
sale during the banking hours on or after March 14, 2005 having regard to the
requirements of the State Governments and the subscriptions received. The date
of payment will be March 16, 2005.
Applications in the prescribed form obtainable from the Public Debt
Offices at the Reserve Bank of India will be received at the Reserve Bank offices
during banking hours while the sale is open.
