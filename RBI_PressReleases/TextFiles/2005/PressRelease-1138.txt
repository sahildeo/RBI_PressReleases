Geodesic Information Systems Ltd.:
Monitoring FIIs investment in Indian Companies
The Reserve Bank of India has notified on April 13, 2005 that Foreign
Institutional Investors (FIIs) can now purchase under Portfolio Investment
Scheme (PIS) equity shares and convertible debentures of Geodesic
Information Systems Ltd., through primary/secondary markets in India upto
100 per cent limits of its paid up capital, as the company has passed
resolution to this effect at its Board of Directors' and a Special Resolution in its
General Body Meeting.
