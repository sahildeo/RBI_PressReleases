December 2, 2005
 Investment by NRIs/ PIOs under PIS
       The Reserve Bank of India on December 2, 2005 has notified that no
further purchases of equity shares of the following companies should be made
on behalf of Non-Resident Indians (NRIs) and Persons of Indian Origin (PIOs)
in the secondary markets in India. The Reserve Bank has stated that the
following companies have reached the overall limit of 10 per cent of their paid
up capital.
1. K Sera Sera Productions Ltd.
2. SGN Telecom Ltd.
