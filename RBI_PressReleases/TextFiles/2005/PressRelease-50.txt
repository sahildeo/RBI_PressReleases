Investment by FIIs/NRIs under PIS: Vijaya Bank
The Reserve Bank of India on December 22, 2005 has notified that the
aggregate foreign share holdings in Vijaya Bank has gone below 18 percent of
its paid up capital. The Reserve Bank has stated that it would be in order for
Foreign Institutional Investors (FIIs) and Non-Resident Indians (NRIs) to
purchase the shares of Vijaya Bank under Portfolio Investment Scheme (PIS)
through stock exchanges in India without its prior permission.
