July 18, 2005
Financial Sector Technology Vision Document on RBI Site
Governor had, in the Annual Policy Statement 2005-06, announced that
the Reserve Bank would be bringing out a ‘Financial Sector Technology Vision
Document’ aimed at providing banks with inputs on medium term policy
perspectives. A draft ‘Vision Document’ prepared on the basis of comments /
observations received from banks in the country was put on the Bank’s web
site for wider comments, in May, 2005. Based on the feedback, the ‘Financial
Sector Technology Vision Document’ has been finalised and has been put in
the web site of the Bank.
In a nutshell, the ‘Financial Sector Technology Vision’ document, elucidates
the Technology Vision for the financial sector, with coverage including the
following:
• IT for regulation and supervision
• IT and IDRBT
• IT for the financial sector
• IT for Government related functions
Vision Statement:
 ‘ IT for Efficiency and excellence’
Corporate Objective:
“Enabling banks to leverage on IT for better customer service,
improved housekeeping and overall systemic efficiency”
The Financial Sector Technology Vision document outlines the previews
and prospects as envisioned by the Reserve Bank for the medium term period
covering the period 2005-2008.
