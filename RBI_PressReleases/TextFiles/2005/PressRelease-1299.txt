March 2, 2005
Mahindra Gesco Developers Ltd. : Investment by FIIs under PIS
       The Reserve Bank of India on March 2, 2005 has notified that no further
purchases of equity shares of Mahindra Gesco Developers Ltd., should be
made on behalf of Foreign Institutional Investors (FIIs) through stock
exchanges in India. The Reserve Bank has stated that Mahindra Gesco
Developers Ltd., has reached the overall limit of 24 per cent of its paid up
capital.
