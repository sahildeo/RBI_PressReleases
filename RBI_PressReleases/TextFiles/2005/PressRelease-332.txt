October 19, 2005
Monitoring of Investment Limits of FIIs under PIS
       The Reserve Bank of India on October 19, 2005, has notified that no
further purchases of equity shares of Indraprastha Gas Limited should be
made on behalf of Foreign Institutional Investors (FIIs) under portfolio
investment scheme (PIS) in the primary/secondary markets in India without
the prior permission of the Reserve Bank. The Reserve Bank has stated that
Indrapgrastha Gas Limited has reached the limit of 22 per cent of its paid up
capital.
