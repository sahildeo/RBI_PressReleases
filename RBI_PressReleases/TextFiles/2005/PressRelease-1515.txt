January 5, 2005
Reserve Bank Cancels Certificate of Registration of
M/s. Ideal Commerce Limited, Ahmedabad
The Reserve Bank of India, has on December 31, 2004 cancelled the certificate of
registration issued to M/s. Ideal Commerce Limited, having its registered office at 42,
Swastik Chambers, Near Gujarat Vidyapith, Off Ashram Road, Ahmedabad-380 014 for
carrying on the business of a non-banking financial institution.
Under powers conferred by Section 45IA of the Reserve Bank of India Act, 1934, the
Reserve Bank can cancel the registration certificate of non-banking financial company. The
business of a non-banking financial institution is defined in clause (a) of Section 45IA of the
Reserve Bank of India Act, 1934.
