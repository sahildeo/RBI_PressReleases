December 16, 2005
Monitoring FII Investments under PIS
The Reserve Bank of India has on December 16, 2005 notified that the
Foreign Institutional Investors (FIIs) holdings in the undernoted companies
have gone down below 22 per cent of their paid up capital. The Reserve Bank
has further notified that it would be in order to purchase the shares of these
companies in the secondary market on behalf of Foreign Institutional
Investors (FIIs) under Portfolio Investment Scheme (PIS) subject to the
existing ceilings and guidelines.
1. Hindalco Industries Ltd.
2. Trent Ltd.
