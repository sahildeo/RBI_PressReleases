Mahindra & Mahindra Ltd.:
Monitoring FIIs Investments
The Reserve Bank of India has notified on August 17, 2005 that
Foreign Institutional Investors (FIIs) can now purchase under Portfolio
Investment Scheme (PIS) equity shares and convertible debentures of
Mahindra & Mahindra Ltd., through primary markets and stock exchanges in
India upto 49 per cent of its paid up capital, as the company has passed
resolutions to this effect at its Board of Directors' and General Body Meetings.
