894 Bank Branches to accept IT
The Reserve Bank of India has advised income-tax assessees in the cities
of Mumbai and Navi Mumbai to take advantage of paying their income tax duties at
any of the designated bank branches. The income-tax department has authorised
894 computerised branches of public sector banks and private sector banks in
Mumbai and Navi Mumbai to accept payment of income-tax dues. Of these, 833
are public sector bank branches, 23 branches of HDFC Bank, 9 branches of ICICI
Bank, 16 Branches of UTI Bank and 12 branches of IDBI Bank and an internet
branch of IDBI bank.
The Reserve Bank has also advised income-tax assessees to avoid last
minute rush at the Reserve Bank's/designated banks' counters and to deposit the
income-tax dues sufficiently in advance.
