June 22, 2005
RBI releases the Report of the Expert Group
on Investment Credit for comments
The Reserve Bank of India today released the Report of the Expert Group
on Investment Credit. The Expert Group was constituted by the Reserve Bank of
India under the Chairmanship of Shri Y.S.P.Thorat, Managing Director of
National Bank for Agriculture and Rural Development (NABARD) to, inter-alia,
analyse the trends in investment credit in the agriculture sector over the last
decade in terms of flow of credit for different purposes and land holding groups
and to suggest a road map for increasing the investment credit in a time bound
manner.
The report of the Group is available on the Reserve Bank’s website
(www.rbi.org.in). Views may please be sent to cgmincrpcd@rbi.org.in or
csmurthy@rbi.org.in or can be faxed to 022-22658276/022-22658273/022-
