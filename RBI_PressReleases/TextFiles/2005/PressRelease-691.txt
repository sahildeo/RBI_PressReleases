July 27, 2005
Cancellation of Liquidity Adjustment Facility (LAF)
  
The Reserve Bank of India announces that no reverse repo/repo
auction will be held under the Liquidity Adjustment Facility today i.e., July 27,
2005.The reversal of yesterday's LAF transactions will now take place on July
28, 2005 instead of today.
 This measure is being taken due to incessant rainfall in Mumbai
disrupting normal financial activities.
