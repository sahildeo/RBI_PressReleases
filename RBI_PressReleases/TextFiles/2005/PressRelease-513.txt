September 8, 2005
 Reserve Bank rejects application for certificate of
registration of Usha Martin Power Company Limited, Kolkata
The Reserve Bank of India, has on August 13, 2005 rejected the application
for certificate of registration submitted by Usha Martin Power Company Limited
having its office at 2A, Shakespeare Sarani, Kolkata-700 071.
Following rejection of application for certificate of registration, Usha Martin
Power Company Limited can not transact the business of a non-banking financial
institution.
The Reserve Bank of India has taken this action under powers conferred on
it by Clause (a) of Section 45-I of the Reserve Bank of India Act, 1934.
