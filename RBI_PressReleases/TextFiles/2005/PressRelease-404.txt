September 30, 2005
Investment by FIIs/NRIs/PIOs under PIS
       The Reserve Bank of India on September 30, 2005, has notified that no
further purchases of equity shares of Vijaya Bank should be made on behalf
of Foreign Institutional Investors (FIIs)/Non-Resident Indians (NRIs)/Persons
of Indian Origin (PIOs) through stock exchanges in India without the prior
permission of the Reserve Bank. The Reserve Bank has stated that Vijaya
Bank has reached the limit of 18 per cent of its paid up capital.
