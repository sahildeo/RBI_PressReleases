RBI to release fortnightly statement of
all scheduled banks' business in India
The Reserve Bank of India today released the statement all scheduled
banks' business in India. The statement which was so far issued every fortnightly
by the Reserve Bank's Department of Banking Operations and Development in
terms of Section 43 of the Reserve Bank of India Act relates to the scheduled
banks' position in India as on the close of business of February 18, 2005. The
statement covers, among other things, liabilities to the banking system including
demand and time deposits from banks, bank credit and investments. The
statement further covers the business data from scheduled commercial banks
including Regional Rural Banks and all scheduled banks, which include urban
cooperative banks. The statement additionally includes food credit outstanding of
scheduled commercial and state cooperative banks. The statement also
comprises comparative position for Friday of the preceding fortnight and
corresponding Friday of the previous year.
It may be noted that the Reserve Bank has, so far, been releasing data
relating to scheduled commercial banks' business in India on a fortnightly basis in
its Weekly Statistical Supplement to the Reserve Bank of India Bulletin. The
supplement is available on the RBI website at www.wss.rbi.org.in The additional
press release on scheduled banks' data gives business data of all scheduled
banks apart from the data on scheduled commercial banks' business given in the
Weekly Statistical Supplement. The press release will be issued every fortnight
and will be placed on the RBI website under Statistics section – Fortnightly.
