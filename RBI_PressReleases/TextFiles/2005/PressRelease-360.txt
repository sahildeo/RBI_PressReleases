Mid-term Review of Annual Policy
for 2005-06 on October 25
As announced in the Annual Policy Statement for 2005-06, Dr. Y.V.Reddy,
Governor, Reserve Bank of India will present the Mid-term Review of Annual Policy
Statement for 2005-06 on Tuesday, October 25, 2005. He will meet the chief
executives of major scheduled commercial banks on that date at 11.00 a.m. in the
Reserve Bank of India, Central Office, Mumbai.
