January 5, 2005
On Tap Sale of State Development Loans on January 11, 2005
The Governments of 20 States have offered to sell 7.02 per cent State
Development Loan, 2015 having maturity of ten years aggregating about  Rs.6000
crore on January 11, 2005 by way of tap sale under the normal market borrowing
programme and pre-repayment of their high cost debt to the Central Government.
The States which are not participating in the tap sale are Arunachal Pradesh,
Chhatisgarh, Goa, Gujarat, Mizoram, Orissa, Sikkim and Uttaranchal.
The sale will be held at the respective regional offices of the Reserve Bank of
India and at Mumbai (for all the 20 States). The Reserve Bank of India reserves the
right to close the sale during banking hours on or after January 11, 2005 having
regard to the requirements of the State Governments and the subscriptions received.
The date of payment will be January 13, 2005.
Applications in the prescribed form obtainable from the Public Debt Offices at
the Reserve Bank of India will be received at the aforesaid offices during banking
hours while the sale is open.
