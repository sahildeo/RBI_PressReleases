Gujarat Ambuja Cement Ltd.: Investment by FIIs under PIS
       The Reserve Bank of India on January 20, 2005 has notified that no
further purchases of equity shares of Gujarat Ambuja Cement Ltd., should be
made on behalf of Foreign Institutional Investors (FIIs) through
primary/secondary markets in India without the prior permission of the
Reserve Bank. The Reserve Bank has stated that Gujarat Ambuja Cement
Ltd., has reached the trigger limit of 28 per cent of its paid up capital.
