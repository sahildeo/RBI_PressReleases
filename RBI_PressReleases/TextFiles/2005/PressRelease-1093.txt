April 26, 2005
Sivagangai District Central Co-operative Bank Ltd.,
Sivagangai, Tamil Nadu
There have been enquiries regarding the present status of the Sivagangai
District Central Co-operative Bank Ltd., which has been placed under directions
recently.
National Bank for Agriculture and Rural Development (NABARD) has
undertaken an inspection of the bank to find out its financial position as on
March 31, 2005. It has been ascertained from NABARD that the audit work of the
bank as on March 31, 2005 is still under progress and the relevant data
pertaining to the financial position as on that date is yet to be made available to
the inspection team.
NABARD expects the inspection to be completed and the Report made
available by the first week of May 2005.
