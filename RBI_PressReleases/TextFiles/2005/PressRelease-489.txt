Reserve Bank Cancels Certificate of Registration of
Daffordils Finance Limited, Seohara
The Reserve Bank of India, has on July 25, 2005 cancelled the certificate of
registration granted to Daffordils Finance Limited, Seohara, having its registered office at
Jain Street, Seohara, Bijnor District, Uttar Pradesh for carrying on the business of a non-
banking financial institution.
Following cancellation of registration certificate, Daffordils Finance Limited cannot
transact the business of a non-banking  financial institution. The company has also been
prohibited from acceptance of deposits and alienation of assets.
Under powers conferred by Section 45-IA (6) of the Reserve Bank of India Act, 1934,
the Reserve Bank can cancel the registration certificate of non-banking financial company.
Under Section 45MB (1) of this Act, the Reserve Bank can prohibit non-banking financial
company from accepting deposits; and under Section 45MB (2), it can prohibit the company
from alienating its assets. The business of a non-banking financial institutions is defined in
clause (a) of Section 45-I of the Reserve Bank of India Act, 1934.
