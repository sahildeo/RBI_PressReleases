RBI to issue Rs.50 and Rs.100 denomination banknotes
with inset letter `L'
The Reserve Bank of India will shortly issue Rs.50 and Rs.100
denomination banknotes with inset letter `L' in both numbering panels in
Mahatma Gandhi Series – 2005 bearing the signature of Dr. Y.V. Reddy,
Governor. Except for the change in the inset letter, the design of these notes
to be issued now is similar in all respects to the banknotes in Mahatma
Gandhi Series with additional/new security features issued in August 2005. All
banknotes in the denomination of Rs.50 and Rs.100 issued by the Bank in the
past will continue to be legal tender.
