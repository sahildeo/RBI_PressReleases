July 27, 2005
Cancellation of
91 day and 182 day Treasury Bill Auctions
 
 The Reserve Bank of India announces the cancellation of auction of
91-day Government of India treasury bills and 182-day Government of India
treasury bills scheduled for today, i.e., July 27, 2005, for Rs.2000 crore and  Rs.
1500 crore, respectively, due to incessant rainfall in Mumbai disrupting normal
financial activities.
 
It may be recalled that the announcement of the aforesaid auctions was
announced on July 23, 2005.
