Punjab National Bank: Investment by FIIs/NRIs/PIOs under PIS
       The Reserve Bank of India has notified on March 31, 2005 that the holding
of Foreign Institutional Investors (FIIs)/Non-Resident Indians (NRIs)/Persons of
Indian Origin (PIOs) under the portfolio investment scheme (PIS) in the paid up
capital of the Punjab National Bank has gone below the limit of 20 per cent of its
paid up capital. The Reserve Bank has stated that further purchase of equity
shares of Punjab National Bank on behalf of FIIs/NRIs/PIOs through stock
exchanges in India would be made only with its prior approval.
