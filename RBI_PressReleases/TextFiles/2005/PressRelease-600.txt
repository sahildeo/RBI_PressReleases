Underwriting by Primary Dealers
The Reserve Bank of India has set  the cut-off rate for underwriting bids
by Primary Dealers for the auctions of the "7.37 per cent Government Stock,
2014" and 7.50 per cent Government Stock, 2034 to be held on August 18,
2005 at Re.0.03 (three paise) and Re.0.05 (five paise) per Rupees 100
respectively.
