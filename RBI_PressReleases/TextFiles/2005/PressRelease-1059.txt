May 4, 2005
Oriental Bank of Commerce: Investment by FIIs/NRIs/ POIs under PIS
       The Reserve Bank of India on May 4, 2005 has notified that no further
purchases of equity shares of Oriental Bank of Commerce should be made on
behalf of Foreign Institutional Investors ( FIIs)/Non-Resident Indians ( NRIs)
and Persons of Indian Origin (POIs) through stock exchanges in India without
its prior permission. The Reserve Bank has stated that Oriental Bank of
Commerce has reached the trigger limit of 18 per cent of its paid up capital.
