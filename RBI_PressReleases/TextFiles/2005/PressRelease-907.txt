June 9, 2005
 11 State Governments announce the auction of
State Government Loans 2015
The following State Governments have announced the sale of 10-year State
Development Loans for an aggregate amount of Rs.2481.15 crore through a yield
based auction using multiple price auction method.
Sr. State Notified Amount
No. (Rs. in Crore)

7 Madhya Pradesh 292.75
8 Maharashtra 407.39
9 Manipur 27.23
10 Tamil Nadu 300.00
11 West Bengal 449.45
Total 2481.15
The auction will be conducted by the Reserve Bank of India (RBI) at Mumbai on
June 14, 2005 (Tuesday). Bids in the prescribed form obtainable from the Regional
Director, Reserve Bank of India, Mumbai Office (Public Debt Office), Fort, Mumbai-400
001 should be submitted to that Office on Tuesday, June 14, 2005. The competitive
bids by the NDS members should be submitted only in electronic format using NDS.
All bids should be submitted by 12.30 P.M. On the basis of bids received, RBI will
determine the maximum yield at which offers will be accepted.
 The results of the auction will be announced on June 14, 2005 and payment by
successful bidders will be during banking hours on June 15, 2005 (Wednesday) at
Mumbai and at respective Regional Offices of Reserve Bank of India. The stocks will
qualify for the ready forward facility.
