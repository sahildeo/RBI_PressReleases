September 13, 2005
Tap sale of 7.53 per cent State Development Loan, 2015
closed for all the eleven States
The 'on-tap' sale of 7.53 per cent ten-year State Development Loan, 2015 has
closed in respect of all the eleven states, viz, Arunachal Pradesh, Haryana, Himachal
Pradesh, Jammu and Kashmir, Madhya Pradesh, Meghalaya, Mizoram, Nagaland,
Sikkim, Uttar Pradesh and West Bengal on receipt of the respective target amounts.
Amounts of allotment in respect of these states are being advised to the investors
concerned.
It may be recalled that the sale opened on September 13, 2005 on behalf of
11 states for an aggregate amount of around Rs. 2900 crore. Against this, applications
for an aggregate amount of Rs. 8,850 crore were received of which, subscriptions for an
aggregate amount of Rs. 2931 crore are being retained.
As notified earlier, payment will be required to be made by the allottees on
September 15, 2005, in respect of subscriptions received on September 13, 2005.
