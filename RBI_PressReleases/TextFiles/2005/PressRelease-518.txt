Underwriting by Primary Dealers
The Reserve Bank of India has set the cut-off rate for underwriting bids
by Primary Dealers for the auction of  "5.69 per cent Government Stock, 2018
and 30 year Government Stock, 2035" to be held on September 8, 2005 at
Re.0.3 (three paise) and Re.0.5 (five paise) respectively  per Rupees 100.
