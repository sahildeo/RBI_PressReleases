July 14, 2005
Government of India announce the sale of dated security
The Government of India have announced the sale (re-issue) of "10.25
percent Government Stock 2021” for a notified amount of Rs.5,000 crore through a
price based auction using multiple price method. The auction will be conducted by
the Reserve Bank of India (RBI) at Mumbai on Monday, July 18, 2005.
Up to 5% of the notified amount of the sale of the stock will be allotted to
eligible individuals and institutions as per the Scheme for Non-Competitive Bidding
Facility in the Auction of Government Securities for the auction.
Bids in the prescribed form obtainable from the Regional Director, Reserve
Bank of India, Mumbai Office, Public Debt Office, Fort, Mumbai-400 001 and RBI
website www.rbi.org.in should be submitted to that Office on Monday, July 18, 2005.
The NDS members should submit competitive as well as non-competitive bids in
electronic format using Primary Market Operation (PMO) module of NDS. All bids
should be submitted by 12.30 p.m.
The result of the auction will be announced on July 18, 2005 and payment by
successful bidders will be during banking hours on July 19, 2005 (Tuesday).
The stock will qualify for the ready forward facility.
Bids for underwriting of the Government Security under auction can be
submitted by the ‘Primary Dealers’ up to 12.30 p.m. on July 16, 2005(Saturday)
using PMO module of NDS and physical bids to the Regional Director, Reserve Bank
of India, Public Debt Office, Fort, Mumbai - 400 001.
