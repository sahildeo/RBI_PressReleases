January 11, 2005
Tap sale of 7.02 per cent state Development Loans 2015
closed for nine states
The 'on tap' sale of 7.02 per cent ten years State Development Loans,
2015 has closed in respect of nine States, i.e. Andhra Pradesh, Haryana,
Jharkhand, Karnataka, Kerala, Madhya Pradesh, Maharashtra, Rajasthan and
Tamilnadu on receipt of the respective target amounts.  Amounts of allotment
in respect of these states are being advised to the investors concerned.
The sale on tap in respect of loans for remaining eleven States, viz.,
Assam, Bihar, Himachal Pradesh, Jammu & Kashmir, Manipur, Meghalaya,
Nagaland, Punjab, Tripura, Uttar Pradesh and West Bengal will be kept open
on January 12, 2005 to collect the balance target amount. Applications in the
prescribed form can be obtained from the Public Debt Office of the respective
offices of the Reserve Bank of India and Mumbai Office.
Applications will be received at these offices during banking hours
while the tap sale is open.  The Reserve Bank of India reserves the right to
close the tap sale during the banking hours on January 12, 2005 having
regard to the borrowing requirements of the State Governments and the
subscriptions received.
As notified earlier, payment will be required to be made by the
investors on January 13, 2005 in respect of subscriptions received/allotted
both on January 11 and January 12, 2005.
