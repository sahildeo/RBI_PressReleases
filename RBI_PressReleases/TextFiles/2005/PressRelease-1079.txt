Monitoring of Overall Ceiling - Investment by FIIs
       The Reserve Bank of India has notified on April 29, 2005 that the holdings
of Foreign Institutional Investors (FIIs) under the portfolio investment scheme
(PIS) in the paid up capital of undernoted companies have gone below the
caution limit. It will, therefore, be in order for FIIs to purchase the shares of
these companies in the secondary market under the portfolio investment
scheme. The investments will be subject to the existing ceilings and
guidelines in this regard.
1. ACC Ltd.
2. Shasun Chemical & Drugs Ltd.
