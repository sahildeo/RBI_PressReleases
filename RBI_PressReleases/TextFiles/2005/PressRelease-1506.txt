January 6, 2005
Donations to PMNRF can be online free of charge
The Reserve Bank of India today placed in its public domain the names, account
numbers and MICR codes of branches, which are authorised to collect funds for the
Prime Minister’s National Relief Fund (PMNRF), to enable people to contribute to this
fund through electronic fund transfer (EFT) or special electronic fund transfer (SEFT).
The Reserve Bank had earlier asked banks offering online remittance services to offer
this facility free of charge for remitting donations to PMNRF. By placing the name,
account number and MICR code of branches accepting these donations, the process of
online remittance would be facilitated.
