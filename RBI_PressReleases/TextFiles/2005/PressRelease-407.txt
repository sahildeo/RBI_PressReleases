     September 30, 2005
RBI imposes penalty on Kangra Central Co-operative Bank Limited,
Kangra, Himachal Pradesh
The Reserve Bank of India imposed a penalty of Rs.5,00,000/- (Rupees five
lakh only) on Kangra Central Co-operative Bank Limited, Kangra, Himachal Pradesh,
under Section 46(4) read with Section 47(A)(1)(b) of the Banking Regulation Act,
1949 (as applicable to Co-operative Societies) for violation of instructions/guidelines
issued by the Reserve Bank in regard to non-SLR investment.
