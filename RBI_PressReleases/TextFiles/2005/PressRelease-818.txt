June 29, 2005
Monitoring of overall ceiling - Investment by FIIs
The Reserve Bank of India has notified on June 29, 2005 that Foreign
Institutional Investors (FIIs) can now purchase under Portfolio Investment
Scheme (PIS) equity shares and convertible debentures of Apollo Hospitals
Enterprise Ltd. through primary markets and stock exchanges in India upto 74
per cent of its paid up capital, as Apollo Hospitals Enterprise Ltd. have passed
resolutions to this effect at its Board of Directors' and General Body Meetings.
