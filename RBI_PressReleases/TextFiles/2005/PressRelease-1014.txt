May 14, 2005
RBI releases Report on Money Market
The Reserve Bank of India placed on its website the report of an internal
technical group on money market. The Reserve Bank had set up the group in February
2005 to review developments and current status of money market in India and cross-
country experience in money market and to suggest measures for further development
of the money market in India. The Governor had, in his Annual Policy Statement on
April 28, 2005, stated that the report of the technical group would be placed on the RBI
website for wider dissemination.
The Group has maintained that the policy thrust should be to encourage
collateralised market, develop the rupee yield curve, ensure transparency and better
price discovery, provide avenues for better risk management and strengthen monetary
operations. With this in view, it ahs made detailed recommendations relating to
call/notice money market, repo/CBLO, term money, certificate of deposits, commercial
paper, FRAs/IRS, MIBOR-Linked Short-term Papers, timing of LAF.
