Kotak Mahindra Bank Ltd. :
Monitoring FIIs investment in Indian Companies
The Reserve Bank of India has notified on March 17, 2005 that Foreign
Institutional Investors (FIIs) can now purchase under Portfolio Investment
Scheme (PIS) equity shares and convertible debentures of Kotak Mahindra
Bank Ltd.  through primary/secondary markets in India upto 30 per cent limits
of its paid up capital, as the company has passed resolution to this effect at its
Board of Directors' and a Special Resolution in its General Body Meeting.
