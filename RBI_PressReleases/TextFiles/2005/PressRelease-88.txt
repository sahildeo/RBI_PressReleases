Marksans Pharma Ltd.,
Monitoring FII Investments
The Reserve Bank of India has notified on December 14, 2005 that the
paid up capital of M/s. Marksans Pharma Ltd., formerly known as Tasc
Pharma Ltd., has increased due to its amalgamation with M/s. Glenmark
Laboratories Ltd. The Reserve Bank has further notified that it would be in
order to purchase the shares of the company in the secondary market on
behalf of Foreign Institutional Investors (FIIs) under Portfolio Investment
Scheme (PIS) subject to the existing ceilings and guidelines.
