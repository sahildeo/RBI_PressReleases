Reserve Bank permits trading in Power Bonds
maturing on October 1, 2009 and April 1, 2010
The Reserve Bank of India has permitted transfer of/trading in the Power
Bonds maturing on October 1, 2009 and April 1, 2010, issued by various States
to Central Public Sector Undertakings (CPSUs) in terms of Tripartite Agreement
among the 27 State Governments, Ministry of Power, Government of India and
Reserve Bank, under one time settlement of dues of State Electricity Boards. It
may be recalled that as provided in the Tripartite Agreement, these bonds would
be released for trading in a phased manner. Two bonds maturing on October 1,
2008 and April 1, 2009 are tradable since January 1, 2005.
