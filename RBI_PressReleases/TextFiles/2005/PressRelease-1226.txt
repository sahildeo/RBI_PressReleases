Reserve Bank cancels the certificate of registration
The Reserve Bank of India, in exercise of the powers conferred on it under
Section 45 IA of the Reserve Bank of India Act, 1934, has cancelled the
certificate of registration issued to the following companies for carrying on the
business of a Non-Banking Financial Institution:
Name of the Company Cancelled on
1. Twin Cities Investments & Finances Limited,
SBI Staff Colony, New Bakaram, Hyderabad-500 080 March 2, 2005
2. Parasnath Fin-Sec Pvt. Ltd., 78, Jai Jawan Colony,
Scheme No.3, Tonk Road, Jaipur, Rajasthan March 4, 2005
3. Sri Ramalingeshwara Leasing & Autofin Pvt. Ltd.,
Door No.5-7-72, Opposite Congress Office,
Prakasham Bazar, Nalgonda-508 001 March 2, 2005
As such, the above companies cannot transact the business of a non-
banking financial institution as defined in Clause (a) of Section 45 I of the
Reserve Bank of India Act, 1934.
