Monitoring of Investments by FIIs/NRIs under PIS
       The Reserve Bank of India has notified on December 22, 2005 that the
aggregate foreign share holdings in the undernoted banks have gone down
below the limit shown against their names. The Reserve Bank has further
stated that it would be in order to purchase the shares of these banks through
stock exchanges on behalf of Foreign Institutional Investors (FIIs) and Non-
Resident Indians (NRIs) under Portfolio Investment Scheme (PIS) after
obtaining its prior approval.
1. Bank of Baroda : 20 per cent
2. ICICI Bank : 74 per cent
