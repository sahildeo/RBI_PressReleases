December 22, 2005
Monitoring of foreign investments in Indian Companies
The Reserve Bank of India has notified on December 22, 2005 that
Non-Residents Indians (NRIs) can now purchase under Portfolio Investment
Scheme (PIS) equity shares and convertible debentures of the following
companies through stock exchanges in India upto 24 per cent of their paid up
capital, as these companies have passed resolutions to this effect at their
General Body Meetings.
1. K. Sera Sera Productions Ltd.
2. Shanti Gears Ltd.
