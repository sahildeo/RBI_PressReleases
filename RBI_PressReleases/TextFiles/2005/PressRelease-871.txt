June 17, 2005
RBI to remain closed for Public on
 July 1, 2005 for account closing
The Reserve Bank of India has decided that beginning this year for its
annual closing of accounts, it will remain closed for public transactions on July
1, 2005 or the first working day of July.  So far, for closing its accounts, the
Reserve Bank of India remained closed for public transactions on June 30 or
the last working day of June every year.
