Television Eighteen India Ltd.:
Investment by FIIs/NRIs/PIOs under PIS
       The Reserve Bank of India has notified on July 15, 2005 that the holding of
Foreign Institutional Investors (FIIs)/Non-Resident Indians (NRIs)/Persons of
Indian Origin (PIOs) under the portfolio investment scheme (PIS) in the paid up
capital of the Television Eighteen India Ltd., has gone below the limit of 24 per
cent of its paid up capital. The Reserve Bank has stated that purchase of equity
shares of Television Eighteen India Ltd., through secondary markets in India on
behalf of such FIIs/NRIs/PIOs, who have obtained its permission to invest under
PIS is in order.
