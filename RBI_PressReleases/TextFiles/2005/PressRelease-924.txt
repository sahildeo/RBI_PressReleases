Oriental Bank of Commerce: Investment by FIIs/NRIs/ PIOs under PIS
       The Reserve Bank of India on June 6, 2005 has notified that no further
purchases of equity shares of Oriental Bank of Commerce should be made on
behalf of Foreign Institutional Investors ( FIIs)/Non-Resident Indians ( NRIs)
and Persons of Indian Origin (PIOs) through stock exchanges in India. The
Reserve Bank has stated that Oriental Bank of Commerce has reached the
overall limit of 20 per cent of its paid up capital.
