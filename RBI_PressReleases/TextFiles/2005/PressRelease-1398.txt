February 5, 2005
Grasim Industries Ltd.: Investment by FIIs under PIS
       The Reserve Bank of India on February 5, 2005 has notified that no
further purchases of equity shares of Grasim Industries Ltd., should be made
on behalf of Foreign Institutional Investors (FIIs) through stock exchanges in
India. The Reserve Bank has stated that Grasim India Ltd., has reached the
trigger limit of 24 per cent of its paid up capital.
