Jain Irrigation Systems Ltd.: Investment by FIIs under PIS
       The Reserve Bank of India on August 5, 2005 has notified that no further
purchases of equity shares of Jain Irrigation Systems Ltd., under Portfolio
Investment Scheme (PIS) should be made on behalf of Foreign Institutional
Investors (FIIs) through stock exchanges in India. The Reserve Bank has
stated that Jain Irrigation Systems Ltd., has reached the overall limit of 24 per
cent of its paid up capital.
