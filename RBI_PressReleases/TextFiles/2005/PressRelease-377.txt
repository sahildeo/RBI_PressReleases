M/s. Shanti Gears Ltd.: Investment by NRIs/PIOs under PIS
       The Reserve Bank of India on October 6, 2005 has notified that no further
purchases of equity shares of M/s. Shanti Gears Ltd., should be made on
behalf of Non-Resident Indians(NRIs)/Persons of Indian Origin (PIOs) through
secondary markets in India without the prior permission of the Reserve Bank.
The Reserve Bank has stated that M/s. Shanti Gears Ltd., has reached the
limit of 8 per cent of its paid up capital.
