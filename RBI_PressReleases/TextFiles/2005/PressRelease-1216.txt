March 24, 2005
Centurion Bank Ltd.: Investment by FIIs under PIS
       The Reserve Bank of India on March 24, 2005 has notified that no further
purchases of equity shares of Centurion Bank Ltd., should be made on behalf
of Foreign Institutional Investors ( FIIs)/NRIs and PIOs through stock
exchanges in India. The Reserve Bank has stated that Centurion Bank Ltd.,
has reached the overall limit of 74 per cent of its paid up capital.
