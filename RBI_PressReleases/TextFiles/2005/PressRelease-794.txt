Underwriting by Primary Dealers
The Reserve Bank of India has set the cut-off rate for underwriting bids
by Primary Dealers for the auctions of the "7.27 per cent Government Stock,
2013" and 10.25 per cent Government Stock, 2021 to be held on July 5, 2005
at Re.0.12 (Twelve paise) and Re.0.23 (twenty three paise) per Rupees 100
respectively.
