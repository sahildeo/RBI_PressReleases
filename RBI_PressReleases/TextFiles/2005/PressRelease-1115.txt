Punjab National Bank: Investment by FIIs/NRIs/ POIs under PIS
       The Reserve Bank of India on April 20, 2005 has notified that no further
purchases of equity shares of Punjab National Bank should be made on
behalf of Foreign Institutional Investors ( FIIs)/Non-Resident Indians ( NRIs)
and Persons of Indian Origin (POIs) through stock exchanges in India. The
Reserve Bank has stated that Punjab National Bank has reached the overall
limit of 20 per cent of its paid up capital.
