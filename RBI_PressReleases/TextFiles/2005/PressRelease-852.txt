Underwriting by Primary Dealers
The Reserve Bank of India has set the cut-off rate for underwriting bids
by Primary Dealers for the auction of the "7.49 per cent Government Stock,
2017" to be held on June 23, 2005 at Re.0.0395. (three point nine five paise)
per Rupees 100.
