February 2, 2005
Government of India announce the sale (re-issue) of a
dated security through price based auction
The Government of India have announced the sale (re-issue) of “8.35 percent
Government Stock 2022” for a notified amount of Rs.5,000 crore . The Government
Stock will be sold through price based auction using multiple price auction method.
The auction will be conducted by the Reserve Bank of India (RBI), Mumbai Office,
Fort, Mumbai on Monday, February 7, 2005.
Up to 5% of the notified amount of the sale of the stock will be allotted to
eligible individuals and institutions as per the Scheme for Non-Competitive Bidding
Facility in the Auctions of Government Securities.
Bids in the prescribed form obtainable from the Regional Director, Reserve
Bank of India, Mumbai Office (Public Debt Office), Fort, Mumbai-400 001 and the
Reserve Bank of India web site www.rbi.org.in should be submitted to that Office on
Monday, February 7, 2005. The competitive bids by the NDS members should be
submitted only in electronic format using NDS, while all the non-competitive bids
should be submitted in the prescribed physical form. All bids should be submitted by
12.30 p.m.. On the basis of bids received, RBI will determine the minimum price up
to which offers will be accepted.
. The result of the auction will be announced on February 7, 2005 and payment
by successful bidders will be during banking hours on February 8, 2005 (Tuesday).
The stock will qualify for the ready forward facility.
 Bids for underwriting of the Government Security under auction can be
submitted by the ‘Primary Dealers’ up to 12.30 p.m. on February 5, 2005 (Saturday)
to the Chief General Manager-in-Charge, Internal Debt Management Department,
Reserve Bank of India, Central Office, Fort, Mumbai 400 001.
