September 16, 2005
Reserve Bank rejects application for certificate of
Registration of Belani Finance Limited, Kolkata
The Reserve Bank of India, has on September 1, 2005 rejected the application
for certificate of registration submitted by Belani Finance Limited having its office at
th
"India House", 9 floor, 69, Ganesh Chandra Avenue, Kolkata – 700 013.
Following rejection of application for certificate of registration, Belani Finance
Limited can not transact the business of a non-banking financial institution.
The Reserve Bank of India has taken this action under powers conferred on it by
Clause (a) wof Section 45-I of the Reserve Bank of India Act, 1934.
