February 15, 2005
SB & T International Ltd.: Investment by FIIs under PIS
       The Reserve Bank of India on February 15, 2005 has notified that no
further purchases of equity shares of SB & T International Ltd., should be
made on behalf of Foreign Institutional Investors (FIIs) through stock
exchanges in India. The Reserve Bank has stated that SB & T International
Ltd., has reached the trigger limit of 24 per cent of its paid up capital.
