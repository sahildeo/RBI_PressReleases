Secondary market transactions in
Government Securities
In view of severe disruptions in financial markets due to incessant rainfall
in Mumbai, there will be no secondary market transactions in Government
Securities today, i.e., July 27, 2005. All secondary market transactions scheduled
for settlement today will accordingly settle tomorrow i.e. July 28, 2005.
