September 6, 2005
Tap Sale of State Government Loans
Eleven State Governments viz; Arunachal Pradesh, Haryana, Himachal Pradesh,
Jammu & Kashmir, Madhya Pradesh, Meghalaya, Mizoram, Nagaland, Sikkim, Uttar
Pradesh and West Bengal have offered to sell 7.53 per cent State Development Loan,
2015 having maturity of ten years aggregating around Rs. 2900 crore on
September 13, 2005 by way of tap sale under the normal market borrowing programme.
The sale will be held at Mumbai for all the 11 States as also at the respective
Regional Offices of the Reserve Bank of India. The Reserve Bank of India reserves the
right to close the sale during or after close of banking hours on or after September 13,
2005 having regard to the subscriptions received. The payment by the successful
applicants will be made on September 15, 2005.
Applications in the prescribed form obtainable from the Public Debt Offices at the
Reserve Bank of India will be received at the aforesaid Offices during banking hours
while the sale is open.
