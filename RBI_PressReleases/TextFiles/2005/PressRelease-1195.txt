March 30, 2005
Receipt of tax for the financial year 2004-05
The Reserve Bank of India offices at Mumbai and Navi Mumbai have
made arrangements for the convenience of taxpayers to receive cash deposits
representing taxes at the counters upto 3.30 p.m. on March 31, 2005.
Cheques/instruments representing payment of taxes to Government would be
received upto 6.30 p.m. on March 31, 2005. Instructions have also been issued
to the agency banks, viz., State Bank of India and its associates, public sector
banks and designated private sector banks to receive taxes beyond normal
banking hours on March 31, 2005. The Reserve Bank has advised taxpayers to
take advantage of these facilities.
