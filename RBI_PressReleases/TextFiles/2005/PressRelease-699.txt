Vaibhav Gems Ltd – Investment by FIIs
The Reserve Bank of India on July 25, 2005 has notified that no further
purchases of equity shares of Vaibhav Gems Ltd., under Portfolio Investment
Scheme (PIS) should be made on behalf of Foreign Institutional Investors (FIIs)
through stock exchanges in India. The Reserve Bank has stated that Vaibhav
Gems Ltd., has reached the overall limit of 24 per cent of its paid up equity
capital.
