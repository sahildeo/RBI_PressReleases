M/s. Max India Ltd. : Monitoring of investments by NRIs/POIs
under PIS
The Reserve Bank of India has notified on April 6, 2005 that Non-
Resident Indians (NRIs) and Persons of Indian Origin (POIs) can now
purchase equity shares and convertible debentures of M/s. Max India Ltd.
upto 24 per cent of its paid up capital through stock exchanges in India, as the
company has passed a resolution to this effect at its General Body Meeting.
