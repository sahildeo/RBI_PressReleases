RBI's Technical Advisory Committee on Monetary Policy Meets
The First Meeting of the Technical Advisory Committee on Monetary
Policy was held today in the Reserve Bank of India, Central Office, Mumbai. The
meeting was chaired by Dr.Y.V.Reddy, Governor.
The Committee reviewed the macroeconomic developments in the context
of the forthcoming first quarterly review of the Annual Policy Statement for the
year 2005-06, scheduled for July 26, 2005.
It may be recalled that with a view to further strengthening the consultative
process in monetary policy, the Reserve Bank of India had set up a Technical
Advisory Committee on Monetary Policy with external experts in the areas of
monetary economics, central banking, financial markets and public finance.
