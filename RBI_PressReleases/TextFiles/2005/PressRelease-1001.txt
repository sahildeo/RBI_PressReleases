Tap sale of 7.77 per cent State Development Loan, 2015
closed for twenty five States
The 'on-tap' sale of 7.77 per cent ten-year State Development Loan,
2015 has closed in respect of twenty five States, viz, Andhra Pradesh,
Arunachal Pradesh, Assam, Bihar, Goa, Gujarat, Haryana, Himachal Pradesh,
Jammu & Kashmir, Jharkhand, Karnataka, Kerala, Madhya Pradesh,
Maharashtra, Manipur, Meghalaya, Mizoram, Nagaland, Orissa, Rajasthan,
Sikkim, Tamilnadu, Tripura, Uttaranchal and West Bengal on receipt of the
respective target amounts.  Amounts of allotment in respect of these States
are being advised to the investors concerned.
The on-tap sale in respect of loans for Uttar Pradesh will be kept open
on May 18, 2005 to collect the balance target amount.  Applications in the
prescribed form can be obtained from the Public Debt Office, Reserve Bank of
India, Kanpur and Mumbai Office.
Applications will be received at these offices during banking hours
while the tap sale is open.  The Reserve Bank of India reserves the right to
close the tap sale during the banking hours on May 18, 2005 having regard to
the borrowing requirements of the State Governments and the subscriptions
received.
As notified earlier, payment will be required to be made by the
investors on May 19, 2005 in respect of subscriptions received / allotted both
on May 17 and May 18, 2005.
