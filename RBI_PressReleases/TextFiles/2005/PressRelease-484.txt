Monitoring FIIs investment in Indian Companies
The Reserve Bank of India has notified on September 20, 2005 that
Foreign Institutional Investors (FIIs) can now purchase under Portfolio
Investment Scheme (PIS) equity shares and convertible debentures of
Vaibhav Gems Ltd. through primary markets and stock exchanges in India
upto 60 per cent limits of its paid up capital, as the company has passed
resolutions to this effect at its Board of Directors' and General Body Meetings.
