February 22, 2005
Tap sale of 7.17 per cent State Development Loans 2017
closed for six states
The 'on tap' sale of 7.17 per cent twelve years State Development
Loans, 2017 has closed in respect of six States, i.e. Andhra Pradesh,
Himachal Pradesh, Jammu & Kashmir, Kerala, Orissa and Punjab on receipt
of the respective target amounts.  Amounts of allotment in respect of these
states are being advised to the investors concerned.
The sale on tap in respect of loans for remaining ten States, viz.,
Assam, Bihar, Maharashtra, Manipur, Meghalaya, Nagaland, Sikkim, Tripura,
Uttar Pradesh and West Bengal will be kept open on February 23, 2005 to
collect the balance target amount.  Applications in the prescribed form can be
obtained from the Public Debt Office of the respective offices of the Reserve
Bank of India and Mumbai Office.
Applications will be received at these offices during banking hours
while the tap sale is open.  The Reserve Bank of India reserves the right to
close the tap sale during the banking hours on February 23, 2005 having
regard to the borrowing requirements of the State Governments and the
subscriptions received.
As notified earlier, payment will be required to be made by the
investors on February 24, 2005 in respect of subscriptions received / allotted
both on February 22 and February 23, 2005.
