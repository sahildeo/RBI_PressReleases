August 11, 2005
Reserve Bank transfers surplus profit to Government of India
The Reserve Bank’s Central Board, in its meeting held today, approved
the transfer of surplus profit to the Government of India amounting to Rs.5, 400
crore for the year ended June 30, 2005.
