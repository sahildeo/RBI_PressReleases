March 12, 2005
Max India Ltd.: Investment by FIIs under PIS
       The Reserve Bank of India on March 12, 2005 has notified that no further
purchases of equity shares of Max India Ltd., should be made on behalf of
Foreign Institutional Investors (FIIs) through primary/secondary markets in
India without the prior permission of the Reserve Bank. The Reserve Bank
has stated that Max India Ltd., has reached the trigger limit of 22 per cent of
its paid up capital.
