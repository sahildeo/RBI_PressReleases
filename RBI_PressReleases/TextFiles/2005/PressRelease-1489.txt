January 11, 2005
Public Comments invited on
Report on Revitalisation of the Cooperative Credit Structure
The Reserve Bank of India has today made available on its
website, the Report of the Task Force on Revitalisation of the Cooperative
Credit Structure with a view to inviting public comments. The report is also
available on the website of the Ministry of Finance, Government of
India at ( http://finmin.nic.in/downloads/reports/ReportTFCoopCrins.html)
as also on the website of NABARD at
(http://www.nabard.org/whats/mdtf/mdtfrep.htm)
It may be recalled that the Government of India had, on August 5,
2004, appointed a task force under the chairmanship of Prof. A
Vaidyanathan. To recommend an implementable action plan for reviving
the rural cooperative banking institutions and to suggest for them an
appropriate regulatory framework were among the terms of reference of
the Task Force. The Task Force submitted its draft report to the
Government on January 5, 2005.
Comments may please be emailed to the Reserve Bank of India at
cgmincrpcd@rbi.org.in or be sent to the Chief General Manager-in-charge,
Rural Planning and Credit Department, Reserve Bank of India, Central
Office, Shahid Bhagat Singh Marg, Mumbai 400 001 or may be faxed at
2265 8276. The report will be finalised after three weeks taking into
account the feedback received.
