July 18, 2005
Investment by FIIs under PIS
       The Reserve Bank of India on July 18, 2005 has notified that no further
purchases of equity shares of undernoted companies should be made under
portfolio investment scheme (PIS) on behalf of Foreign Institutional Investors
(FIIs) through primary/secondary markets in India without the prior permission
of the Reserve Bank. The Reserve Bank has stated that these companies
have reached the limit of 22 per cent of their paid up capital.
1. Grasim Industries Ltd.
2. Tata Tea Ltd.
