October 1, 2005
Monitoring of investment limits of FIIs under PIS
       The Reserve Bank of India has notified on October 1, 2005 that the
aggregate foreign share holdings by Foreign Institutional Investors (FIIs) in
Grasim Industries Limited have gone below the limit of 22 per cent of its paid
up capital. The Reserve Bank has stated that it will be in order for FIIs to
purchase the shares of the company in the secondary markets under the
portfolio investment scheme subject to the existing ceilings and guidelines.
