Scheme of Amalgamation under section 44A of Banking Regulation Act,
1949 – Bank of Punjab Ltd. with Centurion Bank Ltd
In exercise of the powers contained in Sub-section (4) of Section 44A of
the Banking Regulation Act, 1949, the Reserve Bank of India sanctioned the
Scheme of Amalgamation of Bank of Punjab Ltd. with Centurion Bank Ltd. The
Scheme shall come into force with effect from October 1, 2005. All the branches
of Bank of Punjab Ltd. shall from October 1, 2005, function as branches of
Centurion Bank Ltd.
