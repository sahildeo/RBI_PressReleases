ING Vysya Bank Ltd.: Investment by FIIs/NRIs/PIOs under PIS
       The Reserve Bank of India has notified on July 19, 2005 that the holding of
Foreign Institutional Investors (FIIs)/Non-Resident Indians (NRIs)/Persons of
Indian Origin (PIOs) under the portfolio investment scheme (PIS) in the paid up
capital of the ING Vysya Bank Ltd., has gone below the limit of 72 per cent of its
paid up capital. The Reserve Bank has stated that further purchase of equity
shares of ING Vysya Bank Ltd., on behalf of such FIIs/NRIs/PIOs who have
obtained permission to invest under PIS through secondary market in India is in
order.
