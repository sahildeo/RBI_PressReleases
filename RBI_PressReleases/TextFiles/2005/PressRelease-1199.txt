The issuance of Treasury Bills covered by this Calendar will be in
addition to the issuances of Treasury Bills as given in the indicative Schedule
for Issuance of Treasury Bills/Bonds under Market Stabilisation Scheme
(MSS) released on March 28, 2005 by the Reserve Bank.
The Reserve Bank of India retains the right to vary the amount and
timing, if emerging circumstances warrant, without assigning any reason. The
calendar is thus subject to change, if circumstances so warrant, including for
reasons such as intervening holidays.  Such changes, if any, will be
communicated through regular press releases.
The auction of treasury bills will be subject to the terms and conditions
specified in the General Notification No. F 2(12)- W&M/97, dated March 31,
1998 issued by Government of India, as amended from time to time.
