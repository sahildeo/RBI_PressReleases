February 18, 2005
Reserve Bank Cancels Certificate of Registration of
Vinayaka Sai Finvest  Ltd., Hyderabad
The Reserve Bank of India, has on February 14, 2005 cancelled the certificate of
registration issued to Vinayaka Sai Finvest Limited, having its registered office at 5-5-61,
Khaleelwadi, Nizamabad-503 003 and 2-5-43 Jawahar Road, Nizamabad and 1-607,
Divyashakti Apartments, Ameerpet, Hyderabad-500 016 for carrying on the business of a
non-banking financial institution.
Under powers conferred by Section 45IA of the Reserve Bank of India Act, 1934, the
Reserve Bank can cancel the registration certificate of non-banking financial company. The
business of a non-banking financial institution is defined in clause (a) of Section 45IA of the
Reserve Bank of India Act, 1934.
