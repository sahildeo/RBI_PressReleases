May 2, 2005
Underwriting by Primary Dealers
The Reserve Bank of India has set the cut-off rate for underwriting bids
by Primary Dealers for the auctions of the "7.55 per cent Government Stock,
2010" and 7.50 per cent Government Stock, 2034 to be held on May 3, 2005
at Re.0.13 (Thirteen paise) and Re.0.24 (twenty four paise) per Rupees 100
respectively.
