September 22, 2005
 Six State Governments announce the auction of
State Development Loans 2015
The following State Governments have announced the sale of 10-year State
Development Loans for an aggregate amount of Rs.839.420 crore through a yield
based auction using multiple price auction method.
Sr. State Notified Amount
No. (Rs. in Crore)
1 Assam 208.839
2 Jharkhand 117.812
3 Kerala 145.818
4 Maharashtra 290.000
5 Manipur 36.951
6 Tripura 40.000
Total 839.420
The auction will be conducted by the Reserve Bank of India (RBI) at Mumbai on
September 27, 2005 (Tuesday). Bids in the prescribed form obtainable from the
Regional Director, Reserve Bank of India, Mumbai office (Public Debt Office), Fort,
Mumbai-400 001 should be submitted to that office on Tuesday, September 27, 2005.
The competitive bids by the NDS members should be submitted only in electronic
format using NDS. All bids should be submitted by 12.30 p.m. On the basis of bids
received, the Reserve Bank will determine the maximum yield at which offers will be
accepted.
 The results of the auction will be announced on September 27, 2005 and
payment by successful bidders will be during banking hours on September 28, 2005
(Wednesday) at Mumbai and at respective Regional Offices of the Reserve Bank of
India. The stocks will qualify for the ready forward facility.
