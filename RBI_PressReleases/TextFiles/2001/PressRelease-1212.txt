New Banking Ombudsman at 
May 5, 2001
The Reser ve Bank of India has appointed Shri G.P.Muniappan, former Executive
Director, Reserve Bank of India as Banking Ombudsman at Thiruvananthpuram from
April  26,  2001.
As Banking Ombudsman, Shri G.P.Muniappan would have his office at Reserve
Bank of India Building, Bakery Junction, Thiruvananthpuram-695033 and his jurisdiction
and authority would extend to complaints against branches and offices of banks located
Lakshadweep.
