The Reserve Bank of India has reiterated that non-banking non-financial
companies need not submit the return of deposits to the Reserve Bank. There is,
however, no change in the instructions in relation to non-banking financial
It may be recalled that in terms of the provisions contained in the Companies
Act, 1956, the non-banking non-financial companies are required to submit the return
on deposits mobilised by them to the Registrar of Companies. Copies of these returns
were also being received in the Reserve Bank for statistical purposes. The Reserve
Bank of India discontinued the system of receiving copies of return on deposits from
the non-banking non-financial companies from March 31, 1999.
