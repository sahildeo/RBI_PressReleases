Government of India announces the sale (re-issue) of "9.85 percent Government
Stock, 2015"   through price based auction.
November 15, 2001
The Government of India have announced sale (re-issue) by auction of “9.85
percent Government Stock, 2015” for an aggregate amount of Rs 4,000 crore (nominal).
The auction will be price based and will be conducted by the Reserve Bank of
India (RBI) at Mumbai Office on Monday, November 19, 2001. Bids in prescribed form
obtainable from the Regional Director, RBI, Public Debt Office, Fort, Mumbai should be
submitted  at that Office, on November 19, 2001 during the banking hours. The auction
will be conducted on "Multiple- Price basis". On the basis of the bids received, RBI will
determine the minimum price up to which tenders for purchase of Government Stock will
be accepted at the auction. Bids quoted at rates lower than the minimum price determined
by the Reserve Bank of India will be rejected. The results of the auction will be
announced on November 19, 2001 and payment by successful bidders will be during the
banking hours on November 20, 2001.
Bids for underwriting of the Government Security under auction can be submitted
by the ‘Primary Dealers in the Government Securities Market’ up to 12.30 p.m. on
Saturday, November 17, 2001 to the Chief General Manager, Internal Debt Management
Cell, Reserve Bank of India, Central Office , Fort, Mumbai 400 001.
Alpana Killawala
General Manager
