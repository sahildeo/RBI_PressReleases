Tap Sale of Maharashtra Development Loan, 2011 closed
December 20, 2001
The sale of 8.37 per cent Maharashtra Development Loan, 2011 which opened for
subscription on December 20, 2001 on tap at the Reserve Bank of India, Mumbai was closed at
11.50 hours today after receipt of approximately Rs.141.20 crore.
P.V. Sadanandan
Asst. Manager
