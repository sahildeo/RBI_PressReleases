May 11, 2001
The Reserve Bank of India has today notified that it has withdrawn the restriction placed
on the purchase of shares of ICICI Bank Ltd. on behalf of Foreign Institutional Investors
( FIIs).  The restriction has been withdrawn as the aggregate holdings of FIIs in ICICI
Bank Ltd. have gone below 22 per cent of its paid up capital.  This has happened as the
capital of ICICI Bank Ltd. has increased on account of the merger of Bank of
