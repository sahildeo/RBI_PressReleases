FII Investments in Indian companies to go by sectoral caps
September 20, 2001
It has been decided in consultation with the Government of India that Foreign Institutional
Investors (FII) investments in Indian companies can now be increased beyond 24 per cent
upto the sectoral cap/statutory ceiling, as applicable, provided this has the approval of the
Indian company's board of directors as also its general body.
Full notification No.FEMA 45/2001-RB dated September 20, 2001 issued by the Reserve
Bank of India is available on RBI site at www.rbi.org.in or at www.fema.rbi.org.in
Alpana Killawala
