RBI to function normally on September 29, 2001
September 27, 2001
The Reserve Bank of India has stated that its offices in Mumbai and Navi
Mumbai will conduct normal business on Saturday, September 29, 2001. Commercial
banks and co-operative banks will remain closed on that day on account of half-yearly
closing of their accounts. There will, however, be no clearing on that day and settlement
of MICR cheques presented on September 28 will be done on Monday, October 1, 2001.
Alpana Killawala
General Manager
