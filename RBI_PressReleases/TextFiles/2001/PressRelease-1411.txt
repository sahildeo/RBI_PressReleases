IFCI to service its SLR Bonds
March 29, 2001
The Reserve Bank of India has stated that with effect from May 1, 2001 the IFCI
Ltd., will take over the servicing of IFCI Bonds (SLR).  As such, payment of interest,
redemption proceeds as also other servicing such as splitting , consolidation of bonds will
be handled by IFCI Limited.  Consequently, the servicing of these bonds by Public Debt
Offices of the Reserve Bank of India will be discontinued from May 1, 2001.
The Reserve Bank of India has requested all the bond hold ers/investors to
approach the concerned office of IFCI for servicing of all the IFCI Bonds (SLR) from
May 1, 2001.  The addresses and telephone numbers of RBI's Public Debt Offices and
corresponding IFCI offices are available on the RBI
