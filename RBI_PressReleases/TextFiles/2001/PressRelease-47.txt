Investments by Foreign Institutional Investors
December 20, 2001
The Reserve Bank of India on December 20, 2001 has notified that foreign
institutional investors (FIIs) can now purchase equity shares and convertible debentures
of the following companies up to 49 per cent of their paid up capital through primary and
secondary markets in India, as these companies have passed  resolutions at their Board of
Directors and General Body Meetings to this effect.
1. Blue Dart Express Ltd.
2. Panacea Biotec Ltd.
