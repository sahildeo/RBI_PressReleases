9.45 per cent State Development Loans, 2011 oversubscribed
October 11, 2001
The 9.45 per cent ten year State Development Loans in respect of twenty-two
State Governments opened for subscription for an aggregate notified amount of
Rs.2,759.42 crore on October 10, 2001. The subscriptions received aggregated
Rs.3279.63 crore. Some subscriptions received at designated branches of State Bank of
India/Associate Banks at District headquarters of the States are still being reported. As
the loans of seven States viz. Andhra Pradesh, Goa, Gujarat, Haryana, Himachal Pradesh,
Tamilnadu and West Bengal were oversubscribed, partial allotments ranging from 38.83
per cent to 75.50 per cent have been made in respect of these States. Excess subscription
amounting to about Rs.483 crore is being refunded to the applicants today.
