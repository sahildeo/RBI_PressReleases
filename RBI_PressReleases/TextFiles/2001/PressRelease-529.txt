RBI adds one more Institution as non-competitive bidder
for 364 day Treasury Bill Auction
September 14, 2001
It has been decided to permit `Custodian of Enemy Property for India'  to participate
in the auctions of 364 day Treasury Bills on a non-competitive basis. Presently, State
Governments are permitted to bid on a non-competitive basis in the auctions of 364-day
Treasury Bills conducted by the Reserve Bank of India.
Alpana Killawala
General Manager
