The Reserve Bank of India on July 26, 2001 has notified that Foreign
Institutional Investors ( FIIs) can now purchase equity shares and convertible
debentures of NIIT Limited up to 49 per cent of its  paid up capital through primary
and secondary markets in India under Portfolio Investment Scheme. The company has
passed  resolution at its Board of Directors' and General Body Meetings for purchase
