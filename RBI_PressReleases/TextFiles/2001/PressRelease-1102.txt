Auction 
May 25, 2001
Government of India have announced sale by auction of a new 12-year
Government Stock for Rs 3,000 crore (nominal) and of a new 20-year Government Stock
for Rs 2,000 
The auctions will be yield based and will be conducted by the Reserve Bank of
India (RBI) at Mumbai on Tuesday, May 29, 2001. Bids in the prescribed form
obtainable from the Regional Director, Reserve Bank of India, Public Debt Office , Fort,
Mumbai should be submitted to that office on May 29, 2001 during banking hours. On
the basis of bids received, Reserve Bank of India will determine in each auction, the
maximum rate of yield at which offers will be accepted. The results of the auctions will
be announced on May 29, 2001 and payment by successful bidders will be during
banking hours on May 30, 2001. The new Stocks will qualify for the Ready Forward
Bids for underwriting the issue of Government Security under auction can be
submitted by the ‘Primary Dealers in the Government Securities Market’ up to 2.30 p.m.
on May 28, 2001 to the CGM-In-Charge, Internal Debt Management Cell, Reserve Bank
of India, Central Office, Fort,
