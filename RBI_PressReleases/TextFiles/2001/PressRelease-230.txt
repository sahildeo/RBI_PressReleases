91 Day Treasury Bills - Auction Announced
November 10, 2001
      The Reserve Bank of India has announced the auction of 91 day Government of India
treasury bills for a notified amount of Rs.250 crore. The Auction will be conducted using
"Uniform Price Auction" method. The allocation to non-competitive bidders will be outside
the notified amount, at the discretion of the Bank. The sale will be subject to terms and
conditions specified in the General Notification No.F.2(12)-W&M/97 dated 31 March 1998
issued by Government of India.
Tenders should be submitted in the prescribed form on Tuesday, November 13, 2001
(Wednesday, November 14, 2001 being a holiday) before 1430 hours.  The results will be
announced the same evening.  Payment by successful bidders will be on Saturday, November
17, 2001 as Friday, November 16, 2001 is a holiday.
N. L. Rao
Asst. Manager
