application for certificate of registration submitted by the M/s. Shariah Islamic Finance &
Investment Co. (I) Ltd., 7-B/244, Shariah House, petwala Compound, Maulana 
Road,

financial institution as defined in Clause (a) of Section 45 IA of the Reserve Bank 
India Act, 1934. The company has also been prohibited from acceptance of deposits
under Section 45MB(1) and prohibited from alienation of assets under Section 45MB(2)
