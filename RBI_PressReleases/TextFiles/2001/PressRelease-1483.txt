  Co-operative Bank Ltd., Burdwan, West Bengal to carry on  banking
busines in India.  The bank is precluded from transacting the business of ‘banking’ as
defined in the Banking Regulation Act, 1949 (as applicable to Co-operative Societies).
Thus, it will not be in order for it, among other things, to accept deposits from the public
