Three State Government to raise funds
from market on December 14, 2001
Three State Governments have announced the sale of 10-year stocks to the public
by auction for an aggregate amount of Rs.123.17 crore as detailed below :
Name of State Amount Rs. crore
Arunachal Pradesh 5.17
Jammu & Kashmir 75.00
Madhya Pradesh 43.00
----------
123.17
----------
The auction will be conducted by Mumbai (Fort) office of the Reserve Bank of India on
Friday, December 14, 2001.  Tenders should be submitted in the prescribed form on
Friday, December 14, 2001 before 14.30 hrs.  Results will be announced the same
evening.  Payment by successful bidders will be on Tuesday, December 18, 2001.
