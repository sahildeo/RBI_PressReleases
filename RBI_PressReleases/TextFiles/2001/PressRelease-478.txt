RBI announces Guidelines on Margin Trading
September 22, 2001
The Reserve Bank of India has today issued detailed guidelines to banks on
margin trading. The guidelines are available on RBI web site http://www. rbi. org. in
    Earlier, on September 18, 2001 the Reserve Bank of India had announced its
decision to permit banks to finance margin trading.  It had indicated at that time that it
would issue detailed guidelines separately.
