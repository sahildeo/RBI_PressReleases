Investment by NRIs/OCBs/PIOs
In shares of Indian companies
October 23, 2001
The Reserve Bank of India has notified on October 23, 2001, that non-resident
Indians (NRIs) / overseas corporate bodies (OCBs) and persons of Indian origin (PIOs)
can now purchase in the secondary market equity shares of the following companies upto
8 per cent of their paid up capital under the portfolio investment scheme, as the holdings
of NRIs / OCBs / and PIOs have gone down below 8 per cent.
1. Mac Charles (India) Ltd.
2. Nalco Chemicals India Ltd.
