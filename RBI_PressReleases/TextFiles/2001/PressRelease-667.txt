Kerala State Government Loan on tap
August 18, 2001
Government of Kerala have offered to sell on tap 9.56 per cent State Development Loan,
2011 having a maturity of ten years. The sale will be held at the offices of the Reserve Bank
of India, Thiruvananthapuram and Mumbai from August 23, 2001. The Reserve Bank of
India reserves the right to close the sale during or after the close of banking hours on or after
August 23, 2001 having regard to the borrowing requirement of the State Government and
the subscriptions received. Applications in the prescribed form obtainable from the Public
Debt Office of the Reserve Bank of India will be received at the Thiruvananthapuram and
