March 7, 2001
The Reserve Bank of India has extended the relaxation on the limits of Ways and Means
advances and the terms of Overdraft regulations for a further period of one month till end
of March 2001 to enable the Government of Gujarat to meet the expenditure on relief and
rehabilitation measures.  It may be recalled that earlier, in view of the unprecedented
nature of the calamity in the State of Gujarat, the Reserve Bank of India had completely
relaxed the limits for Ways and Means Advances and the terms of Overdraft regulations
