Mid-term Review of Policy on October 22
October 3, 2001
Dr. Bimal Jalan, Governor, Reserve Bank of India will meet the Chief Executives
of major scheduled commercial banks for a Mid-term Review of Monetary and Credit
Policy for the year 2001-2002 on Monday, October 22, 2001.

Assistant Manager
