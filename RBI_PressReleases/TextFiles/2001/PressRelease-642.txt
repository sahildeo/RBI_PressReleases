91 Day Treasury Bills - Auction Announced
      The Reserve Bank of India has announced the auction of 91 day Government of India
treasury bills for a notified amount of Rs.250 crore.  The auction will be conducted using
"Uniform Price Auction" method The allocation to non-competitive bidders will be outside the
specified in the General Notification No.F.2(12)-W&M/97 dated 31 March 1998 issued by
Tenders should be submitted in the prescribed form on Wednesday, August 29, 2001
before 1430 hours.  The results will be announced the same evening.  Payment by successful
bidders will be on Friday, August 31, 2001.
