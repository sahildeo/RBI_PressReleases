Underwriting by Primary Dealers
December 4, 2001
The Reserve Bank of India has announced that the cut-off rate for underwriting by
Primary Dealers for the auction of Government of India Floating Rate Bonds, 2009 and
price based auction of 10.71% Government Stock, 2016 to be held on December 5, 2001
was 1.00 paisa and 0.90 paisa respectively.
