Issue of Rs.500 notes (revised colour scheme) in Mahatma Gandhi Series with
capital letter `C' inset in numbering panels
November 22, 2001
The Reserve Bank of India will shortly be issuing Rs.500 notes (revised colour
scheme)  in Mahatma Gandhi Series with capital letter "C" inset in both numbering
panels bearing the signature of Dr. Bimal Jalan, Governor. Except for the change in inset
letter, the design of Rs.500 notes to be issued now is similar in all respects to those issued
earlier in the revised colour scheme in Mahatma Gandhi Series.  All Rs.500
denomination notes issued  by the Bank in the past will continue to be legal tender.
N. L. Rao
Asst. Manager
