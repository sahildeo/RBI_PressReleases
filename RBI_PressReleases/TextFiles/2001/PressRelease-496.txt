List of Securities for Purchase
through Auction on September 20
September 19, 2001
The Reserve Bank of India has decided to purchase following dated securities through
auction on September 20, 2001 :
1. 11.19% GOI 2005
2. 11.90% GOI 2007
3. 11.30% GOI 2010
4. 11.60% GOI 2020
All SGL and Current Account holders with the Reserve Bank of India at Mumbai will
be eligible to participate in the auction.
Minimum bid amount should be Rs. one crore (face value) and multiples of Rs.one
crore.
Application forms may be obtained from Internal Debt Management Cell, Reserve
Bank of India. 16th Floor, Central Office Building, Mumbai or from Public Debt Office,
Reserve Bank of India, Mumbai Office.
Bids should be tendered at the Reserve Bank of India, Fort, Mumbai up to 12.00 Noon
on September 20, 2001. On the basis of the tenders received, the Reserve Bank of India will
determine the maximum price upto which tenders for purchase of Government securities will
be accepted at the auction. In other words, the auction will be conducted on multiple price
basis. The settlement will be on September 21, 2001.
The Reserve Bank of India has the right to accept or reject any or all the bids either
wholly or partially if deemed fit without assigning any reason.
Alpana Killawala
