RBI gives final approval to two more Primary Dealers
The Reserve Bank of India has granted final approval to Banc of America Securities
(India) Pvt. Ltd. and Standard Chartered – UTI Securities India Pvt. Ltd. to operate as
Primary Dealers in the Government Securities market.
Banc of America Securities (India) Pvt. Ltd. and Standard Chartered – UTI Securities
India Pvt. Ltd. were granted “in principle” approval on March 2, 2001. With the grant of
final approval to these entities the number of primary dealers in the Government
securities market has now gone up to seventeen (17).
