Investment by NRIs/OCBs under PIS
in shares of Indian companies
October 1, 2001
The Reserve Bank of India on October 1, 2001 has notified that non-resident Indian
(NRIs) and overseas corporate bodies (OCBs) can now purchase through stock exchanges
equity shares and convertible debentures of M/s. Garware Shipping Corporation Ltd. upto 24
per cent of its paid up capital under the portfolio investment scheme (PIS), as the company
has passed a resolution to this effect in its general body meeting.
