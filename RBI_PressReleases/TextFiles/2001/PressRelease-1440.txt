March 23, 2001
The Reserve Bank of India has on March 23, 2001 notified that no further
purchases of equity shares of State Bank of India should be made on behalf of Foreign
Institutional Investors ( FIIs)/Non Resident Indians ( NRIs)/Overseas Corporate Bodies
( OCBs)/Persons of Indian Origin ( PIOs) in the primary/ secondary markets without
obtaining prior clearance of the Reserve Bank  of India. The aggregate net purchases of
the equity shares of this bank has reached the limit of 18 per cent of its paid up equity
