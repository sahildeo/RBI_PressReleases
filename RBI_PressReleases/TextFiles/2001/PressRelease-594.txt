certificate of registration submitted by the following non-banking financial companies:

168/A, Cotton Street, 4th Floor, Kolkata-700 007.
3. Radharaman Tie-up Pvt. Ltd., August 30, 2001
168/A, Cotton Street, 4th Floor, Kolkata-700 007.

institutions as defined in Clause (a) of Section 45 IA of the Reserve Bank of  India Act,
1934.
