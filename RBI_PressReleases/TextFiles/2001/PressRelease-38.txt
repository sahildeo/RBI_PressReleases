Licence of Urban Co-operative Bank Ltd. Allahabad,
Uttar Pradesh cancelled
December 21, 2001
The Reserve Bank of India has cancelled the licence of The Urban Co-operative
Bank Ltd., Allahabad, Uttar Pradesh to carry on banking business in India.  The bank is
precluded from transacting the business of `banking' as defined in the Banking
Regulation Act, 1949 (As applicable to Co-operative Societies) and it will not be in order
for it, among other things, to accept deposits from public and allow withdrawal by
cheque, draft, order or otherwise.
P.V. Sadanandan
Asst. Manager
