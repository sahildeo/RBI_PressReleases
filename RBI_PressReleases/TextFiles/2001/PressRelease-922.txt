June 29, 2001
The Reserve Bank of India has on June 29, 2001 notified that it would be in order
to purchase the shares of Dr.  Laboratories Ltd. in the secondary market on behalf
of Foreign Institutional Investors ( ), as the paid up capital of the company has
increased and the aggregate Foreign Institutional Investors holding has gone down below
28 per cent.
