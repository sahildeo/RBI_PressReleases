364 Day Treasury Bills - Auction Announced
June 23, 2001
      The Reserve Bank of India has announced the auction of 364 day Government of
India treasury bills for a notified amount of Rs.750 crore.   State Governments are
allowed to participate as non-competitive bidders and the allocation to non-competitive
bidders will be outside the notified amount, at the discretion of the Bank. The sale will be
subject to terms and conditions specified in the General Notification No.F.2(12)-
Tenders should be submitted in the prescribed form on Wednesday, June 27,
2001, before 1430 hours.  The results will be announced the same evening.  Payment by
successful bidders will be on Friday, June 29, 2001.
