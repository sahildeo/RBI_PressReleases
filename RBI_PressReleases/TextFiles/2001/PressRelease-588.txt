Open Market Operations :
RBI revises Sale List, withdraws one security
September 4, 2001
The Reserve Bank of India withdrew 9.81% 2013 from the Open Market
Operations(OMO) sale window with immediate effect. Sale of 11.75% 2006 continues at
the price of Rs.116.13.
