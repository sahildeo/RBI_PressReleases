Investment by OCBs/ PIOs in Indian Companies
August 1, 2001
The Reserve Bank of India on August 1, 2001 has notified that no further
purchases of shares of M/s. DSQ Biotech Ltd ., should be made on behalf of
PIOs in the secondary markets.
The Reserve Ban k has stated that M/s. DSQ Biotech Ltd. has reached the
overall limit of 10 per cent investment in its paid up equity capital by
PIOs.
