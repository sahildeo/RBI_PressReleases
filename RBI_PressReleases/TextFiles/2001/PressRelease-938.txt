Investment by OCBs/ PIOs in
June 27, 2001
The Reserve Bank of India on June 27, 2001 has notified that Nexus Software
Ltd. has reached the limit of 8% of its paid-up equity capital. No further purchases of
shares of Nexus Software Ltd. should, therefore, be made in the secondary market on
behalf of non-resident Indians ( NRIs)/overseas corporate bodies ( OCBs) and persons of
PIOs).
