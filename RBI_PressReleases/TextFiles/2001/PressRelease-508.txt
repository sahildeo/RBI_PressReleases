Non-Renewal of Authorisation to act as Primary Dealer
September 18, 2001
The Reserve Bank of India has decided not to renew its authorisation to Ceat
Financial Services Ltd. (CFSL) for Primary Dealer business. Accordingly, CFSL ceases to be
a Primary Dealer with immediate effect.
P.V. Sadanandan
Assistant Manager
