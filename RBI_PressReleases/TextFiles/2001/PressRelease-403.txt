RBI rejects Star Venture Private Limited's application
for certificate of registration as NBFC
October 5, 2001
The Reserve Bank of India, in exercise of the powers conferred on it under
Section 45 IA of the Reserve Bank of India Act, 1934, has rejected on September 28,
2001 the application for grant of certificate of registration submitted by M/s Star Venture
Private Limited. The company has its Registered Office at Shantivan-II, G-6 Doctors
Colony, Lohia Nagar, Kankarbagh, Patna - 800020. As such, it cannot transact the
business of a non-banking financial institution as defined in Clause (a) of Section 45 I of
the Reserve Bank of  India Act, 1934.
Further, the Reserve Bank of India, in exercise of the powers conferred on it
under Section 45 MB of the Reserve Bank of India Act, 1934, has, with immediate effct,
(i) prohibited the Company from accepting deposits from any person in any form or by
way of renewal or otherwise, and (ii) directed it not to sell, transfer, create charge or
mortgage or deal in any manner with its properties and assets without prior written
permission of the Reserve Bank  of India, until further orders.
The Reserve Bank of India has also directed the company to advise its depositors
(i) that the Reserve Bank has rejected its application for Certificate of Registration and
(ii) its plan of action for repayment of deposits as per the terms and conditions of the
contract.
