April 27, 2001
The Reserve Bank of India, in exercise of the powers conferred on it under
Section 45 IA of the Reserve Bank of India Act, 1934, has rejected on April  20, 2001,
the application for certificate of registration submitted by M/s. Authentic Finance Pvt.
Ltd. , 16, Subhas Road, 4th Floor, Kolkata -700 001.
As such, the above company cannot transact the business of non-
financial institution as defined in Clause (a) of Section 45 I of the Reserve Bank of  India
Act, 1934.
