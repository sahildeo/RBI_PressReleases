14 Day Treasury Bills - Auction Announced
March 17, 2001
      The Reserve Bank of India has announced the auction of 14 day Government of India
treasury bills for a notified amount of Rs.100 crore. The allocation to non-competitive bidders
will be outside the notified amount, at the discretion of the Bank. The sale will be subject to
terms and conditions specified in the General Notification No.F.2(12)-W&M/97 dated 31 March
Tenders should be submitted in the prescribed form on Fr iday, March 23, 2001 before
1430 hours.  The results will be announced the same evening.  Payment by successful bidders
will be on Tuesday, March 27, 2001.
