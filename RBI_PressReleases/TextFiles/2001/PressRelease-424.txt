RBI rejects licence application of The Daltonganj Central
Co-operative Bank Ltd.
October 3, 2001
The Reserve Bank of India has rejected the licence application of The Daltonganj
Central Co-operative Bank Ltd., Daltonganj (Jharkhand) to carry on  banking business.
The bank is precluded from transacting the business of ‘banking’ as defined in the
Banking Regulation Act, 1949 (as applicable to Co-operative Societies) and it will not be
in order for it, among other things, to accept deposits from the public and allow
withdrawals by cheque, draft, order or otherwise.
