11-Year & 25-year Government stock :
Underwriting Commission for PDs at 15 and 7 paise
September 10, 2001
The Reserve Bank of India has announced cut-off rate for underwriting by
Primary Dealers for the Yield Based Auction of 11-Year and 25- Year Government Stock
to be held on September 10, 2001 was 15.00 and 7.00 paise respectively.
P.V. Sadanandan
Assistant Manager
