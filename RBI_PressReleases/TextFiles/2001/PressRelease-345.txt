Lists of Suit filed Accounts as on March 31, 2001
October 16, 2001
The Reserve Bank of India has released the lists of suit filed accounts of (a) Rs.1 crore
and above and (b) willful defaulters of Rs.25 lakh and above as on March 31, 2001.
These are available on the Reserve Bank of India website on a special URL:
defaulters.rbi.org.in   These lists in a single booklet form will shortly be available for sale
at the Reserve Bank’s Sales Division at Amar Building, Fort, Mumbai.
It may be recalled that since March 1994, the Reserve Bank has been publishing the list
of suit filed accounts of Rs.1 crore and above every year.
Alpana Killawala
General Manager
