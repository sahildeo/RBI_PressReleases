Amalgamation of Bank of  Ltd. with ICICI Bank Ltd.
March 9, 2001
In exercise of the powers contained in sub- section(4) of Section 44A of the Banking
Regulation Act, 1949, the Reserve Bank of India  sanctioned the Scheme of
Amalgamation of Bank of Madura Ltd. with ICICI Bank Ltd.  The Scheme shall come
into force with effect from March 10, 2001.  All the branches/offices of Bank of 
2001, function as branches/offices of ICICI Bank Ltd.
