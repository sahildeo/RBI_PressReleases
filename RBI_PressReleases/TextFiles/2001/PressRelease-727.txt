August 6, 2001
Government of India have announced sale (re-issue) by auction of “9.81 percent
Government Stock, 2013” for an aggregate amount of Rs 4,000 crore (nominal).  The
auction will be price based and will be conducted by the Reserve Bank of India (RBI) at
Mumbai on Wednesday, August 8, 2001. Bids in the prescribed form obtainable from the
Regional Director, RBI, Public Debt Office, Fort, Mumbai should be submitted to that
Office on August 8, 2001, during the banking hours. On the basis of bids received, RBI
will determine the minimum price at which offers will be accepted.
