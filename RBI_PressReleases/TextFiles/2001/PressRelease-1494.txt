March 14, 2001
The Reserve Bank of India, in exercise of the powers conferred on it under
Section 45 IA of the Reserve Bank of India Act, 1934, has rejected on March 12, 2001
the application for certificate of registration submitted by M/s.  Securities Pvt.
Ltd., 8/1,  Bazar Street,  Building, Room No.20, Calcutta 700 001.
As such, the above company cannot commence the business of a non-banking
financial institution as defined in Clause (a) of Section 45 I of the Reserve Bank of  India
Act, 1934.
