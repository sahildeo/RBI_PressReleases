Reserve Bank of India offices to remain open on December 31, 2001
December 28, 2001
The offices of the Reserve Bank of India in Mumbai and Navi Mumbai will  remain open
on December 31/last working day of December and undertake all public transactions.
Earlier, the Reserve Bank offices used to be closed for public transactions on this day for
the half-yearly closing of its accounts.
P.V. Sadanandan
Asst. Manager
