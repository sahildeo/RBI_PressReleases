Monitoring FIIs investments in Indian companies
November 13, 2001
The Reserve Bank of India has advised that Foreign Institutional Investors (FIIs) can
now purchase equity shares and convertible debentures of M/s. Housing Development
Finance Corporation Ltd. up to 74 per cent. The company has passed resolution at its Board
of Directors ' meeting and  Special Resolution in its General Body meeting agreeing for the
purchase of their equity shares and convertible debentures by FIIs up to 74 per cent of its
paid-up capital through primary/secondary markets in India under the Portfolio Investment
Scheme.
