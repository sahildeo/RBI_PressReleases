Underwriting Commission for PDs
November 20, 2001
The Reserve Bank of India has announced that the cut-off rate for  underwriting by
Primary Dealers for the Auction of Government of India Floating Rate Bonds, 2006 to be
held on November 21, 2001 was 0.50 paisa.
