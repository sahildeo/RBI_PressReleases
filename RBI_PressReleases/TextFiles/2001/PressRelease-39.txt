Tap Sale of Andhra Pradesh and Uttar Pradesh
Development Loan, 2011 closed
December 21, 2001
The sale of 8.37 per cent Andhra Pradesh and Uttar Pradesh Development Loan,
2011 which opened for subscription on December 20, 2001 on tap at the Mumbai,
Hyderabad and Kanpur offices of the Reserve Bank of India was closed today as at the
close of business hours after receipt of Rs.350 crore and Rs.207.35 crore respectively.
P.V. Sadanandan
Asst. Manager
