RBI to open Purchase Window for Government Securities Tomorrow
September 17, 2001
In view of the extraordinary circumstances in the Government securities market, the Reserve
Bank of India has decided to open a purchase window for select government securities on a
daily auction basis. The purchase window will open tomorrow and will last upto Friday,
September 21, 2001.
The details of the securities to be purchased on auction basis in the window tomorrow will be
notified later today.
Alpana Killawala
General Manager
