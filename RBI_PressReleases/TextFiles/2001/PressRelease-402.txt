Rejection of application for certificate of registration
October 5, 2001
The Reserve Bank of India, in exercise of the powers conferred on it under
Section 45 IA of the Reserve Bank of India Act, 1934, has on October 3, 2001  rejected
the application for certificate of registration submitted by  Siel Financial Services Ltd.,
4th floor, Soni Mansion, 12-B, Ratlam Kothi, Indore-452001(M.P.).
As such, the above company cannot transact the business of a non-banking
financial institution as defined in Clause (a) of Section 45 I of the Act.  The company has
also been prohibited from acceptance of deposits under Section 45MB(1) as also from
alienation of assets under Section 45MB(2) of the Act without the prior permission of the
Reserve Bank.
P.V. Sadanandan
Assistant Manager
