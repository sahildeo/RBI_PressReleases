Tap Sale of Kerala Development Loan, 2011 closed
The sale of 8.37 per cent Kerala Development Loan, 2011 which opened for
subscription on December 20, 2001 on tap at the Reserve Bank of India, Mumbai and
Thiruvananthapuram was closed at 11.30 hours today after receipt of approximately
Rs.138.96 crore.

Asst. Manager
