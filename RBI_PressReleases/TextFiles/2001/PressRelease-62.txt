Sale of State Government Loans on tap
December 18, 2001
The Government of Andhra Pradesh, Kerala, Maharashtra and Uttar Pradesh have
offered to sell on tap 8.37 per cent State Development Loan, 2011 having a maturity of
ten years. The sale will be held at the Hyderabad, Thiruvananthapuram, Mumbai and
Kanpur offices of the Reserve Bank of India, on December 20, 2001. The Reserve Bank
of India reserves the right to close the sale during or after the close of banking hours on
or after December 20, 2001 having regard to the borrowing requirement of these State
Governments and the subscriptions received. Applications in the prescribed form
obtainable from the Public Debt Office of these offices of Reserve Bank of India will be
received at the aforesaid offices during office hours while the sale is open.
