Shri N. Sadasivan takes over as Executive Director of RBI
December 10, 2001
Shri N. Sadasivan has today taken over as Executive Director of the Reserve Bank
of India.  Shri Sadasivan will be in-charge exclusively of the Department of Non-Banking
Supervision.  Earlier Shri Sadasivan was Deputy Managing Director of State Bank of
India.
Alpana Killawala
General Manager
