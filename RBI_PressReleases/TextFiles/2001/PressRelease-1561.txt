March 1, 2001
For the convenience of income tax (IT) assessees, arrangements have been made
for receipt of advance income tax at all the computerised branches of all the public sector
banks (approximately 810) in Mumbai and Navi Mumbai, the Reserve Bank of India has
stated in a press release. The designated branches are authorised by the income tax
The Reserve Bank has advised assessees to take advantage of these standing
arrangements made for their convenience.  The Reserve Bank has also requested
assessees in Mumbai and Navi Mumbai that to avoid inconvenience and last minute rush
at the Reserve Bank's counters, they may deposit their income tax dues sufficiently in
advance of the last date.
