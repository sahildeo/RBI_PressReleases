CD of Instructions on Banking Operations
December 26, 2001
The Reserve Bank of India's Department of Banking Operations and
Development has brought out a compact disc containing all the circulars issued by it
since inception to June 30, 2001. While the circulars/instructions issued up to end
December 1995 have been put in a compendium form, the full text of each circular issued
thereafter has been included, as it is. The circulars can be viewed either subject-wise or
date-wise. The disc also includes the master circulars issued by the Department during
2001. A section on frequently asked questions has also been added.
Banks can obtain copies of the compact disc from Information Section of the
Department of Banking Operations and Development, Reserve Bank of India, 4th floor,
Centre 1, World Trade Centre, Mumbai 400 005. The CD has been priced at Rs.350
including postage, payable by either pay order or demand draft favouring `Reserve Bank
of India'.
P.V. Sadanandan
