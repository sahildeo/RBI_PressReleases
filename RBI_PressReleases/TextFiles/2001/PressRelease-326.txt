Investment by FIIs
October 19, 2001
The Reserve Bank of India has on October 19, 2001 notified that Foreign
Institutional Investors (FIIs) can now purchase equity shares and convertible debentures
of Dr. Reddy's Laboratories Ltd., upto 49 per cent of its issued and paid up capital instead
of the earlier 30 per cent through primary/secondary markets in India under portfolio
investment scheme (PIS). The company has passed a resolution to this effect at its Board
of Directors and a special resolution in its General Body meeting.
