Distribution of Coins : RBI reaches out to Public
September 18, 2001
The Reserve Bank of India will on September 20, 2001 make available coins up
to Rs 800 to members of public at Union Bank of India, Karim Building, 56 Mohammed
Ali Road,  Mumbai-400 003. The coins worth Rs 800 will be available in `Potlas' of 100
pieces each of Re 1, Rs.2 and 5.  The facility at this branch will be available to members
of public between 11.30a.m. to 2.30p.m.
The Reserve Bank has extended this special facility as a part of its overall efforts
to mitigate the hardship faced by members of public in obtaining coins.
