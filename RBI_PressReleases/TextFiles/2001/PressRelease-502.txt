New Banking Ombudsman at Thiruvananthapuram
September 19, 2001
The Reserve Bank of India has appointed Shri G. R. Sundaravadivel, as Banking
Ombudsman at Thiruvananthapuram from September 10, 2001. Before taking over as
Banking Ombudsman, Shri Sundaravadivel was the Executive Director of United Bank of
India.
As Banking Ombudsman, Shri Sundaravadivel would have his office at  Reserve
Bank of India Building, Thiruvananthapuram and his jurisdiction and authority would extend
to complaints against branches and offices of banks located within the territorial limits of the
State of Kerala and Union Territory of Lakshadweep.
