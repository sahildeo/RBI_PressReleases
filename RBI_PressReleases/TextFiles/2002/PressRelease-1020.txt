Investment by Foreign Institutional Investors
May 28, 2002
The Reserve Bank of India has today notified that no further purchases of equity shares
of United Breweries Ltd., should be made on behalf of Foreign Institutional Investors
(FIIs) in the primary/secondary markets without the prior permission of the Reserve
Bank.  The Reserve Bank has stated that United Breweries Ltd., has reached the overall
ceiling of 22 per cent of its paid up capital.
P. V. Sadanandan
Manager
