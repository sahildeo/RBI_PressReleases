Investment by FIIs/NRIs/PIOs under PIS
August 3, 2002
The Reserve Bank of India has notified on August 3, 2002 that the holdings of
foreign institutional investors (FIIs)/non-resident Indians (NRIs) and persons of Indian
origin (PIOs) under the portfolio investment scheme (PIS) in  the paid up capital of
Hughes Tele.Com (India) Ltd., have gone below 47 per cent. The Reserve Bank has
further notified that it will be in order for FIIs/NRIs and PIOs to purchase the shares of
the company in the secondary market under the portfolio investment scheme subject to
the existing ceilings and guidelines in this regard.
