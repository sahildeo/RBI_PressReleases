Sale of State Government Loans on tap
January 22, 2002
The Government of 26 States have offered to sell on tap 8.30 per cent State
Development Loan, 2012 having a maturity of ten years. The sale will be held at the
respective Regional Offices of the Reserve Bank of India and at Mumbai (for 26 States), on
January 28, 2002. The Reserve Bank of India reserves the right to close the sale during or
after the close of banking hours on or after January 28, 2002 having regard to the borrowing
requirements of the State Governments and the subscriptions received. Applications in the
prescribed form obtainable from the Public Debt Office of these offices of the Reserve Bank
of India will be received at the aforesaid offices during office hours while the sale is open.
