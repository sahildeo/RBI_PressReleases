Underwriting by Primary Dealers
June 29, 2002
The Reserve Bank of India has set the cut-off rate for underwriting by Primary Dealers
for the Price Based Auctions of 7.55 per cent Government Stock, 2010 and FRB
Government Stock, 2017 to be held on July 1, 2002 at Re.0.02 (two paise) and Re.0.07
(seven paise) respectively.
P.V. Sadanandan
Manager
