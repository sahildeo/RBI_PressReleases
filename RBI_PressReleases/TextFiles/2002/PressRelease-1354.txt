RBI gives final approval to one more Primary Dealer
March 16, 2002
The Reserve Bank of India has granted final approval to BOB Capital Markets
Ltd., to operate as a Primary Dealer in the Government Securities Market. With the
grant of final approval to the aforesaid entity, the number of Primary Dealers in the
Government Securities market has gone up to Eighteen (18).

Manager
