Monitoring FIIs investment in Indian Companies
October 22, 2002
The Reserve Bank of India has notified on October 22, 2002 that Foreign
Institutional Investors (FIIs) can now purchase equity shares and convertible
debentures of the following companies, up to 100 per cent of  their paid up capital
through primary/secondary markets in India, as these companies have passed
resolution to this effect at their Board of Directors' meeting and  special resolution in
their General Body Meeting.
1. Pentamedia Graphics Ltd.
2. Pentasoft Technologies Ltd.
