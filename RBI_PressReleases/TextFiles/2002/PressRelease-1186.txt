 
20 Apr 2002 
 
 
Moratorium on The Benaras State Bank Ltd. extended 
 
The Central Government on the recommendation of the Reserve Bank of India and in partial 
modification of the Government's earlier Notification dated January 22, 2002 extended the period 
of moratorium in respect of The Benaras State Bank Ltd., for a further period up to and inclusive 
of July 21, 2002. The notification has been issued on April 19, 2002.  
Ajit Prasad 
Manager
