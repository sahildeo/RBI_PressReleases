Underwriting by Primary Dealers
August 1, 2002
The Reserve Bank of India has set the cut-off rate for underwriting bids by Primary Dealers for
the Price Based Auctions of 9.39 per cent Government Stock, 2011 and 10.18 per cent
Government Stock, 2026 to be held on August 2, 2002 at Re. 0.02 (two pasie) and Re.0.03 (three
paise) respectively.
P V Sadanandan
Manager
