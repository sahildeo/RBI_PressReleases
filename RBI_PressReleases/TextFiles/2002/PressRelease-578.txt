Monitoring FII Investments in Indian Companies
August 28, 2002
The Reserve Bank of India has notified today that Foreign Institutional Investors
(FIIs) can now purchase equity shares and convertible debentures of Hero Honda Motors
Ltd. up to 40 per cent of its paid-up capital through primary/secondary markets in India,
as the company has passed a resolution to this effect at its Board of Directors' Meeting
and a special resolution in its General Body Meeting.
P.V. Sadanandan
Manager
