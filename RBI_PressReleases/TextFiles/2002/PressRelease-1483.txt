Investment by FIIs/NRIs/PIOs in
shares of Indian companies
February 18, 2002
The Reserve Bank of India on February 18, 2002 has notified that no further
purchases of shares of Bharti Tele Ventures Ltd., should be made on behalf of foreign
institutional investors (FIIs)/ non-resident Indians (NRIs) and persons of Indian origin
(PIOs) under the portfolio investment scheme (PIS) without obtaining prior clearance of
the Reserve Bank.  The Reserve Bank has stated that the net purchases of equity shares of
the company in the secondary markets have reached the limit of 47 per cent of its paid up
equity capital.
Alpana Killawala
General Manager
