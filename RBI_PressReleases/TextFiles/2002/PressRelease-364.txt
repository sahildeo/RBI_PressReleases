Underwriting by Primary Dealers
October 14, 2002
The Reserve Bank of India has set the cut-off rate for underwriting by Primary
Dealers for the Price Based Auction of "7.46% Government Stock, 2017"  to be
auctioned on October 16, 2002 at Re.0.009 (0.90  paisa).
