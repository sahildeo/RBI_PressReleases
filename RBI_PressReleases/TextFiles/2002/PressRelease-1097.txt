364 Day Treasury Bills - Auction Announced
May 11, 2002
      The Reserve Bank of India has announced the auction of 364 day Government
of India treasury bills for a notified amount of Rs.1000 crore. The allocation to non-
competitive bidders will be outside the notified amount, at the discretion of the Bank.
The sale will be subject to the terms and conditions specified in the General
Notification No.F.2(12)-W&M/97 dated 31 March 1998 issued by Government of
India.
Tenders should be submitted in the prescribed form on Wednesday, May 15,
2002 (Wednesday, May 1, 2002 being a holiday), before 1430 hours.  The results will
be announced the same evening.  Payment by successful bidders will be on Friday,
May 17, 2002.
