Underwriting by Primary Dealers
June 26, 2002
The Reserve Bank of India has set the cut-off rate for underwriting by Primary
Dealers for the Yield Based Auctions of 10 Year Government Stock of the following
State Governments to be held on June 27, 2002 as below :
Sr.No. State Government Cut-off Rate for underwriting
1 Gujarat 14 paise (Re.0.14)
2 Maharashtra 19 paise (Re.0.19)
3 Jammu & Kashmir 50 paise (Re.0.50)
