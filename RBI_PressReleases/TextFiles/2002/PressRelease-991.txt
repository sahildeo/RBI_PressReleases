RBI announces rate of interest on FRBs 2009
June 3, 2002
The rate of interest on the Floating Rate Bonds, 2009 (FRB, 2009) applicable for
the half year ending December 5, 2002 shall be 6.37 per cent per annum. It may be
recalled that the rate of interest on the FRB, 2009 was set at a mark-up (decided in the
auction) over and above the variable base rate. The variable base rate for payment of
interest shall be the average rate (rounded off up to two decimal places) of the implicit
yields at cut-off prices of the last six auctions of Government of India 364 day Treasury
Bills held up to the commencement of the respective half yearly coupon period. The
mark-up decided in the auction held on December 5, 2001 was (-) 0.01 (minus 0.01). The
base rate based on the average rate of the implicit yields at cut-off prices of the last six
auctions of Government of India 364 day Treasury Bills held up to May 29, 2002 works
out to 6.38 per cent. The coupon rate has been fixed accordingly.
