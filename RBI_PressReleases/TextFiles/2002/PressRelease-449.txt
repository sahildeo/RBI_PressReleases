Investment by FIIs/NRIs/PIOs under PIS
September 25, 2002
       The Reserve Bank of India on September 25, 2002 has notified that no further
purchases of equity shares of Bharti Tele-Ventures Ltd., should be made on behalf of
Foreign Institutional Investors (FIIs)/Non-Resident Indians (NRIs) and Persons of Indian
Origin (PIOs) in the primary/secondary markets without the prior permission of the
Reserve Bank.  The Reserve Bank has stated that Bharti Tele-Ventures Ltd., has reached
the trigger limit of 47 per cent of its paid up capital.
