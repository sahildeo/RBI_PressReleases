 
 
19 Apr 2002 
 
 
Sale of State Government Loans on tap 
 
In partial modification to the earlier Press Release dated April 18, 2002 in connection with the 
sale of State Government Loans on `tap', it may be noted that the State Government of Sikkim 
will not be participating in the `tap' issue to be opened on April 23, 2002. Other terms and 
conditions of the `tap' issue remain unchanged. 
Ajit Prasad 
Manager 
Press Release : 2001-02/1170
