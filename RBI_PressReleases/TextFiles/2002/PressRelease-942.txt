RBI reject  licence application to
the Janata Sahakari Bank Maryadit
June 12, 2002
The Reserve Bank of India has rejected the licence application of the Janata
Sahakari Bank Maryadit, Dewas, Madhya Pradesh to carry on banking business in India.
The bank is precluded from transacting the business of banking as defined in the Banking
Regulation Act, 1949 (As applicable to Co-operative Societies) with effect from March
16, 2002 and it will not be in order for it, among other things, to accept deposits from
public and allow withdrawal by cheque, draft, order or otherwise.
