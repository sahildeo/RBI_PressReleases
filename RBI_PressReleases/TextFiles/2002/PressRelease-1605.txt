Sale of State Government Loans on tap
January 23, 2002
It may be recalled that 26 State Governments have offered to sell on tap 8.30 per cent
State Development Loan, 2012 having a maturity of ten years on January 28, 2002 as
per Bank's Press Release dated January 22, 2002. RBI expects to raise about Rs.3700
crore in this `tap issue' tranche.

Manager
