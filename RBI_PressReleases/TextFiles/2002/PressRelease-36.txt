Tap Issue of State Development Loans, 2012 closed on over-subscription
December 23, 2002
The sale `on tap' of 6.80 per cent ten year State Development Loans 2012 in respect of 22
State Governments received overwhelming response with all the States being oversubscribed.
The tap sale of 6.80 per cent of SDL 2012 was, therefore, closed at 11.30 a.m.
The   issue  opened for subscription  today  for a  target  amount  of  Rs.3340 crore.
As the loans of all the participating States have been oversubscribed, partial
allotments are being made and excess subscriptions will be refunded to the applicants.
P. V. Sadanandan
Manager
