364 day Treasury bills Auction - Enhancement of Notified Amount
March 27, 2002
It has been decided to enhance the notified amount in respect of auctions of 364 day
Treaury Bills from Rs 750 crore to Rs 1000 crore for the auctions to be held during the next
financial year i.e. from April 1, 2002 to March 31, 2003.  The calendar for issue of Treasury
Bills for the next financial year will be as under :
Calendar for Auction of Government of India Treasury Bills
The Reserve Bank of India announces the following calendar of Treasury Bills issuance.
This calendar is valid till March 31, 2003

The Reserve Bank of India retains the right to vary the amount and timing, if
circumstances warrant, without assigning any reason.  The calendar is thus subject to change, if
circumstances so warrant, including for reasons such as intervening holidays.  Such changes, if
any, will be communicated through regular press releases.
The auction of treasury bills will be subject to the terms and conditions specified in the
General Notification No.F 2(12)-W&M/97 dated March 31, 1998 issued by Government of
India.
