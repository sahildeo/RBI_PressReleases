Government Stock 2017 Auction and LAF Timings extended
April 16, 2002
In response to requests received from banks, Primary Dealers and others, who
have been successful in the auction of the 7.49 Government Stock 2017 held on April 15,
2002, it has been decided that allottees can make payment either on April 16, 2002 or on
April 17, 2002.  Allottees will have to pay accrued interest for one day in case they pay
on April 17, 2002.
The Reserve Bank of India also extended the time for repo and reverse repo under
the Liquidity Adjustment facility (LAF) upto 12.30 p.m. for today.
Alpana Killawala
General Manager
