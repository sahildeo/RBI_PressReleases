Underwriting by Primary Dealers
April  3, 2002
The Reserve Bank of India has announced that the cut-off rate for underwriting
by Primary Dealers for the Uniform Price Based Auction of 7-Year and 10-Year
Government Stock to be held on 04th April 2002, was Re 0.0050 (half paise) for both
the issues.
