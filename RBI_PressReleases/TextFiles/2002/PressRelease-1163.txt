Tap sale of 7.80 per cent State Development Loans, 2012 - Closure
April 26, 2002
The sale `on tap' of the 7.80 per cent State Development Loans, 2012 was closed
today in respect of Assam, Bihar, Gujarat, Jammu & Kashmir, Madhya Pradesh, Orissa,
Rajasthan and Uttar Pradesh.
It may be recalled that the `on tap' sale of the 7.80 per cent State Development
Loans, 2012 which opened on April 23, 2002 for 26 States was closed on the same day in
respect of 10 States, viz., Arunachal Pradesh, Chhattisgarh, Goa, Haryana, Jharkhand,
Karnataka, Manipur, Punjab, Tamil Nadu and Uttaranchal on receipt of the respective
target amounts.
The tap sale was closed on the second day i.e. on April 24, 2002 in respect of 8
more States viz., Andhra Pradesh, Himachal Pradesh, Maharashtra, Meghalaya, Mizoram,
Nagaland, Tripura and West Bengal.
Accordingly, the tap issue for all the 26 States which had gone to the market has
been closed as at the close of business hours on April 26, 2002.
