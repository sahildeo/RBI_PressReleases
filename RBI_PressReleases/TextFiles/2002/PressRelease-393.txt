No Plan to introduce Polymer/Plastic notes: RBI
October 8, 2002
The Reserve Bank of India has been receiving several queries about the possibility of
introducing polymer/plastic currency notes to replace the existing paper notes, especially in
the denominations of Rs.1000, Rs.500 Rs.100 and Rs.50.  The Reserve Bank of India has
clarified that there is no proposal to introduce polymer/plastic notes in place of paper notes.
Ajit Prasad
Manager
