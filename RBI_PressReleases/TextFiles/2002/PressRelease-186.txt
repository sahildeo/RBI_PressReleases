Rs.500 denomination notes (AP Series) continue to be legal tender
November 21, 2002
It has been brought to the notice of the Reserve Bank of India, that some cashiers of
commercial and co-operative banks do not accept Rs.500 denomination notes of Ashoka
Pillar (AP) Series, i.e., the notes with Ashoka Pillar Watermark. Members of the public are
requested to note that all Rs.500 denomination notes continue to be legal tender and shall be
freely accepted by banks and the Reserve Bank of India in settlement of transactions.
If any bank is not accepting these notes, the members of the public are requested to
bring the same to the notice of the Regional Director of the Reserve Bank's concerned office
or to the Chief General Manager, Reserve Bank of India, Department of Currency
Management, Central Office, Shahid Bhagat Singh Road, Mumbai.
Ajit Prasad
Manager
