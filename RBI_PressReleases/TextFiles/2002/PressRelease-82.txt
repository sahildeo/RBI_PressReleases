RBI cancels  licence of the Sevalal Urban Co-operative Urban Bank Ltd.
December 13, 2002
The Reserve Bank of India has cancelled vide Order dated December 4, 2002 the
licence granted to the Sevalal Urban Co-operative Bank Ltd., Mandrup, Dist. Solapur
(Maharashtra) to carry on banking business.  The bank is precluded from transacting the
business of `banking' as defined in the Banking Regulation Act, 1949 (As applicable to Co-
operative Societies) and it will not be in order for it, among other things, to accept deposits
from public and allow withdrawals by cheque, draft, order or otherwise.
