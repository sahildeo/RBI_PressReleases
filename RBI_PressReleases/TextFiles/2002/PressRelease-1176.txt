Tap sale of 7.80 per cent State Development Loans 2012
April 23, 2002
The sale `on tap' of 7.80 per cent State Loans, 2012 was closed in respect of  ten
States i.e., Arunachal Pradesh, Chhattisgarh, Goa, Haryana, Jharkhand, Karnataka,
Manipur, Punjab, Tamil Nadu and Uttaranchal on April 23, 2002 on receipt of the
respective target amounts.
The sale in respect of remaining 16 States (Kerala & Sikkim  not included) will
continue on April 24, 2002. The Reserve Bank of India reserves the right to close the sale
during or after April 24, 2002 having regard to the borrowing requirements of the State
Governments and the subscriptions received. Applications in the prescribed form can be
obtained from the Public Debt Office of the respective offices of the Reserve Bank of
India and Mumbai ( for all States).
Applications along with the payment will be received at those offices during
business hours while sale is open. Applicants, submitting the bids on April 24, 2002
would also have to pay interest for one day at 7.80 per cent per annum for the securities
they apply for.
