Investment  by  Foreign  Institutional Investors
May 3, 2002
The Reserve Bank of India has notified on May 3, 2002 that the aggregate net
purchases of equity shares of Zee Telefilms Ltd., by foreign institutional investors (FIIs)
in the primary/secondary markets has reached the limit of 28 per cent of its paid up equity
capital.  No further purchases of equity shares of Zee Telefilms Ltd., will, therefore, be
allowed on behalf of the FIIs without obtaining prior approval of the Reserve Bank.
N.S.D. Lobo
Asst. Manager
