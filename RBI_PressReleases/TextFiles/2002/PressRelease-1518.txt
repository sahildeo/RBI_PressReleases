RBI rejects  licence application of
The Aryan Co-operative Urban Bank Ltd.
February 11, 2002
The Reserve Bank of India has rejected the licence application of The Aryan Co-
operative Urban Bank Ltd., Hyderabad, (Andhra Pradesh) to carry on banking business in
India.  The bank is precluded from transacting the business of `banking' as defined under
Section 22 of the Banking Regulation Act, 1949 (As applicable to Co-operative
Societies) and it will not be in order for it, among other things, to accept deposits from
public and allow withdrawals by cheque, draft, order or otherwise.
