Extension of market timings
April 4, 2002
In order to facilitate the market participants for trading in Government Securities
after the announcement of result of Government securities auctions being held today, the
Reserve Bank of India has decided to extend the market timings for both the NDS
members and non-NDS members upto 7.30 p.m. today, i.e. April 4, 2002.
P.V.Sadanandan
Manager
