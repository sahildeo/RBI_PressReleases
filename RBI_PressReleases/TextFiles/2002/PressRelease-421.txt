RBI rejects  licence application of
Manamadurai Co-operative Urban Bank Ltd.
October 1, 2002
The Reserve Bank of India has rejected the licence application of the
Manamadurai Co-operative Urban Bank Ltd., Manamadurai, Tamil Nadu to carry on
banking business.  The bank is precluded from transacting the business of `banking' as
defined in the Banking Regulation Act, 1949 (As applicable to Co-operative Societies)
and it will not be in order for it, among other things, to accept deposits from public and
allow withdrawal by cheques, draft, order or otherwise.
