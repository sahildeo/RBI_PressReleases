Venture Capital Fund Companies
December 2, 2002
In order to obviate the need for dual regulation over venture capital fund
companies, the Reserve Bank of India has, in consultation with the Securities and
Exchange Board of India (SEBI), exempted venture capital fund companies holding a
certificate of registration obtained from SEBI under Section 12 of the Securities and
Exchange Board of India Act, 1992 and are not holding or accepting public deposits, from
the requirement of registration with the Reserve Bank of India, maintenance of liquid
assets, creation of special reserve fund under Sections 45IA, 45-IB and 45IC, respectively
of the Reserve Bank of India Act, as also from the  Directions of Reserve Bank on
acceptance of public deposits and prudential norms for Non-Banking Financial
Companies.
The relative notification and circular are available on RBI website at
www.rbi.org.in
