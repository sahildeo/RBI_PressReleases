RBI rejects  licence application of  The Pithapuram Co-operative Urban Bank
Ltd.
November 21, 2002
The Reserve Bank of India has rejected the licence application of The
Pithapuram Co-operative Urban Bank Ltd., Pithapuram (Andhra Pradesh) to carry on
banking business.  The bank is precluded from transacting the business of `banking' as
defined in the Banking Regulation Act, 1949 (As applicable to Co-operative
Societies) and it will not be in order for it, among other things, to accept deposits from
public and allow withdrawal by cheques, draft, order or otherwise.
