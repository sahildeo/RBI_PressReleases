Cancellation of the certificate of registration
May 4, 2002
The Reserve Bank of India, in exercise of the powers conferred on it under
Section 45 IA of the Reserve Bank of India Act, 1934, has on April 9, 2002 cancelled the
certificate of registration granted to Cobol Management Pvt. Limited having its registered
office at 4, Synagogue Street (Room No.903), Kolkata-700 001, for carrying on the
business of a non-banking financial institution.
As such, the above company cannot transact the business of a non-banking
financial institution as defined in Clause (a) of Section 45 I of the Reserve Bank of  India
Act, 1934.
