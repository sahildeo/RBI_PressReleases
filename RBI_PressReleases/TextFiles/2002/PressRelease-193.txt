RBI to conduct OMO Auction Sales on November 21, 2002
20November 20, 2002
9.40% GOI 2012  to be sold on auction basis for upto Rs.6000 crore
The Reserve Bank of India has decided to offer from its investment portfolio the following
security for sale through uniform price auction on November 21, 2002.
9.40% GOI 2012  to be sold on auction basis for upto Rs.6000 crore
The auction will be held at Mumbai. The bids may be tendered in electronic format using the
NDS or may be deposited in the tender box specially kept for the purpose at the Reserve
Bank of India, Fort, Mumbai on November 21, 2002 up to 12.30 pm. The application form
together with the terms and conditions of the auction may be obtained from the Chief General
Manager, Internal Debt Management Cell, Reserve Bank of India, 16th Floor, Central Office
Building, Mumbai or from Public Debt Office, Reserve Bank of India, Mumbai Office.
On the basis of the tenders received, the RBI will determine the minimum price upto which
tenders for sale of Government securities will be accepted at the auction. The auction will be
conducted on uniform price basis. The results of the auction will be displayed at the Reserve
Bank of India, Fort, Mumbai. The settlement will be on November 22, 2002.  The Reserve
Bank has the right to accept or reject any or all the bids either wholly or partially if deemed
fit without assigning any reason.
