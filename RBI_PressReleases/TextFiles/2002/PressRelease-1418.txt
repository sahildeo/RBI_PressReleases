91-day Treasury Bills Auction Result partially Modified
March 4, 2002
In partial modification of the press release dated February 27, 2002 advising the
results of the auction of 91-day Treasury Bills held on February 27, 2002, it is advised
that the single non-competitive bid was accepted for Rs.125.885 crore instead of Rs.200
crore as advised earlier.
P.V. Sadanandan
Manager
