Annual Monetary and Credit Policy on April 29, 2002
March 15, 2002
Governor, Dr. Bimal Jalan will hold a meeting with the Chief Executives of major
scheduled commercial banks for the annual Monetary and Credit Policy for 2002-03
on Monday, April 29, 2002. The meeting will be held at 11.00 a.m. in the Reserve
Bank of India, Central Office, Mumbai.
Ajit Prasad
Manager
