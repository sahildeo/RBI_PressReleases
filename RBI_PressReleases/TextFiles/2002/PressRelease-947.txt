Investment by Foreign Institutional Investors under PIS
June 11, 2002
The Reserve Bank of India has notified today that Foreign Institutional Investors (FIIs)
can now purchase equity shares and convertible debentures of United Breweries Limited up to 49
per cent of its  paid up capital through primary and secondary markets in India. The company has
passed resolution at its Board of Directors' and a Special Resolution in their General Body
Meeting for purchase of  equity shares and convertible debentures by FIIs upto 49 per cent of its
paid-up capital through primary/secondary markets in India.
