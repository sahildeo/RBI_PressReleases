RBI advances Bidding Time for Auctions to 12.30 p.m.
October 1, 2002
The Reserve Bank of India has advanced the time for submission of bids for
auctions of treasury bills and Government of India dated securities. The revised timing
for submission of bids will come into effect from October 16, 2002 when the Reserve
Bank will conduct auctions for 91-Day and 364-Day Treasury Bills. The timing has been
advanced to 12.30 p.m. from 2.30 p.m. with a view to making the results of auctions
available to market well within the normal time of the Negotiated Dealing System. The
position will be reviewed in three months time.
