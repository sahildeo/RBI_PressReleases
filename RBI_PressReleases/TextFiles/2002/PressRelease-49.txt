Investment by Foreign Institutional Investors
The Reserve Bank of India has notified on December 20, 2002 that Foreign
Institutional Investors (FIIs) can now purchase equity shares and convertible debentures of
Mascon Global Limited up to 100 per cent of its paid up capital through primary/secondary
markets in India, as the company has passed a resolution to this effect at its Board of
Directors' meeting and a special resolution in its General Body Meeting.
