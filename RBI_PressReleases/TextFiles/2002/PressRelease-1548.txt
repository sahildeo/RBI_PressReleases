Rejection of application for certificate of registration
February 4, 2002
The Reserve Bank of India, in exercise of the powers conferred on it under
Section 45 IA of the Reserve Bank of India Act, 1934, has rejected on January 31, 2002
the application for certificate of registration submitted by M/s. Panchvati Leasing and
Hire Purchase Pvt. Ltd., Kali Nadi Road, Bulandsahar, Uttar Pradesh for carrying on the
business of a Non-Banking Financial Institution
As such, the above company cannot transact the business of a non-banking
financial institution as defined in Clause (a) of Section 45 IA of the Reserve Bank of
India Act, 1934.
P.V. Sadanandan
Asst. Manager
