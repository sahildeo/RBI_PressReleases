RBI to conduct OMO Auction Sales on August 9, 2002
August 8, 2002
Two Securities to be sold on auction basis are
(i) 7.37% GOI 2014 to be sold on auction basis for up to Rs.2000 crore
(ii) 8.35% GOI 2022 to be sold on auction basis for up to Rs.2000 crore
The Reserve Bank of India has decided to offer from its investment portfolio the
following two securities for sale through uniform price auction on August 9, 2002.
(i) 7.37% GOI 2014 for up to Rs.2000 crore
(ii) 8.35% GOI 2022 for up to Rs.2000 crore
The application form together with the terms and conditions of the auction may be
obtained from the Chief General Manager, Internal Debt Management Cell, Reserve Bank of
th
India, 16 Floor, Central Office Building, Mumbai or from Public Debt Office, Reserve Bank
of India, Mumbai Office.
The bids should be tendered on August 9, 2002 up to 12.30 pm and should be
deposited in the tender box specially kept for the purpose at the Reserve Bank of India, Fort,
Mumbai.
On the basis of the tenders received, the RBI will determine the minimum price upto
which tenders for sale of Government securities will be accepted at the auction. The auction
will be conducted on uniform price basis. The results of the auction will be displayed at
Reserve Bank of India, Fort, Mumbai. The settlement will be on August 12, 2002.  The
Reserve Bank has the right to accept or reject any or all the bids either wholly or partially if
deemed fit without assigning any reason.
