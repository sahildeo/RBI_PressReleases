RBI cancels licence of  Mother Theresa Hyderabad Co-operative Urban Bank
Ltd.
October 24, 2002
The Reserve Bank of India has cancelled the licence of The Mother Theresa
Hyderabad Co-operative Urban Bank Ltd., Narayanaguda, Andhra Pradesh to carry on
banking business.  The bank is precluded from transacting the business of ‘banking’
as defined in the Banking Regulation Act, 1949 (as applicable to Co-operative
Societies). Thus, it will not be in order for it, among other things, to accept deposits
from the public and allow withdrawals by cheque, draft, order or otherwise.
