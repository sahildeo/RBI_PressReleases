Underwriting by Primary Dealers
December 4, 2002
The Reserve Bank of India has set the cut-off rate for underwriting bids by
Primary Dealers for the price based auction of "8.35% Government Stock, 2022" to be
held on December 5, 2002 at Re.0.0075 (0.75 paisa).
P. V. Sadanandan
Manager
