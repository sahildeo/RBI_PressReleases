Investment by Foreign Institutional Investors
March 5, 2002
The Reserve Bank of India on March 5, 2002 has notified that Foreign Institutional
Investors (FIIs) can now purchase equity shares and convertible debentures of Soffia
Software Ltd., up to 49 per cent of its paid up capital through primary and secondary
markets in India, as the company has passed a resolution at its Board of Directors'
Meeting and a special resolution in its General Body Meeting to this effect.
P.V. Sadanandan
Manager
