On tap sale of 8.30 per cent State Development Loans, 2012
January 29, 2002
         The sale 'on tap' 8.30 per cent State Loans, 2012 was closed in respect of Orissa,
Nagaland and West Bengal, on January 29, 2002 on receipt of the respective target
amounts.
The sale in respect of the loans offered by  States of Assam, Bihar, Jammu &
Kashmir, Jharkhand, Madhya Pradesh, Meghalaya and Uttar Pradesh will continue on
January 30, 2002.  The Reserve Bank of India reserves the right to close the sale during
or after January 30, 2002 having regard to the borrowing requirements of the State
Governments and the subscriptions received. Applications in the prescribed form can be
obtained  from the Public Debt Office of the  respective offices of the Reserve Bank of
India and Mumbai (for all States).
Applications  alongwith the payment will be received at those  offices during
office hours while the sale is open. Applicants, submitting the bids on January 30, 2002
would also have to pay interest for two days at 8.30 per cent per annum for the securities
they apply for.
         It may be recalled that the Governments of 26 States  offered to sell `on tap' 8.30
per cent State Loans, 2012.  The loans  opened for subscription on January 28, 2002 at
the respective Regional Offices of the Reserve Bank of India and at Mumbai for 26
States. Sale `on tap' in respect of 16 other States was closed as at the close of business
hours on January 28, 2002.
