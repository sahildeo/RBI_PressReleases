Monitoring FII Investments in Indian Companies
August 9, 2002
The Reserve Bank of India has advised that Foreign Institutional Investors (FIIs) can
now purchase equity shares and convertible debentures of M/s. Himachal Futuristic
Communications Ltd. up to 49 per cent of its paid-up capital through primary/secondary
markets in India under the Portfolio Investment Scheme.. The company's board of directors
and general body have passed a resolution agreeing for the purchase.
Ajit Prasad
Manager
