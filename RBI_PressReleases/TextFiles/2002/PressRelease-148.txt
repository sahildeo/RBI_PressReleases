RBI to conduct OMO auction sale of 10.95% GOI 2011 on December 2, 2002
November 29, 2002
The Reserve Bank of India has decided to offer from its investment portfolio the
10.95% GOI 2011 for sale through uniform price auction on December 2, 2002 for upto
Rs.4500 crore. The settlement will be on December 3, 2002.  The auction will be held at
Mumbai. The bids may be tendered in electronic format using the NDS or may be deposited
in the tender box specially kept for the purpose at the Reserve Bank of India, Fort, Mumbai
on December 2, 2002 up to 12.30 pm.
