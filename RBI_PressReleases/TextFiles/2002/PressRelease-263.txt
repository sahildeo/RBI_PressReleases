Underwriting by Primary Dealers
November 5, 2002
The Reserve Bank of India has set the cut-off rate for underwriting bids by Primary Dealers
for the price based auctions of "10.03% Government Stock, 2019" and "10.18% Government
Stock, 2026"  to be held on November 6, 2002 at Re.0.01 (one paisa) and Re.0.009 (0.90
paisa) respectively.

Manager
