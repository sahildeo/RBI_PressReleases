Investment by Foreign Institutional Investors under PIS
June 10, 2002
The Reserve Bank of India has notified today that Foreign Institutional Investors
(FIIs) can now purchase equity shares and convertible debentures of Zee Telefilms
Limited up to 49 per cent of its  paid up capital through primary and secondary markets
in India. The company has passed  resolution at its Board of Directors' and General Body
Meetings for purchase of  equity shares and convertible debentures by FIIs upto 49 per
cent of its paid-up capital through primary/secondary markets in India.
