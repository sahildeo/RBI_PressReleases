Sale of State Government Loans on tap
March 8, 2002
The Governments of 26 States have offered to sell on tap 8.00 per cent State
Development Loan, 2012 having a maturity of ten years. The sale will be held at the
respective Regional Offices of the Reserve Bank of India and at Mumbai (for all 26 States),
on March 13, 2002. The Reserve Bank of India reserves the right to close the sale during the
banking hours on or after March 13, 2002 having regard to the borrowing requirements of the
State Governments and the subscriptions received. Applications in the prescribed form
obtainable from the Public Debt Office of these offices of the Reserve Bank of India will be
received at the aforesaid offices during office hours while the sale is open. The Reserve Bank
expects to mobilise about Rs.2800 crore in the `tap' issue.
P. V. Sadanandan
Manager
