15 Year Government Stock :
Underwriting Commission for PDs at 5 paise
April 12, 2002
The Reserve Bank of India has announced that the cut-off rate for  underwriting
by Primary Dealers for the Yield Based Auction of 15 year Government Stock to be held
on April 15, 2002, was Re.0.05 (five paise).

Manager
