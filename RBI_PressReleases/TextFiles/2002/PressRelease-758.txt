RBI to conduct OMO Sales on July 23, 2002
July 22, 2002
The Reserve Bank of India has decided to offer from its investment portfolio the
following two securities for sale through uniform price auction on July 23, 2002.
- 11.98% GOI 2004 for upto Rs.2000 crore
- 11.90% GOI 2007 for upto Rs.1500 crore
The application form together with the terms and conditions of the auction may be
obtained from the Chief General Manager, Internal Debt Management Cell, Reserve
th
Bank of India, 16 floor, Central Office Building, Mumbai or from Public Debt Office,
Reserve Bank of India, Mumbai Office.
The bids should be tendered on July 23, 2002 upto 12.30 pm and should be
deposited in the tender box specially kept for the purpose at Reserve Bank of India, Fort,
Mumbai.
On the basis of the tenders received, the Reserve Bank will determine the
minimum price upto which tenders for sale of Government securities will be accepted at
the auction. The auction will be conducted on uniform price basis. The results of the
auction will be displayed at the Reserve Bank of India, Fort, Mumbai. The settlement will
be on July 24, 2002. The Reserve Bank has the right to accept or reject any or all the bids
either wholly or partially if deemed fit without assigning any reason.
Alpana Killawala
General Manager
