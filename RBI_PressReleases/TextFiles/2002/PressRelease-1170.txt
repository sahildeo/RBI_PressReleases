Tap sale of 7.80 per cent State Development Loans 2012 extended
April 24, 2002
The sale `on tap' of 7.80 per cent State Development Loans, 2012 was closed in
respect of  Andhra Pradesh, Himachal Pradesh, Maharashtra, Meghalaya, Mizoram,
Nagaland, Tripura and West Bengal on receipt of the respective target amounts.
The sale in respect of remaining eight States, viz., Assam, Bihar, Gujarat, Jammu
& Kashmir, Madhya Pradesh, Orissa, Rajasthan and Uttar Pradesh will continue on April
26, 2002 (April 25, 2002 being Holiday). The Reserve Bank of India reserves the right to
close the sale during or after April 26, 2002 having regard to the borrowing requirements
of the State Governments and the subscriptions received. Applications in the prescribed
form can be obtained from the Public Debt Office of the respective offices of the Reserve
Bank of India and Mumbai ( for all States). Applications along with the payment will be
received at those offices during business hours while sale is open. Applicants, submitting
the bids on April 26, 2002 would also have to pay interest for three days at 7.80 per cent
per annum for the securities they apply for.
It may be recalled that the `on tap' sale of the 7.80 per cent State Development
Loans, 2012 which opened on April 23, 2002 for 26 States was closed on the same day in
respect of 10 States, viz., Arunachal Pradesh, Chhattisgarh, Goa, Haryana, Jharkhand,
Karnataka, Manipur, Punjab, Tamil Nadu and Uttaranchal.
