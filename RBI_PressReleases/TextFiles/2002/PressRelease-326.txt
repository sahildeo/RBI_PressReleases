Rejection of applications for certificate of registration
October 23, 2002
The Reserve Bank of India, in exercise of the powers conferred on it under
Section 45 IA(4) of the Reserve Bank of India Act, 1934, has rejected the applications for
certificate of registration of the following companies for commencing the business of
non-banking financial institutions:
Name of the Company Rejected on
1) Manorama Tracom Private Limited October 12, 2002
2, Ganesh Chandra Avenue, 2nd Floor, Kolkata-700 013
2) Jayanti Agency Pvt. Ltd., October 12, 2002
P-27, Princep Street, 3rd Floor, Kolkata-700 072
3) Darpan Dealers Pvt. Ltd., October 12, 2002
2, Ganesh Chandra Avenue, 2nd Floor, Room No.5, Kolkata-700 013
4) Lighthouse Commercial Pvt. Ltd., October 12, 2002
168/A, Cotton Street, 4th Floor, Kolkata-700 007
As such, the above companies cannot transact the business of a non-banking financial
institution as defined in Clause (1)(a) of Section 45 IA of the Reserve Bank of India Act,
1934.
