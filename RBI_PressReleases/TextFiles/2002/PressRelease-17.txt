RBI announces rate of interest on FRBs 2017
December 27, 2002
The rate of interest on the Floating Rate Bonds, 2017 (FRB, 2017) applicable for the half-
year January 2, 2003 - July 1, 2003 shall be 5.92 per cent per annum. It may be recalled that
the rate of interest on the FRB, 2017 was set at a mark-up (as decided in the auction held on
July 1, 2002) over and above the variable base rate. The variable base rate for payment of
interest shall be average rate (rounded off up to two decimal places) of the implicit yields at
cut-off prices of the last six auctions of Government of India 364-day Treasury Bills held up
to the commencement of the respective half-yearly coupon period. The variable base rate
based on the average rate of the implicit yields at cut-off prices of the last six auctions of
Government of India 364-day Treasury Bills held up to December 24, 2002 works out to 5.58
per cent. The mark-up decided in the auction held on July 1, 2002 was (+) 0.34 percent. The
coupon rate has been fixed accordingly.
