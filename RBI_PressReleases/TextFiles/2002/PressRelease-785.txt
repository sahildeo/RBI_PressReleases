Underwriting by Primary Dealers
July 16, 2002
The Reserve Bank of India has set the cut-off rate for underwriting by Primary
Dealers for the Yield Based Auction of 10-year Government Stock and Price Based
Auction of 8.07 per cent Government Stock, 2017 to be held on July 17, 2002 at Re.0.02
(two paise) and Re.0.15 (fifteen paise) respectively.
P.V. Sadanandan
Manager
Press Release : 2002-03/59
