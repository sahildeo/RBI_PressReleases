Underwriting by Primary Dealers
September 7, 2002
The Reserve Bank of India has set the cut-off rate for underwriting by Primary
Dealers for the Price Based Auction of "7.46% Government Stock, 2017" and "8.35%
Government Stock, 2022" to be auctioned on September 9, 2002 at Re.0.05 (five paise)
and Re.0.08 (eight paise) respectively.
P.V. Sadanandan
Manager
