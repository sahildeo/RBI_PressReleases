On tap sale of 8.30 per cent State Development Loans, 2012
The sale 'on tap' 8.30 per cent State Loans, 2012 was closed in respect of Andhra
Pradesh, Arunachal pradesh, Chhatisgarh, Goa, Gujarat, Haryana, Himachal Pradesh,
Karnataka, Kerala, Maharashtra, Manipur, Mizoram, Punjab, Rajasthan, Tamil Nadu and
Uttaranchal, on receipt of the respective target amounts.
The sale in respect of the loans offered by  States of Assam, Bihar, Jammu &
Kashmir, Jharkhand, Madhya Pradesh, Meghalaya,  Nagaland, Orissa, Uttar Pradesh and
West Bengal will continue on January 29, 2002.  The Reserve Bank of India reserves the
right to close the sale during or after the close of banking hours on or after January 29,
2002 having regard to the borrowing requirements of the State Governments and the
subscriptions received. Applications in the prescribed form can be obtained  from the
Public Debt Office of the  respective offices of the Reserve Bank of India and Mumbai
(for all States).
Applications  alongwith the payment will be received at those  offices during
office hours while the sale is open. Applicants, submitting the bids on January 29, 2002
would also have to pay interest for one day at 8.30 per cent per annum for the securities
they apply for.
         It may be recalled that the Governments of 26 States  offered to sell `on tap' 8.30
per cent State Loans, 2012.  The loans  opened for subscription on January 28, 2002 at
the respective Regional Offices of the Reserve Bank of India and at Mumbai for 26
States.
