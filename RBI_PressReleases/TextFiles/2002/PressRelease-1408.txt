Investments by FIIs/NRIs /PIOs  - Monitoring of overall ceiling
March 5, 2002
The Reserve Bank of India on March 5, 2002 has notified that no further
purchases of shares of State Bank of India should be made through stock exchanges in
India on behalf of foreign institutional investors (FIIs)/ non-resident Indians (NRIs) and
persons of Indian origin (PIOs) as the holdings by FIIs / NRIs and PIOs have reached the
overall ceiling of 20 per cent of its paid up equity capital.
P.V. Sadanandan
Manager
