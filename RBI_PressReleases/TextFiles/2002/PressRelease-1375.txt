Tap sale of 8.00 per cent State Development Loans, 2012
March 13, 2002
         The 'on tap' sale of 8.00 per cent State Loans, 2012 was closed in respect of Andhra
Pradesh, Arunachal Pradesh,  Goa, Gujarat, Haryana, Himachal Pradesh, Karnataka,
Kerala, Maharashtra, Manipur, Meghalaya, Mizoram, Punjab, Tamil Nadu and
Uttaranchal on March 13, 2002, on receipt of the respective target amounts.
The sale in respect of the loans offered by  States of Assam, Bihar, Chhattisgarh,
Jammu & Kashmir, Jharkhand, Madhya Pradesh, Nagaland, Orissa, Rajasthan, Uttar
Pradesh and West Bengal will continue on March 14, 2002.  The Reserve Bank of India
reserves the right to close the sale during or after the business hours on March 14, 2002
having regard to the borrowing requirements of the State Governments and the
subscriptions received.
Applications in the prescribed form can be obtained  from the Public Debt Office
of the  respective offices of the Reserve Bank of India and Mumbai (for all remaining
States). Applications  alongwith the payment will be received at those  offices during
business hours while the sale is open. Applicants, submitting the bids on March 14, 2002
would also have to pay interest for one day at 8.00 per cent per annum for the securities
they apply for.
