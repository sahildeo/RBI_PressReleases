Lower Ceilings on Interest Rate on Rupee Export Credit
now available till April 30, 2003
September 21, 2002
The Reserve Bank of India has today extended the dispensation of lower ceiling
on interest rates charged by scheduled commercial banks for export credit. The
dispensation will now be valid till April 30, 2003.
The ceiling on interest rates charged by scheduled commercial banks on rupee
export credit was reduced by one percentage point across the board with effect from
September 26, 2001. The reduction was applicable to both pre-shipment and post-
shipment credits. It had, at that time, stated that the concession would be available up to
March 31, 2002. The concession was extended till September 30, 2002 in March this
year.
