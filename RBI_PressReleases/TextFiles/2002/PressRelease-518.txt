RBI cancels licence of
Armoor co-operative Urban Bank Ltd.
September 9, 2002
The Reserve Bank of India has cancelled the licence of Armoor Co-operative Urban
Bank Ltd., Armoor, Andhra Pradesh to carry on  banking business.  The bank is precluded
from transacting the business of ‘banking’ as defined in the Banking Regulation Act, 1949
(as applicable to Co-operative Societies). Thus, it will not be in order for it, among other
things, to accept deposits from the public and allow withdrawals by cheque, draft, order or
otherwise.
