Government of India announces the sale (re-issue) of
“8.07 per cent Government Stock 2017”
through price based auction
July 12, 2002
The Government of India have announced sale (reissue) by auction of “8.07 per
cent Government Stock 2017 “for an aggregate amount of Rs 4,000 crore (nominal) by
price based auction using multiple price auction method.
Government Stock upto 5% of the notified amount of the sale, i.e., Rs.200 crore
will be allotted to the eligible individuals and institutions under the Scheme for Non-
Competitive Bidding Facility in the auction of Government Securities.
The auction will be conducted by the Reserve Bank of India, Mumbai Office,
Fort, Mumbai on July 17, 2002 (Wednesday). Applicants who wish to bid for the Stock
will have to clearly indicate in the prescribed form (obtainable from the Regional
Director, RBI, Mumbai Office, Fort, Mumbai) the nominal value of the Stock applied for,
and the price expected by them (up to two decimal places). The competitive bids by the
NDS members should be submitted only in electronic format using NDS, while all the
non-competitive bids should be submitted in the prescribed physical form. All bids must
be submitted by 2.30 P.M.
The results of the auctions will be announced on July 17, 2002 (Wednesday) and
payment by successful bidders will be during banking hours on July 18, 2002
(Thursday). The new stock will qualify for the ready forward (repo) facility.
Bids for underwriting of the Stock under auction can be submitted by the
Primary Dealers in the Government Securities Market up to 2.30 p.m. on Tuesday, July
16, 2002 to the Chief General Manager, Internal Debt Management Cell, Reserve Bank
of India, Central Office, Fort,  Mumbai 400 001.
Alpana Killawala
General Manager
