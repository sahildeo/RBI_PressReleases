Tap sale of 8.30 per cent State Development Loans, 2012 closed
January 30, 2002
         The sale 'on tap' 8.30 per cent State Loans, 2012 was closed today in respect of Assam,
Bihar, Jammu and Kashmir, Jharkhand, Madhya Pradesh, Meghalaya and Uttar Pradesh.
It may be recalled that the `on tap' of 8.30 per cent State Loans, 2012 which opened
on January 28, 2002 was closed on the same day in respect of 16 States, viz., Andhra
Pradesh, Arunachal Pradesh, Chhatisgarh, Goa, Gujarat, Haryana, Himachal Pradesh,
Karnataka, Kerala, Maharashtra, Manipur, Mizoram, Punjab, Rajasthan, Tamil Nadu and
Uttaranchal.
The issue was closed on second day, i.e., on January 29, 2002 in respect of three
States, viz., Orissa, Nagaland and West Bengal.
The subscriptions for 26 State Governments aggregated approximately to Rs.4150
crore. The tap issue for all the States has been closed as at the close of business hours on
January 30,  2002.
Alpana Killawala
