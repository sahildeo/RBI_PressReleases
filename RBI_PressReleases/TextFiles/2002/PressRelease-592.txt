RBI cancels licence of
The Maa Sharda Mahila Nagari Sahakari Bank Ltd.
August 26, 2002
The Reserve Bank of India has cancelled the licence of The Maa Sharda Mahila
Nagari Sahakari Bank Ltd., Akola, Maharashtra to carry on  banking business.  The bank
is precluded from transacting the business of ‘banking’ as defined in the Banking
Regulation Act, 1949 (as applicable to Co-operative Societies). Thus, it will not be in
order for it, among other things, to accept deposits from the public and allow withdrawals
by cheque, draft, order or otherwise.
