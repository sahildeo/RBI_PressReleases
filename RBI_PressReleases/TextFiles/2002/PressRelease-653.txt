On tap Sale of State Government Loans
August 13, 2002
All the State Governments have offered to sell on tap 7.80 per cent State
Development Loan, 2012 having a maturity of ten years. The sale will be held at the
respective Regional Offices of the Reserve Bank of India and at Mumbai (for all 28
States), on August 19, 2002. The Reserve Bank of India reserves the right to close the
sale during the business hours on or after August 19, 2002 having regard to the borrowing
requirements of the State Governments and the subscriptions received.
Applications in the prescribed form obtainable from the Public Debt Office of the
concerned offices of the Reserve Bank of India will be received at these offices during
business hours while the sale is open. The Reserve Bank expects to mobilise about
Rs.4478 crore in the `tap' issue.
This is the second tranche of the market borrowing programme for the State
Governments in the current fiscal.
Alpana Killawala
General Manager
