Underwriting by Primary Dealers
August 26, 2002
The Reserve Bank of India has set the cut-off rate for underwriting bids by
Primary Dealers for the Yield Based Auctions of 15 Year Government Stock and 30 Year
Government Stock to be held on August 27, 2002 at Re.0.02 (two paise) for both the
issues.
P.V. Sadanandan
Manager
