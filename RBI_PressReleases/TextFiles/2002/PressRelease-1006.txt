Sale (Re-issue) of Government Securities to Reserve Bank
On Private Placement basis
May 30, 2002
The Government of India, vide notification No.F.4(9)-W&M/2002 dated May 30,
2002 have announced the sale (re-issue) of Government security, viz., `7.49 per cent
Government Stock 2017' to the Reserve Bank of India on `private placement' basis as per
the following details :
1. Nomenclature of Stock 7.49 per cent Government Stock 2017
2. Notified Amount Rs. 4,000 crore (nominal)
3. Issue Price Rs.96.45 per Rs.100 (face value)
4. Date of redemption of the security April 16, 2017
5. Interest At the rate of 7.49 per cent on the nominal
value of the stock, payable half yearly on
October 16 and April 16.
The stock will be issued to the Reserve Bank of India on May 30, 2002 (Thursday).
