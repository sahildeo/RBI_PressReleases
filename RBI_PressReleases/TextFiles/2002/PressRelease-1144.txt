10-Year Government stock :
Underwriting Commission for PDs at 7 paise
April 30, 2002
The Reserve Bank of India has announced that the cut-off rate for underwriting by
Primary Dealers for the Yield Based Auction of 10-Year Government Stock to be held on
May 2, 2002 was Re.0.07 (seven paise).
P.V. Sadanandan
Manager
