Government of India announces the sale (re-issue) of two dated securities
through price based auctions
June 3, 2002
The Government of India have announced auctions of two dated securities, viz.
`7.40 per cent Government Stock, 2012' for a notified amount of Rs.4,000 crore
(Nominal) and “8.35 percent Government Stock, 2022” for a notified amount of Rs 2,000
crore (nominal).
The auction will be price based and will be conducted by the Reserve Bank of
India (RBI) at Mumbai on Wednesday, June 5, 2002. Bids in prescribed form obtainable
from the Regional Director, RBI, Public Debt Office, Fort, Mumbai should be submitted
on Wednesday, June 5, 2002 during the banking hours. The NDS members should submit
their bids only in electronic format using NDS. On the basis of bids received, RBI will
determine the minimum price at which offers will be accepted.
The results of the auctions will be announced on June 5, 2002 and payment by
successful bidders will be during banking hours on June 6, 2002. The stocks will qualify
for the ready forward facility.
Bids for underwriting of the Government Securities under auctions can be
submitted by the `Primary Dealers in the Government Securities Market' up to 2.30 p.m.
on June 4, 2002 (Tuesday) to the Chief General Manager, Internal Debt Management
Cell, Reserve Bank of India, Central Office, Fort, Mumbai 400 001.
Alpana Killawala
General Manager
