RBI Instructions to Cooperative Banks for G-Sec Transactions
May 4, 2002
In response to press queries, following a report in a section of the press, the
Reserve Bank of India encloses its circular dated April 20, 2002 issued to cooperative
banks reiterating its existing guidelines for transactions in Government securities. The
full text of the circular is also available on the RBI website www.rbi.org.in
Alpana Killawala
General Manager
Press Release No. 2001-2002/1223
