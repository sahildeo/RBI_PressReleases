RBI to conduct OMO Auction Sales on August 31, 2002
August 30, 2002
7.40% GOI 2012  to be sold on auction basis for upto Rs.6000 crore
The Reserve Bank of India has decided to offer from its investment portfolio the
following  security for sale through uniform price auction on August 31, 2002.
7.40% GOI 2012  to be sold on auction basis for upto Rs.6000 crore
The application form together with the terms and conditions of the auction may be
obtained from the Chief General Manager, Internal Debt Management Cell, Reserve
th
Bank of India, 16 Floor, Central Office Building, Mumbai or from Public Debt
Office, Reserve Bank of India, Mumbai Office.
The bids should be tendered on August 31, 2002 up to 12.30 pm and should be
deposited in the tender box specially kept for the purpose at Reserve Bank of India,
Fort, Mumbai.
On the basis of the tenders received, the RBI will determine the minimum price upto
which tenders for sale of Government securities will be accepted at the auction. The
auction will be conducted on uniform price basis. The results of the auction will be
displayed at the Reserve Bank of India, Fort, Mumbai. The settlement will be on
September 2, 2002.  The Reserve Bank has the right to accept or reject any or all the
bids either wholly or partially if deemed fit without assigning any reason.
P.V. Sadanandan
