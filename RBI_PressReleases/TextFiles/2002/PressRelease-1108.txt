Bhutan central bank to be non-competitive bidder
in 91-day Treasury Bill Auctions
May 9, 2002
It has been decided to permit the Royal Monetary Authority of Bhutan to participate
in the auctions of 91-day Treasury Bills on a non-competitive basis. Presently, state
governments, eligible provident funds and Nepal Rashtra Bank are permitted to bid on non-
competitive basis in the auctions of 91-day Treasury Bills conducted by the Reserve Bank of
India.
P.V. Sadanandan
Manager
