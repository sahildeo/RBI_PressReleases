RBI to conduct OMO Sale on April 10, 2002 :
April 9, 2002
Security to be sold is 10.95% GOI 2011 for upto Rs.3000 crore
The Reserve Bank of India has decided to offer from its investment portfolio the
following security for sale through Uniform Price Auction on April 10, 2002.
10.95% GOI 2011 for upto Rs.3000 crore
The application form together with the terms and conditions of the auction may
be obtained from the Chief General Manager, Internal Debt Management Cell, Reserve
th
Bank of India, 16 Floor, Central Office Building, Mumbai or from Public Debt Office,
Reserve Bank of India, Mumbai Office.
The bids should be tendered on April 10, 2002 up to 12.30 pm and should be
deposited in the tender box specially kept for the purpose at the Reserve Bank of India,
Fort, Mumbai.
On the basis of the tenders received, the RBI will determine the minimum price
upto which tenders for sale of Government securities will be accepted at the auction. The
auction will be conducted on uniform price basis. The results of the auction will be
displayed at the Reserve Bank of India, Fort, Mumbai. The settlement will be on April
11, 2002.  The Reserve Bank has the right to accept or reject any or all the bids either
wholly or partially if deemed fit without assigning any reason.
