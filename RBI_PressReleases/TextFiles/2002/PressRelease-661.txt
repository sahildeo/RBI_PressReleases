RBI cancels licence of The Megacity
Co-operative Urban Bank Ltd.
August 10, 2002
The Reserve Bank of India has cancelled the licence of The Megacity Co-operative
Urban Bank Ltd., Hyderabad, Andhra Pradesh to carry on  banking business in India.  The
bank is precluded from transacting the business of ‘banking’ as defined in the Banking
Regulation Act, 1949 (as applicable to Co-operative Societies). Thus, it will not be in order
for it, among other things, to accept deposits from the public and allow withdrawals by
cheque, draft, order or otherwise.
