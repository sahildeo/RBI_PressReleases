Mid-term Review of Monetary and Credit Policy
on October 29, 2002
September 19, 2002
Dr. Bimal Jalan, Governor, Reserve Bank of India will hold a meeting with the chief
executives of major scheduled commercial banks for the Mid-term Review of Monetary
and Credit Policy for 2002-2003 on Tuesday, October 29, 2002. The meeting will be held
at 11:00 am in the Reserve Bank of India, Central Office, Mumbai.
Alpana Killawala
General Manager
Press Release : 2002-2003/307
