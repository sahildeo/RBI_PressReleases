Security Review in Banks
June 10, 2002
Shri Vepa Kamesam, Deputy governor had a meeting with the banks today to
review the prevailing security situation and emphasise on the banks to take appropriate
precautions. The meeting was attended by the Chief Executive Officers alongwith their
Chief Officers responsible for security. On behalf of the Reserve Bank, Deputy Governor
assured the banks that supplies of cash will be maintained as required and exhorted them
to be ready with their  contingency plans.
H.N. Prasad
Chief General Manager
