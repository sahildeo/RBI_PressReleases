Tap sale of 8.00 per cent State Development Loans 2012 closed
March 15, 2002
The `on tap' sale of 8.00 per cent State Loans, 2012 was closed today in respect of
Bihar and Uttar Pradesh.
It may be recalled that the `on tap' of 8.00 per cent State Development Loans, 2012
which opened on March 13, 2002 was closed on the same day in respect of 15 States, viz.,
Andhra Pradesh, Arunachal Pradesh, Goa, Gujarat, Haryana, Himachal Pradesh, Karnataka,
Kerala, Maharashtra, Manipur, Meghalaya, Mizoram, Punjab, Tamil Nadu and Uttaranchal.
The issue was closed on the second day, i.e., on March 14, 2002 in respect of nine
States, viz., Assam, Chhattisgarh, Jammu & Kashmir, Jharkhand, Madhya Pradesh,
Nagaland, Orissa, Rajasthan and West Bengal.
Accordingly, the tap issue for all the States has been closed as at the close of business
hours on March 15, 2002.
