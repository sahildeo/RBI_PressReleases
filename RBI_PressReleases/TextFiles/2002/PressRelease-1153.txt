Auction of Government of India 10-year Stock announced
April 29, 2002
Government of India have announced sale by auction of a new 10-year Government
Stock for Rs 6,000 crore (nominal).
2. The auction will be yield based and will be conducted by the Reserve Bank of India,
Mumbai Office, Fort, Mumbai on Thursday, May 2, 2002 using “Multiple Price Auction”
method. Bids in the prescribed form obtainable from the Regional Director, Reserve
Bank of India, Mumbai Office (Public Debt Office), Fort, Mumbai should be submitted at
that Office on May 2, 2002 during banking hours. The competitive bids by the NDS
members should be submitted only in electronic format using NDS, while all the non-
competitive bids should be submitted in the prescribed physical form. On the basis of
bids received, Reserve Bank of India will determine the maximum rate of yield at which
offers will be accepted. The results of the auction will be announced on May 2, 2002 and
payment by successful bidders will be during banking hours on May 3, 2002. The new
Stock will qualify for the Ready Forward facility.
3.   Government Stock up to 5% of the notified amount of the sale will be allotted to
eligible individuals and Institutions under the Scheme for Non-Competitive Bidding
Facility in the Auction of Government Securities.
4. Bids for underwriting the issue of Government Security under auction can be
submitted by the ‘Primary Dealers in the Government Securities Market’ up to 2.30 p.m.
on Tuesday, April 30, 2002 to the CGM-In-Charge, Internal Debt Management Cell,
Reserve Bank of India, Central Office, Mumbai 400 001.
