Investment by Foreign Institutional Investors
October 29, 2002
The Reserve Bank of India on October 29, 2002 has notified that Foreign Institutional
Investors (FIIs) can now purchase equity shares and convertible debentures of Hindustan
Lever Ltd., up to 49 per cent of its paid up capital through primary and secondary markets in
India, as the company has passed a resolution at its Board of Directors'  and General Body
Meeting to this effect.
P.V. Sadanandan
Manager
