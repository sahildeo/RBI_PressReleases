RBI announces rate of interest on FRBs 2006
November 14, 2002
The rate of interest on the Floating Rate Bonds, 2006 (FRB 2006) was set at a mark-
up (decided in the auction) over and above the variable base rate. The variable base rate for
payment of interest shall be the average rate (rounded off upto two decimal places) of the
implicit yields at cut-off prices of the last six auctions of Government of India 364 day
Treasury Bills held up to the commencement of the respective half-yearly coupon period.
The mark-up decided in the auction held on November 21, 2001 was (-) 0.05 (minus
0.05). The base rate using the average rate of the implicit yields at cut-off prices of the last
six auctions of Government of India 364 day Treasury Bills held before November 21, 2002
is 5.77 per cent. Accordingly, the rate of interest on the FRB, 2006 applicable for the half-
year ending May 21, 2003 shall be 5.72 per cent per annum.
