RBI's Dividend to GoI
August 14, 2002
In response to a press query, the RBI spokesperson confirmed that the Reserve
Bank Board has, at its meeting held today, decided to transfer a dividend of Rs.10,320
crore to the Government of India out of its surplus profit for the year ended June 30,
2002.
Alpana Killawala
General Manager
Press Release : 2002-2003/169
