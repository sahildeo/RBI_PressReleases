Government of India announces the sale (re-issue) of "8.07 percent Government Stock,
2017" through price based auction
February 12, 2002
The Government of India have announced sale (re-issue) by auction of “8.07 percent
Government Stock, 2017” for an aggregate amount of Rs 5,000 crore (nominal).
The auction will be price based and will be conducted by the Reserve Bank of India
at Mumbai Office, Fort,  Mumbai on Thursday, February 14, 2002. Bids in prescribed form
obtainable from the Regional Director, RBI, Mumbai Office, Fort, Mumbai should be
submitted  at that Office, on February 14, 2002 during the banking hours. The auction will be
conducted on "Multiple- Price" basis. On the basis of the bids received, RBI will determine
the minimum price up to which tenders for purchase of Government Stock will be accepted at
the auction. Bids quoted at prices lower than the minimum price determined by Reserve Bank
of India will be rejected. The results of the auction will be announced on February 14, 2002
and payment by successful bidders will be during the banking hours on February 15, 2002.
Bids for underwriting of the Government Stock under auction can be submitted by the
‘Primary Dealers in the Government Securities Market’ up to 2.30 p.m. on Wednesday,
February 13, 2002 to the Chief General Manager-in-Charge, Internal Debt Management Cell,
Reserve Bank of India, Central Office , Fort, Mumbai 400 001.
Alpana Killawala
General Manager
