Investment by Foreign Institutional Investors
September 23, 2002
The Reserve Bank of India on September 23, 2002 has notified that Foreign Institutional
Investors (FIIs) can now purchase equity shares and convertible debentures of the
following companies up to 40/74 per cent of their paid up capital through primary and
secondary markets in India, as these companies have passed resolutions at their Board of
Directors' Meetings and special resolutions in their General Body Meetings to this effect.
1. Balaji Telefilms Ltd. (40 per cent)
2. GTL Ltd. (formerly Global Tele-Systems Ltd.) (74 per cent)
