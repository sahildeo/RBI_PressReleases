Monitoring FIIs investment in Indian Companies
October 17, 2002
The Reserve Bank of India has notified on October 17, 2002 that Foreign Institutional
Investors (FIIs) can now purchase equity shares and convertible debentures of HDFC Bank
Ltd., up to 49 per cent of its  paid up capital through primary/secondary markets in India, as
the HDFC Bank Ltd., has passed a resolution to this effect at its Board of Directors' meeting
and a special resolution in its General Body Meeting.
