RBI rejects  licence application of
Chapra District Central Co-operative Bank Ltd.
February 14, 2002
The Reserve Bank of India has rejected the licence application of the Chapra District
Central Co-operative Bank Ltd., Bihar to carry on banking business.  The bank is precluded
from transacting the business of `banking' as defined in the Banking Regulation Act, 1949
(As applicable to Co-operative Societies) and it will not be in order for it, among other things,
to accept deposits from public and allow withdrawal by cheques, draft, order or otherwise.
