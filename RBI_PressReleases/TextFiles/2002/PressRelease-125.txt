RBI rejects  licence application of The Gulbarga Urban Co-operative Bank Ltd.
December 4, 2002
The Reserve Bank of India has rejected the licence application of The
Gulbarga Urban Co-operative Bank Ltd., Gulbarga  (Karnataka) to carry on banking
business.  The bank is precluded from transacting the business of `banking' as defined
in the Banking Regulation Act, 1949 (As applicable to Co-operative Societies) and it
will not be in order for it, among other things, to accept deposits from public and
allow withdrawal by cheques, draft, order or otherwise.
