Underwriting by Primary Dealers
June 4, 2002
The Reserve Bank of India has set the cut-off rate for underwriting by Primary
Dealers for the Price Based Auction of "7.40% Government Stock, 2012"  to be
auctioned on June 5, 2002 at Re.0.08 (eight paise).
The Reserve Bank of India has not accepted any bid for Price Based Auction of
"8.35% Government Stock, 2022" to be auctioned on June 5, 2002.
Ajit Prasad
Manager
