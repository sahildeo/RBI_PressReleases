RBI rejects  licence application of
Rajampet Co-operative Town Bank Ltd.
The Reserve Bank of India has rejected the licence application of the
Rajampet Co-operative Town Bank Ltd., Rajampet (Andhra Pradesh) to carry on
banking business.  The bank is precluded from transacting the business of `banking'
as defined in the Banking Regulation Act, 1949 (As applicable to Co-operative
Societies) and it will not be in order for it, among other things, to accept deposits
from public and allow withdrawals by cheque, draft, order or otherwise.
