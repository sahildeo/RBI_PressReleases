RBI gives `In Principle' approval for
one more Primary Dealer
May 2, 2002
The Reserve Bank of India has given `in principle' approval to Citicorp Finance
(India) Ltd. to set up a 100% subsidiary company for accreditation as Primary Dealer in the
Government securities market.
The new company will become operational as a primary dealer after terms and
conditions are settled between it and the Reserve Bank of India.
