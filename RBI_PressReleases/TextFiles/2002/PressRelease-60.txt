Investment by Foreign Institutional Investors
December 18, 2002
The Reserve Bank of India has notified on December 18, 2002 that Foreign Institutional
Investors ( FIIs) can now purchase equity shares and convertible debentures of Sun
Pharmaceutical Industries Ltd., up to 49 per cent of its paid up capital through primary and
stock exchanges, as the company has passed a resolution to this effect at its Board and
General Body Meeting.
P. V. Sadanandan
Manager
