Banking Licence rejected
August 17, 2002
The Reserve Bank of India has rejected under Section 22 of the Banking Regulation
Act, 1949 (AACS), the licence application of the District Central Co-op. Bank Ltd., Gonda,
Uttar Pradesh to carry on banking business in India.  The bank is precluded from transacting
the business of banking as defined in the Banking Regulation Act, 1949 (As applicable to Co-
operative Societies) and it will not be in order for it, among other things, to accept deposits
from public and allow withdrawal by cheque, draft, order or otherwise.
