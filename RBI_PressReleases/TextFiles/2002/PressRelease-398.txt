Underwriting by Primary Dealers
October 7, 2002
The Reserve Bank of India has set the cut-off rate for underwriting by Primary
Dealers for the Price Based Auction of "7.27% Government Stock, 2013" and "7.95%
Government Stock, 2032"  to be held on October 8, 2002 at Re.0.01 (one paisa) and Re.0.03
(three paise) respectively.
Ajit Prasad
Manager
