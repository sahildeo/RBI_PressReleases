RBI to conduct OMO Sales on August 6, 2002
August 5, 2002
The Reserve Bank of India has decided to offer from its investment portfolio the
following security for sale through uniform price auction on August 6, 2002.
- 11.75% GOI 2004 for upto Rs.3000 crore
The application form together with the terms and conditions of the auction may be
obtained from the Chief General Manager, Internal Debt Management Cell, Reserve Bank of
th
India, 16 floor, Central Office Building, Mumbai or from Public Debt Office, Reserve Bank of
India, Mumbai Office.
The bids should be tendered on August 6, 2002 upto 12.30 pm and should be deposited in
the tender box specially kept for the purpose at Reserve Bank of India, Fort, Mumbai.
On the basis of the tenders received, the Reserve Bank will determine the minimum price
upto which tenders for sale of Government securities will be accepted at the auction. The auction
will be conducted on uniform price basis. The results of the auction will be displayed at the
Reserve Bank of India, Fort, Mumbai. The settlement will be on August 7, 2002. The Reserve
Bank has the right to accept or reject any or all the bids either wholly or partially if deemed fit
without assigning any reason.
