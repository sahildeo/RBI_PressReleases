Investment by FIIs/NRIs /PIOs - Monitoring of overall ceiling
April 4, 2002
The Reserve Bank of India on April 4, 2002 has notified that no further purchases
of shares of ICICI Ltd., should be made through stock exchanges in India on behalf of
foreign institutional investors (FIIs)/ non-resident Indians (NRIs) and persons of Indian
origin (PIOs) as the holdings by FIIs / NRIs and PIOs have reached the overall ceiling of
49 per cent of its paid up equity capital.
