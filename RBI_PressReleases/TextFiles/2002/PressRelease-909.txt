Liquidity Adjustment Facility (LAF)
Quarterly Auction Calendar
June 19, 2002
The Reserve Bank of India announces the calendar for the daily Repo and Reverse Repo and
fortnightly Repo for the quarter July – September 2002  under the Liquidity Adjustment
Facility (LAF).
There is no change in the existing terms and conditions under which the LAF auctions are
conducted.
