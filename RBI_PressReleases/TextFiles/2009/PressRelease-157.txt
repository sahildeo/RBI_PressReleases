November 25, 2009 
Monitoring of Foreign Investment by FIIs under PIS :  
Pantaloon Retail (India) Limited 
The Reserve Bank of India has today notified that the aggregate share 
holdings in Pantaloon Retail (India) Limited by Foreign Institutional Investors (FIIs) 
under Portfolio Investment Scheme (PIS) has gone below the prescribed threshold 
limit stipulated under the extant FDI policy. Therefore, no further purchases of equity 
shares of the company in the primary/secondary markets on behalf of FIIs would be 
allowed without obtaining prior clearance of the Reserve Bank of India.
