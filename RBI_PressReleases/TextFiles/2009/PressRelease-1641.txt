February 27, 2009 
 
RBI announces Auction based OMO 
Will purchase Dated Securities of Rs 6,000 crore  
(with green-shoe option of Rs.3,000 crore) on March 5, 2009 
 
 
The Reserve Bank of India has decided to purchase dated Government securities for 
an aggregate amount of Rs.6,000 crore with a green-shoe option to purchase up to 50 
per cent of the notified amount through a price-based auction using the uniform price 
method on March 5, 2009. The settlement would be on March 6, 2009.  The other 
details of the auction such as individual securities, modalities, etc., would be announced 
at market close on March 4, 2009.  
 
Alpana Killawala
