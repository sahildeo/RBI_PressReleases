 April 24, 2009 
 
Three State Governments announce Auction of  
State Development Loans 2019 for Rs. 2638.850 on April 28, 2009 
 
The following three State Governments have announced the sale of their 10-
year State Development Loans (SDLs) for an aggregate amount of Rs.2638.850 
crore through yield based auction using multiple price auction method.  
 
Sr. State Notified Amount 
No. (Rs. crore) 
1 Andhra Pradesh 1600.000 
2 Jammu & Kashmir 538.850 
3 Punjab  500.000 
 Total 2638.850 
 
The auction will be conducted by the Reserve Bank of India (RBI) at Mumbai 
on April 28, 2009 (Tuesday). Bids in the prescribed form obtainable from the 
Regional Director, Reserve Bank of India, Public Debt Office, Main Building, Fort, 
Mumbai - 400001 should be submitted to that Office on Tuesday, April 28, 2009. The 
competitive bids by the NDS members should be submitted only in electronic format 
using Primary Market Operation (PMO) module of NDS. All bids should be submitted 
by 12.30 P.M on April 28, 2009.  On the basis of bids received, RBI will determine 
the maximum yield at which offers will be accepted. 
 
The results of the auction will be announced on April 28, 2009 and payment 
by successful bidders will be made during banking hours on April 29, 2009 
(Wednesday) at Mumbai and at respective Regional Offices of Reserve Bank of 
India.  
 
The investment in Government Stocks will be reckoned as an eligible 
investment in Government Securities by banks for the purpose of Statutory Liquidity 
Ratio (SLR) under Section 24 of the Banking Regulation Act, 1949. The stocks will 
qualify for the ready forward facility.
