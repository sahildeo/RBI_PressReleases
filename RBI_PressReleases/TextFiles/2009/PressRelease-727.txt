Open Market Operations ñ Auction based 
Purchase of dated Government security of Rs 6000 crore on Aug 6, 2009 
The Reserve Bank of India has decided to purchase dated Government securities for an 
aggregate amount of Rs 6000 crore through multi-security auction using the multiple price 
method on Aug 6, 2009 (Thursday). The settlement would be on Aug 7, 2009 (Friday).

Market participants may take note of the following: 
There is an overall aggregate ceiling amount of Rs 6000 crore for all the securities in the    
basket put together. There is no security-wise notified amount. 
 RBI reserves the right to: 
ï Decide on the quantum of purchase of individual securities  
ï Accept less than the aggregate amount of Rs 6000 crore  
ï Purchase marginally higher than the aggregate amount due to rounding effects.  
ï  Accept or reject any or all of the offers either wholly or partially if deemed fit without    
          assigning reason. 
The auction shall be conducted by the Reserve Bank of India, Mumbai Office, Fort, Mumbai on 
Aug 6, 2009 (Thursday). 
Offers in the prescribed form obtainable from the Regional Director, Reserve Bank of India, 
Mumbai Office, Fort, Mumbai-400001 and/or RBI website www.rbi.org.in should be submitted to 
that Office on Aug 6, 2009. The amount and price of offer in the physical offer tender forms 
should be indicated both in number and words. The NDS members should submit their 
competitive offers in electronic format using Open Market Operation (OMO) module of NDS. All 
offers should be submitted by 12.30 P.M. 
The result of the auction will be announced on Aug 6, 2009 and payment to successful 
offerers will be made during banking hours on Aug 7, 2009 (Friday). Successful offerers may 
note to make available the requisite amount of securities in their SGL account in the forenoon on 
Aug 7, 2009 (Friday).
