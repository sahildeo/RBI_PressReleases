Our Goal Must Be to make A Difference: RBI Governor tells Staff;  
Releases Special RBI Platinum Jubilee Logo 
 
Marking Platinum Jubilee (75 years) birthday is a momentous milestone in the 
history of the Reserve Bank and a time for joy and celebration. It is also an occasion to 
reflect on the evolution of the Reserve Bank and to ensure how in the changing 
environment to fulfill our mandate "to operate the currency and credit system of the 
country to its advantage"ÖìOur goal must be to make a difference to the life of every 
Indian, every day,î said Dr. D Subbarao, Governor, Reserve Bank of India in his 
message to all RBI employees today on the occasion of the beginning of the Reserve 
Bank of Indiaís Platinum Jubilee Celebrations.  
The Reserve Bank of India, established on April 1, 1934 celebrates the year 
2009-2010 as its Platinum Jubilee Year.   
The Governor also released a special logo on the occasion. The logo uses 
colours of the national flag indicating the strong linkage that the Reserve Bank has with 
the country's economy. The logo also has Mahatma Gandhi in it taken from the 
currency note which is the link between the Reserve Bank and the common person. In 
sum, the logo represents the continuation of the Reserve Bank's commitment to be a 
more responsive, relevant, professional and effective public policy institution. The logo 
will be used throughout the Platinum Jubilee year in all RBI communication.  
The Reserve Bank of India will soon announce a detailed plan for marking its 
Platinum Jubilee.
