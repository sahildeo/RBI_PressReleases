April 02, 2009 
Auction based OMO 
Purchase of Dated securities of Rs 6000 crore on April 8, 2009 
The Reserve Bank of India has decided to purchase dated Government 
securities for an aggregate amount of Rs.6000 crore through multi-security auction 
using the uniform price method on April 8, 2009 (Wednesday). The settlement would be 
on April 9, 2009 (Thursday).  
List of Eligible securities 
Sr. 
No Security Maturity date 
1. 7.59% GS 2016 April 12, 2016 
2. 7.49% GS 2017 April 16, 2017 
3. 8.35% GS 2022 May 14, 2022 
4. 7.95% GS 2032 August 28, 2032 
5. 8.33% GS 2036 June 7, 2036 
Market participants may take note of the following: 
1. There is an overall aggregate ceiling amount of Rs.6000 crore for all the 
securities in the basket put together. There is no security-wise notified amount. 
2. RBI reserves the right to 
a. Decide on the quantum of purchase of individual securities 
b. Accept less than the aggregate amount of Rs.6000 crore 
c. Purchase marginally higher than the aggregate amount due to rounding 
effects 
d. Accept or reject any or all of the offers either wholly or partially if deemed 
fit without assigning reason.  
The auction shall be conducted by the Reserve Bank of India, Mumbai Office, 
Fort, Mumbai on April 8, 2009 (Wednesday).  
Offers in the prescribed form obtainable from the Regional Director, Reserve 
Bank of India, Mumbai Office, Fort, Mumbai-400001 and/or RBI website www.rbi.org.in 
should be submitted to that Office on April 8, 2009. The amount and price of offer in the 
physical offer tender forms should be indicated both in number and words.  The NDS 
members should submit their competitive offers in electronic format using Open Market 
Operation (OMO) module of NDS. All offers should be submitted by 12.30 P.M.  
The result of the auction will be announced on April 8, 2009 and payment to 
successful offerers will be made during banking hours on April 9, 2009 (Thursday).
