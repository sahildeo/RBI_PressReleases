Reserve Bank Rejects Application for Certificate of Registration of 
M/s Sahu Mutual Benefit & Finance Ltd.  
 
 The Reserve Bank of India has on December 18, 2008 rejected the application for 
certificate of registration of M/s Sahu Mutual Benefit & Finance Ltd., Sahu Theatre Building, 
Hazratganj, Lucknow for carrying on the business of a non-banking financial institution. 
Following rejection of the application for certificate of registration M/s Sahu Mutual Benefit & 
Finance Ltd., cannot transact the business of a non-banking financial institution.  
 
 The Reserve Bank can reject the application for Certificate of Registration of a non-
banking financial company under powers conferred by Section 45-IA (6) of the Reserve Bank 
of India Act, 1934. The business of a non-banking financial institution is defined in clause (a) 
of Section 45-I of the Reserve Bank of India Act, 1934.
