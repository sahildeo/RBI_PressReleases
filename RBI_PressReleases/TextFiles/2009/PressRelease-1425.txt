Underwriting Auctions for Government securities ñ  
ì7.56 per cent Government Stock 2014î and  
"7.94 per cent Government Stock 2021" 
 
The Government of India have announced the sale (reissue) of securities 
ì7.56 per cent Government Stock 2014î for a notified amount of Rs.8,000 crore 
(nominal) and ì7.94 per cent Government Stock 2021î for a notified amount of Rs. 
4,000 crore (nominal)  respectively, through auctions to be held on April 2, 2009. The 
underwriting auctions for the captioned securities will be conducted on March 31, 
2009 (Tuesday). 
 
As per the revised scheme of underwriting, dated November 14, 2007, the 
amounts of Minimum Underwriting Commitment (MUC) and the minimum bidding 
commitment under Additional Competitive Underwriting (ACU) for the underwriting 
auctions, per Primary Dealer, are as under:

The underwriting auctions will be conducted using multiple price based 
auction method. Primary Dealers (PDs) may submit their bids for ACU auctions 
electronically through NDS-PDO module between 10.30 AM and 12.30 PM on March 
31, 2009 (Tuesday). 
 
The underwriting commission will be credited to the current account of the 
respective PDs at the RBI, Fort, Mumbai on the date of issue of securities.
