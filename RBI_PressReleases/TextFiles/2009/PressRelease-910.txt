SECOND LIQUIDITY ADJUSTMENT FACILITY ON  
REPORTING FRIDAYS 
 
In response to suggestions from the market participants, the Reserve Bank of 
India will now conduct the auctions under the Second Liquidity Adjustment Facility 
(SLAF) between 4.15 PM and 4.45 PM from July 3, 2009.  
It may be recalled that Reserve Bank of India has been conducting SLAF 
between 4.00 PM and 4.30 PM on reporting Fridays or the immediate preceding working 
day in case the reporting Friday is a Bank holiday in Mumbai with effect from August 1, 
2008.
