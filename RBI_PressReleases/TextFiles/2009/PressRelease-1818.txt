CGFS Report on Capital Flows and Emerging Market Economies released 
 
The Committee on the Global Financial System (CGFS) of the Bank for 
International Settlements (BIS) has today released a report entitled Capital Flows and 
Emerging Market Economies prepared by a working group chaired by Dr Rakesh Mohan, 
Deputy Governor, Reserve Bank of India.  
 
The Report can be accessed at the BIS website 
http://www.bis.org/publ/cgfs33.pdf?noframes=1
