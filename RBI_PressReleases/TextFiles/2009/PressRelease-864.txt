Market Stabilisation Scheme: Ceiling For Fiscal Year 2009-10 
 
In accordance with the provisions of the Memorandum of Understanding 
(MoU) on the Market Stabilisation Scheme (MSS), the ceiling for the outstandings 
under the MSS for the fiscal year 2009-10 has been fixed at Rs.50,000 crore. The 
threshold at which this ceiling will be reviewed   is when the outstandings reach 
Rs.35,000 crore. The ceiling and the threshold have been agreed between the 
Government of India and the Reserve Bank.  
 
The current MSS outstanding balance (face value) is Rs.23,273 crore. MSS 
redemptions of Rs.15,536 crore are due during the remaining part of the current 
fiscal year.
