July 21, 2009  
Open Market Operations ñ Auction based 
Purchase of dated Government security of Rs 6000 crore on July 23, 2009 
The Reserve Bank of India has decided to purchase dated Government 
securities for an aggregate amount of Rs 6000 crore through multi-security auction 
using the multiple price method on July 23, 2009 (Thursday). The settlement would be 
on July 24, 2009 (Friday). 
List of Eligible securities 
Sr. No  Security Maturity date 
1. 7.56% GS 2014  03-Nov-2014 
2. 6.35% GS 2020  02-Jan-2020 
3. 8.33% GS 2036  07-Jun-2036 
Market participants may take note of the following: 
There is an overall aggregate ceiling amount of Rs 6000 crore for all the securities 
in the basket put together. There is no security-wise notified amount. 
RBI reserves the right to: 
ï Decide on the quantum of purchase of individual securities  
ï Accept less than the aggregate amount of Rs 6000 crore  
ï Purchase marginally higher than the aggregate amount due to rounding effects.  
Accept or reject any or all of the offers either wholly or partially if deemed fit 
without assigning reason. 
The auction shall be conducted by the Reserve Bank of India, Mumbai Office, 
Fort, Mumbai on July 23, 2009 (Thursday). 
Offers in the prescribed form obtainable from the Regional Director, Reserve 
Bank of India, Mumbai Office, Fort, Mumbai-400001 and/or RBI website www.rbi.org.in 
should be submitted to that Office on July 23, 2009. The amount and price of offer in 
the physical offer tender forms should be indicated both in number and words. The 
NDS members should submit their competitive offers in electronic format using Open 
Market Operation (OMO) module of NDS. All offers should be submitted by 12.30 P.M. 
The result of the auction will be announced on July 23, 2009 and payment to 
successful offerers will be made during banking hours on July 24, 2009 
(Friday). Successful offerers may note to make available the requisite amount of 
securities in their SGL account in the forenoon on July 24, 2009 (Friday).
