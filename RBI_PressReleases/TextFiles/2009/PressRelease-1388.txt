Underwriting Auctions for Government securities ñ  
ì6.05 per cent Government Stock 2019î and  
"7.50 per cent Government Stock 2034" 
 
The Government of India have announced the sale (reissue) of securities ì6.05 
per cent Government Stock 2019î for a notified amount of Rs. 8,000 crore (nominal) 
and ì7.50 per cent Government Stock 2034î for a notified amount of Rs. 4,000 crore 
(nominal)  respectively, through auctions to be held on April 9, 2009. The underwriting 
auctions for the captioned securities will be conducted on April 8, 2009 (Wednesday). 
 
As per the revised scheme of underwriting, dated November 14, 2007, the 
amounts of Minimum Underwriting Commitment (MUC) and the minimum bidding 
commitment under Additional Competitive Underwriting (ACU) for the underwriting 
auctions, per Primary Dealer, are as under:

The underwriting auctions will be conducted using multiple price based auction 
method. Primary Dealers (PDs) may submit their bids for ACU auctions electronically 
through NDS-PDO module between 10.30 AM and 12.30 PM on April 8, 2009 
(Wednesday). 
 
The underwriting commission will be credited to the current account of the 
respective PDs at the RBI, Fort, Mumbai on the date of issue of securities.
