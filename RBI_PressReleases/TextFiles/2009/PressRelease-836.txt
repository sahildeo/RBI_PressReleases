Issuance Calendar for Marketable Dated Securities 
Consequent upon the revision in the market borrowing requirement of the 
Government announced in the regular Budget for the fiscal 2009-10, it has become 
necessary for the Government of India to raise additional resources. Accordingly, in 
modification of the issuance calendar announced through the RBI Press Release 
2008-2009/1603 dated March 26, 2009, the Government of India, in consultation with 
the Reserve Bank of India, has decided to issue a new issuance calendar for 
marketable dated securities. The following indicative calendar for issue of the dated 
securities covering the period from July 18, 2009 to September 30, 2009 is, 
therefore, being issued:
