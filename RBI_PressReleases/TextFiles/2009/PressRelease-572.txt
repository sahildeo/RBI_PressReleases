September 1, 2009 
 
Open Market Operations ñ Auction based Purchase of  
dated Government security of Rs 6000 crore on September 3, 2009 
The Reserve Bank of India has decided to purchase dated Government 
securities for an aggregate amount of Rs 6000 crore through multi-security auction 
using the multiple price method on September 3, 2009 (Thursday). The settlement 
would be on September 4, 2009 (Friday). 
 
Sr. No Security Maturity Date 
1. 7.38% GS 2015 03-Sep-2015 
2. 8.24% GS 2018 22-Apr-2018 
3. 7.95% GS 2032 28-Aug-2032 
Market participants may take note of the following: 
There is an overall aggregate ceiling amount of Rs 6000 crore for all the 
securities in the basket put together. There is no security-wise notified amount. 
 
RBI reserves the right to: 
ï Decide on the quantum of purchase of individual securities  
ï Accept less than the aggregate amount of Rs 6000 crore  
ï Purchase marginally higher than the aggregate amount due to rounding 
effects.  
ï Accept or reject any or all of the offers either wholly or partially if deemed fit 
without assigning reason. 
The auction shall be conducted by the Reserve Bank of India, Mumbai Office, 
Fort, Mumbai on September 3, 2009 (Thursday). 
Offers in the prescribed form obtainable from the Regional Director, Reserve 
Bank of India, Mumbai Office, Fort, Mumbai-400001 and/or RBI website 
www.rbi.org.in should be submitted to that Office on September 3, 2009. The amount 
and price of offer in the physical offer tender forms should be indicated both in 
number and words. The NDS members should submit their competitive offers in 
electronic format using Open Market Operation (OMO) module of NDS. All offers 
should be submitted by 12.30 P.M. 
         The result of the auction will be announced on September 3, 2009 and 
payment to successful offerers will be made during banking hours on September 4, 
2009 (Friday). Successful offerers may note to make available the requisite amount 
of securities in their SGL account in the forenoon on September 4, 2009 (Friday).
