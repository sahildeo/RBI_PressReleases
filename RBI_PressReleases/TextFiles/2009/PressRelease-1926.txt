January 16, 2009 
Eight State Governments announce Auction of  
State Development Loans 2019 for Rs.6,551.160 on January 22, 2009 
The following State Governments have announced the sale of their 10-year State 
Development Loans (SDLs) for an aggregate amount of Rs. 6,551.160 crore through a yield 
based auction using multiple price auction method.  
Sr. No. State Notified Amount 
(Rs. crore) 
1 Andhra Pradesh 1,632.600 
2 Bihar 730.930 
3 Gujarat 1,000.000 
4 Himachal Pradesh 300.000 
5 Madhya Pradesh 885.000 
6 Manipur 103.300 
7 Tamil Nadu 800.000 
8 Uttar Pradesh 1099.330 
 
Total   6,551.160 
 
Note: Governments of Gujarat and Tamil Nadu will have the option to 
retain additional subscription upto a maximum of Rs.250.00 crore and 
Rs.200.00 crore, respectively over and above their respective notified 
amount. 
The auction will be conducted by the Reserve Bank of India (RBI) at Mumbai on 
January 22, 2009 (Thursday). Bids in the prescribed form obtainable from the Regional 
Director, Reserve Bank of India, Public Debt Office, Main Building, Fort, Mumbai - 400001 
should be submitted to that Office on Thursday, January 22, 2009. The competitive bids by 
the NDS members should be submitted only in electronic format using Primary Market 
Operation (PMO) module of NDS. All bids should be submitted by 12.30 P.M. on January 22, 
2009.  On the basis of bids received, RBI will determine the maximum yield at which offers 
will be accepted. 
  The results of the auction will be announced on January 22, 2009 and payment by 
successful bidders will be made during banking hours on January 23, 2009 (Friday) at 
Mumbai and at respective Regional Offices of Reserve Bank of India.  
The investment in Government Stocks will be reckoned as an eligible investment in 
Government Securities by banks for the purpose of Statutory Liquidity Ratio (SLR) under 
Section 24 of the Banking Regulation Act, 1949. The stocks will qualify for the ready forward 
facility.
