September 2, 2009 
  
Monitoring of Ceiling for Investment by FIIs under PIS :  
M/s Adani Enterprises Ltd. 
 
The Reserve Bank India has today notified that no further purchases of 
shares of M/s Adani Enterprises Ltd., should be made through stock exchanges in 
India on behalf of Non-Resident Indians (NRIs)/ Persons of Indian Origin (PIOs). The 
Reserve Bank of India has stated that the aggregate share holding of NRIs/PIOs 
under Portfolio Investment Scheme (PIS) in M/s Adani Enterprises Ltd., has reached 
the overall limit of 10 per cent of its paid up equity capital.
