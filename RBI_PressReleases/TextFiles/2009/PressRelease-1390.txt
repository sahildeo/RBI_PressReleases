April 06, 2009 
RBI's Special Fixed Rate Term Repo Auction for Rs. 56,000 Crore Today 
The Reserve Bank of India shall conduct a special fixed rate term repo at 5.0 per 
cent per annum against eligible securities for Rs. 56,000 crore today i.e, April 06, 2009, 
due for reversal on April 20, 2009, with a view to enabling banks to meet the liquidity 
requirements of Mutual Funds (MFs), Non-Banking Financial Companies (NBFCs) and 
Housing Finance Companies (HFCs) either on incremental or on rollover basis. Of the 
outstanding amount of Rs.4,140 crore as on April 02, 2009, Rs.140 crore is due for 
redemption today. 
  
The special fixed rate term repo under Liquidity Adjustment Facility is available 
till September 30, 2009 up to a cumulative amount of Rs.60,000 crore on outstanding 
basis to banks exclusively for the purpose of meeting requirements of MFs, NBFCs and 
HFCs.
