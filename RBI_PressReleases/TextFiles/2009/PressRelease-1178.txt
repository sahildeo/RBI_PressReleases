May 14, 2009 
 
RBI seeks Comments on Draft Guidelines on Stripping/  
Reconstitution of Government Securities 
 
The Reserve Bank of India has today placed on its website "Draft Guidelines 
on Stripping/Reconstitution of Government Securities" for comments/views. 
Comments/views on the draft guidelines may be sent by post to the Chief General 
Manager, Internal Debt Management Department, Reserve Bank of India, Mumbai 
400001 or can be emailed on or before May 29, 2009. 
 
The Reserve Bank of India will finalise and issue the guidelines after 
examining the views/feedback received.
