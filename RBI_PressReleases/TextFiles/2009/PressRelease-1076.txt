Underwriting Auctions for Government securities  
 
The Government of India have announced the sale of New Government Stock of 6 years 
for a notified amount of Rs.8,000 crore (nominal), sale (reissue) of î8.20 per cent Government 
Stock 2022î  for a notified amount of Rs.5,000 crore (nominal)  and ì7.50 per cent Government 
Stock 2034î for a notified amount of   Rs.2,000 crore (nominal), through auctions to be held on 
June 5, 2009. The underwriting auctions for the captioned securities will be conducted on    
June 4, 2009 (Thursday).
