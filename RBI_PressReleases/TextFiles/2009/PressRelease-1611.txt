March 04, 2009 
 
Ten State Governments announce Auction of State Development Loans 2019  
on March 9, 2009 for Rs.14,997.515 crore 
 
The following State Governments have announced the sale of their 10-year 
State Development Loans (SDLs) for an aggregate amount of Rs.14,997.515 crore 
through a yield based auction using multiple price auction method. 
  
Sr. State Notified Amount 
No. (Rs. crore) 
1 Assam 2,505.640 
2 Bihar 1438.000

4 Jammu & Kashmir 207.000

6 Maharashtra  3,898.810 
7 Meghalaya 25.640 
8 Tamil Nadu  1,216.800 
9 Uttar Pradesh 3,000.000 
10 West Bengal  1,967.095 
  Total 14,997.515 
 
The auction will be conducted by the Reserve Bank of India (RBI) at Mumbai on 
March 09, 2009 (Monday). Bids in the prescribed form obtainable from the Regional 
Director, Reserve Bank of India, Public Debt Office, Main Building, Fort, Mumbai - 
400001 should be submitted to that Office on Monday, March 09, 2009. The 
competitive bids by the NDS members should be submitted only in electronic format 
using Primary Market Operation (PMO) module of NDS. All bids should be submitted by 
12.30 P.M on March 09, 2009.  On the basis of bids received, RBI will determine the 
maximum yield at which offers will be accepted. 
 
  The results of the auction will be announced on March 09, 2009 and payment by 
successful bidders will be made during banking hours on March 12, 2009 (Thursday) at 
Mumbai and at respective Regional Offices of Reserve Bank of India.  
 
The investment in Government Stocks will be reckoned as an eligible investment 
in Government Securities by banks for the purpose of Statutory Liquidity Ratio (SLR) 
under Section 24 of the Banking Regulation Act, 1949. The stocks will qualify for the 
ready forward facility.
