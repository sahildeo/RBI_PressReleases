                                                                                                                  May 08, 2009 
 
Government of India announce the sale of  
two dated securities for Rs.12,000 crore on May 14, 2009 
 
The Government of India have announced the sale of a “New 5-year 
Government Stock” for a notified amount of Rs.8,000 crore (nominal) through yield 
based auction using uniform price method. The Government of India have also 
announced the sale (re-issue) of “8.20 per cent Government Stock 2022” for a notified 
amount of Rs.4,000 crore (nominal) through price based auction using uniform price 
method. The auctions will be conducted by the Reserve Bank of India, Mumbai Office, 
Fort, Mumbai on May 14, 2009 (Thursday). 
 
 Up to 5% of the notified amount of the sale of both the stocks will be allotted 
to eligible individuals and institutions as per the Scheme for Non-Competitive Bidding 
Facility in the Auction of Government Securities. 
 
 Bids in the prescribed form obtainable from the Regional Director, Reserve 
Bank of India, Mumbai Office (Public Debt Office), Fort, Mumbai- 400001 and RBI 
website www.rbi.org.in should be submitted to that Office on May 14, 2009. The NDS 
members should submit competitive as well as non-competitive bids in electronic 
format using Primary Market Operation (PMO) module of NDS. All bids should be 
submitted by 12.30 P.M.  
 
 The result of the auctions will be announced on May 14, 2009 and payment by 
successful bidders will be during banking hours on May 15, 2009 (Friday).  
 
 The Stocks will qualify for the ready forward facility. 
 
 The underwriting of the Government Securities under auctions by the ‘Primary 
Dealers’ will be as per the “Revised Scheme of Underwriting Commitment and 
Liquidity Support” announced by the Reserve Bank vide circular RBI/2007-08/186 
dated November 14, 2007. Bids for underwriting of the Additional Competitive 
Underwriting (ACU) portion can be submitted by ‘Primary Dealers’ up to 12.30 P.M. 
on May 13, 2009 (Wednesday) using PMO module of NDS. 
 
The Stocks will be eligible for “When Issued” trading for a period commencing 
from May 11-14, 2009 in accordance with the guidelines on ‘When Issued 
transactions in Central Government Securities’ issued by the Reserve Bank of India 
vide circular No. RBI /2006-07/178 dated November 16, 2006 as amended from time 
to time.
