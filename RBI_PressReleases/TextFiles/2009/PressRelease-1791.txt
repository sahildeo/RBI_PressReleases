Investment by FIIs/NRIs/PIOs under PIS  
The Reserve Bank of India has today notified that the aggregate share 
holding in Punjab National Bank by Foreign Institutional Investors (FIIs)/Non 
Resident Indians (NRIs)/Persons of Indian Origin (PIOs) under Portfolio Investment 
Scheme (PIS) has gone below stipulated limit of 20 per cent of their paid up capital. 
Further purchases of equity shares of the bank would be allowed only after obtaining 
prior approval of the Reserve Bank of India.
