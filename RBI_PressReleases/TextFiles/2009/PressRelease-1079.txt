June 02, 2009 
Auction based OMO - Purchase of Dated securities of Rs 6000 crore on June 4, 2009 
The Reserve Bank of India has decided to purchase dated Government 
securities for an aggregate amount of Rs.6000 crore through multi-security auction 
using the multiple price method on June 4, 2009 (Thursday). The settlement would be 
on June 5, 2009 (Friday). 
List of Eligible securities 
Sr. No Security Maturity date 
1. 7.49% GS 2017 April 16, 2017 
2. 8.35% GS 2022 May 14, 2022 
3. 7.95% GS 2032 August 28, 2032 
Market participants may take note of the following: 
There is an overall aggregate ceiling amount of Rs.6000 crore for all the 
securities in the basket put together. There is no security-wise notified amount. 
RBI reserves the right to: 
ï Decide on the quantum of purchase of individual securities 
ï Accept less than the aggregate amount of Rs.6000 crore 
ï Purchase marginally higher than the aggregate amount due to rounding effects. 
 
           Accept or reject any or all of the offers either wholly or partially if deemed fit 
without assigning reason. 
            The auction shall be conducted by the Reserve Bank of India, Mumbai Office, 
Fort, Mumbai on June 4, 2009 (Thursday). 
            Offers in the prescribed form obtainable from the Regional Director, Reserve 
Bank of India, Mumbai Office, Fort, Mumbai-400001 and/or RBI website www.rbi.org.in 
should be submitted to that Office on June 4, 2009. The amount and price of offer in the 
physical offer tender forms should be indicated both in number and words. The NDS 
members should submit their competitive offers in electronic format using Open Market 
Operation (OMO) module of NDS. All offers should be submitted by 12.30 P.M. 
            The result of the auction will be announced on June 4, 2009 and payment to 
successful offerers will be made during banking hours on June 5, 2009 (Friday).  
Successful offerers may note to make available the requisite amount of securities in 
their SGL account in the forenoon on June 5, 2009 (Friday).
