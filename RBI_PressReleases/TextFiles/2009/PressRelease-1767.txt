February 9, 2009 
Investment by FIIs under PIS  
The Reserve Bank of India has today notified that the aggregate share 
holdings in Arihant Foundations & Housing Limited by Foreign Institutional Investors 
(FIIs) under Portfolio Investment Scheme (PIS) has gone below the  stipulated 
caution limit of its paid up capital. FIIs/PIOs who have obtained permission to invest 
under the Portfolio Investment Scheme are now allowed to purchase equity shares 
of the company in the secondary market subject to the existing ceilings and 
guidelines in this regard.
