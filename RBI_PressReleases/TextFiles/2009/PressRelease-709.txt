Auction of dated securities scheduled for August 7, 2009- Rejection of bids 
 
The Government of India had announced the sale (re-issue) of (i) ì6.49 percent 
Government Stock 2015î for a notified amount of Rs.4,000 crore (nominal) (ii) ì6.90

India in consultation with the Reserve Bank of India have rejected all the bids submitted
