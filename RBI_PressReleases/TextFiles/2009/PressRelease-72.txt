December 15, 2009 
Investment by FIIs/NRIs/PIOs under PIS : Union Bank of India 
The Reserve Bank of India has today notified that the aggregate net 
purchases of equity shares in Union Bank of India by Foreign Institutional Investors 
(FIIs)/Non-Resident Indians (NRIs)/Persons of Indian Origin (PIOs) under Portfolio 
Investment Scheme (PIS) has gone below the prescribed trigger limit of their paid up 
capital. Therefore, further purchases of shares of the above bank in the secondary 
market can be made on behalf of those FIIs/NRIs/PIOs who have obtained 
permission to invest under the Portfolio Investment Scheme subject to the existing 
ceilings and guidelines in this regard.
