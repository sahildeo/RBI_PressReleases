Investment by FIIs under PIS  
 
 The Reserve Bank of India today notified that the undernoted companies have 
agreed to enhance the limit for purchase of its equity shares and convertible 
debentures by Foreign Institutional Investors (FIIs) up to 49 per cent of its total paid 
up capital. The company has passed necessary resolution at the Annual General 
Meeting/Extra Ordinary General Meeting agreeing for enhancing the limit for the 
purchase of its equity shares and convertible debentures by FIIs and a special 
resolution by its shareholders to do so. 
 
1. Hindustan Construction Co. Ltd. 
2. Suzlon Energy Ltd. 
 
 Foreign Institutional Investors can now purchase under the Portfolio 
Investment Scheme through primary market and stock exchanges, equity shares and 
convertible debentures of the above companies, provided :  
 
(i) The total purchases of all FIIs do not exceed the applicable overall ceiling limits 
of 49% of total paid-up equity capital and total paid-up value of each series of 
convertible debentures of the company. 
 
(ii) The purchases of equity shares by a single FII/SEBI approved sub-account of a 
registered FII in the company does not exceed 10% (Ten per cent) of the paid-
up equity capital of the company.
