April 21, 2009 
 
Auction based OMO - Purchase of Dated securities of Rs 6000 crore on April 
23, 2009 
 
The Reserve Bank of India has decided to purchase dated Government securities 
for an aggregate amount of Rs.6000 crore through multi-security auction using the 
uniform price method on April 23, 2009 (Thursday). The settlement would be on 
April 24, 2009 (Friday).  
 
List of Eligible securities 
Sr. 
No Security Maturity date 
1. 7.38% GS 2015 September 3, 2015 
2. 8.07% GS 2017 January 15, 2017 
3. 8.24% GS 2018 April 22, 2018 
4. 8.35% GS 2022 May 14, 2022 
5. 7.95% GS 2032 August 28, 2032 
 
Market participants may take note of the following: 
 
There is an overall aggregate ceiling amount of Rs.6000 crore for all the securities in 
the basket put together. There is no security-wise notified amount. 
RBI reserves the right to 
Decide on the quantum of purchase of individual securities 
Accept less than the aggregate amount of Rs.6000 crore 
Purchase marginally higher than the aggregate amount due to rounding effects 
Accept or reject any or all of the offers either wholly or partially if deemed fit without 
assigning reason.  
 
The auction shall be conducted by the Reserve Bank of India, Mumbai Office, Fort, 
Mumbai on April 23, 2009 (Thursday).  
 
Offers in the prescribed form obtainable from the Regional Director, Reserve Bank of 
India, Mumbai Office, Fort, Mumbai-400001 and/or RBI website www.rbi.org.in 
should be submitted to that Office on April 23, 2009. The amount and price of offer in 
the physical offer tender forms should be indicated both in number and words. The 
NDS members should submit their competitive offers in electronic format using Open 
Market Operation (OMO) module of NDS. All offers should be submitted by 12.30 
P.M.  
 
The result of the auction will be announced on April 23, 2009 and payment to 
successful offerers will be made during banking hours on April 24, 2009 (Friday).
