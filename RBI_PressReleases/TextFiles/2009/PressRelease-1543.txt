March 13, 2009 
Eleven State Governments announce Auction of State  
Development Loans 2019 on March 17, 2009 for Rs. 12,114.920 crore 
The following State Governments have announced the sale of their 10-year 
State Development Loans (SDLs) for an aggregate amount of Rs. 12,114.920 crore 
through a yield based auction using multiple price auction method.  
Sr. State Notified Amount 
No. (Rs. crore) 
1 Andhra Pradesh 1915.000 
2 Gujarat 1660.000 
3 Himachal Pradesh* 400.000 
4 Karnataka 2917.000 
5 Kerala 500.000 
6 Madhya Pradesh 1010.250 
7 Nagaland 184.580 
8 Rajasthan 592.610 
9 Uttar Pradesh 2594.000 
10 Uttarakhand 94.000 
11 Puducherry 247.480 
 Total 12,114.920 
*- Government of Himachal Pradesh will have the option to retain additional subscription upto a 
maximum of Rs.100.000 crore over and above their respective notified amount. 
 
The auction will be conducted by the Reserve Bank of India (RBI) at Mumbai 
on March 17, 2009 (Tuesday). Bids in the prescribed form obtainable from the 
Regional Director, Reserve Bank of India, Public Debt Office, Main Building, Fort, 
Mumbai - 400001 should be submitted to that Office on Tuesday, March 17, 2009. 
The competitive bids by the NDS members should be submitted only in electronic 
format using Primary Market Operation (PMO) module of NDS. All bids should be 
submitted by 12.30 P.M on March 17, 2009.  On the basis of bids received, RBI will 
determine the maximum yield at which offers will be accepted. 
The results of the auction will be announced on March 17, 2009 and payment 
by successful bidders will be made during banking hours on March 18, 2009 
(Wednesday) at Mumbai and at respective Regional Offices of Reserve Bank of 
India.  
The investment in Government Stocks will be reckoned as an eligible 
investment in Government Securities by banks for the purpose of Statutory Liquidity 
Ratio (SLR) under Section 24 of the Banking Regulation Act, 1949. The stocks will 
qualify for the ready forward facility.
