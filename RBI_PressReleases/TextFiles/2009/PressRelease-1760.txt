Issuance Calendar for Marketable Dated Securities  
To meet the additional expenditure approved by the Parliament by way of two 
Supplementary Demands for Grants and various stimulus packages announced by the Government, 
it has been considered necessary by the Government of India to raise additional resources. The 
Government of India, in consultation with the Reserve Bank of India, has therefore, decided to issue 
a new issuance calendar for marketable dated securities in continuation of the issuance calendar 
announced through the RBI Press Release 2008-2009/1044 dated January 06, 2009. Accordingly, 
the following indicative calendar for issue of dated securities covering the period from February 20, 
2009 to March 31, 2009 as given below is being issued in consultation with the Government of India.

All the auctions covered by the calendar will have the facility of non-competitive bidding scheme 
under which 5 per cent of the notified amount will be reserved for specified retail investors. 
 Variable rate bonds may be issued depending upon market conditions. 
As in the past, the Central Government/Reserve Bank reserves the right to have the flexibility to 
modify the above calendar in terms of notified amount, issuance period, maturity, etc. keeping in 
view the emerging requirements of the Government, market conditions and other relevant factors 
after giving due notice.
