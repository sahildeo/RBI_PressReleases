Auction for sale (re-issue) of "7.46 per cent Government Stock 2017", "8.35 per 
cent Government Stock 2022" and "7.50 per cent Government Stock 2034"  
 
The Reserve Bank of India has rejected all the bids received in the auctions held 
today, March 13, 2009, for the sale (re-issue) of "7.46 per cent Government Stock 
2017", "8.35 per cent Government Stock 2022" and "7.50 per cent Government Stock 
2034". The revised date of the auction, the details of the securities, the method of 
auction, etc. will be announced in due course.
