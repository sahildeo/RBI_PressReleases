March 18, 2009  
 
Open Market Operations ñ Auction based Purchase of  
dated Government security of Rs 10,000 crore on March 19, 2009 
 
The Reserve Bank of India has decided to purchase the following dated 
Government securities for an aggregate amount of Rs.10,000 crore through price-
based auction using multiple price method on March 19, 2009 (Thursday).  
 
Sr. 
No Security Notified Amount (Rs. crore) 
1 7.37% GS 2014 3000 
2 7.56% GS 2014 3000 
3 7.99% GS 2017 4000 
 
The auction shall be conducted by the Reserve Bank of India, Mumbai Office, 
Fort,  Mumbai on March 19, 2009 (Thursday).  
 
Offers in the prescribed form obtainable from the Regional Director, Reserve 
Bank of India, Mumbai Office, Fort, Mumbai-400001 and/or RBI website 
www.rbi.org.in should be submitted to that Office on March 19, 2009. The NDS 
members should submit their competitive offers in electronic format using Open 
Market Operation (OMO) module of NDS. All offers should be submitted by 12.30 
P.M.  
 
The result of the auction will be announced on March 19, 2009 and payment 
to successful offerers will be made during banking hours on March 23, 2009 
(Monday).  
 
Reserve Bank has the right to accept or reject any or all of the offers either 
wholly or partially if deemed fit without assigning any reason.
