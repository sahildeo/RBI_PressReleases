Investment by FIIs under PIS : Matra Realty Ltd. 
The Reserve Bank of India has today notified that the aggregate share 
holding in Matra Realty Ltd. by Foreign Institutional Investors (FIIs) under Portfolio 
Investment Scheme (PIS) in the primary/secondary markets has reached the limit of 
22 per cent of their paid up capital and no further purchases of equity shares of the  
above company on behalf of FIIs would be allowed without obtaining prior clearance 
of the Reserve Bank of India.
