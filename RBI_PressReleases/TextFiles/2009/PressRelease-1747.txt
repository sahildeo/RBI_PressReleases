Investment by FIIs/NRIs/PIOs under PIS  
The Reserve Bank of India has today notified that the aggregate share 
holdings in (1) Bank of Baroda and (2) Union Bank of India by Foreign Institutional 
Investors (FIIs)/ Non Resident Indians (NRIs) / Persons of Indian Origin (PIOs) under 
Portfolio Investment Scheme (PIS)  have gone below the  stipulated caution limit of 
20% of their paid up capital. FIIs/NRIs/PIOs who have obtained permission to invest 
under the Portfolio Investment Scheme would be allowed only after obtaining prior 
approval of the Reserve Bank of India to purchase equity shares of the banks in the 
primary/secondary market.
