January 30, 2009 
 
Seven State Governments announce Auction of State Development  
Loans 2019 for Rs.4,748.970 on February 5 
 
The following State Governments have announced the sale of their 10-year State 
Development Loans (SDLs) for an aggregate amount of Rs.4,748.970 crore through a yield 
based auction using multiple price auction method.  
 
Sr. No. State Notified Amount 
(Rs. crore) 
1 Goa 200.000 
2 Haryana 1,295.000 
3 Himachal Pradesh 100.000 
4 Punjab  353.970 
5 Rajasthan # 800.000 
6 Tamil Nadu # 1,000.000 
7 West Bengal  1,000.000 
 Total   4,748.970 
 
# Governments of Rajasthan and Tamil Nadu will have the option to retain 
additional subscription upto a maximum of Rs.200.00 crore each over and 
above their respective notified amount. 
 
The auction will be conducted by the Reserve Bank of India (RBI) at Mumbai on February 
5, 2009 (Thursday). Bids in the prescribed form obtainable from the Regional Director, Reserve 
Bank of India, Public Debt Office, Main Building, Fort, Mumbai - 400001 should be submitted to 
that Office on Thursday, February 5, 2009. The competitive bids by the NDS members should 
be submitted only in electronic format using Primary Market Operation (PMO) module of NDS. 
All bids should be submitted by 12.30 P.M. on February 5, 2009.  On the basis of bids received, 
RBI will determine the maximum yield at which offers will be accepted. 
 
  The results of the auction will be announced on February 5, 2009 and payment by 
successful bidders will be made during banking hours on February 6, 2009 (Friday) at Mumbai 
and at respective Regional Offices of Reserve Bank of India.  
 
The investment in Government Stocks will be reckoned as an eligible investment in 
Government Securities by banks for the purpose of Statutory Liquidity Ratio (SLR) under 
Section 24 of the Banking Regulation Act, 1949. The stocks will qualify for the ready forward 
facility.
