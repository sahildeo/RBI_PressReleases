April 28, 2009 
 
State Government of West Bengal announces Auction of  
State Development Loan 2019 for Rs.2500.00 crore on May 4, 2009 
 
The State Government of West Bengal has announced the sale of its 10-year 
State Development Loan for an aggregate amount of Rs.2500.00 crore through yield 
based auction using multiple price auction method.  
 
The auction will be conducted by the Reserve Bank of India (RBI) at Mumbai 
on May 4, 2009 (Monday). Bids in the prescribed form obtainable from the Regional 
Director, Reserve Bank of India, Public Debt Office, Main Building, Fort, Mumbai - 
400001 should be submitted to that Office on Monday, May 4, 2009. The competitive 
bids by the NDS members should be submitted only in electronic format using 
Primary Market Operation (PMO) module of NDS. All bids should be submitted by 
12.30 P.M on May 4, 2009.  On the basis of bids received, RBI will determine the 
maximum yield at which offers will be accepted. 
 
The results of the auction will be announced on May 4, 2009 and payment by 
successful bidders will be made during banking hours on May 5, 2009 (Tuesday) at 
Mumbai and at Kolkata Regional Office of Reserve Bank of India.  
 
The investment in Government Stocks will be reckoned as an eligible 
investment in Government Securities by banks for the purpose of Statutory Liquidity 
Ratio (SLR) under Section 24 of the Banking Regulation Act, 1949. The stocks will 
qualify for the ready forward facility.
