Investment by FIIs  under PIS  
The Reserve Bank of India has today notified that the aggregate share 
holdings in Grasim Industries Limited by Foreign Institutional Investors (FIIs) under 
Portfolio Investment Scheme (PIS) have reached the trigger limit of their paid up 
capital. Hence, further purchases of equity shares in the primary/secondary market 
of this company would be allowed only after obtaining prior approval of the Reserve 
Bank of India.
