March 03, 2009 
Mapusa Urban Co-operative Bank of Goa Ltd., Mapusa, Goa - Penalised 
The Reserve Bank of India has imposed a monetary penalty of Rs.5.00 lakh 
(Rupees Five Lakh only) on Mapusa Urban Co-operative Bank of Goa Ltd., Mapusa, 
Goa in exercise of powers vested in it under the provisions of Section 47A(1)(b) read 
with Section 46(4) of the Banking Regulation Act, 1949 (As Applicable to Co-operative 
Societies) for violation of the Reserve Bank of India's directives relating to maximum 
limits on unsecured advances and directions on lending operations. 
The Reserve Bank of India had issued a show cause notice to the bank, in 
response to which the bank submitted a written reply. Based on the reply, the Reserve 
Bank of India came to the conclusion that the violation was substantiated and warranted 
imposition of penalty. Accordingly, it penalised the bank.
