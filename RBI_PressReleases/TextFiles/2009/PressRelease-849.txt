July 15, 2009 
Underwriting Auctions for Government securities  
The Government of India have vide their notifications dated July 10, 2009, 
announced the sale (reissue) of securities ì6.07 per cent Government Stock 2014î 
for a notified amount of Rs. 6,000 crore (nominal), ì7.94 per cent Government Stock 
2021î for a notified amount of Rs. 4,000 crore (nominal),  and  "7.40 per cent 
Government Stock 2035" for a notified amount of Rs.2,000 crore (nominal) 
respectively, through auctions to be held on July 17, 2009. The underwriting auctions 
for the captioned securities will be conducted on July 16, 2009 (Thursday). 
 As per revised scheme of underwriting, dated November 14, 2007, the 
amounts of Minimum Underwriting Commitment (MUC) and the minimum bidding 
commitment under Additional Competitive Underwriting (ACU) for the underwriting 
auctions, per Primary Dealer, are as under:

 The underwriting auctions will be conducted using multiple price based

 The underwriting commission will be credited to the current account of the
