Underwriting Auctions for Government of India Securities  
 
The Government of India have announced the sale (reissue)  of ì6.07 per cent 
Government Stock 2014î for a notified amount of Rs.6,000 crore (nominal), î7.94 per 
cent Government Stock 2021î  for a notified amount of Rs.4,000 crore (nominal), ì8.24 
per cent Government Stock 2027î for a notified amount of Rs.3,000 crore (nominal) and 
ì7.40 per cent Government Stock 2035î for a notified amount of Rs.2,000 crore 
(nominal), through auctions to be held on June 26, 2009. The underwriting auctions for 
the captioned securities will be conducted on June 25, 2009 (Thursday). 
 
 As per the Revised Scheme of Underwriting, dated November 14, 2007, the 
amounts of Minimum Underwriting Commitment (MUC) and the minimum bidding 
commitment under Additional Competitive Underwriting (ACU) for the underwriting 
auctions, per Primary Dealer, are as under:

 The underwriting auctions will be conducted using multiple price based auction 
method. Primary Dealers (PDs) may submit their bids for ACU auctions electronically 
through Negotiated Dealing System (NDS) between 10.30 AM and 12.30 PM on June 
25, 2009 (Thursday). 
 
 The underwriting commission will be credited to the current account of the 
respective PDs at the RBI, Fort, Mumbai on the date of issue of securities.
