December 23, 2009 
 
RBI seeks comments on Draft Guidelines on 
Note Authentication and Fitness Sorting Parameters 
 
The Reserve Bank of India has placed on its website today "Draft Guidelines 
on Note Authentication and Fitness Sorting Parameters" for comments /views, which 
may be sent by post to the Chief General Manager, Department of Currency 
Management, Reserve Bank of India, 4th floor, Amar Building, Sir P.M. Road, 
Mumbai 400001 or may be sent by email on or before January 06, 2010. 
 
The Reserve Bank of India will finalize and issue the guidelines after 
examining the views/feedback received.
