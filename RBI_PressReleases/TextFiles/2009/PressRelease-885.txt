July 07, 2009  
Auction based OMO - Purchase of Dated securities of Rs 7500 crore on July 9, 2009 
The Reserve Bank of India has decided to purchase dated Government 
securities for an aggregate amount of Rs.7500 crore through multi-security auction 
using the multiple price method on July 9, 2009 (Thursday). The settlement would be 
on July 10, 2009 (Friday). 
List of Eligible securities
Sr. No Security Maturity date
1. 6.05% GS 2019 (Feb) 02-Feb-2019 
2. 6.30% GS 2023 09-Apr-2023 
3. 7.95% GS 2032 28-Aug-2032 
Market participants may take note of the following: 
           There is an overall aggregate ceiling amount of Rs.7500 crore for all the 
securities in the basket put together. There is no security-wise notified amount. 
RBI reserves the right to: 
ï Decide on the quantum of purchase of individual securities 
ï Accept less than the aggregate amount of Rs.7500 crore 
ï Purchase marginally higher than the aggregate amount due to rounding effects. 
 
           Accept or reject any or all of the offers either wholly or partially if deemed fit 
without assigning reason. 
           The auction shall be conducted by the Reserve Bank of India, Mumbai Office, 
Fort, Mumbai on July 9, 2009 (Thursday). 
            Offers in the prescribed form obtainable from the Regional Director, Reserve 
Bank of India, Mumbai Office, Fort, Mumbai-400001 and/or RBI website 
www.rbi.org.in should be submitted to that Office on July 9, 2009. The amount and 
price of offer in the physical offer tender forms should be indicated both in number 
and words. The NDS members should submit their competitive offers in electronic 
format using Open Market Operation (OMO) module of NDS. All offers should be 
submitted by 12.30 P.M. 
         The result of the auction will be announced on July 9, 2009 and payment to 
successful offerers will be made during banking hours on July 10, 2009 (Friday).  
Successful offerers may note to make available the requisite amount of securities in 
their SGL account in the forenoon on July 10, 2009 (Friday).
