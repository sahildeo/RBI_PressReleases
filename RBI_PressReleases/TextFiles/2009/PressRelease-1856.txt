More Outlets for Sale of RBI Publications 
 
 The Reserve Bank of India publications will now be available at book stores of 
M/s. Jain Book Agency, C-9, Cannaught Place, New Delhi - 110 001 and through its 
more than 100 dealer network (Annex I). This arrangement is in addition to the existing 
sales counters at the Reserve Bank, Central Office and 19 of its  regional offices 
(Annex II). The additional arrangements have been made keeping in view the 
increasing demand of RBI publications and their wider dissemination. The Reserve 
Bank of India releases a large number of publications containing information on areas 
relating to the economy, banking and finance sector.
