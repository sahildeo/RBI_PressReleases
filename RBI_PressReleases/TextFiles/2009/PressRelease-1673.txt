 February 24, 2009 
Ten State Governments announce Auction of State Development  
Loans 2019 for Rs.12,759.110 crore on February 27, 2009  
The following State Governments have announced the sale of their 10-year 
State Development Loans (SDLs) for an aggregate amount of Rs.12,759.110 crore 
through a yield based auction using multiple price auction method.  
Sr. State Notified Amount 
No. (Rs. crore)

* Government of Madhya Pradesh, Rajasthan and Tamil Nadu have Green Shoe 
option of Rs.215 crore, Rs.250 crore and Rs.200 crore, respectively. 
The auction will be conducted by the Reserve Bank of India (RBI) at Mumbai 
on February 27, 2009 (Friday). Bids in the prescribed form obtainable from the 
Regional Director, Reserve Bank of India, Public Debt Office, Main Building, Fort, 
Mumbai - 400001 should be submitted to that Office on Friday, February 27, 2009. 
The competitive bids by the NDS members should be submitted only in electronic 
format using Primary Market Operation (PMO) module of NDS. All bids should be 
submitted by 12.30 P.M on February 27, 2009.  On the basis of bids received, RBI 
will determine the maximum yield at which offers will be accepted. 
The results of the auction will be announced on February 27, 2009 and 
payment by successful bidders will be made during banking hours on March 02, 
2009 (Monday) at Mumbai and at respective Regional Offices of Reserve Bank of 
India.  
The investment in Government Stocks will be reckoned as an eligible 
investment in Government Securities by banks for the purpose of Statutory Liquidity 
Ratio (SLR) under Section 24 of the Banking Regulation Act, 1949. The stocks will 
qualify for the ready forward facility.
