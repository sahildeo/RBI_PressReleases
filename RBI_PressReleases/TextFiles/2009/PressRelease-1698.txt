February 18, 2009 
 
Open Market Operations ñ Auction based 
Purchase of Dated securities of Rs 6000 crore on February 19, 2009 
 
The Reserve Bank of India has decided to purchase the following dated 
securities for an aggregate amount of Rs.6000 crore through price-based auction using 
multiple price method on February 19, 2009. 
 
(1)     8.24% GS, 2018 for Rs.3000 crore. 
(2)     7.94% GS, 2021 for Rs.2000 crore. 
(3)     7.40% GS, 2035 for Rs.1000 crore. 
 
The auction shall be conducted by the Reserve Bank of India, Mumbai Office, 
Fort, Mumbai on February 19, 2009 (Thursday).  
 
Offers in the prescribed form obtainable from the Regional Director, Reserve 
Bank of India, Mumbai Office (Public Debt Office), Fort, Mumbai-400001 and RBI 
website www.rbi.org.in should be submitted to that Office on February 19, 2009. The 
NDS members should submit their competitive offers in electronic format using Open 
Market Operation (OMO) module of NDS. All offers should be submitted by 12.30 P.M.  
 
The result of the auction will be announced on February 19, 2009 (Thursday) 
and payment to successful offerers will be made during banking hours on February 21, 
2009 (Saturday).  
 
The Reserve Bank has the right to accept or reject any or all of the offers either 
wholly or partially if deemed fit without assigning any reason.
