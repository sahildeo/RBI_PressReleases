Reserve Bank transfers surplus profit to Government of India 
 
The Reserve Bank's Central Board, in its meeting held today, approved the 
transfer of surplus profit to the Government of India amounting to Rs. 25,009 crore for 
the year ended June 30, 2009 as against Rs. 15,011 crore for the year ended June 30, 
2008.
