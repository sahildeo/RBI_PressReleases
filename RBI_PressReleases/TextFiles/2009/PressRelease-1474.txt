 March 23, 2009 
Auction based OMO 
Purchase of Dated securities of Rs 10,000 crore on March 25, 2009 
The Reserve Bank of India has decided to purchase dated Government 
securities for an aggregate amount of Rs.10,000 crore through multi-security auction 
using the uniform price method on March 25, 2009 (Wednesday).  The eligible 
securities to be offered for this purchase auction are the following:  
Sr. 
No Security Maturity date 
1 7.38% GS 2015 September 3, 2015 
2 8.24% GS 2018 April 22, 2018 
3 7.94% GS 2021 May 24, 2021 
4 8.24% GS 2027 February 15 2027 
5 8.33% GS 2036 June 7, 2036 
Market participants may take note of the following: 
There is an overall aggregate ceiling amount of Rs.10,000 crore for all the 
securities in the basket put together. There is no security-wise notified amount. 
RBI reserves the right to 
a. Decide on the quantum of purchase of individual securities 
b. Accept less than the aggregate amount of Rs.10,000 crore 
c. Purchase marginally higher than the aggregate amount due to 
rounding effects 
d. Accept or reject any or all of the offers either wholly or partially if 
deemed fit without assigning reason.  
The auction shall be conducted by the Reserve Bank of India, Mumbai Office, 
Fort, Mumbai on March 25, 2009 (Wednesday).  
Offers in the prescribed form obtainable from the Regional Director, Reserve 
Bank of India, Mumbai Office, Fort, Mumbai-400001 and/or RBI website 
www.rbi.org.in should be submitted to that Office on March 25, 2009. The NDS 
members should submit their competitive offers in electronic format using Open 
Market Operation (OMO) module of NDS. All offers should be submitted by 12.30 
P.M.  
The result of the auction will be announced on March 25, 2009 and payment 
to successful offerers will be made during banking hours on March 26, 2009 
(Thursday).
