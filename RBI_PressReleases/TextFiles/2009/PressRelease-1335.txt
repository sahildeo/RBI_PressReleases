 April 16, 2009 
RBI's Special Fixed Rate Term Repo Auction for Rs. 59,910 Crore Today 
The Reserve Bank of India shall conduct a special fixed rate term repo at 5.0 
per cent per annum against eligible securities for Rs. 59,910 crore today i.e, April 16, 
2009, due for reversal on May 04, 2009, with a view to enabling banks to meet the 
liquidity requirements of Mutual Funds (MFs), Non-Banking Financial Companies 
(NBFCs) and Housing Finance Companies (HFCs) either on incremental or on 
rollover basis. The outstanding amount under this facility as on April 15, 2009 stood 
at Rs. 90 crore. 
  
The special fixed rate term repo under Liquidity Adjustment Facility is 
available till September 30, 2009 up to a cumulative amount of Rs.60,000 crore on 
outstanding basis to banks exclusively for the purpose of meeting requirements of 
MFs, NBFCs and HFCs.
