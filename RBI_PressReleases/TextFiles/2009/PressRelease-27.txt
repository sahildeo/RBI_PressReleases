Third Quarter Review of Monetary Policy 2009-10 on January 29, 2010 
 
Dr. D. Subbarao, Governor, Reserve Bank of India will release the Third 
Quarter Review of Monetary Policy 2009-10 on Friday, January 29, 2010. The 
Review will be released in a meeting with the chief executives of major scheduled 
commercial banks at 11.00 a.m. on January 29, 2010 in the Reserve Bank of India, 
Central Office, Mumbai.
