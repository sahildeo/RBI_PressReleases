July 20, 2009 
Investment by FIIs under PIS  
The Reserve Bank of India has today notified that the aggregate share 
holdings by Foreign Institutional Investors (FIIs) under Portfolio Investment Scheme 
(PIS) in Alchemist Ltd. have gone below the prescribed threshold limit stipulated 
under the FDI Policy. It will, therefore, be in order to purchase shares of the above 
company in the secondary market on behalf of FIIs who have obtained permission to 
invest under the Portfolio Investment Scheme subject to the existing ceilings and 
guidelines.
