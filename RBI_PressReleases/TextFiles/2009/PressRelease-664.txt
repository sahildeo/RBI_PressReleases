Reserve Bank Platinum Jubilee Celebrations - 
Panel Discussion on Challenges for Central Banks 
 The Reserve Bank of India today arranged a panel discussion on `Challenges 
for the Central Banks' in its Platinum Jubilee Celebrations programme.  The panelists 
were Shri M. Narasimham, Dr. C. Rangarajan and Dr. Y.V. Reddy, all former 
Governors of the Reserve Bank and Dr. D. Subbarao, present Governor of the 
Reserve Bank. 
 Dr. Shankar Acharya, Member, Board of Governors and Honorary Professor, 
Indian Council for Research on International Economic Relations (ICRIER) 
moderated the discussion.  The panel discussion was held in Hyderabad.  Eminent 
personalities from state government, research institutions, former and current 
Reserve Bank employees and media attended the discussion. 
 The web-cast of the discussion is available at the url:
