November 26, 2009 
Investment by FIIs under PIS :  Hindalco Industries Limited 
 The Reserve Bank of India today notified that Hindalco Industries Limited has 
agreed to enhance the limit for purchase of its equity shares and convertible 
debentures by Foreign Institutional Investors (FIIs) under the Portfolio Investment 
Scheme up to 40 per cent of its total paid up capital. The company has passed a 
resolution at the board of directors' level and extra-ordinary general meeting by its 
shareholders to do so. 
 Foreign Institutional Investors can now purchase under the Portfolio 
Investment Scheme through primary market and stock exchanges, equity shares and 
convertible debentures of Hindalco Industries Limited provided :  
(i) The total purchases of all FIIs does  not exceed the applicable overall ceiling 
limits of 40 per cent of total paid-up equity capital and total paid-up value of 
each series of convertible debentures of the company. 
(ii) The purchases of equity shares by a single FII/SEBI approved sub-account of a 
registered FII in the company does not exceed 10% (Ten per cent) of the paid-
up equity capital of the company.
