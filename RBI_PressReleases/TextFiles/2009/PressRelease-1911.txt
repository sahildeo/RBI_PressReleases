New Ferritic Stainless Steel (FSS) Coin of  Rs.2 – 
"Birth Bi-Centenary of Louis Braille, 1809 – 2009" 
 
The Reserve Bank of India will shortly put into circulation new Ferritic Stainless Steel 
coins of Rs.2 issued by Government of India to commemorate "Birth Bi-Centenary of Louis 
Braille,1809 - 2009". 
 
The coins shall conform to the following dimensions, designs and compositions namely: 
 
Denomination Shape Weight & Metal Composition 
of the Coins Outside diameter 
Two Rupees Circular with 27 mm diameter  Ferritic Stainless Steel 
Weight – 5.62 gms.                  Iron - 83% 
       Chromium -17%
