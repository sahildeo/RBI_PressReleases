September 15, 2009 
Release of Handbook of Statistics on Indian Economy 2008-09 
The Reserve Bank of India today released the eleventh volume of its annual 
statistical publication Handbook of Statistics on the Indian Economy (HBS) 2008-09. 
This publication is one of the major initiatives of the Reserve Bank aimed at 
improving data dissemination by providing statistical information at one place. 
Through this publication, the Reserve Bank has been providing time series data on 
various economic and financial indicators for the Indian economy. To mark the 
Platinum Jubilee of the Bank, this yearís Handbook is also being released in 
electronic form on the Data Base on Indian Economy (DBIE): Reserve Bankís Data 
Warehouse, http://dbie.rbi.org.in which would facilitate more frequent updating of 
information. 
The current volume contains 251 statistical tables covering national income 
aggregates, output, prices, money, banking, financial markets, public finances, 
foreign trade and balance of payments and select socio-economic indicators.  
The Handbook is brought out in four versions ñ print, CD-Rom, website and 
on the DBIE platform. This year's Handbook is a landmark as it provides for the 
dynamic updation of Handbook making it almost real time. This is an outcome of joint 
efforts of two research departments of the Bank, Department of Economic Analysis 
and Policy and Department of Statistics and Information Management. Data tables 
from the CD-Rom and DBIE platform can be saved in the Microsoft excel format in 
order to facilitate their use for modeling and forecasting by various statistical and 
econometric packages.
