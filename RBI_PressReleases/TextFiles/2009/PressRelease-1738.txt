 February 12, 2009 
 
Nine State Governments announce Auction of State  
Development Loans 2019  for Rs.8,362.240 on February 17, 2009 
 
The following State Governments have announced the sale of their 10-year 
State Development Loans (SDLs) for an aggregate amount of Rs. 8,362.240 crore 
through a yield based auction using multiple price auction method.  
 
Sr. State Notified Amount 
No. (Rs. crore) 
1 Andhra Pradesh        1,211.550  
2 Gujarat        1,499.000  
3 Himachal Pradesh           100.000  
4 Jharkhand           400.000  
5 Kerala           800.000  
6 Maharashtra        3,000.000  
7 Nagaland           100.000  
8 Tamil Nadu        1,000.000  
9 Uttarkhand           251.690  
 Total   8,362.240 
 
The auction will be conducted by the Reserve Bank of India (RBI) at Mumbai 
on February 17, 2009 (Tuesday). Bids in the prescribed form obtainable from the 
Regional Director, Reserve Bank of India, Public Debt Office, Main Building, Fort, 
Mumbai - 400001 should be submitted to that Office on Tuesday, February 17, 2009. 
The competitive bids by the NDS members should be submitted only in electronic 
format using Primary Market Operation (PMO) module of NDS. All bids should be 
submitted by 12.30 P.M. on February 17, 2009.  On the basis of bids received, RBI 
will determine the maximum yield at which offers will be accepted. 
 
The results of the auction will be announced on February 17, 2009 and 
payment by successful bidders will be made during banking hours on February 18, 
2009 (Wednesday) at Mumbai and at respective Regional Offices of Reserve Bank 
of India.  
 
The investment in Government Stocks will be reckoned as an eligible 
investment in Government Securities by banks for the purpose of Statutory Liquidity 
Ratio (SLR) under Section 24 of the Banking Regulation Act, 1949. The stocks will 
qualify for the ready forward facility.
