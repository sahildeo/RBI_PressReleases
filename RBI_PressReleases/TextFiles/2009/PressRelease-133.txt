RBI expands coverage of its ìDatabase on Indian Economyî 
 
The Reserve Bank of India has further expanded the coverage of its 
"Database on Indian Economy", an on line database built on the data warehousing 
technology with enhanced functionality, to include static reports on Directory of 
Authorised Dealers Branches of Scheduled Commercial Banks (SCBs) & 
Cooperative Banks, List of SLR securities. Business of SCBs State-wise distribution 
of deposit and credit of SCBs in India, Population group-wise distribution of deposit 
and credit of SCBs, External Commercial Borrowings ñ Annual Flows and 
Outstanding, etc. 
 
Simple Query (frequency wise) data on FEDAI Spot Rates ( Rupees), LIBOR 
Rates-Overnight ñ USD, EUR, BPG, JPY, Weighted Average - Call Money Rates, 
REPO / Reverse REPO under LAF (subject wise), Central Govt. Market Borrowings, 
etc., also provided. 
 
With the release of these data series, the Database on Indian Economy would 
now have 425 static reports (subject and frequency wise), 74 subject wise and 67 
frequency wise data query templates covering various sectors of the Indian 
economy.  
 
 The Reserve Bank of India's Database on Indian Economy is available on its 
website http://www.rbi.org.in under the Database Section. This can also be directly 
accessed through URL http://dbie.rbi.org.in. The coverage of the Database is 
continuously being expanded on the basis of the feedback received from the users of 
the database.  
 
 Users are requested to provide their feedback through a feedback link on the 
site so as to enable further improvements in coverage of data series in the system.
