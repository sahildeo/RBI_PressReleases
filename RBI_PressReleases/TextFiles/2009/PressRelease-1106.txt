May 27, 2009 
Investment by FIIs under PIS  
The Reserve Bank of India has today notified that the aggregate share 
holdings in Brandhouse Retails Ltd. by Foreign Institutional Investors (FIIs) under 
Portfolio Investment Scheme (PIS) has gone below 22% of its paid up capital. FIIs 
who have permission to invest under the Portfolio Investment Scheme are now 
allowed to purchase equity shares of the company in the secondary market subject 
to the existing ceilings and guidelines in this regard.
