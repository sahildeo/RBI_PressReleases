Underwriting Auctions for Government securities - ì7.46 per cent Government 
Stock 2017î, "8.35 per cent Government Stock 2022" and 
ì7.50 per cent Government Stock 2034î 
 
The Government of India have announced the sale (reissue) of securities ì7.46 
per cent Government Stock 2017î for a notified amount of Rs. 8,000 crore (nominal), 
ì8.35 per cent Government Stock 2022î for a notified amount of Rs. 2,000 crore 
(nominal) and "7.50 per cent Government Stock 2034" for a notified amount of Rs.2,000 
crore (nominal) respectively, through auctions to be held on March 13, 2009. The 
underwriting auctions for the captioned securities will be conducted on March 12, 2009 
(Thursday). 
 
As per the revised scheme of underwriting, dated November 14, 2007, the 
amounts of Minimum Underwriting Commitment (MUC) and the minimum bidding 
commitment under Additional Competitive Underwriting (ACU) for the underwriting 
auctions, per Primary Dealer, are as under:

The underwriting auctions will be conducted using multiple price based auction 
method. Primary Dealers (PDs) may submit their bids for ACU auctions electronically 
through NDS-PDO module between 10.30 AM and 12.30 PM on March 12, 2009 
(Thursday). 
 
The underwriting commission will be credited to the current account of the 
respective PDs at the RBI, Fort, Mumbai on the date of issue of securities.
