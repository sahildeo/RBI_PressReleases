June 25, 2009 
Investment by FIIs/NRIs/PIOs under PIS :  Network 18 Media & 
Investments Ltd (formerly Network 18 Fincap Ltd) 
The Reserve Bank of India has today notified that the aggregate share 
holdings in Network 18 Media & Investments Ltd. (formerly Network 18 Fincap Ltd.) 
by Foreign Institutional Investors (FIIs)/ Non Resident Indians (NRIs)/Persons of 
Indian Origin (PIOs) under Portfolio Investment Scheme (PIS) have gone below the 
caution limit of its paid up capital. Therefore, the restrictions placed on the purchase 
of shares of the above company may be treated as withdrawn with immediate effect. 
It will, therefore, now be in order to purchase the shares of the above company in the 
secondary market on behalf of FII and NRI/PIO clients who have obtained 
permission to invest under the Portfolio Investment Scheme subject to the existing 
ceilings and guidelines in this regard.
