January 06, 2009 
Issuance Calendar for Marketable Dated Securities 
Consequent upon the approval of the second Supplementary Demands for Grants by 
the Parliament and the fiscal stimulus packages announced by the Government, it has become 
necessary for the Government of India to raise additional resources. Accordingly, in 
supersession of the issuance calendar announced through the RBI Press Release 2008-
2009/839 dated December 5, 2008, the Government of India, in consultation with the Reserve 
Bank of India, has decided to issue a new issuance calendar for marketable dated securities. 
Accordingly, the following indicative calendar for issue of dated securities covering the period 
from January 5, 2009 to March 31, 2009 (as given below) is being issued in consultation with 
the Government of India.

All the auctions covered by the calendar will have the facility of non-competitive bidding 
scheme under which 5 per cent of the notified amount will be reserved for specified retail 
investors. 
Variable rate bonds may be issued depending upon market conditions. 
As in the past, the Central Government/Reserve Bank reserve the right to have the 
flexibility to modify the above calendar in terms of notified amount, issuance period, maturity 
etc., keeping in view the emerging requirements of the Government, market conditions and 
other relevant factors after giving due notice.
