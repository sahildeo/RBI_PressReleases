 April 13, 2009 
 
Four State Governments announce Auction of  
State Development Loans 2019 for Rs. 3577.450 crore on April 16, 2009  
The following four State Governments have announced the sale of their 10-
year State Development Loans (SDLs) for an aggregate amount of Rs.3577.45 crore 
through yield based auction using multiple price auction method.  
Sr. State Notified Amount 
No. (Rs. crore) 
1 Andhra Pradesh * 800.000 
2 Jharkhand 517.450 
3 Nagaland 260.000 
2 West Bengal 2000.000 
 Total 3577.450 
 
*Government of Andhra Pradesh will have the option to retain 
additional subscription to a maximum of Rs.200.00 crore over and 
above its notified amount. 
The auction will be conducted by the Reserve Bank of India (RBI) at Mumbai on 
April 16, 2009 (Thursday). Bids in the prescribed form obtainable from the Regional 
Director, Reserve Bank of India, Public Debt Office, Main Building, Fort, Mumbai - 
400001 should be submitted to that Office on Thursday, April 16, 2009. The 
competitive bids by the NDS members should be submitted only in electronic format 
using Primary Market Operation (PMO) module of NDS. All bids should be submitted 
by 12.30 P.M on April 16, 2009.  On the basis of bids received, RBI will determine 
the maximum yield at which offers will be accepted. 
The results of the auction will be announced on April 16, 2009 and payment by 
successful bidders will be made during banking hours on April 17, 2009 (Friday) at 
Mumbai and at respective Regional Offices of Reserve Bank of India.  
The investment in Government Stocks will be reckoned as an eligible 
investment in Government Securities by banks for the purpose of Statutory Liquidity 
Ratio (SLR) under Section 24 of the Banking Regulation Act, 1949. The stocks will 
qualify for the ready forward facility.
