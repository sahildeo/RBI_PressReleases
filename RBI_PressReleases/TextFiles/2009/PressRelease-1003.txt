Monitoring of Ceiling for Investment by FIIs under PIS : Saw Pipes Ltd. 
 
The Reserve Bank India has notified today that the Foreign Institutional 
Investors (FIIs) share holding of Saw Pipes Ltd. has reached 22 per cent of its paid 
up capital and has also requested to intimate immediately the total number and face 
value of the equity shares of this company proposed to be purchased under Portfolio 
Investment Scheme (PIS) through stock-exchange/s in India on behalf of FIIs. On 
receipt of such proposals, clearance will be given by the Reserve Bank of India, on 
first-come-first-served basis, after taking into account the purchases proposed to be 
made by all designated bank branches till 24 per cent limit is reached.
