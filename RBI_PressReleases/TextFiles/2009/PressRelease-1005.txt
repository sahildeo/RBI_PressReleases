Purchase of Dated securities of Rs 6000 crore on June 18, 2009 
The Reserve Bank of India has decided to purchase dated Government 
securities for an aggregate amount of Rs.6000 crore through multi-security auction 
using the multiple price method on June 18, 2009 (Thursday). The settlement would 
be on June 19, 2009 (Friday). 
List of Eligible securities 
Sr. No Security Maturity date 
1. 7.38% GS 2015 03-September-2015 
2. 8.35% GS 2022 14-May-2022 
3. 8.33% GS 2036 07-June-2036 
Market participants may take note of the following: 
There is an overall aggregate ceiling amount of Rs.6000 crore for all the 
securities in the basket put together. There is no security-wise notified amount. 
RBI reserves the right to: 
ï Decide on the quantum of purchase of individual securities 
ï Accept less than the aggregate amount of Rs.6000 crore 
ï Purchase marginally higher than the aggregate amount due to 
rounding effects. 
ï Accept or reject any or all of the offers either wholly or partially if 
deemed fit without assigning reason.  
ï The auction shall be conducted by the Reserve Bank of India, Mumbai 
Office, Fort, Mumbai on June 18, 2009 (Thursday). 
Offers in the prescribed form obtainable from the Regional Director, Reserve 
Bank of India, Mumbai Office, Fort, Mumbai-400001 and/or RBI website 
www.rbi.org.in should be submitted to that Office on June 18, 2009. The amount and 
price of offer in the physical offer tender forms should be indicated both in number 
and words. The NDS members should submit their competitive offers in electronic 
format using Open Market Operation (OMO) module of NDS. All offers should be 
submitted by 12.30 P.M. 
  The result of the auction will be announced on June 18, 2009 and payment to 
successful offerers will be made during banking hours on June 19, 2009 (Friday).  
Successful offerers may note to make available the requisite amount of securities in 
their SGL account in the forenoon on June 19, 2009 (Friday).
