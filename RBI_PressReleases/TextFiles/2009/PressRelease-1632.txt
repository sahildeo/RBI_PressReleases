March 02, 2009 
Shahada Peoples Co-operative Bank Ltd., Near Dondaicha Road, Shahada, 
District Nandurbar - Penalised 
The Reserve Bank of India has imposed a monetary penalty of Rs.1.00 lakh 
(Rupees One Lakh only) on Shahada Peoples Co-operative Bank Ltd., Near Dondaicha 
Road, Shahada, District Nandurbar in exercise of powers vested in it under the 
provisions of Section 47A(1)(b) read with Section 46(4) of the Banking Regulation Act, 
1949 (As Applicable to Co-operative Societies) for violation of the Reserve Bank of 
India's directives/guidelines. The bank had exceeded individual exposure ceiling by 
investing in IDFC Bonds. 
The Reserve Bank of India had issued a show cause notice to the bank, in 
response to which the bank submitted a written reply. Based on the reply, the Reserve 
Bank of India came to the conclusion that the violation was substantiated and warranted 
imposition of penalty. Accordingly, it penalised the bank.
