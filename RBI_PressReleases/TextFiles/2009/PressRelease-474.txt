September 17, 2009 
 
RBI seeks Comments on Draft Guidelines on  
Repo in Corporate Debt Securities  
 
The Reserve Bank of India has placed on its website today "Draft Guidelines 
on Repo in Corporate Debt Securities" for comments/views, which may be sent by 
post to the Chief General Manager, Internal Debt Management Department, 
Reserve Bank of India, Mumbai 400001 or may be sent by email on or before 
October 05, 2009. 
 
 The Reserve Bank of India will finalise and issue the guidelines after 
examining the views/ feedback received.
