September 22, 2009 
Open Market Operations ñ Auction based Purchase of dated Government 
security of Rs 6000 crore on September 24, 2009 
The Reserve Bank of India has decided to purchase dated Government 
securities for an aggregate amount of Rs 6,000 crore through multi-security auction 
using the multiple price method on September 24, 2009 (Thursday). The settlement 
would be on September 25, 2009 (Friday). 
 
Sr. No Security Maturity Date 
1. 7.56% GS 2014 03-Nov-2014 
2. 6.05% GS 2019 (Feb) 02-Feb-2019 
3. 7.94% GS 2021 24-May-2021 
4. 7.50% GS 2034 10-Aug-2034 
Market participants may take note of the following: 
There is an overall aggregate ceiling amount of Rs 6,000 crore for all the 
securities in the basket put together. There is no security-wise notified amount. 
RBI reserves the right to: 
ï Decide on the quantum of purchase of individual securities  
ï Accept less than the aggregate amount of Rs 6,000 crore  
ï Purchase marginally higher than the aggregate amount due to rounding effects.  
ï Accept or reject any or all of the offers either wholly or partially if deemed fit 
without assigning reason. 
The auction shall be conducted by the Reserve Bank of India, Mumbai Office, 
Fort, Mumbai on September 24, 2009 (Thursday). 
Offers in the prescribed form obtainable from the Regional Director, Reserve 
Bank of India, Mumbai Office, Fort, Mumbai-400001 and/or RBI website www.rbi.org.in 
should be submitted to that Office on September 24, 2009. The amount and price of 
offer in the physical offer tender forms should be indicated both in number and words. 
The NDS members should submit their competitive offers in electronic format using 
Open Market Operation (OMO) module of NDS. All offers should be submitted by 12.30 
P.M. 
           The result of the auction will be announced on September 24, 2009 and 
payment to successful offerers will be made during banking hours on September 25, 
2009 (Friday). Successful offerers may note to make available the requisite amount of 
securities in their SGL account in the forenoon on September 25, 2009 (Friday).
