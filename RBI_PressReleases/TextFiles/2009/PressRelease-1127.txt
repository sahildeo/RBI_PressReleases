Investment by FIIs/NRIs/PIOs under PIS : Punjab National Bank  
 
The Reserve Bank of India has today notified that the aggregate foreign share 
holding in the Punjab National Bank had reached 18% of its paid up capital i.e., less 
than 2% of the sectoral cap of 20% for the public sector banks (PSBs). Therefore, 
any further purchases of equity shares on behalf of Foreign Institutional Investors 
(FIIs)/ Non Resident Indians (NRIs)/Persons of Indian Origin (PIOs) who have 
permission under the Portfolio Investment Scheme (PIS) will now be allowed after 
obtaining prior approval of Reserve Bank of India.
