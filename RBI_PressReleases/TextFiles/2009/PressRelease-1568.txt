Issue of Rs.500 Denomination Banknotes with `R' Inset Letter  
in both numbering panels in Mahatma Gandhi Series ñ 2005  
bearing the signature of Dr. D. Subbarao, Governor 
 
The Reserve Bank of India will shortly issue Rs.500 denomination banknotes 
with `R' inset letter in both numbering panels in Mahatma Gandhi Series - 2005 
bearing the signature of Dr. D. Subbarao, Governor. Except for the change in the 
inset letter, the design of these notes to be issued now is similar in all respects to the 
banknotes in Mahatma Gandhi Series - 2005, with additional / new security features 
issued on October 21, 2005. All banknotes in the denomination of Rs.500 issued by 
the Reserve Bank in the past will continue to be legal tender.
