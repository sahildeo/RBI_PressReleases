Investment by FIIs/NRIs/PIOs under PIS  
The Reserve Bank of India has today notified that the aggregate share 
holding in the following banks by Foreign Institutional Investors (FIIs) / Non Resident 
Indians (NRIs) / Persons of Indian Origin (PIOs) under Portfolio Investment Scheme 
(PIS) have gone below the trigger limit of 18 per cent of their paid up capital. It will 
therefore be in order to purchase shares of the following banks in the secondary 
market on behalf of FIIs/ NRIs/PIOs who have obtained permission to invest under 
the Portfolio Investment Scheme.  
1) Indian Overseas Bank 
2) Punjab National Bank 
3) Bank of Baroda 
4) Union Bank of India
