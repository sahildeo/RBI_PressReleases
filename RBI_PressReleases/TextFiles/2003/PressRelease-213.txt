Underwriting by Primary Dealers
November 6, 2003
The Reserve Bank of India has set  the cut-off rate for underwriting bids by
Primary Dealers for the auction of the "GOI Floating Rate Bonds, 2012" to be held on
October 21, 2003 at Re.0.01 (1 paisa) per Rupees 100.

Manager
