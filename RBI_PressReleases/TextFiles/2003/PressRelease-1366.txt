8 per cent Relief Bonds, 2002 and 7 per cent Savings Bonds, 2002 discontinued :
New Bonds to be shortly Notified
February 28, 2003
As notified today by the Government of India, 8 per cent Relief Bonds, 2002 will cease
with effect from the close of business on February 28, 2003.
Similarly, the issue of 7 per cent Savings Bonds, 2002 will also cease with effect from the
close of business on February 28, 2003.
The issue of new bonds with revised coupon rates etc., will be notified by the
Government in due course.
Alpana Killawala
General Manager
