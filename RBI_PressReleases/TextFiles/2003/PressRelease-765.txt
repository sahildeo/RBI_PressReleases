Underwriting by Primary Dealers
July 14, 2003
The Reserve Bank of India has set  the cut-off rate for underwriting bids by Primary
Dealers for the price based auction of 7.95% Government Stock, 2032 and 6.35%
Government Stock, 2020"  to be held on July 15, 2003 at Re.0.02 (2 paise) and at Re.0.015
(1.5 Paise) per Rupees 100, respectively.
