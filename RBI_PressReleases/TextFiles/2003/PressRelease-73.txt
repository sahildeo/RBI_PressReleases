Investment by FIIs/NRIs/PIOs under PIS
December 12, 2003
The Reserve Bank of India has notified on December 12, 2003 that the holdings of
foreign institutional investors (FIIs)/non-resident Indians (NRIs) and persons of Indian origin
(PIOs) under the portfolio investment scheme (PIS) in  the paid up capital of Bharti Tele
Ventures Ltd., have gone below 48.5 per cent. The Reserve Bank has further notified that it will
be in order for FIIs/NRIs and PIOs to purchase the shares of the company in the secondary
market under the portfolio investment scheme subject to the existing ceilings and guidelines in
this regard.
