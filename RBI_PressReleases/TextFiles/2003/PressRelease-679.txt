Investment by Foreign Institutional Investors
July 31, 2003
       The Reserve Bank of India has notified on July 31, 2003 that Foreign Institutional
Investors (FIIs) can now purchase equity shares and convertible debentures of United
Breweries Ltd., up to 49 per cent of its paid up capital through primary and secondary
markets in India, as the company has passed a resolution at its Board and General
Body Meeting.
P.V. Sadanandan
Manager
