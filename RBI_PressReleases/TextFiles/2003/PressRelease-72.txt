RBI releases the Report of the Working Group
on instruments of Sterilization for public comments
December 12, 2003
The Reserve Bank of India today placed the full text of the Report of the Working
Group on Instruments of Sterilisation on its website.
It may be recalled that a summary of the Report was placed on the Bank's website on
December 2, 2003 for wider dissemination and comments. Comments on the Report on
Instruments of Sterlisation may be sent to the Chief General Manager, Internal Debt
Management Department, Reserve Bank of India, Central Office, 16th Floor, Central Office
Building, Shahid Bhagat Singh Marg, Fort, Mumbai-400 001 by post or by e-mail to
cgmidmc@rbi.org.in by December 31, 2003.
P. V. Sadanandan
Manager
