Underwriting by Primary Dealers
October 20, 2003
The Reserve Bank of India has set  the cut-off rate for underwriting bids by Primary
Dealers for the auction of the "5.69% Government Stock, 2018" to be held on October 21,
2003 at Re.0.01 (1 paisa) per Rupees 100.

Manager
