Certificate of Registration cancelled - Kalpavrikasha Industries Ltd.
(formerly Orchaltree Leasing & Financing Ltd.)
November 28, 2003
The Reserve Bank of India, in exercise of the powers conferred on it under  Section 45-IA of
the Reserve Bank of India Act, 1934, has cancelled  on November 13, 2003 the certificate of
registration issued to Kalpavriksha Industries Ltd. (formerly Orchaltree Leasing & Financing
Ltd.), Bombay Cotton Mill Compound, Dattaram Lad Path (Kalachowkly Road), Mumbai-
400 033 subsequently changed its address to 703, Dharmanand Tower, Opp. Prabhat Cinema,
Bhander Ali, Thane (W)-400 602 for carrying on the business of a Non-Banking Financial
Institution.
As such, the above company cannot transact the business of a non-banking  financial
institution as defined in Clause (a) of Section 45-I of the Reserve Bank of  India Act, 1934.
