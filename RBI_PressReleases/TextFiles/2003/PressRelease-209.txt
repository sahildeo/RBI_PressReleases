RBI rejects  licence application of
Jila Sahakari Kendriya Bank Maryadit, Raigarh
November 7, 2003
The Reserve Bank of India under Section 22 of the Banking Regulation Act, 1949 has
rejected the licence application of the Jila Sahakari Kendriya Bank Maryadit, Raigarh
(Chhattisgarh State) to carry on banking business. The bank is precluded from transacting the
business of `banking' as defined in the Banking Regulation Act, 1949 (As applicable to Co-
operative Societies).
As such, it will not be in order for the above bank, among other things, to accept
deposits from public and allow withdrawal by cheques, draft, order or otherwise.
