State Development Loans get Overwhelming Response
July 30, 2003
The sale `on tap' of 6.20 per cent ten year State Development Loans, 2013 in respect
of 28 State Governments received overwhelming response of about Rs.18,500 crore. The
issue opened for subscription on July 30, 2003 for a target amount of Rs.8000 crore.
The tap sale has been closed today. Excess subscriptions are being refunded to the
applicants after making partial allotment in respect of states whose loans are oversubscribed.
P. V. Sadanandan
Manager
