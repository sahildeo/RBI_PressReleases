State Government Loans on Tap to be sold on August 25
August 18, 2003
All the State Governments have offered to sell on tap 6.20 per cent State
Development Loan, 2015 having maturity of twelve years for an aggregate target amount of
Rs.8000 crore as the third tranche of additional market borrowing on account of debt swap
scheme in the current financial year. The sale will be held at the respective regional offices of
the Reserve Bank of India and at Mumbai (for all the 28 States) on August 25, 2003. The
Reserve Bank of India reserves the right to close the sale during the banking hours on or after
August 25, 2003 having regard to the requirements of the State Governments and the
subscriptions received.
Applications in the prescribed form obtainable from the Public Debt Office at the
Reserve Bank of India will be received at the aforesaid offices during business hours while
the sale is open.
It may be recalled that during the current fiscal year so far two tranches of the
additional market borrowings of the debt swap scheme were conducted on June 12, 2003 and
July 30, 2003 for Rs.7000 crore and Rs.8000 crore respectively.
