RBI cancels  licence of
the Pratibha Mahila Sahakari  Bank Ltd.
March 7, 2003
The Reserve Bank of India has cancelled vide Order dated February 25, 2003 the licence
granted to the Pratibha Mahila Sahakari Bank Ltd., Jalgaon, Dist. Jalgaon (Maharashtra) to
carry on banking business.  The bank is precluded from transacting the business of `banking'
as defined in the Banking Regulation Act, 1949 (As applicable to Co-operative Societies) and
it will not be in order for it, among other things, to accept deposits from public and allow
withdrawals by cheque, draft, order or otherwise.
