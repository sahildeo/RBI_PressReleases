Underwriting by Primary Dealers
August 6, 2003
The Reserve Bank of India has set  the cut-off rate for underwriting bids by Primary
Dealers for the auction of the new "25 year Government Stock" and "Floating rate Bond
2011" to be held on August 7, 2003 at Re.0.01 (1 paisa) and at Re.0.02 (2 Paise) per Rupees
100, respectively.
P. V. Sadanandan
Manager
