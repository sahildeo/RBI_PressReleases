Payment for 91-day Treasury Bills on March 15
March 12, 2003
In view of Friday, March 14, 2003 being declared as a holiday by the Maharashtra
State Government, the payment by successful bidders in 91-day Treasury Bills auction would
be on Saturday, March 15, 2003.
As per the press release of March 8, 2003, the auction of 91-day Government of India
Treasury Bills is to be held on Wednesday, March 12, 2003 for a notified amount of Rs. 500
crore. Successful bidders were earlier required to make payment on Friday, March 14, 2003.
P. V. Sadanandan
Manager
