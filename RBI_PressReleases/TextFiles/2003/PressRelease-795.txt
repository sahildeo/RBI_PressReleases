RBI rejects  licence application of
The Solapur Merchants' Co-operative Bank Ltd., Solapur
July 8, 2003
The Reserve Bank of India under Section 22 of the Banking Regulation Act, 1949 has
rejected the licence application of the Solapur Merchants  Co-operative Bank Ltd., Solapur,
Dist. Solapur, Maharashtra to carry on banking business.  The bank is precluded from
transacting the business of `banking' as defined in the Banking Regulation Act, 1949 (As
applicable to Co-operative Societies).
As such, it will not be in order for the above bank, among other things, to accept
deposits from public and allow withdrawal by cheques, draft, order or otherwise.
