Calendar for Auction of Government of India Treasury Bills
March 28, 2003
The Reserve Bank of India has announced the calendar of Treasury Bills issuance for the period
April 1, 2003 to September 30, 2003.

The Reserve Bank of India retains the right to vary the amount and timing, if emerging
circumstances warrant, without assigning any reason. The calendar is thus subject to change, if
circumstances so warrant, including for reasons such as intervening holidays.  Such changes, if
any, will be communicated through regular press releases.
The auction of treasury bills will be subject to the terms and conditions specified in the
General Notification No. F 2(12)- W&M/97 dated March 31, 1998 issued by the Government of
India.
