Tap sale of 5.85 per cent State Development Loans 2015
October 13, 2003
The sale `on tap' of 5.85 per cent State Loans, 2015 was closed in respect
of 21 States, i.e., Andhra Pradesh, Arunachal Pradesh, Chhatisgarh, Goa, Gujarat,
Haryana, Himachal Pradesh, Jammu & Kashmir, Jharkhand, Karnataka, Kerala, Madhya
Pradesh, Maharashtra, Meghalaya, Mizoram Rajasthan, Sikkim, Tamil Nadu, Tripura,
Uttaranchal and West Bengal on October 13, 2003 on receipt of the respective target
amounts
The tap sale for the loans of six States, viz., Assam, Bihar, Manipur, Nagaland,
Orissa and Uttar Pradesh will be kept open on October 14, 2003 to collect the balance
target amount. Applications in the prescribed form can be obtained from the Public Debt
Office of the respective offices of the Reserve Bank of India and Mumbai Office (for six
States).
Applications alongwith the payment will be received at these offices during
banking hours while the tap is open. Applicants, submitting the applications on October
14, 2003 would also have to pay interest for one day at 5.85 per cent per annum for the
securities they apply for. The Reserve Bank of India reserves the right to close the tap
sale during or after the banking hours on October 14, 2003 having regard to the
borrowing requirements of the State Governments and the subscriptions received.
