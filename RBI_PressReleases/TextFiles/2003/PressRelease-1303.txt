Tap sale of 6.75 per cent SDL 2013 closed for 11 states
March 13, 2003
The sale `on tap' of 6.75 per cent State Loans, 2013 was closed in respect of 11 States, i.e.,
Bihar, Chhattisgarh, Gujarat, Jammu & Kashmir, Jharkhand, Nagaland, Orissa, Tamil Nadu,
Tripura, Uttar Pradesh and Uttaranchal on March 13, 2003 on receipt of the respective target
amounts.
The sale on tap in respect of 3 States, viz., Assam, Rajasthan and West Bengal will be open
on March 15, 2003 to collect the balance target amount. Applications in the prescribed form
can be obtained from the Public Debt Office of the respective offices of the Reserve Bank of
India and Mumbai Office (for three States).
Applications alongwith the payment will be received at these offices during banking
hours while the tap is open. Applicants, submitting the applications on March 15, 2003 would
also have to pay interest for three days at 6.75 per cent per annum for the securities they
apply for. The Reserve Bank of India reserves the right to close the tap sale during the
banking hours on March 15, 2003 having regard to the borrowing requirements of the State
Governments and the subscriptions received.
