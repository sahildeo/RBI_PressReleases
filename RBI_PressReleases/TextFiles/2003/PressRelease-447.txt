Supreme Court appoints Special Officer to take charge of Sanchayani's Assets
September 16, 2003
The Hon'ble Supreme Court of India has, by its order dated April 18, 2003 appointed
M/s. N. C. Banerjee and Company, Chartered Accountants, Commerce House, 2, Ganesh
Chandra Avenue, First Floor, Room No.9, Kolkata-700 013 as the Special Officer to take
charge of the assets of               M/s. Sanchayani Savings and Investment (I) Ltd. The
Supreme Court has also directed the Special Officer to make appropriate suggestions so that
small investors can be paid the amount due to them as early as possible.
