Investment by FIIs in shares of Indian companies
March 13, 2003
The Reserve Bank of India has notified on March 13, 2003 that Foreign Institutional
Investors ( FIIs) can now purchase equity shares and convertible debentures of Punjab
Tractors Ltd., up to 64 per cent of its paid up capital through primary and secondary markets
in India, as the company has passed a resolution at its Board of Directors' and General Body
Meetings to this effect.
P. V. Sadanandan
Manager
