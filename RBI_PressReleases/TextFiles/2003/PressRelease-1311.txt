Tap sale of 6.75 per cent SDL 2013 closed for 12 States
March 12, 2003
The sale `on tap' of 6.75 per cent State Loans, 2013 was closed in respect of 12 States,
i.e., Andhra Pradesh, Arunachal Pradesh, Goa, Haryana, Himachal Pradesh, Karnataka,
Kerala, Madhya Pradesh, Manipur, Meghalaya, Mizoram and Punjab on March 12, 2003 on
receipt of the respective target amounts.
The sale on tap in respect of remaining 14 States, viz., Assam, Bihar, Chhattisgarh,
Gujarat, Jammu & Kashmir, Jharkhand, Nagaland, Orissa, Rajasthan, Tamil Nadu, Tripura,
Uttar Pradesh, Uttaranchal and West Bengal will remain open on March 13, 2003 for a
balance target amount of Rs.996 crore.
Applications in the prescribed form can be obtained from the Public Debt Office of
the respective offices of the Reserve Bank of India and Mumbai Office (for all States).
Applications alongwith the payment will be received at these offices during banking hours
while the tap is open. Applicants, submitting the applications on March 13, 2003 would also
have to pay interest for one day at 6.75 per cent per annum for the securities they apply for.
The Reserve Bank of India reserves the right to close the tap sale during the banking hours on
or after March 13, 2003 having regard to the borrowing requirements of the State
Governments and the subscriptions received.
