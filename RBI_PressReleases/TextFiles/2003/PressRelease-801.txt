Basic Statistical Returns of Scheduled Commercial Banks in India Volume-31, March
2002
July 7, 2003
The Basic Statistical Returns of Scheduled Commercial Banks in India-Volume 31,
March 2002 is available for sale. The Department of Statistical Analysis and Computer
Services of Reserve Bank of India brings out this publication annually. The Volume, thirty-
first in the series, presents comprehensive data on Number of Offices, Employees, Deposits
and Credit based on Basic Statistical Returns in respect of the offices of the scheduled
commercial banks in India as at the end of March 2002. The Volume is divided into five
different Sections disseminating credit and deposit data of scheduled commercial banks with
reference to various classificatory characteristics. Section 1 presents general information on
commercial banking as also the summary data on deposits and credit at all-India and State
level. Section 2 gives State/District-wise distribution of deposits and outstanding credit
classified according to population groups and bank groups. Data on deposits by type of
deposits is presented in Section 3. Classification of outstanding credit according to different
categories such as size of credit limit, rate of interest, type of organisation, type of account,
bank group, State and population group, etc. are given in Section 4. In Section 5, these are
further classified according to occupation of borrower. District and occupation-wise
distribution of outstanding credit is also given in the final section.
