UTI-I to participate in Repo Market
October 31, 2003
On application from the Administrator of the Specified Undertaking of the Unit
Trust of India (UTI-I), the Reserve Bank of India has decided to permit them to
participate in the Government Securities Repo market with immediate effect, in
accordance with the guidelines issued in this behalf.
Alpana Killawala
General Manager
