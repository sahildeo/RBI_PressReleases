Underwriting by Primary Dealers
April 7, 2003
The Reserve Bank of India has set the cut-off rate at Re.0.01 (1 paisa) for
underwriting bids by Primary Dealers for the price based auction of `7.37% Government
Stock, 2014' as well as for the yield based auction of 20-year Government Stock, both to be
held on April 8, 2003.
P. V. Sadanandan
Manager
