Withdrawal of Authorisation to act as Primary Dealer
February 6, 2003
M/s. Tata TD Waterhouse Securities Private Limited, Mumbai have requested the
Reserve Bank of India that they may be allowed to exit from their Primary Dealership
activities as their company proposes to focus on their other business activities.  The Reserve
Bank of India have acceded to their request and accordingly, Tata TD Waterhouse Securities
Private Limited ceases to be a Primary Dealer with effect from close of business on
Thursday, February 6, 2003.
P. V. Sadanandan
Manager
