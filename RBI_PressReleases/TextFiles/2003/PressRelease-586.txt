Investment by Foreign Institutional Investors - Monitoring of Overall Ceiling
August 19, 2003
The Reserve Bank of India on August 13, 2003 has notified that no further purchases
of shares of M/s. Mphasis BFL Software Ltd., should be made on behalf of Foreign
Institutional Investors (FIIs) without the prior approval of the Reserve Bank of India. The
Reserve Bank has stated that M/s. Mphasis BFL Software Ltd., has reached the trigger limit
of 22 per cent of its paid up capital.
