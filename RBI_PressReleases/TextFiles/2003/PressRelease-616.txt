RBI's Dividend to GoI
August 13, 2003
In response to query, the RBI spokesperson confirmed that the Reserve Bank Board
has, at its meeting held today, decided to transfer a dividend of Rs.8,834 crore to the
Government of India out of its surplus profit for the year ended June 30, 2003.
Alpana Killawala
General Manager
Press Release : 2003-2004/206
