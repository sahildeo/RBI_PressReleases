RBI's Directory of Commercial Bank Offices in India
on CD-ROM/Site, Vol.II - September 2003
December 15, 2003
The Reserve Bank of India's Department of Statistical Analysis and Computer Services has
brought out the Directory of Commercial Bank Offices in India. The Directory is available on
CD-ROM as well as on the RBI Website.
Based on latest available information on bank branches, the Directory contains
information on various functional and locational details of 68,723 branches/offices of 295
commercial banks as on September 30, 2003. The Directory can be accessed through
geographical region, state, district, centre; as well as through bank group/bank. It also
provides details of branches/offices of banks functioning in a particular locality within a
centre authorised dealers category-wise and type of business activity-wise details of branches
of banks also can be accessed.
The price of the CD is Rs.200 each in India and US$ 40 abroad. Prices are inclusive
of postage. Drafts/cheques/pay-orders should be drawn in favour of "Reserve Bank of India",
payable in Mumbai. CDs are available for sale from the Sale Section, Division of Reports,
Review and Publications, Department of Economic Analysis and Policy, Reserve Bank of
India, Amar Building, Ground Floor, P. M. Road, Fort, Mumbai-400001.
