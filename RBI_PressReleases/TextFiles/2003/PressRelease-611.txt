State Tap Sale for Debt Swaps
August 13, 2003
In consultation with the Government of India it has been decided to conduct a tap sale of
State Government securities for Rs.8,000 crores on August 25, 2003 as the third tranche of on-
going State Government debt swap programme in the current year. Operational details regarding
the tap sale will be announced nearer the date of the sale.
Alpana Killawala
General Manager
