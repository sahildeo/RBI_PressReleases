28 May 2003
RBI to conduct OMO Auction on May 29, 2003 -
6.72 per cent GOI 2014 to be sold on Auction basis
for up to Rs.5,500 crore
The Reserve Bank of India has decided to offer from its investment portfolio the following security
for sale through Multiple Price Auction on May 29, 2003:
6.72 per cent GOI 2014
for an amount up to Rs.5,500 crore
The application form together with the terms and conditions of the auction may be obtained from
th
the Chief General Manager, Internal Debt Management Department, Reserve Bank of India, 16
floor, Central Office Building, Mumbai or from the Public Debt Office, Reserve Bank of India,
Mumbai Office.
The bids may be tendered in electronic format using the Negotiated Dealing System or may be
deposited in the tender box specially kept for the purpose at Reserve Bank of India, Fort, Mumbai
on May 29, 2003 up to 12.30 pm.
On the basis of the tenders received the Reserve Bank of India will determine the minimum price
up to which tenders for sale of Government securities will be accepted at the auction. The results
of the auction will be displayed at the Reserve Bank of India, Fort, Mumbai. The settlement will be
on May 30, 2003. The Reserve bank has the right to accept or reject any or all the bids either
wholly or partially if deemed fit without assigning any reason.
Alpana Killawala
General Manager
