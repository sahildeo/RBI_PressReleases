Investment by Foreign Institutional Investors
July 22, 2003
       The Reserve Bank of India has notified on July 22, 2003 that Foreign Institutional
Investors (FIIs) can now purchase equity shares and convertible debentures of Satyam
Computer Services Ltd., up to 60 per cent of its paid up capital, as the company has
passed a resolution at its Board and General Body Meeting.
P.V. Sadanandan
Manager
