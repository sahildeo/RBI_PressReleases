On-tap Sale of State Government Loans to open on February 4
January 29, 2003
State Governments of Andhra Pradesh, Himachal Pradesh and Jammu & Kashmir have
offered to sell on tap 6.60 per cent State Development Loan, 2013 having maturity of ten
years. The sale will be held at the respective Regional Offices of the Reserve Bank of India
and at Mumbai (for all the three States), on February 4, 2003. The Reserve Bank of India
reserves the right to close the sale during the business hours on or after February 4, 2003
having regard to the borrowing requirements of the State Governments and the subscriptions
received.
Applications in the prescribed form obtainable from the Public Debt Office of the concerned
offices of the Reserve Bank of India will be received at these offices during business hours
while the sale is open. The Reserve Bank of India expects to mobilise about Rs.912 crore in
the 'tap' issue.
