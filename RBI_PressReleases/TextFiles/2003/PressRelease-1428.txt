RBI appoints Banking Ombudsman at Guwahati
February 15, 2003
The Reserve Bank of India has appointed Shri H. W. T. Syiem as the Banking
Ombudsman for the states of Assam, Arunachal Pradesh, Manipur, Meghalaya,
Mizoram, Nagaland and Tripura with effect from February 13, 2003. As Banking
Ombudsman, Shri Syiem will have his office at the Reserve Bank of India Building,
Station Road, Guwahati-781 011.
The Banking Ombudsman Scheme was introduced in June 1995 with  a view
to providing to the bank customers quick and low-cost redressal of grievances relating
to banking services. Its scope of authority and functions include redressal of
grievances against deficiency in banking services, concerning loans and advances and
other specified matters and arbitration for specified disputes. All commercial banks,
