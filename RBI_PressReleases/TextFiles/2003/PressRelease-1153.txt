Underwriting by Primary Dealers
April 21, 2003
The Reserve Bank of India has set the cut-off rate for underwriting bids by Primary
Dealers for the price based auctions of "6.25% Government Stock, 2018" and "7.95%
Government Stock, 2032" to be held on April 22, 2003 at Re.0.009 (0.90 paise) and
Re.0.0075 (0.75 paise) per Rupees 100, respectively.
Ajit Prasad
Manager
