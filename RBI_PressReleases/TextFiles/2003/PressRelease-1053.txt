TAP sale of 6.40 per cent State Development Loans, 2013 closed
May 13, 2003
The sale on tap of 6.40 per cent State Loans, 2013 in respect of remaining six States
viz., Assam, Bihar, Nagaland, Orissa, Tripura and Uttar Pradesh has been closed on May 13,
2003 on receipt of their respective target amounts.
P. V. Sadanandan
Manager
