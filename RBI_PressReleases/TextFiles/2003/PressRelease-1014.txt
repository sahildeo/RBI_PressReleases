RBI Cancels licence of Astha Corporation Private Limited, Hyderabad as 'surrendered'
May 23, 2003
The Reserve Bank of India has cancelled as surrendered on May 21, 2003 the licence
of Astha Corporation Private Limited, B-1, Industrial Estate, Sanathnagar, Hyderabad-500
018 to carry on the business of a non-banking financial institution. The company has also
been prohibited from acceptance of deposit from public under Section 45 MB (1) and
prohibited from alienation of assets under Section 45 MB (2) of the Reserve Bank of India
Act, 1934.
The Reserve Bank has cancelled the licence of the above company under Section 45-
1A (6) of the Reserve Bank of India Act, 1934
P. V. Sadanandan
Manager
