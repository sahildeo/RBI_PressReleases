Investment by Foreign Institutional Investors – Monitoring of Overall Ceiling
September 19, 2003
The Reserve Bank of India on September 19, 2003 has notified that no further purchases of
shares of M/s Digital GlobalSoft Ltd., should be made on behalf of Foreign Institutional
Investors (FIIs) without the prior approval of the Reserve Bank of India. The Reserve Bank
has stated that M/s Digital GlobalSoft LTd., has reached the trigger limit of 22 per cent of its
paid up equity capital
