Rejection of application for certificate of registration
February 8, 2003
The Reserve Bank of India, in exercise of the powers conferred on it under Section 45
IA(4) of the Reserve Bank of India Act, 1934, has rejected on February 4, 2003 the
application for certificate of registration submitted by Yule Financing and Leasing Company
Limited, Yule House, 8, Dr. Rajendra Prasad Sarani, Kokata-700 001 for carrying on the
business of a Non-Banking Financial Institution
As such, the above company cannot transact the business of a non-banking financial
institution as defined in Clause (a) of Section 45 IA of the Reserve Bank of  India Act, 1934.
P. V. Sadanandan
Manager
