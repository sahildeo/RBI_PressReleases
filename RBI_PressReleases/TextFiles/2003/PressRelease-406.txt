Shri Vepa Kamesam's term as Deputy Governor extended
September 23, 2003
The Government of India has granted an extension in service to Shri Vepa Kamesam,
Deputy Governor of the Reserve Bank of India.  The extension has been granted for a period
of three months from September 24, 2003 beyond the age of superannuation of 62 years.
Shri Kamesam was originally appointed as a Deputy Governor of the Reserve Bank of
India, on July 1, 2001, for a period of two years. He was reappointed as a Deputy Governor
of the Reserve Bank of India from July 1, 2003 to September 23, 2003 in June this year till he
attained the age of 62 years.
Alpana Killawala
General Manager
