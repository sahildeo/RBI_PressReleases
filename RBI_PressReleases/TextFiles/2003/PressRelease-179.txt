RBI rejects application for Certificate of Registration of
Better Deals Developers Private Limited
November 15, 2003
The Reserve Bank of India, has on November 5, 2003 rejected the application for
certificate of registration submitted by Better Deals Developers Private Limited, 11,
Middleton Row, New Asiatic Mansion, Flat No.7, Kolkata-700071 for carrying on the
business of a non-banking financial institution.
Following rejection of application for certificate of registration, Better Deals
Developers Private Limited cannot transact the business of a non-banking financial
institution.
The Reserve Bank of India has taken this action under powers conferred on it by
Section 45 of the Reserve Bank of India Act, 1934.
