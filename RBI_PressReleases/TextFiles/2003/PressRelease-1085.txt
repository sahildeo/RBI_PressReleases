State Government Loans on Tap
to be sold on May 12
May 6, 2003
All the State Governments except Chhattisgarh have offered to sell on tap 6.40 per cent State
Development Loan, 2013 having maturity of ten years for an aggregate target amount of Rs.6747
crore, with an option to retain excess subscription up to 30 per cent. The sale will be held at the
respective Regional offices of the Reserve Bank of India and at Mumbai (for all the 27 States) on
May 12, 2003. The Reserve Bank of India reserves the right to close the sale during the business
hours on or after May 12, 2003 having regard to the requirements of the State Governments and
the subscriptions received.
Applications in the prescribed form obtainable from the Public Debt Office at the
Reserve Bank of India will be received at the aforesaid offices during business hours while the
sale is open.
It may be recalled that the market participants were informed on April 30, 2003 that the
State Governments would be accessing the market on May 12, 2003 for part of their borrowing
requirements for the fiscal 2003-04.
