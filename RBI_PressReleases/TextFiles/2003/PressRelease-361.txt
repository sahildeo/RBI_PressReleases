RBI Cancels registration certificate of Vasavi Financial Services Limited, Hyderabad as
`surrendered'
October 1, 2003
The Reserve Bank of India has cancelled as surrendered on September 24, 2003 the
registration certificate of Vasavi Financial Services Limited, 505, Topaz Complex, 6-3-883,
Panjagutta, Hyderabad-500 082 to carry on the business of a non-banking financial
institution. The company has also been prohibited from acceptance of deposit from public
under Section 45 MB (1).
The Reserve Bank has cancelled the registration certificate of the above company
under Section 45-1A (6) of the Reserve Bank of India Act, 1934
P.V. Sadanandan
Manager
