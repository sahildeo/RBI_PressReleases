Two New Executive Directors appointed in RBI
March 3, 2003
Shri Y.S.P. Thorat, Chief General Manager, Reserve Bank of India, Central Office,
Mumbai and Shri P.K. Biswas, Principal, Reserve Bank  Staff College, Chennai have been
appointed as Executive Directors of the Reserve Bank with effect from March 1, 2003.
Alpana Killawala
General Manager
