Reserve Bank rejects application for Certificate of Registration of
 Sthapati Credit Limited
November 3, 2003
The Reserve Bank of India, has on October 28, 2003 rejected the application for
certificate of registration submitted by Sthapati Credit Limited having its office at 203-B, 2nd
Floor, Ivory Terrace, R. C. Dutt Road, Baroda for carrying on the business of a non-banking
financial institution.
Following rejection of application for certificate of registration, Sthapati Credit
Limited can not transact the business of a non-banking financial institution.
The Reserve Bank of India has taken this action under powers conferred on it by
Section 45 of the Reserve Bank of India Act, 1934.
