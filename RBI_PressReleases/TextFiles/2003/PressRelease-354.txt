State Governments to raise funds from market on October 13
October 3, 2003
All the State Governments except Manipur and Punjab will be raising market
borrowings for an aggregate amount of about Rs.6400 crore as the second trance of
their regular market borrowing programme on October 13, 2003. The exact amount of
the loans, coupon rate and other details will be notified later.
