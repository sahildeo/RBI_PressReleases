Recommendations on Micro Finance Issues on RBI Site
November 6, 2003
The Reserve Bank of India has placed on its website, the recommendations of the
informal groups on micro finance which studied issues relating to (i) Structure and
Sustainability; (ii) Funding; (iii) Regulations; and (iv) Capacity Building for greater public
debate.
Comments may please be sent to Chief General Manager, Rural Planning and Credit
Department, Reserve Bank of India, Central Office, Shahid Bhagat Singh Road, Mumbai 400
001 (fax: 022-22658276 / email: cgmincrpcd@rbi.org.in).
It may be recalled that the Reserve Bank had in October 2002 constituted four
informal groups to further stimulate the process of constructive dialogue amongst the major
players in the area of micro finance. The four groups studied issues relating to (i) Structure
and Sustainability; (ii) Funding; (iii) Regulations; and (iv) Capacity Building. Shri Vepa
Kamesam, Deputy Governor, Reserve Bank of India on August 6, 2003 chaired a high level
meeting to discuss the recommendations of the groups. It was decided at that meeting to
circulate the recommendations of the groups for greater public debate.
