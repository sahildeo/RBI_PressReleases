State Government Loans on Tap to be sold on July 30
July 24, 2003
All the State Governments have offered to sell on tap 6.20 percent State Development
Loan, 2013 having maturity of ten years for an aggregate target amount of Rs.8000 crore as the
second tranche of additional market borrowing on account of debt swap scheme. The sale will be
held at the respective Regional Offices of the Reserve Bank of India and at Mumbai (for all the
28 states) on July 30, 2003. The Reserve Bank of India reserves the right to close the sale during
the banking hours on or after July 30, 2003 having regard to the requirements of the State
Governments and the subscriptions received.
2. Applications in the prescribed for obtainable from the Public Debt Office at the Reserve Bank
of India will be receive at the aforesaid Offices during banking hours while the sale is open.
3. It may be recalled that the first tranche of the additional market borrowings of the debt swap
scheme was conducted on June 12, 2003 for a target amount of Rs.7000 crore.
