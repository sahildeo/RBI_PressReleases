States to Sell 6.35 per cent 2013 Loans
for Rs.7000 crore on June 12
June 4, 2003
All the State Governments have offered to sell on tap 6.35 per cent State
Development Loan, 2013 having maturity of ten years for an aggregate target amount of
Rs.7000 crore on account of debt swap scheme. The sale will be held at the respective
Regional Offices of the Reserve Bank of India and at Mumbai (for all the 28 States) on June
12, 2003. The Reserve Bank of India reserves the right to close the sale during the banking
hours on or after June 12, 2003 having regard to the requirements of the State Governments
and the subscriptions received.
Applications in the prescribed form obtainable from the Public Debt Office at the
Reserve Bank of India will be received at the aforesaid Offices during banking hours while
the sale is open.
It   may be  recalled  that  the market participants were informed on May 28, 2003 that
the State Governments would be accessing the market on June 12, 2003 as the first tranche on
account of debt-swap scheme for the fiscal 2003-04.
