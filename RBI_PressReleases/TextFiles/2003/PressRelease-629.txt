Four State Governments to raise funds from market on August 13, 2003
August 9, 2003
Four State Governments have announced the sale of 10 year Stock to the public by
auction for an aggregate amount Rs.1250.00 crore as detailed below:
(Rs. in crore)
Sr. No. Sate Notified Amount
1 Andhra Pradesh 500.00
2 Gujarat 250.00
3 Kerala 250.00
4 Tamil Nadu 250.00
Total 1250.00
 The auction will be conducted by Mumbai (Fort) Office of the Reserve Bank of India
on Wednesday, August 13, 2003. Tenders should be submitted in the prescribed form on
Wednesday, August 13, 2003 before 12.30 hrs. NDS members shall submit their applications
through NDS only. Results will be announced the same evening. Payment by successful
bidders will be on Thursday, August 14, 2003.
P.V. Sadanandan
Manager
