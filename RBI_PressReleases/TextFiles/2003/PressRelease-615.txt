Investment by Foreign Institutional Investors - Monitoring of Overall Ceiling
August 13, 2003
The Reserve Bank of India on August 13, 2003 has notified that no further
purchases of shares of ACC Ltd., should be made on behalf of Foreign Institutional
Investors (FIIs) without prior approval of the Reserve Bank of India. The Reserve Bank
has stated that ACC Ltd., has reached the overall ceiling of 22 per cent of its paid up
capital.
