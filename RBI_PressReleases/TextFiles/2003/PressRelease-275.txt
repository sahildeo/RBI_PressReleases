Investment by Foreign Institutional Investors
October 22, 2003
The Reserve Bank of India has notified on October 22, 2003  that Foreign Institutional
Investors (FIIs) can now purchase equity shares and convertible debentures of I-flex
Solutions Ltd., up to 60 per cent of its paid up capital through primary and secondary markets
in India, as the company has passed a resolution at its Board and General Body Meeting.
P.V. Sadanandan
Manager
