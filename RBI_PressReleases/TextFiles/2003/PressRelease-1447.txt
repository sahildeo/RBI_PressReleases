RBI cancels licence of Yeshwant Sahakari Bank Ltd.
February 11, 2003
The Reserve Bank of India has cancelled the licence of the Yeshwant Sahakari Bank Ltd.,
Mumbai, Maharashtra to carry on  banking business.  The bank is precluded from transacting the
business of ‘banking’ as defined in the Banking Regulation Act, 1949 (as applicable to Co-
operative Societies). Thus, it will not be in order for it, among other things, to accept deposits
from the public and allow withdrawals by cheque, draft, order or otherwise.
