Good Response to State Loans:
RBI closes Tap for Most States
February 25, 2003
The response to the State Development Loans has been good and the tap has been
closed for 26 of the 27 States which accessed the market on February 25, 2003 with
ten year security bearing coupon rate of 6.95%.  For the remaining one State, namely,
Rajasthan, the tap will be kept open for one more day.  Applications for the Rajasthan
State Loans will be received at the respective Regional Office and Mumbai Office
during office hours while the tap is open.  Payment for the loan will also include
accrued coupon for one day for the applications submitted tomorrow alongwith that of
the stock.
Alpana Killawala
General Manager
