Payment Facilities - Foreigners in India
May 21, 2003
In response to an enquiry whether foreign nationals, including persons of Indian origin, while
in India are required to pay the charges towards booking of airline/train tickets, hotels,
hospitals, etc., in foreign currency, the Reserve Bank has clarified that under the extant
Foreign Exchange Regulations, any foreign national in India is free to pay either in rupees or
in equivalent foreign exchange for such purposes.
Alpana Killawala
General Manager
