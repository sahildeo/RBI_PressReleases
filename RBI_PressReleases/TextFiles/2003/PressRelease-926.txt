United Forum of Bank Unions meets RBI Governor
to discuss Clean Note Policy
June 9, 2003
Dr. Bimal Jalan, Governor, Reserve Bank of India met the United Forum of Bank
Unions here today. The meeting was held at the request of the United Forum of Bank Unions
on the implementation of the Reserve Bank of India's clean note policy. A free exchange of
views took place at the meeting between the Forum and the Reserve Bank. The Governor
pointed out that the Reserve Bank was keen to implement its clean note policy for the benefit
of members of the public who deserved clean notes. It was, however, not intended to cause
any problem or difficulty to the bank staff or bank customers. The Forum expressed the
difficulties faced by the bank staff while implementing the clean note policy. It was agreed
that the bank management, the unions and the Reserve Bank of India will work together to
supply good clean notes to members of the public.
Alpana Killawala
General Manager
