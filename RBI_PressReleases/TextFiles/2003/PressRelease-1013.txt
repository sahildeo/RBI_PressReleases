RBI rejects  licence application of Madhepura-Supaul Central Co-operative Bank Ltd.
May 23, 2003
The Reserve Bank of India under Section 22 of the Banking Regulation Act, 1949 has
rejected the licence application of the Madhepura-Supaul Central  Co-operative Bank Ltd.,
Bihar to carry on banking business.  The bank is precluded from transacting the business of
`banking' as defined in the Banking Regulation Act, 1949 (As applicable to Co-operative
Societies).
As such, it will not be in order for the above bank, among other things, to accept
deposits from public and allow withdrawal by cheques, draft, order or otherwise.
