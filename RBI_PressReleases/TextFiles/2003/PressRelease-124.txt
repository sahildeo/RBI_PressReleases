RBI releases LAF Report and Sterilisation Paper For Public Comment
December 2, 2003
The Reserve Bank of India today placed the executive summary of the report of the
Internal Group on Liquidity Adjustment Facility (LAF) on its website. The Reserve Bank has
also placed the summary of the report of the Working Group on Instruments of Sterilisation
on its website.
It may be recalled that in the Mid term Review of the Monetary and Credit Policy for
2003-2004 released on November 3, 2003, it was indicated that the report of the Internal
Group on Liquidity Adjustment Facility (LAF) would be placed on the RBI website for wider
dissemination and comments.
The Reserve Bank has stated that comments on the report of the Internal Group on
Liquidity Adjustment Facility (LAF) may be sent to the Principal Monetary Policy Adviser,
