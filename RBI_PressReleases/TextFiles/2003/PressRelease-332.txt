Mid-term Review of Monetary and Credit Policy for 2003-04 on November 3, 2003
October 10, 2003
Dr. Y.V. Reddy, Governor, Reserve Bank of India will announce the Mid-term Review of
Monetary and Credit Policy for 2003-04 on Monday, November 3, 2003. He will meet the chief
executives of major scheduled commercial banks for announcing the policy which will take
place at 11.00 am in the Reserve Bank of India, Central Office, Mumbai.
P.V. Sadanandan
Manager
