Tap sale of 6.75 per cent SDL 2013
March 15, 2003
The sale on tap of 6.75 per cent State Loans, 2013 in respect of 3 States, viz., Assam,
Rajasthan and West Bengal will be kept open on March 17, 2003 to collect the balance target
amount. Applications in the prescribed form can be obtained from the Public Debt Office of
the respective offices of the Reserve Bank of India and Mumbai Office (for all the three
States).
Applications alongwith the payment will be received at these offices during banking
hours while the tap is open. Applicants, submitting the applications on March 17, 2003 would
also have to pay interest for five days at 6.75 per cent per annum for the securities they apply
for. The Reserve Bank of India reserves the right to close the tap sale during the banking
hours on or after March 17, 2003 having regard to the borrowing requirements of the State
Governments and the subscriptions received.
