Investment by FIIs/NRIs/PIOs
In shares of Indian companies
January 11, 2003
The Reserve Bank of India has notified on January 11, 2003 that Foreign Institutional Investors
(FIIs)/Non-Resident Indians (NRIs) and Persons of Indian Origin (PIOs) can now purchase in the
secondary market equity shares of Bharti Tele-Ventures Ltd., upto 47 per cent of its paid up
capital under the portfolio investment scheme, as the FIIs/NRIs/OCBs/ and PIOs holdings in the
company have gone down below 47 per cent.
P. V. Sadanandan
Manager
