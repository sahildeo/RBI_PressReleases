Underwriting by Primary Dealers
May 2, 2003
The Reserve Bank of India  has set the cut-off rate for underwriting bids by Primary Dealers at
Re.0.02 (2 paise) per Rupees 100 for both the price based auctions of "6.30% Government Stock,
2023" and "7.27% Government Stock, 2013" to be held on 3rd May 2003.
P. V. Sadanandan
Manager
