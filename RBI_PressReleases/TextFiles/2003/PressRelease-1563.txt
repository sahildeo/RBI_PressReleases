The Reserve Bank cancels licence of
The Star Cooperative Urban Bank Ltd.
January 16, 2003
The Reserve Bank of India has cancelled the licence of The Star Cooperative Urban Bank
Ltd., Dilsukhnagar, Hyderabad (Andhra Pradesh) to carry on banking business.  The bank is
precluded from transacting the business of 'banking' as defined in the Banking Regulation
Act, 1949 (As Applicable to Co-operative Societies) and it will not be in order for it, among
other things, to accept deposits from the public and allow withdrawals by cheque, draft, order
or otherwise.
