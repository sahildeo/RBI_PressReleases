Reserve Bank cancels the  Certificate of Registration of
TCI Securities Limited, Secunderabad
November 20, 2003
The Reserve Bank of India has cancelled on October 31, 2003, the certificate of registration
issued to  TCI Securities Limited, a Non-Banking Financial Company having its head office
at 1-7-293, M. G. Road, Secunderabad-500 026 as the company has opted to exit from the
business of Non-Banking Financial Institution pursuant to its amalgamation with TCI
Finance Limited, Secunderabad.
The Reserve Bank of India has cancelled the certificate in exercise of the powers
conferred on it under Section 45-IA (6) of the Reserve Bank of India Act, 1934.
