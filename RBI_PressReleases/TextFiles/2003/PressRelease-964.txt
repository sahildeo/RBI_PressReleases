Underwriting by Primary Dealers
June 2, 2003
The Reserve Bank of India has set  the cut-off rate for underwriting bids by Primary
Dealers for the price based auction of 6.85% Government Stock 2012 and yield based auction
of new 25 Year Government Stock  to be held on June 3, 2003 at Re.0.02 (2 paise) and at
Re.0.01 (1 Paisa) per Rupees 100, respectively.

Manager
