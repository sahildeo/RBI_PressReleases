Investment by NRIs/PIOs in
shares of Indian companies
March 12, 2003
The Reserve Bank of India on March 12, 2003 has notified that no further purchases
of shares of Dalmia Cement (Bharat) Ltd. should be made on behalf of non-resident Indians
(NRIs) and persons of Indian origin (PIOs)  without obtaining prior clearance of the Reserve
Bank.  The Reserve Bank has stated that the net purchases of equity shares of the company in
the secondary markets have reached the limit of 8 per cent of its paid up equity capital.
