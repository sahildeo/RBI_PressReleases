Investment by NRIs/PIOs – Cautioning under PIS
November 27, 2003
The Reserve Bank of India on November 27, 2003 has notified that no further purchases
of equity shares of M/s. Heg Ltd., should be made on behalf of Non-Resident Indians
(NRIs)/Persons of Indian Origins (PIOs) without the prior approval of the Reserve Bank of India.
The Reserve Bank has stated that M/s. Heg Ltd., has reached limit of 8 per cent of its paid up
equity capital.
