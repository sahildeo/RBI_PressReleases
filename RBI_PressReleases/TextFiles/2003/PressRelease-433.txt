Punjab State Government to raise funds from market on September 20, 2003
September 18, 2003
The Government of Punjab have announced the sale of 10-year Stock to the public by
action for an amount of Rs.189.78 crore. The auction will be conducted by Mumbai (Fort)
Office of the Reserve Bank of India on September 20, 2003. Tenders should be submitted in
the prescribed form at that office on Saturday, September 20, 2003 before 12.30 hrs. NDS
members shall submit applications through NDS only. Results will be announced the same
day. Payment by successful bidders will be on Monday, September 22, 2003.
