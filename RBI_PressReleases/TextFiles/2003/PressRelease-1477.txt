Tap sale of 6.60 per cent State Development Loans, 2013 closed
February 4, 2003
The sale `on tap' of 6.60 per cent State Development Loans closed today in respect of
all three States viz., Andhra Pradesh, Himachal Pradesh and Jammu & Kashmir, as the
subscriptions received exceeded the target amount of the respective States.
P. V. Sadanandan
Manager
