Change in the format of application form for the Auction of Treasury Bills
December 13, 2003
For the sake of operational convenience the lay out of the format of physical
application form for competitive bids has been modified by the Government of India vide
notification no. F.No.2 (1)-W&M/2001 dated December 11, 2003.  The modified lay out
confirms to the lay out of the NDS electronic screen. Accordingly, in case of non- availability
of NDS, the physical bids for 91-Day and 364-Day Treasury Bills auctions have to be
henceforth submitted only in the new enclosed format to Public Debt Office, Reserve bank of
India, Mumbai on the date of auction of Treasury bills.
