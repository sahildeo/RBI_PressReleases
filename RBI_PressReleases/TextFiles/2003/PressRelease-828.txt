Underwriting by Primary Dealers
June 30, 2003
The Reserve Bank of India has set the cut-off rate at Re.0.02 (2 paise) per Rupees 100
for underwriting bids by Primary Dealers for each of the price based auctions of `7.37%
Government Stock, 2014', `6.05% Government Stock, 2019' and `6.13% Government Stock,
2028' to be held on July 1, 2003.
P.V. Sadanandan
Manager
