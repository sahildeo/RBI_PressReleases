Government not to pay interest on matured Relief Bonds
April 26, 2003
The Government of India had notified that it will not pay any interest on the relief
bonds that had matured but not encashed after February 28, 2003. The Reserve Bank of
India has advised investors to present the matured relief bonds under the various Relief
Bonds Schemes for encashment to the offices that had issued the relief bonds. This
applies to 9 per cent Relief Bond 1987, 10 per cent Relief bond 1993, 9 per cent Relief
Bond, 1993 and 10 per cent Relief Bond 1995. The Reserve Bank offices have also issued
advices to all individual investors holding matured bonds for immediate encashment.
Alpana Killawala
