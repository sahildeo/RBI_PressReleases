Investment by Foreign Institutional Investors - Monitoring of Overall Ceiling
July 9, 2003
The Reserve Bank of India on July 9, 2003 has notified that no further purchases of
shares of M/s. Satyam Computer Services Ltd., should be made on behalf of Foreign Institutional
Investors (FIIs). The Reserve Bank has stated that M/s. Satyam Computer Services Ltd., has
reached the overall ceiling of 49 per cent of its paid up capital.
Alpana Killawala
General Manager
Press Release : 2003-2004/42
