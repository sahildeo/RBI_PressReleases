Two New Executive Directors appointed in RBI
June 12, 2003
Shri N. H. Siddiqui, Regional Director, Reserve Bank of India, Bhubaneshwar has been
appointed as Executive Director of Deposit Insurance and Credit Guarantee Corporation. Shri A.
V. Sardesai, Chief General Manager-in-Charge, Rural Planning and Credit Department, Reserve
Bank of India, Central Office, Mumbai has been appointed as Executive Director of the Reserve
Bank of India. Both appointments are effective from June 12, 2003.
Alpana Killawala
General Manager
