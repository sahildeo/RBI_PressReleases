RBI rejects  licence application of the Narasaraopet Co-operative Urban Bank Ltd.,
Narasaraopet
June 13, 2003
The Reserve Bank of India under Section 22 of the Banking Regulation Act, 1949 has
rejected the licence application of the Narasaraopet Co-operative Urban Bank Ltd.,
Narasaraopet, Guntur District (Andhra Pradesh) to carry on banking business.  The bank is
precluded from transacting the business of `banking' as defined in the Banking Regulation
Act, 1949 (As applicable to Co-operative Societies).
As such, it will not be in order for the above bank, among other things, to accept
deposits from public and allow withdrawal by cheques, draft, order or otherwise.
