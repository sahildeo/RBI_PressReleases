On Tap Sale of State Government Loans to open on May 12
April 30, 2003
State Governments will be entering the market for raising market loans carrying
tenure of 10 years by way of tap sale on May 12, 2003. The aggregate target amount would
be around Rs.7000 crore, with an option to retain excess subscription up to 30 per cent. The
coupon rate and other details of the tap issue will be notified later.
P. V. Sadanandan
Manager
