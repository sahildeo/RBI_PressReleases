Underwriting by Primary Dealers
September 19, 2003
RBI has set the cut-off rate for underwriting bids by Primary Dealers for the auction of the
“10-year Punjab State Government Stock” to be held on September 20, 2003 at Re.0.02 (two
paise) per Rupees hundred.
P.V. Sadanandan
Manager
