Rejection of application for certificate of registration
March 17, 2003
It is hereby notified for the information of public that the Reserve Bank of India, in
exercise of the powers conferred on it under  Section 45 IA of the Reserve Bank of
India Act, 1934, has rejected on March 10, 2003 the application for certificate of
registration submitted by Bharat Projects Limited, 24, Dakshineswar, 10, Hailey
Road, New Delhi-110 001 for carrying on the business of a Non-Banking Financial
Institution.
As such, the above company cannot transact the business of a non-banking financial
institution as defined in Clause (a) of Section 45 IA of the Reserve Bank of  India Act,
1934.
P. V. Sadanandan
Manager
