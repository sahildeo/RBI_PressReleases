Monetary and Credit Policy for the year 2003-04 on Tuesday, April 29, 2003
March 19, 2003
Dr. Bimal Jalan, Governor, Reserve Bank of India will announce the Monetary
and Credit Policy for the year 2003-04 on Tuesday, April 29, 2003.  He will meet the
chief executives of major scheduled commercial banks for announcing the Policy which
will take place at 11.00 a.m. in the Reserve Bank of India, Central Office, Mumbai.
P. V. Sadanandan
Manager
