ARCs - RBI Clarification
March 13, 2003
The Reserve Bank of India has clarified that the last date of March 20, 2003 for asset
reconstruction companies (ARCs) to make an application to the Reserve Bank for registration
is applicable only to companies existing as on June 21, 2002. This is in terms of the second
proviso to paragraph 3(1) of the Securitisation and Reconstruction of Financial Assets and
Enforcement of Security Interest Act, 2002. This deadline is not applicable to companies
incorporated after that date.
