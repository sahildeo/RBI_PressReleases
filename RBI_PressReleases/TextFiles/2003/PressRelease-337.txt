RBI to conduct OMO auction sale on October 10, 2003
October 9, 2003
The Reserve Bank of India has decided to offer from its investment portfolio the
following securities for sale through Multiple Price Auction on October 10, 2003.
-    5.48% GOI 2009 to be sold on auction basis for up to Rs.5000 crore
-   6.17% GOI 2023 to be sold on auction basis for up to Rs.6000 crore
The application form together with the terms and conditions of the auction may be
obtained from the Chief General Manager, Internal Debt Management Department, Reserve
Bank of India, 16th Floor, Central Office Building, Mumbai or from the Public Debt Office,
Reserve Bank of India, Mumbai Office.
The bids may be tendered in electronic format using the NDS or may be deposited in
the tender box specially kept for the purpose at Reserve Bank of India, Fort, Mumbai on
October 10, 2003 up to 12.30 pm.  
On the basis of the tenders received, the RBI will determine the minimum price upto
which tenders for sale of Government securities will be accepted at the auction.  The results
of the auction will be displayed at the Reserve Bank of India, Fort, Mumbai.  The settlement
will be on October 11, 2003. The Reserve Bank has the right to accept or reject any or all the
bids either wholly or partially if deemed fit without assigning any reason.
