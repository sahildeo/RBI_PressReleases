Underwriting by Primary Dealers
January 4, 2003
The Reserve Bank of India has set the cut-off rate for underwriting bids by
Primary Dealers for the price based auction of "7.38% Government Stock, 2015" to be
held on January 6, 2003 at Re.0.0050 (0.50 paisa).
P. V. Sadanandan
Manager
