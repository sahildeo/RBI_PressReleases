Underwriting by Primary Dealers
September 2, 2003
The Reserve Bank of India has set  the cut-off rate for underwriting bids by Primary
Dealers for the auction of the "6.17% Government Stock, 2023" to be held on September 3,
2003 at Re.0.02 (2 paise) per Rupees 100.
P.V. Sadanandan
Manager
