RBI's Auction Schedules revised
May 13, 2003
The Reserve Bank of India today revised the schedule for various auctions it would
conduct today and tomorrow. As per the revised schedule: 
(i) Liquidity Adjustment Facility (LAF) auction on May 14, 2003 will be of five
days;
(ii) The payments for successful bids in the 91/364 day treasury bill auctions will be
on Saturday, May 17, 2003.
Earlier today, the Reserve Bank conducted the LAF auction of one-day after extending
the bidding time to 11.30 am from 10.30 am. It also conducted the 91/364 day treasury bill
auctions today as announced earlier but extended the bid submission time up to 12.30 pm from
11.30 am.
The auction schedules were revised following the Government of Maharashtra shifting
the holiday to May 15, 2003 from May 14, 2003. The Government of Maharashtra declared
May 15, 2003 to be holiday under the Negotiable Instruments Act, 1881.
