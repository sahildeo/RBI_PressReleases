Auction of 91 day Treasury Bills-Reduction in the Notified Amount
September 25, 2003
A reference is invited to our Press Release dated August 4, 2003 announcing
enhancement in the notified amount for auctions of 91 day Treasury Bills from the Rs. 500
crore to Rs. 1,500 crore for eight auctions covering the period August 6, 2003 to September
24, 2003.
It has been decided to reduce the notified amount to Rs. 500 crore for the auctions of
91 Day Treasury Bills with effect from October 1, 2003.
The notified amount of 364 day Treasury Bills will remain unchanged.
The format for auction of 91 day Treasury Bills and 364 day Treasury Bills will
continue to be multiple price auction format.
Accordingly, the revised Calendar for Auction of Government of India Treasury Bills
from October 1, 2003 to March 31, 2004 is as under:

The Reserve Bank of India retains the right to vary the amount and timing, if
emerging circumstances warrant, without assigning any reason. The calendar is thus subject
to change, if circumstances so warrant, including for reasons such as intervening holidays.
Such changes, if any, will be communicated through regular press releases.
The auction of treasury bills will be subject to the terms and conditions specified in
the General Notification No. F 2(12)- W&M/97dated March 31, 1998 issued by Government
of India.
