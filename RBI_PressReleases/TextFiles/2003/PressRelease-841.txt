New Executive Director appointed in RBI
June 27, 2003
Smt. Shyamala Gopinath has today taken over as Executive Director of the Reserve Bank
of India. She will look after the Department of Banking Operations and Development,
Department of Banking Supervision and Financial Institutions Division. Prior to this, Smt.
Gopinath was on two year deputation with the International Monetary Fund.
