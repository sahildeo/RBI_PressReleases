RBI releases Selected Banking Indicators, 1981-2002
August 22, 2003
The Reserve Bank of India has released Selected Banking Indicators - 1981-2002,
both in the form of a book and CD-Rom. The publication presents data on selected important
banking indicators, such as deposits, credit and investments of all scheduled banks on a
fortnightly basis from 1981 to 2002. The data are presented for six bank groups, viz., State
Bank of India and its Associates, Nationalised Banks, Foreign Banks operating in India,
Regional Rural Banks, other Indian Scheduled Commercial Banks and Co-operative banks.
These data are primarily compiled from the statutory returns submitted by scheduled banks
under Section 42(2) of the Reserve Bank of India Act, 1934. In addition, data on monthly
excess reserves maintained by all scheduled commercial banks (excluding RRBs) with the
Reserve Bank over and above the statutory minimum balance for the purpose of cash reserve
ratio (CRR) are also presented.
