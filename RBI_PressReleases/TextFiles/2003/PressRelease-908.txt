Tap sale of 6.35 per cent State Development Loans, 2013 closed
June 12, 2003
The sale `on tap' of 6.35 per cent ten year State Development Loans, 2013 in respect
of 28 State Governments opened for subscription on June 12, 2003 for a target amount of
Rs.7000 crore. The issue received overwhelming response. Accordingly, the tap sale of 6.35
State Development Loans, 2013 has been closed today. Excess subscriptions are being
refunded to the applicants after making partial allotment in respect of states whose loans are
oversubscribed.
P. V. Sadanandan
Manager
