Investment by Foreign Institutional Investors -
Monitoring of Overall Ceiling
October 24, 2003
The Reserve Bank of India on October 24, 2003 has notified that no further purchases of
shares of Television Eighteen India Ltd., should be made on behalf of Foreign Institutional
Investors (FIIs)/Non-Resident Indians (NRIs) without the prior approval of the Reserve Bank of
India. The Reserve Bank has stated that Television Eighteen India Ltd., has reached the trigger
limit of 24 per cent of its paid up capital.
