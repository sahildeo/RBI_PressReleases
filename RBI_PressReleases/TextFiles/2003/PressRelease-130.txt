Investment by Foreign Institutional Investors -
Monitoring of Overall Ceiling
October 24, 2003
The Reserve Bank of India on November 29, 2003 has notified that no further purchases
of equity shares of Bharti Tele-Ventures Ltd., should be made on behalf of Foreign Institutional
Investors (FIIs)/Non-Resident Indian (NRIs)/ Persons of Indian Origin (PIOs) without the prior
approval of the Reserve Bank of India. The Reserve Bank has stated that Bharti Tele-Ventures
Ltd., has reached the trigger limit of 48.5 per cent of its paid up capital.
