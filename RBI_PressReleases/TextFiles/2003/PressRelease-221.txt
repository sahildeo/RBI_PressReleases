Investment by Foreign Institutional Investors
November 5, 2003
       The Reserve Bank of India has notified on November 5, 2003  that Foreign
Institutional Investors (FIIs) can now purchase equity shares and convertible
debentures of Moser Baer India Ltd., up to 40 per cent of its paid up capital through
primary and secondary markets in India, as the company has passed a resolution at its
Board and General Body Meeting.

Manager
