Reserve Bank rejects application for Certificate of Registration of
 Phoenix International Finance Limited
March 25, 2003
The Reserve Bank of India, has on March 19, 2003 rejected the application for
certificate of registration submitted by Phoenix International Finance Limited having its
office at 3rd floor, Gopala Tower, 25, Rajendra Place, New Delhi-110 008 for carrying on the
business of a non-banking financial institution.
Following rejection of application for certificate of registration, Phoenix International
Finance Limited can not transact the business of a non-banking financial institution. The
company has also been prohibited from acceptance of deposits and alienation of assets. The
company is, however, under an obligation to repay public deposits as per the terms and
conditions of the contract of deposit.
The Reserve Bank of India has taken this action under powers conferred on it
by Section 45 of the Reserve Bank of India Act, 1934.
