Investment by Foreign Institutional Investors - Monitoring of Overall Ceiling
October 31, 2003
The Reserve Bank of India on October 31, 2003 has notified that no further purchases of shares
of Moser Baer Ltd., should be made on behalf of Foreign Institutional Investors (FIIs) without
the prior approval of the Reserve Bank of India. The Reserve Bank has stated that Moser Baer
Ltd., has reached the trigger limit of 22 per cent of its paid up capital.
Alpana Killawala
General Manager
