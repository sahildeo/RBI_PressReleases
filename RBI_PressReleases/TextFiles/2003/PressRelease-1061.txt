Tap sale of 6.40 per cent State Development Loans 2013
May 12, 2003
The sale 'on tap' of 6.40 per cent State Loans, 2013 was closed in respect of 21 States
viz., Andhra Pradesh, Arunachal Pradesh, Goa, Gujarat, Haryana, Himachal Pradesh, Jammu
& Kashmir, Jharkhand, Karnataka, Kerala, Madhya Pradesh, Maharashtra, Manipur,
Meghalaya, Mizoram, Punjab, Rajasthan, Sikkim, Tamil Nadu, Uttaranchal and West Bengal
on May 12, 2003 on receipt of the respective target amounts. Excess subscriptions upto 30
per cent of their target amounts have been retained.
The tap sale for the loans of six States viz., Assam, Bihar, Nagaland, Orissa, Uttar
Pradesh and Tripura will be kept open on May 13, 2003 to collect the balance target amount.
Applications in the prescribed form can be obtained from the Public Debt Office of the
respective offices of the Reserve Bank of India and Mumbai Office (for six States).
Applications along with the payment will be received at these offices during banking
hours while the tap is open. Applicants, submitting the bids on May 13, 2003 would also have
to pay interest for one day at 6.40 per cent per annum for the securities they apply for. The
Reserve Bank of India reserves the right to close the tap sale during the banking hours on
May 13, 2003 having regard to the borrowing requirements of the State Governments and the
subscriptions received.
