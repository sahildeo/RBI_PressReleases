28- Day Fixed Rate Repo from October 20 to October 24, 2003
October 18, 2003
The Reserve Bank of India has decided to offer 28-day fixed rate Repo at 4.50% per
annum in Government of India dated securities and Treasury bills to be held for five consecutive
days , that is from October 20 to October 24, 2003.
Date of Repo Repo period Date of reversal
20.10.2003 28 days 17.11.2003
21.10.2003 28 days 18.11.2003
22.10.2003 28 days 19.11.2003
23.10.2003 28 days 20.11.2003
24.10.2003 28 days 21.11.2003
 This repo will be in addition to the overnight and fortnightly variable rate repo auctions
under Liquidity Adjustment Facility (L.A.F.) which will be held as usual. The eligible banks and
PDs may submit their applications either physically (format available on RBI website
www.rbi.org.in) or electronically through NDS by 10.30 a.m. on the above days (from October
20 to October 24, 2003). The offers would be accepted fully or partially, at the discretion of the
Reserve Bank of India.  Payments by applicants will be on the day of submission of their repo
bid up to 2.30 p.m.
