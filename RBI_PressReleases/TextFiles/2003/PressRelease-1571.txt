RBI to conduct OMO Auction Sales on January 16, 2003
January 15, 2003
5.87% GOI 2010 to be sold on auction basis for upto Rs 5000 crore.
6.25% GOI 2018 to be sold on auction basis for upto Rs 6000 crore.
The Reserve Bank of India has decided to offer from its investment portfolio the
following two securities for sale through Multiple Price Auction on January 16, 2003.
5.87% GOI 2010 to be sold on auction basis for upto Rs 5000 crore.
6.25% GOI 2018 to be sold on auction basis for upto Rs 6000crore.
The application form together with the terms and conditions of the auction may be
obtained from the Chief General Manager, Internal Debt Management Cell, Reserve Bank of
th
India, 16 Floor, Central Office Building, Mumbai or from Public Debt Office, Reserve Bank
of India, Mumbai Office.
The bids may be tendered in electronic format using the NDS or may be deposited in
the tender box specially kept for the purpose at the Reserve Bank of India, Fort, Mumbai on
January 16, 2003 upto 12.30 p.m.
On the basis of the tenders received, the RBI will determine the minimum price upto
which tenders for sale of Government securities will be accepted at the auction. The auction
will be on Multiple Price basis. The results of the auction will be displayed at Reserve Bank
of India, Fort, Mumbai. The settlement will be on January 17, 2003.  The Reserve Bank has
the right to accept or reject any or all the bids either wholly or partially if deemed fit without
assigning any reason.
