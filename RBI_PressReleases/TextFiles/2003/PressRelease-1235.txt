Liquidity Adjustment Facility (LAF)
Quarterly Auction Calendar
March 29, 2003
The Reserve Bank of India announces the calendar for the daily Repo and Reverse Repo
and fortnightly Repo for the first quarter of the new financial year under the Liquidity
Adjustment Facility (LAF).
There is no change in the existing terms and conditions under which the LAF auctions are
conducted.
