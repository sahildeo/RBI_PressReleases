RBI to conduct OMO auction sale on August 14, 2003
August 13, 2003
The Reserve Bank has decided to conduct Open Market Operations sale through an
auction of the following Government of India securities  for a maximum amount  of
Rs.12,000 crore (face value) on August 14, 2003:
1. 7.38% GS 2015 for Rs.3,000 crore
2. 7.49% GS 2017 for Rs.4,000 crore
3. 6.35% GS 2020 for Rs.5,000 crore
All SGL and Current Account holders with the RBI at Mumbai will be eligible to
participate in the auction. The auction will be held at Mumbai. The bids may be tendered in
electronic format using the Negotiated Dealing System (NDS) or may be deposited in the
tender box specially kept for the purpose at Reserve Bank of India, Fort, Mumbai on August
14, 2003 up to 12.30 pm. The application form together with the terms and conditions of the
auction may be obtained from the Chief General Manager, Internal Debt Management
th
Department, Reserve Bank of India, 16 Floor, Central Office Building, Mumbai or from the
Public Debt Office, Reserve Bank of India, Mumbai Office.
The tenderer will have to indicate the face value of the securities offered for purchase
and the intended purchase price (up to two decimal points per hundred of face value) of each
of the stock offered. The purchase price indicated in the tender application should be
exclusive of the broken period interest since last coupon date. The gross amount receivable
by the successful bidders for the security would, however, include the broken period interest
up to August 15, 2003. Tenderer can submit multiple bids but a separate application form
should be submitted as per the prescribed format for each bid. The auction will be conducted
on  multiple price basis. The bid amount should be for a minimum of Rs.1 crore (face value)
and multiples of Rs.1 crore thereafter.
On the basis of the tenders received, the RBI will determine the minimum price up to
which tenders for sale of Government securities will be accepted in respect of each of the
securities at the auction. The results of the auction will be displayed at Reserve Bank of India,
Fort, Mumbai. The settlement will be on August 16, 2003. Reserve Bank has the right to
accept or reject any or all the bids either wholly or partially if deemed fit without assigning
any reason.
Alpana Killawala
