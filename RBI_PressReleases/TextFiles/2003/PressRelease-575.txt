Investment by Foreign Institutional Investors - Monitoring of Overall Ceiling
August 21, 2003
The Reserve Bank of India on August 21, 2003 has notified that Foreign Institutional
Investors (FIIs) can now purchase shares of M/s. Mphasis BFL Software Ltd., in the secondary
market, as the holdings of FIIs in the company have gone down below 22 per cent of its paid up
capital.

Manager
