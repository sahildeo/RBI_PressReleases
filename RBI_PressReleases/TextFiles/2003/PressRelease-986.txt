28 May 2003
State Governments to raise Additional Rs. 7000 crore
for Debt Swap Scheme on June 12
All the State Governments will be raising additional funds on June 12, 2003 on account of debt swap scheme for an
aggregate amount of Rs.7000 crore. The coupon rate and other details will be notified later. This will be the first tranche of
State Governments’ market borrowing programme on account of debt swap scheme during the current fiscal.
It may be recalled that on May 19, 2003 the Reserve Bank of India had announced that the State Governments' debt swap
scheme for repaying high cost loans availed from the Government of India initiated last year would be continued in 2003-04.
