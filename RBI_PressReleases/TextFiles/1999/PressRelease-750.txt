July 23, 1999
The Reserve Bank of India, in exercise of the powers conferred on it under Section 45-IA of the
Reserve Bank of India Act, 1934, has rejected on July 22, 1999, the application for Certificate of
Registration submitted by M/s.  Hire Purchase & Deposits Ltd., having its registered
office at  , , ,  Pradesh for carrying on the business of a
Non-Banking Financial Institution.
As such, the company cannot transact the business of a Non-Banking Financial Institution as
defined in Clause (a) of Section 45-I of the RBI Act, 1934.
