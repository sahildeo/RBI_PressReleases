August 6, 1999
The Reserve Bank of India, in exercise of the powers conferred on it under Section 45-IA of the
Reserve Bank of India Act, 1934, has rejected on August 5, 1999, the application for Certificate
of Registration submitted by M/s. Allied Housing Development Finance Co. (P) Ltd., a Non-
Banking Financial Company having its Registered Office at Sir  Road, P.O. &
P.S.Basirhat , . North 24 
As such, the company cannot transact the business of a Non-Banking Financial Institution as
defined in Clause (a) of Section 45-I of the RBI Act, 1934.
