 
 
RBI Reference Rate for US $ 
March 22, 1999 
     The Reserve Bank of India’s Reference Rate for the US dollar is Rs.42.39 on 22.03.1999 as 
against Rs.42.40 on 19.03.1999. The reference rate is based on 12 noon rates of a few select 
banks in Mumbai. The SDR-rupee rate will be based on this rate.
