August 16, 1999
The Reserve Bank of India, in exercise of the powers conferred on it under Section 45 IA of the
Reserve Bank of India Act, 1934, has rejected on August 12, 1999 the application for certificate
of registration submitted by the financial company M/s.  Mutual Benefit (I) Ltd., 12-A,
 Market,  , Pratapgarh (Uttar Pradesh).
As such, the above company cannot transact the business of a non-banking financial institution
as defined in Clause (a) of Section 45 I of the Reserve Bank of India Act, 1934.
