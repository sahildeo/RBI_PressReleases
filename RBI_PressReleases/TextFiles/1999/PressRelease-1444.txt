February 18, 1999
The Reserve Bank of India has notified on February 18, 1999 that the aggregate net purchases of
equity shares of M/s. Essel Packaging Ltd. by foreign institutional investors ( FIIs) in the
primary/secondary markets has reached the limit of 22 per cent of its paid up capital. No further
purchases of equity shares of M/ s .Essel Packaging Ltd. will, therefore, be allowed on behalf of
the FIIs without obtaining prior approval of the Reserve Bank.
 
Alpana Killawala
General Manager
