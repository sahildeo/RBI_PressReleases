May 28, 1999
The Reserve Bank of India on May 27, 1999 has notified that Non-resident Indians (NRIs),
Persons of Indian Origin (PIOs) and Overseas Corporate Bodies (OCBs) can now purchase
equity shares and convertible debentures of M/s. Alembic Chemical Works Co. Ltd., up to 24 per
cent of their paid up capital.
M/s. Alembic Chemical Works Co. Ltd., has passed a resolution at its General Body Meeting
agreeing for purchase of its equity shares up to 24 per cent of its paid up capital through stock
