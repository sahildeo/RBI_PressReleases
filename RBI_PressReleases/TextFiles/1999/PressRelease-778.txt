July 19, 1999
The Reserve Bank of India, in exercise of the powers conferred on it under Section 45-IA of the
Reserve Bank of India Act, 1934, has rejected on July 15, 1999 the application for Certificate of
Registration submitted by M/s.  Finance & Investment Ltd., a Non-Banking Financial
Company having its Registered Office at B-207, II Floor,  Complex, ,
Ahmedabad.
As such, the company cannot transact the business of a Non-Banking Financial Institution as
defined in Clause (a) of Section 45-I of the RBI Act, 1934.
