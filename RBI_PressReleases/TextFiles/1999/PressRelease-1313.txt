Monitoring FIIs Investments in Indian Companies 
The Reserve Bank of India has advised that Foreign Institutional Investors (FIIs) can now 
purchase equity shares and convertible debentures of M/s. Aptech Ltd., up to 30 per cent of their 
paid up capital instead of the earlier 24 per cent through primary markets and stock exchanges, 
as the company has passed a resolution to this effect, at its Board and General Body Meetings.
