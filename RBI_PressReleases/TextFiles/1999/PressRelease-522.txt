September 6, 1999
The Reserve Bank of India has announced the auction of 364 day Government of India treasury
bills for a notified amount of Rs.500 crore. State Governments are allowed to participate as non-
competitive bidders and the allocation to non-competitive bidders will be outside the notified
amount, at the discretion of the Bank.
Tenders should be submitted in the prescribed form on Wednesday, September 8, 1999 before
1430 hours. Results will be announced the same evening. Payment by successful bidders will be
on Thursday, September 9, 1999.
 
Rupambara Padhi
Manager
