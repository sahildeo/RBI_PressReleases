RBI denies licence to Shri 
Ltd., 
April 10, 1999
The Reserve Bank of India has rejected the licence application of Shri Sarvodaya Co-operative
Bank Ltd., Ahmedabad, Gujarat State, to carry on banking business in India. The bank is
precluded from transacting the business of 'banking' as defined in the Banking Regulation Act,
1949 (as applicable to Co-operative Societies) and it will not be in order for it, among other
things, to accept deposits from the public and allow withdrawals by cheque, draft, order or
Alpana
