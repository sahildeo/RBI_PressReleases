RBI asks banks to waive service charges for
funds remitted for Orissa cyclone relief
November 18, 1999
The Reserve Bank has today issued a circular to all commercial banks advising them to
waive service charges/commission in respect of funds being remitted for credit to the
Prime Minister's Relief Fund, Orissa Cyclone Relief Fund and also other contribution
being remitted to provide relief to cyclone affected people in the Orissa State.
