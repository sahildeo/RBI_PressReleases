July 23, 1999
The Reserve Bank of India, in exercise of the powers conferred on it under Section 45-IA of the
Reserve Bank of India Act, 1934, has rejected on July 21, 1999, the application for certificate of
registration submitted by M/s.  Leasing & Finance Ltd., a Non-Banking Financial
Company having its Registered Office at 101,  II, C.G. Road, B/h  House,
.
As such, the company cannot transact the business of a Non-Banking Financial Institution as
defined in Clause (a) of Section 45-I of the RBI Act, 1934.
