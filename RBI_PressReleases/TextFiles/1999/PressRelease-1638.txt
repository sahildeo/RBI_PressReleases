January 4, 1999
The Reserve Bank has announced a three-day fixed rate repos in Government of India dated
securities on January 5, 1999 for parties holding SGL Account and current account with it at
Mumbai. The repo rate is 8.00 per cent per annum. The eligible banks/institutions may submit
their applications indicating the volume of repos (face value) in the prescribed form obtainable at

Management Cell, Reserve Bank of India, Central Office, Mumbai and submitted to him before
1.30 p.m. on January 5, 1999. The applications may be accepted fully or partially. Payment by
applicants will be on January 5, 1999 up to 2.30 p.m. and the repurchase by the RBI on January
8, 1999.
