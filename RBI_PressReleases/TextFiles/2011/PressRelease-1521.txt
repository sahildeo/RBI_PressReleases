Underwriting Auctions for Government securities  
The Government of India have announced the sale of "New 07 Year 
Government Stock 2018 î for a notified amount of `4,000 crore (nominal), "New 10 
Year  Government Stock 2021" for a notified amount of `5,000 crore (nominal) and " 
8.30 per cent Government Stock  2040î(reissue) for a notified amount of ` 3,000 
crore (nominal) through auctions to be held on April 08, 2011. The underwriting 
auctions for the captioned securities will be conducted on April 07, 2011 (Thursday). 
As per revised scheme of underwriting, dated November 14, 2007, the 
amounts of Minimum Underwriting Commitment (MUC) and the minimum bidding 
commitment under Additional Competitive Underwriting (ACU) for the underwriting
