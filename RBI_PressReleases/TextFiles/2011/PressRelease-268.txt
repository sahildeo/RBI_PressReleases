November 17, 2011
 
Liberalization of Export/Import procedures 
“Set-off” of export receivables against import payables 
 
          The Reserve Bank of India has been allowing requests from the exporters 
through their Authorised Dealer Category 1 banks for “set-off” of export receivables 
against import payables in respect of the same overseas buyer and supplier subject 
to certain terms and conditions. It has now been decided to delegate the power to 
Authorised Dealer Category – I banks to allow such set off with immediate effect. 
Detailed instructions in this regard have been issued in AP(DIR Series) Circular 
No. 47 dated November 17, 2011
