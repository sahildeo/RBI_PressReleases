August 26, 2011 
Investment by FIIs/NRIs  under PIS :   
M/s. Indiabulls Wholesale Services Ltd.   
 The Reserve Bank of India today notified that M/s. Indiabulls Wholesale 
Services Ltd. has passed resolutions at the board of directors' level and at a special 
resolution by the shareholders, to enhance the limit for purchase of its equity shares 
and convertible debentures by Foreign Institutional Investors (FIIs), through primary 
market and stock exchanges, under the Portfolio Investment Scheme to 100 per cent 
of its paid up capital.  
 Foreign Institutional Investors can now purchase equity shares and 
convertible debentures of M/s. Indiabulls Wholesale Services Ltd. through primary 
market and stock exchanges under the Portfolio Investment Scheme, provided :  
• FIIs through primary market and stock exchanges up to 100 per cent of 
the paid up capital of the company under Portfolio Investment Scheme.  
• NRIs through primary market and stock exchanges up to 24 per cent of 
the paid up capital of the company under Portfolio Investment Scheme 
(PIS)
