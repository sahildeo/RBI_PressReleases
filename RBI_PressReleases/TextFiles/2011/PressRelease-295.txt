                                                      November 14, 2011 
Investments by NRIs under PIS  
The Reserve Bank of India has today notified that the aggregate net 
purchases of equity shares in the following companies by Non Resident Indian 
(NRIs) under Portfolio Investment Scheme (PIS) in the primary/secondary markets 
have reached the trigger limit. Hence, these companies have been included in the 
ban list of the Reserve Bank of India for purchase of equity shares by NRIs. 
Accordingly, no further purchases of shares of these company would be allowed 
through stock exchanges in India on behalf of NRIs.  
1) M/s. Goldcrest Finance (India) Limited 
2) M/s. Sabero Organics Gujarat Limited
