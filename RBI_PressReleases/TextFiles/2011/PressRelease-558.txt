September 28, 2011 
The Mansa Nagarik Sahakari Bank Lld., Mansa,  
Dist. Gandhinagar – Penalised 
The Reserve Bank of India has imposed a monetary penalty of `1.00 lakh 
(Rupees one lakh only) on The Mansa Nagarik Sahakari Bank Lld., Mansa, Dist. 
Gandhinagar, in exercise of powers vested in it under the provisions of Section 
47(A)(1)(b) read with Section 46(4) of the Banking Regulation Act, 1949 (AACS) for 
violation of Reserve Bank India's instructions on submission of Cash Transaction 
Reports (CTRs) in respect of cash transaction above ` 10.00 lakh to Financial 
Intelligence Unit-India (FIU-IND), New Delhi under the Anti Money Laundering (AML) 
guidelines. 
The Reserve Bank of India had issued a show cause notice to the bank in 
response to which the bank submitted a written reply. After considering the facts of 
the case and the bank's reply as also personal submissions in the matter, the 
Reserve Bank of India came to the conclusion that the violation was substantiated 
and warranted imposition of the penalty.
