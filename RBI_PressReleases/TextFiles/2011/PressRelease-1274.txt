May 19, 2011 
 
Second LAF on Reporting Fridays 
 
In the light of modified operating procedure of monetary policy introduced 
from May 3, 2011 and introduction of the marginal standing facility (MSF), it has 
been decided to discontinue the second liquidity adjustment facility (SLAF) on 
reporting Fridays effective May 20, 2011.
