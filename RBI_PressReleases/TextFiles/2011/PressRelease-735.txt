Investments by FIIs/NRIs/PIOs under PIS  
The Reserve Bank of India has today notified that the aggregate net 
purchases of equity shares in the companies listed below by Foreign Institutional 
Investors (FIIs) in the primary/secondary markets have reached the trigger limit. 
Accordingly, no further purchases of shares of these companies would be allowed 
through stock exchanges in India on behalf of FIIs.  
1. M/s. Elpro International Limited 
2. M/s. Eclerx Services Limited 
3. M/s. Anil Modi Oil Industries Limited
