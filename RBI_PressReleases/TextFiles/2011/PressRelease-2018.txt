January 1, 2011 
RBI launches Quarterly Industrial Outlook Survey for January-March 2011 
rd
The Reserve Bank of India has launched the 53 round of the Industrial 
Outlook Survey (IOS) for reference period January ñMarch 2011. The Reserve Bank 
is conducting the Industrial Outlook Survey on a quarterly basis since 1998. The 
survey gives an insight into the perception of the public and private limited 
companies engaged in manufacturing activities about their own performance and 
prospects. The survey covers non-financial private and public limited companies with 
a good size/industry representation. The assessment of business sentiments for the 
current quarter and expectations for the ensuing quarter are based on qualitative 
responses to 20 major parameters covering overall business and financial situations, 
demand indicators, price and employment expectations, profit margins, etc. The 
survey provides useful forward looking inputs for policymakers, analysts and 
businesses.  
  The salient features of this survey results at the aggregate level are published 
st
in Monthly Bulletin of the Reserve Bank. The article on 51 round of the survey has 
been published in the December 2010 issue of the Bulletin under the title ìQuarterly 
Industrial Outlook Survey: July-September 2010 (51st Round). The article can also 
be viewed or downloaded from the RBI website http://www.rbi.org.in
M/s Centre for Research Planning and Action, New Delhi has been mandated 
to conduct the survey for this quarter on behalf of the Reserve Bank of India. 
Manufacturing companies will be approached by this agency during the quarter. 
However, other manufacturing companies who are not approached by the agency 
can also participate in this survey by downloading the survey schedule from the 
Reserve Bankís website www.rbi.org.in The survey schedule is placed under the 
head Forms and subhead Survey. The duly authenticated filled-in survey schedule 
may be e-mailed or fax as per contact details given in the survey schedule.
