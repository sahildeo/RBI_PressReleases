September 16, 2011 
Investment by FIIs : M/s Polaris Software Lab Limited    
The Reserve Bank of India has today notified that the aggregate net 
purchases of equity shares in M/s Polaris Software Lab Limited by Foreign 
Institutional Investors (FIIs) have reached the trigger limit. Therefore, no further 
purchases of equity shares of this company would be allowed through stock 
exchanges in India on behalf of FIIs.
