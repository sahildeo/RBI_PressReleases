Monthly Data on India’s International Trade in Services  
for the Month of May 2011 
The Reserve Bank has started releasing provisional aggregate monthly data 
on India's international trade in services with a lag of 45 days. Accordingly, for the 
first time, the data for April 2011 was released on June 15, 2011. 
The exports and imports of international services during the month of May 
2011 are given in the following Table.   
Table: International Trade in Services 
(US$ million)
Month Receipts (Exports) Payments (Imports) 
April 2011 11,469 6,889 
May 2011 11,835 7,088 
Note: Data are provisional. 
 
 These provisional data will undergo revision when the Balance of Payments 
(BoP) data are compiled on a quarterly basis which will be released with a lag of a 
quarter. The quarterly release will also provide disaggregated data on trade in 
services.
