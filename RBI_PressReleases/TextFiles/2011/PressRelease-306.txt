November 11, 2011 
Auction of Dated securities scheduled for November 11, 2011 –  
Rejection of bids in the auction of 7.99% Govt. Stock 2017 
The Government of India have announced the sale (re-issue) of (i) “7.99 
percent Government Stock 2017” for a notified amount of ` 4,000 crore (nominal) 
through price based auctions, (ii) “New 13 Year Government Stock” for a notified 
amount of ` 6,000 crore (nominal) through yield based auctions, and (iii) “8.30 
percent Government Stock 2040” for a notified amount of ` 3,000 crore (nominal) 
through price based auctions on November 11, 2011. The Government of India in 
consultation with the Reserve Bank of India has rejected all the bids submitted only 
in the auction for 7.99 % Government Stock 2017.
