May 18, 2011 
 
RBI releases CD-ROM on Select Financial Data  
of Private Corporate Business Sector in India 
 
The Reserve Bank of India today released 'Compendium on Private 
Corporate Business Sector in India - Select Financial Statistics' in a DVD-ROM.  
 
The compendium, the fifth in the series, is an attempt to present, at one 
place, data on company finances from the various periodical studies up to the 
financial year 2008-09. The DVD-ROM consists of two parts. Part one comprises 
five chapters that briefly describe the scope of the studies, sources of data, 
methodology, concepts and definitions adopted for the studies. Part two contains 
statistical statements. The basic source of data is balance sheet, profit and loss 
account and other material contained in the annual accounts of the companies.  
 
The compendium is available on RBI website (http://www.rbi.org.in). The 
compendium in DVD-ROM is also available from Sales Section, Division of Reports 
and Information Dissemination, Department of Economic and Policy Research, 
Reserve Bank of India, Amar Building, P.M. Road, Mumbai-400001. Order for the 
purchase can be placed on payment through DD/Cheque/Pay Order drawn in favour 
of the Reserve Bank of India, payable at Mumbai. The DVD-ROM is priced at: ` 350 
(Postage free) in India and US $ 70 (Postage free) for abroad. 
 
The Reserve Bank of India (Department of Statistics and Information 
Management) has been publishing studies on the financial performance of the 
corporate sector since 1951. The data contained in these studies have been 
consolidated and brought out as special publications at periodic intervals. So far, 
four such publications with the last one in September 2001 (covering the period 
1974-75 to 1999-2000) have been brought out.
