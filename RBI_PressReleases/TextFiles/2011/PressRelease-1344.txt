May 5, 2011 
The Limbasi Urban Co-operative Bank Limited,  
Limbasi, Dist. Kheda – Penalised 
The Reserve Bank of India has imposed a monetary penalty of ` 1.00 lakh 
(Rupees one lakh only) on The Limbasi Urban Co-operative Bank Limited, Limbasi 
Dist. Kheda, Gujarat, in exercise of powers vested in it under the provisions of 
Section 47(A)(1)(b) read with Section 46(4) of the Banking Regulation Act, 1949 
(AACS) for violation of Reserve Bank of India's instructions for reporting of cash 
transactions in excess of ` 10.00 lakh to Financial Intelligence Unit-India (FIU-IND), 
as required under the Anti Money Laundering (AML) guidelines. 
The Reserve Bank of India had issued a show cause notice to the bank, in 
response to which the bank submitted a written reply. After considering the facts of 
the case and the bank's reply and also personal submissions in the matter, the 
Reserve Bank of India came to the conclusion that the violations were substantiated 
and warranted imposition of the penalty.
