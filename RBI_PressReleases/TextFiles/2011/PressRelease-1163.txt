June 10, 2011 
 
Issue of ` 100/- denomination banknotes with inset letter "L"  
in both numbering panels in Mahatma Gandhi Series  
bearing the signature of Dr. D. Subbarao, Governor 
 
The Reserve Bank of India will shortly issue ` 100/- denomination banknotes 
with inset letter "L" in both numbering panels in Mahatma Gandhi Series with the 
signature of Dr. D. Subbarao, Governor, Reserve Bank of India and the year 2011 
printed on the back of the Bank note.  Except for the change in the inset letter, the 
design of these notes to be issued now is similar in all respects to the banknotes in 
Mahatma Gandhi Series 2005 issued earlier. All banknotes in the denomination of     
` 100/- issued by the Bank in the past will continue to be legal tender.
