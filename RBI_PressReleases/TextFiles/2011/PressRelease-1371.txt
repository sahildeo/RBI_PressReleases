RBI Governor's Monetary Policy Statement to be telecast Live 
The Reserve Bank of India will, on May 3, 2011, telecast live Governor, Dr. D. 
Subbarao's statement to bankers on Monetary Policy for the year 2011-12. The 
live telecast of the Governor's statement will commence from 11.00 a.m. The 
statement would also be live webcast on the Reserve Bank's website 
(www.rbi.org.in). The full Policy document will be released on RBI's website at 
around 11.20 a.m. Live telecast of the Governor’s statement is one more of 
Reserve Bank’s initiatives in transparency in communication.  
In the recent past, the Reserve Bank has taken several initiatives in the 
procedure of announcing the Monetary Policy to bring greater effectiveness in its 
dissemination. Some of these include -  
• Webcasting the Governor’s press conference and placing its audio recording 
and transcript on the Reserve Bank’s website. 
• Facilitating media interaction with the Reserve Bank from across the globe 
through teleconference. 
• Interaction with researchers/analysts on Monetary Policy through 
teleconference and placing its audio recording and transcript on the Reserve 
Bank's website.
