IDBI Bank Limited Authorised as Primary Dealer 
 
 
IDBI Bank Limited has been authorised to undertake Primary Dealer business 
in Government securities market with effect from Wednesday, April 20, 2011 and, 
concomitantly, the authorisation given to IDBI Gilts Limited as Primary Dealer stands 
withdrawn.
