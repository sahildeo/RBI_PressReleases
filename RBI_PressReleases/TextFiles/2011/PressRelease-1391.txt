April 28, 2011 
Investment by FIIs/NRIs/PIOs under PIS : M/s. Lupin Limited 
 The Reserve Bank of India today notified that M/s. Lupin Limited has agreed 
to enhance the limit for the purchase of its equity shares and convertible debentures 
by Foreign Institutional Investors (FIIs) under Portfolio Investment Scheme (PIS), 
through the primary market and stock exchanges, under the portfolio investment 
scheme (PIS)  from 24 per cent to 33 per cent of its paid up capital. The company 
has passed resolutions at the Board of Directors' level and resolution at Extra 
Ordinary General Body Meeting. 
 Accordingly, the equity shares and convertible debentures of the above 
company can now be purchased through primary market and stock exchanges 
provided :  
(i) The overall limit for FII's purchases is raised to 33 per cent.  
(ii) The purchases of equity shares by a single Foreign Institutional Investor 
(FII)/Security and Exchange Board of India (SEBI) approved sub-account of a 
registered FII in the company does not exceed 10% (ten per cent) of the paid-
up equity capital of the company.
