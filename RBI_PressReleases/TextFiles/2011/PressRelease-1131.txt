June 16, 2011 
 
Shri P. Vijaya Bhaskar and Shri B. Mahapatra  
take charge as New EDs at RBI 
Shri P. Vijaya Bhaskar assumed charge as Executive Director of the Reserve 
Bank of India on June 13, 2011. As Executive Director, Shri Bhaskar will look after 
Central Security Cell, Department of Banking Supervision and Department of Non-
Banking Supervision. 
Prior to his appointment as Executive Director, Shri Bhaskar was the Regional 
Director of Karnataka. 
Shri Bhaskar holds a Masters Degree in Commerce, Post Graduate Diploma 
in Business Management, Pragya and CAIIB. 
Shri B. Mahapatra assumed charge as Executive Director of the Reserve 
Bank of India on June 13, 2011.  As Executive Director, Shri Mahapatra will look 
after Department of Banking Operations and Development, Department of 
Government and Bank Accounts, Inspection Department and Legal Department. 
Prior to his appointment as Executive Director, Shri Mahapatra was Chief 
General Manager-in-Charge of Department of Banking Operations and 
Development. 
Shri Mahapatra holds a Masters Degree in English, an M.B.A. (Finance), 
M.S.M. (Finance), Pragya and CAIIB.
