December 26, 2011 
 
Issue of  ` 500 Banknotes with incorporation of  Rupee symbol (`) 
 
The Reserve Bank of India will shortly issue ` 500  denomination  Banknotes 
incorporating " `" symbol, without  inset letter,  in the  Mahatma Gandhi Series-2005  
bearing the  signature  of Dr. D. Subbarao, Governor , Reserve Bank of India, and  
the year of printing 2011 printed on the back of the Banknote.  
 
The design of these notes to be issued now is similar in all respects to the 
Banknotes of `500 in Mahatma Gandhi Series-2005 issued earlier except for the      
` symbol.  
 
All the Banknotes in the denomination of `500 issued by the Bank in the past 
will continue to be legal tender.
