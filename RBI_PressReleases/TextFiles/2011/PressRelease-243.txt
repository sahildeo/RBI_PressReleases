November 22, 2011 
OMO Purchase of Government Securities 
Further to RBI Press Release dated November 16, 2011, wherein it was stated that 
the Reserve Bank of India would conduct OMO purchase auctions of ` 10,000 crore on 
November 24, 2011, Reserve Bank has decided to purchase the following government 
securities through auction for an aggregate amount up to ` 10,000 crore through multi-
security auction using the multiple price method:                       
Sr. No Security Maturity Date 
1. 7.99% GS 2017 July 9, 2017 
2. 7.83% GS 2018 April 11, 2018 
3. 7.80% GS 2021 April 11, 2021 
4. 8.13% GS 2022 September  21, 2022 
The auction shall be conducted by the Reserve Bank of India, Mumbai Office, Fort, 
Mumbai on November 24, 2011 (Thursday). 
Market participants may take note of the following: 
There is an overall aggregate ceiling of ` 10,000 crore for all the securities in the 
basket put together. There is no security-wise notified amount. 
RBI reserves the right to: 
• Decide on the quantum of purchase of individual securities.  
• Accept less than the aggregate amount of ` 10,000 crore. 
•   Purchase marginally higher than the aggregate amount due to rounding effects.  
•   Accept or reject any or all of the offers either wholly or partially if deemed fit 
without assigning reason. 
Offers in the prescribed form obtainable from the Regional Director, Reserve Bank of 
India, Mumbai Office, Fort,  Mumbai-400001 and / or RBI website (www.rbi.org.in) should be 
submitted to that Office on November 24, 2011 (Thursday). The NDS members should 
submit their competitive offers in electronic format using Open Market Operation (OMO) 
module of NDS. All offers should be submitted by 12.30 P.M. 
The result of the auction will be announced on the same day and payment to 
successful offerers will be made during banking hours on November 25, 2011 
(Friday). Successful offerers may note to make available the requisite amount of securities in 
their SGL account in the forenoon on November 25, 2011.
