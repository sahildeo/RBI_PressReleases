June 20, 2011 
 
Smt. Shyamala Gopinath lays down office as Deputy Governor 
 
Smt. Shyamala Gopinath, Deputy Governor relinquished the office of Deputy 
Governor of the Reserve Bank of India in the forenoon today. Smt. Gopinath was 
appointed Deputy Governor of the Reserve Bank of India on September 21, 2004 for 
a period of five years. On completion of her term, she was re-appointed for a further 
period up to June 20, 2011. 
 
Consequent to Smt. Gopinath's laying down of office, the portfolios of the
